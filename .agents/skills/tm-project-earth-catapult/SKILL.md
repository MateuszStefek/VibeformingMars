# Project: Earth Catapult (#066)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 23 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** You pay 2 MC less for all cards you play (including this one).
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (S-Tier Engine Cornerstone)
**Status: S-TIER / AUTO-KEEP**

Earth Catapult is arguably the strongest engine-building card in the game. It provides a universal discount that scales throughout the match.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Universal Discount:** Reducing the cost of *every* card by 2 MC is massive. In a typical engine game where 30+ cards are played, this nets 60+ MC in savings.
2. **Immediate Return:** Unlike production cards, the benefit is immediate upon playing the next card.
3. **Synergies:** 
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers card draw on the Earth tag.
   - **Teractor / Vitor / Credicor:** Benefits from Earth tag discounts or VP-related bonuses.
   - **Mars University:** Can be used to cycle unwanted cards to find more targets for the discount.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requires Card Draw:** A discount is useless if your hand is empty. Pair this with AI Central, Mars University, or Development Center.
- **Timing:** Generally best played in Generation 1 or 2. If drawn after Generation 5, calculate if the 2 VP + remaining discounts justify the 26 MC cost.
- **Rush Vulnerability:** If opponents are "rushing" global parameters, spending 26 MC on an engine might leave you too far behind in TR and board presence.

### Tips
- **Hate Drafting:** If you see an opponent building a science/draw engine, do not pass them Earth Catapult. Take it yourself even if you don't intend to play it immediately.

**Recommendation:** If in opening hand, prioritize playing this in Generation 1. If drawn late, assess card count vs. generations remaining.
