# Prelude: Polar Industries

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 2 steps. Place 1 Ocean tile.
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Board Control Anchor)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER OPENER**

Polar Industries is a high-efficiency Prelude used to secure early board dominance and established a terraforming foundation. While it provides zero liquid income, its combination of heat production and ocean placement makes it a premier tool for both "Rush" strategies and biological engine catalysts.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 2 heat production is worth ~12 MC, and the ocean placement (1 TR + bonus) is worth ~14 MC. At a total value of **~26 MC**, it is significantly more efficient than the standard Prelude benchmark (~21 MC).
2. **Placement Bonus Sniping:** Use the immediate ocean placement to "swipe" a high-value bonus spot (like the **2-card draw** spot on Tharsis or the **3-plant** spot on Elysium) before any other player has a chance to take their first action.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Helion:** The premier synergy. Helion can use the heat production as MC, effectively turning the Prelude into a **+2 MC production** and **1 ocean** start.
   - **Ecoline:** Jumpstarts the temperature track and ocean requirements needed to fuel their greenery engine.
   - **Mining Guild:** The **Building tag** triggers the corporation's passive if the ocean is placed on a metal reserve.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost upon placement.
4. **Requirement Enabler:** Instantly helps hit the requirements for powerful early-game cards like **Arctic Algae** or **Kelp Farming**.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Heat production is slow-burn value. If your starting hand is full of expensive engine cards, Polar Industries may "stall" your economic growth compared to a pure MC production card.
- **Heat Trappers Target:** Having exactly 2 heat production makes you a prime target for the **Heat Trappers** event, which can wipe out your production early and leave you with a -1 VP penalty.

### Tips
- **1st Player Advantage:** If you are the first player, Polar Industries is almost an "Auto-Pick." The ability to take the best ocean bonus on the board is a massive advantage that opponents cannot block.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Safe" pick for any strategy. High priority for **Helion**, **Ecoline**, or anyone Pursuing the **Gardener** milestone. skip only if your hand is strictly focused on a long-term science/Jovian engine.
