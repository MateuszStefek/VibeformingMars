# Project: Beam from a Thorium Asteroid (#041)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 32 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** 1 Jovian Tag.
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 3 steps. Increase your Energy production 3 steps.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The High-Energy Hybrid)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES HANDS**

Beam from a Thorium Asteroid is a massive infrastructure card that provides a unique blend of terraforming progress and industrial power. While its high cost and tag requirement make it difficult to play, its role as a "trading fleet enabler" makes it a top-tier tool in specific expansions.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Infrastructure Yield:** +3 Energy and +3 Heat production is worth ~40 MC of value. Even accounting for the 35 MC investment (cost + draft), the card is "profit-generating" from the moment it is played, providing a stable foundation for the rest of the match.
2. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, this card is prized primarily for the 3 energy production. It provides the exact amount needed to power a full trade fleet, allowing you to begin trading with high-value moons like *Luna* or *Ceres* immediately.
3. **Space Tag Utility:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This makes it a premier way for corporations like **Phobolog** to convert their starting metal into a permanent industrial empire.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** The absolute best pairing. You start with the Jovian tag requirement met and gain +1 MC production upon play.
   - **Credicor:** Triggers the 4 MC rebate for high-cost cards, significantly improving the card's efficiency.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the Titanium conversion even more effective.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** You **must** have 1 Jovian tag already in play. This prevents the card from being a Generation 1 opener unless you have a Jovian-tag Prelude or Corporation.
- **Timing:** To get the full ROI, you **must play this by Generation 3**. If played after Generation 6, the production will not have time to pay off, and you are better off spending 35 MC on direct points or terraforming.
- **Speculation Risk:** **Do not hold this early** if you have zero Jovian tags and zero path to them. Tying up 3 MC of draft capital for a card that may never hit the table is a common mistake.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop the Jovian requirement to 0, allowing you to play the Beam in Gen 1 to jumpstart your trading economy.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Saturn Systems**, **Phobolog**, or anyone playing with *Colonies*. **WARNING:** This is an illegal play in Gen 1 unless you start with a Jovian tag (via Corporation or Prelude). Skip if you lack a path to Jovian tags or if the temperature is already moving rapidly.
