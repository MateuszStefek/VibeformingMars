# Prelude: Society Support

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Increase your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Heat production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Generalist Trap)
**Status: F-TIER / THE WEAKEST PRELUDE**

Society Support is widely considered the worst Prelude in the game. It provides an unfocused set of benefits while simultaneously hurting your most important resource (MC production). While it helps toward specific milestones, its long-term economic impact is almost always negative.

### Why it is usually a "Skip":
1. **Unfocused Value:** It provides a tiny bit of everything (Plant, Energy, Heat) but not enough of any single stat to define a winning engine. You are spreading your value too thin to be effective.
2. **Economic Decay:** Decreasing your MC production in Generation 1 effectively costs you ~10 MC over the course of the game. For most corporations, this "lost snowball" prevents them from established more efficient industrial engines.
3. **Efficiency Paradox:** 1 plant production takes 6-8 generations to produce a single greenery. 1 energy production is useless without a sink. 1 heat production is a negligible contribution to the temperature track.
4. **Opportunity Cost:** Compared to any S-tier Prelude (+4 MC prod or +2 Titanium prod), Society Support is mathematically inferior in every category.

### When to Play It (Rare Situations):
- **The "Generalist" Milestone:** The only common high-level reason to pick this card. On the **Elysium map**, it provides three of the six required production tracks for the *Generalist* milestone. If combined with a corporation like *Thorgate* or *Mining Guild*, you can potentially claim the milestone in Generation 1 or 2.
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the 1 energy and 1 plant production to hit the requirements for a very high-value card in your hand that you otherwise couldn't play.

### Tips
- **Draft Priority:** Treat Society Support as a "last resort." If your other Prelude option is something that actively ruins your hand (like *Loan* without a god-target), only then should you consider the slow value of the support.

**Recommendation:** Almost always a "Never-Pick." Only take if you are strictly Pursuing the **Generalist** milestone on Elysium. skip early in favor of pure economic production. slightly more viable in Solo mode as a way to fix multiple parameter tracks.
