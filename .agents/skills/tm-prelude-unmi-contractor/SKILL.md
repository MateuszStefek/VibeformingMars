# Prelude: UNMI Contractor

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Terraform Rating (TR) 3 steps. Draw 1 card.
- **Tag:** Earth.

## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Accelerator)
**Status: S-TIER / THE VERSATILE POWERHOUSE**

UNMI Contractor is widely considered one of the strongest and most flexible Preludes in the game. It provides a massive surge in both score and income from the very first generation, ensuring a stable foundation for any strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 3 TR increase is worth ~24 MC in score and income (8 MC per TR). Combined with a 1-card draw and a high-value tag, its total value is **~30 MC**, which is well above the Prelude benchmark (~21 MC).
2. **Economic Stability:** Provides a permanent **+3 MC production** (via TR) for zero upfront cost. This ensures you have a reliable liquid income base to fund any engine track you choose.
3. **Earth Tag Catalyst:** The tag is a premier asset.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers a free card draw immediately upon play.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Helps satisfy the "Earth tag" requirement for powerful cards like *Cartels* or *Earth Office* in Generation 1.
4. **Milestone Rushing:** Essential for the **Terraformer** milestone (reaching 26 TR). On the **Tharsis map**, playing this card in Gen 1 gives you a massive head start that is difficult for opponents to catch.
5. **Solo Mode Essential:** Consistently rated as the #1 Prelude for TR-based solo runs.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Industrial Yield:** Provides zero metal or energy production. If your starting hand is full of expensive industrial cards (*Strip Mine*, *Space Elevator*), you may find yourself with high income but no industrial "sinks" to spend it on efficiently.
- **Information Neutrality:** Like *Donation*, it doesn't "lock" you into a specific strategy. Only skip if you have a "high-roll" synergy (e.g., *Ecology Experts* + *Kelp Farming*) that defines the game end faster than the income can pay off.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Value:** Use the 3 MC income increase to fund a critical Generation 1 card like **Research Outpost** or **Acquired Company**. UNMI Contractor is often the "grease" that allows a complex engine to function without needing to sell cards.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" in almost any scenario. Priority 1 for **Point Luna**, **UNMI**, or **Vitor**. Best choice for players who want a reliable, high-income start without being locked into a specific board strategy.
