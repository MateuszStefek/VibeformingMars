# Project: Big Asteroid (#011)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 27 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 2 steps. Gain 4 Titanium. Remove up to 4 plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Rush King)
**Status: S-TIER / PREMIER TERRAFORMING EVENT**

Big Asteroid is one of the most efficient and high-impact cards for players pursuing a "Rush" strategy. It provides a significant temperature jump while rebating a large portion of its cost in high-value resources.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Resource Rebate:** The 4 Titanium you receive is worth 12 MC. This brings the effective cost of the 2-step temperature jump down to only 15 MC (+3 to draft). 
2. **Temperature Control:** Temperature is often the hardest global parameter to move without a dedicated heat engine. Big Asteroid allows you to force progress on this track without investing in long-term production.
3. **Tactical Plant Removal:** Removing 4 plants is enough to destroy half a greenery. In the 2024-2025 meta, this is often used to deny an opponent their final plants needed for a conversion, buying you critical time.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking:** Provides 3 MC and 3 Heat, making the card nearly cost-neutral and accelerating the temperature track even further.
   - **Media Group:** Provides a 3 MC rebate.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows you to pay for the large upfront cost with titanium, while receiving titanium back as a rebate for future plays.
   - **Bonus Sniping:** Perfect for jumping over an opponent to claim the -24°C (Heat Production) or 0°C (Ocean) bonuses.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Titanium Efficiency:** The card's value is tied to the 12 MC worth of titanium it provides. If you have no space cards to spend that titanium on, you are effectively overpaying for 2 TR.
- **Engine Counter-Productivity:** If you are building a long-term engine, playing Big Asteroid too early can be a mistake as it accelerates the game's end, giving your engine less time to outscale opponents.

### Tips
- **Plant Destruction Threshold:** Do not play this immediately just for the TR. Wait until an opponent has at least 4 plants to maximize the "take-that" value and protect your own board presence.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Always-Draft" for almost any strategy. High priority for titanium-heavy engines and terraforming rushers. Essential for controlling the pace of the game.
