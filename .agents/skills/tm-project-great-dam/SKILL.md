# Project: Great Dam (#077)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** 4 Ocean Tiles
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 2 steps.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Mid-Game Power Pivot)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / MID-GAME ENABLER**

Great Dam is a reliable, mid-tier energy card that excels in providing infrastructure for late-game cities or card draw engines. While its ocean requirement pushes it out of the early game, its combination of production and points makes it a solid performer.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **High Infrastructure Yield:** For 12 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 2 energy production and 1 VP. This 7.5 MC per energy ratio is competitive, especially when accounting for the points.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it an excellent way to convert accumulated building resources into energy and points.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking high-tier requirements like *AI Central* or *Power Supply Consortium*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Colonies Expansion:** Provides 2/3 of the energy needed for a trade fleet, which is vital for securing late-game resources from colonies.
   - **Engine Support:** The perfect fuel for "power sinks" like *Strip Mine* or card-draw engines like *Development Center*.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 4-ocean requirement can arrive surprisingly late in slow 2-player games. Do not hold this card from Generation 1 unless you have a path to rapid ocean placement.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you need energy in Generation 1-2 for early trading or card draw, you should prioritize requirement-free cards like *Geothermal Power* or *Power Plant* instead.

### Tips
- **Milestone Race:** Use the 2-step energy boost to reach the 6-energy production requirement for the **Energizer** milestone before an opponent can close the gap.
- **Big Box Variant:** If playing with the Big Box tile variant, use the placement bonus to net 2-4 MC in ocean rebates, significantly improving the card's efficiency.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 4-7 once the ocean track is moving. High priority if you have a surplus of Steel or need a Power tag for a specific requirement. Skip early if you need immediate infrastructure.
