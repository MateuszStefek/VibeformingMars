# Project: Adaptive Bacteria (#028)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Foundation)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER SYNERGY ENABLER**

Adaptive Bacteria is a foundational card for biological engines. While its raw production efficiency is average, its combination of two critical tags with zero requirements makes it a premier early-game pick for players pursuing a bio-focused strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unconditional Bio-Tag:** It is one of the few cards that provides both a **Science** and a **Microbe** tag in Generation 1. This is essential for immediately satisfying requirements for cards like **Advanced Ecosystems** or triggering **Decomposers**.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** 
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University*.
   - **Microbe:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Insects*, and *Advanced Ecosystems*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate plant when played, effectively reducing the card's cost and accelerating your first greenery.
   - **Splice Tactical Genomics:** Triggers Splice's corporation ability for an immediate resource or card advantage.
   - **Ecologist / Scientist Award:** Contributes toward two high-value endgame scoring categories.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At 12 MC (+3 draft) for 1 plant production, you are paying **15 MC per step**. This is statistically worse than *Heather* or *Lichen*. Only play this early if you have the synergies to justify the premium.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** 1 plant production makes you a target for plant-destruction cards (*Birds*, *Virus*) without providing enough volume to quickly convert to a greenery.
- **Late Game Waste:** Playing Adaptive Bacteria after Generation 5-6 is usually poor, as the production won't have time to pay off and the tags are no longer needed for requirements.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Adaptive Bacteria as your "first" microbe card. It provides a tag and production to help you reach the requirements for more powerful, restricted microbe cards like *Nitrite Reducing Bacteria* or *GHG Bacteria*.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for bio-engineers or anyone needing a cheap Science/Microbe tag. Excellent opening hand card. Skip late if you have zero bio-engine plans.
