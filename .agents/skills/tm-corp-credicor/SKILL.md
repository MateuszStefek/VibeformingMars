# Corporation: Credicor

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 57
- **Ability:** After you pay for a card or standard project with a base cost of 20 or more MC, you get 4 MC back.

## Strategic Analysis (The Heavy Hitter)
**Status: S-TIER / VERSATILE**

Credicor is one of the most reliable and powerful corporations in the base game. Its high starting capital and consistent rebate on expensive plays allow it to maintain a high tempo and dominate the board.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Standard Project Rush:** With the 'Standard Technology' card, SP Greeneries effectively cost 16 MC and Cities cost 18 MC. This makes Credicor a powerhouse for board control.
2. **Big Event Specialist:** Credicor excels at playing high-impact "red" events like 'Giant Ice Asteroid' or 'Deimos Down'. These cards become significantly more efficient with the 4 MC rebate.
3. **High Starting Capital:** 57 MC is among the highest in the game, providing a massive Gen 1 snowball potential.

### Synergies:
- **Standard Technology:** The core synergy for a board-focused strategy.
- **Earth Catapult / Research Outpost:** These reduce costs, but be careful not to drop the base cost below 20 MC if you want the rebate (though the rebate is usually based on the *printed* cost before discounts in most digital versions, check local rules). *Correction: In the official rules, it is the 'printed' cost.*
- **Media Group:** Makes expensive events even cheaper.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Milestone Race:** Use your early cash to secure 'Terraformer' or 'Generalist'. Credicor's ability to play big cards makes it easy to branch into different resource types.
- **Tempo Management:** Don't hold too much cash. Credicor wins by putting big pieces on the board early.

**Recommendation:** An excellent pick for any player. High priority if you have expensive, high-impact cards in your starting hand.
