# Project: Lunar Beam (#030)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Power
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Heat production 2 steps. Increase your Energy production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Pivot)
**Status: B-TIER INFRASTRUCTURE / A-TIER IN COLONIES**

Lunar Beam is a powerful infrastructure card that "trades" long-term liquid currency (MC) for immediate industrial production (Heat and Energy). It is a high-impact early-game play for players looking to jumpstart their terraforming engine.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Yield:** For 13 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 4 total production steps. 2 Energy and 2 Heat is a massive boost that normally costs ~25-30 MC via standard projects.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both **Earth** and **Power** tags.
   - **Earth:** Triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and *Cartels* for MC production.
   - **Power:** Helps fulfill requirements for high-tier oxygen cards and provides *Diversifier* progress.
3. **Colonies Enabler:** The 2 Energy production is 2/3 of a trade action. In the 2024-2025 meta, Lunar Beam is a premier card for securing early-game trades with high-value colonies.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Production Penalty:** Decreasing your MC production by 2 steps in Generation 1 is a heavy price. It effectively adds ~18-20 MC to the "true" cost of the card over a 10-generation game. Only play this if you have a clear plan to utilize the energy (trading or card draw) immediately.
- **Heat Lag:** While 2 Heat production is good, it takes 4 generations to yield a single TR. If the game is moving fast, the heat may not pay off in time.

### Tips
- **Phobolog / Advanced Alloys:** If you are paying with Titanium (via the Space tag on other cards) but using Lunar Beam for the production, you can build a massive engine without ever spending your liquid MC.
- **Vitor:** As a non-negative VP-icon card (wait, it has no VP icon). Unlike *Research*, this card does not trigger Vitor.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if playing with *Colonies* or if you have *Point Luna*. Best played in Generations 1-3. Skip in the late game.
