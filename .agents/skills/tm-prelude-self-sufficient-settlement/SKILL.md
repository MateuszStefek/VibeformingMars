# Prelude: Self-Sufficient Settlement

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Place a City tile.
- **Tags:** Building, City.

## Strategic Analysis (The Reliable Urbanizer)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR THARSIS REPUBLIC**

Self-Sufficient Settlement is a solid, mid-tier Prelude that provides a balanced foundation for board-heavy strategies. While it offers a modest income boost, its city placement and tag combination make it a premier tool for securing early board dominance.

### Why it is a good pick:
1. **Exceptional Board Impact:** A city tile in Generation 1 is worth ~14 MC of value. It secures a high-value board spot (like a "2 titanium" or "2 plant" bonus) and puts you 1/3 of the way toward the **Mayor** milestone immediately.
2. **Economic Stability:** Provides **+2 MC production** without any penalties. This ensures you have a stable liquid income base while also establishing board presence.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Building** and a **City** tag in Generation 1.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to trigger building-related discounts or contribute toward the **Builder** milestone early.
   - **City Tag:** Contributes toward the **Mayor** milestone and triggers "whenever a city is played" effects.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Tharsis Republic:** The ultimate pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive income gain and secures board control for a very low net cost.
   - **Rover Construction / Pets:** Triggers these board engines for early resource generation.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Production Volume:** +2 MC production is a modest boost. If you aren't building a city-heavy engine, a pure income Prelude like *Allied Bank* (+4 production) is strictly superior.
- **Placement Logic:** Do not place the city in a remote corner where you cannot easily build greeneries around it. Its point potential is tied to the forest chain you established later.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Self-Sufficient Settlement specifically to hit the Building requirements for **Research Outpost** in Generation 1. This accelerates your industrial rollout by a full generation.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Tharsis Republic** or anyone Pursuing the **Mayor** milestone. skip early in favor of pure production if you have zero interest in the board game.
