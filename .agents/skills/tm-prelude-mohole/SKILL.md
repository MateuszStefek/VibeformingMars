# Prelude: Mohole

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 3 steps. Gain 3 Heat.
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Rush Foundation)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR HELION**

Mohole is a high-efficiency terraforming Prelude used primarily to jumpstart a "Rush" strategy. While it provides zero liquid income, its massive boost to the temperature track makes it a premier tool for players looking to end the game quickly and secure heat-related milestones.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Heat Yield:** +3 Heat production is worth ~18 MC of value, and the 3 starting heat adds another ~4 MC. At a total value of ~22 MC, it is one of the more efficient Preludes for established terraforming engines.
2. **Helion Synergy:** The "God Tier" start. Since Helion can use heat as money, Mohole effectively becomes a **+3 MC production** Prelude with **3 MC starting cash**, while also contributing to their terraforming engine.
3. **The "Robotic Workforce" Target:** Provides a Building-tagged production box. Copying Mohole with *Robotic Workforce* in Generation 1 or 2 is a high-efficiency move that provides a total of +6 heat production for a very low combined cost.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Building tag and the heat production needed to secure the **Specialist** milestone or the **Thermalist** award early.
5. **Solo Mode Essential:** Heat is often the hardest parameter to max out in Solo play. Mohole ensures you have a steady path to the temperature goal from the very first turn.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Heat can only be used to raise temperature (unless you are Helion). If you don't have a plan to end the game quickly, the capital tied up in Mohole is "dead weight" that doesn't snowball your economy.
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Mohole accelerates the game for everyone. By raising the temperature track, you may accidentally help your opponents play high-tier plant cards (like *Trees*) before you are ready to compete for the board.
- **Odd-Number Breakpoint:** 3 heat production is slightly awkward because you need 8 heat to raise the temperature. Pair this with another 1-step heat card (like *Lichen*) to hit the 4-heat per generation mark.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Value:** Only take Mohole if your other Prelude or Corporation already supports a "Rush" strategy. If your hand is full of slow-burn science or Jovian cards, a pure income Prelude is strictly superior.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Helion** or anyone Pursuing a "Rush" strategy. Priority 1 in **Solo Mode** or if holding **Robotic Workforce**. Skip early if you are building a long-term economic engine.
