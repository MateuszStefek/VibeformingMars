# Project: Industrial Center (#126)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 7 MC to increase your Steel production 1 step.
- **Effect:** Place an Industrial Center tile adjacent to a City tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Strategic Teleport)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER BLOCKER**

Industrial Center is one of the most underrated utility cards in the game. While its repeatable action is statistically inefficient, its primary value lies in its unique tile placement, which allows for aggressive board control and "teleporting" across the map.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Aggressive Blocking:** The Industrial Center tile can be placed adjacent to *any* city, including an opponent's. This allows you to "poach" high-value spots that an opponent was saving for a greenery, effectively denying them 1 VP and a TR while securing a placement bonus for yourself.
2. **Cheap Building Tag:** At only 4 MC (+3 to draft), it is one of the most affordable building tags in the game. This is critical for hitting the 8-building requirement for the **Builder** milestone or for contributing to the **Industrialist** award.
3. **Map Presence:** It acts as a "teleport," allowing you to establish a foothold in lucrative, ocean-heavy areas of the board without needing to "walk" your tiles there from your starting city.
4. **Placement Rebates:** If placed on a spot with 2 steel or 2 titanium, the card effectively pays for itself immediately. If adjacent to oceans, you receive 2 MC per ocean in rebates.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Inefficient Action:** Spending 7 MC to increase steel production is generally poor value. It is the same price as a *Mine* card but requires an action and the initial card investment. Only use the action in Generations 1-2 if you have zero other ways to spend your capital.
- **Requirement Constraint:** You **must** have a city on the board to place the tile. While easy in the mid-game, it can be difficult in the very early game unless someone is playing Tharsis Republic.

### Tips
- **Tharsis Counter:** Use Industrial Center to take a high-value spot next to a Tharsis player's city. Since they build many cities, you will have plenty of targets to block their greenery paths.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft for board control. High priority for **Mining Guild** or anyone racing for the **Builder** milestone. Use primarily for the tile placement and tag rather than the steel action. Skip late if the board is already maxed.
