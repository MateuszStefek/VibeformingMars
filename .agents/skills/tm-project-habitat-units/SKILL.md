# Project: Habitat Units (#035)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Plant
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for each Earth tag you have (including this one).
- **VP:** 1 VP for every 2 Plant, Microbe, or Animal tags you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Multiplier)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER BIO-PAYOFF**

Habitat Units is a high-ceiling hybrid card that rewards players for specializing in both Earth and biological strategies. While its upfront cost is high, its dual scaling makes it a premier point-scorer in the final generations of a diversified engine game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Point Density:** In a dedicated biological build (Plant, Microbe, and Animal tags), this card is frequently worth **4-6+ VP**. This makes it more point-efficient than almost any non-multiplier card in the late game.
2. **Economic Synergy:** The immediate MC production boost from Earth tags helps recoup the 19 MC investment (+3 to draft) if played in the mid-game.
3. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both an **Earth** and a **Plant** tag.
   - **Earth:** Triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and *Cartels* for production.
   - **Plant:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Ecological Zone*.
4. **Milestone Powerhouse:** Contributes to the **Ecologist**, **Gardener**, and **Benefactor** milestones/awards simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Timing:** **Do not play this early.** Playing Habitat Units in Generations 1-4 is usually a mistake, as the 19 MC is better spent on pure production. It is a "payoff" card that works best when your tag count is already high.
- **Vacuum Value:** In a vacuum (zero other bio/earth tags), you are paying 19 MC for 1 production and 0 VP. Only draft if you have a path to at least 6 relevant tags total.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you are racing for the *Gardener* milestone, ensure the 16 MC spent here wouldn't be better used on a greenery conversion instead.

### Tips
- **Stealth Point-Slam:** Hold Habitat Units until the final generation. Opponents often forget to account for tag-based multipliers when calculating the score, allowing you to secure a surprise victory in the final round.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-9). High priority for players with *Point Luna* or a strong biological engine. Skip early unless you need a cheap Earth/Plant tag for a specific requirement.
