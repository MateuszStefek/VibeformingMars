# Project: Olympus Conference (#185)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Earth, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play a Science tag (including this one), either add 1 Science resource to this card, or remove 1 Science resource from this card to draw 1 card.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Filter)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / TOP-TIER DRAW SOURCE**

Olympus Conference is one of the most powerful draw engines in the game. It provides a steady source of net card advantage and acts as the "little brother" to *Mars University*, often forming an unstoppable science-focused deck.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Source of Net Card Advantage:** Unlike *Mars University* (which swaps cards), Olympus Conference actually increases your hand size. Over a 10-generation game, it can easily draw 3-5+ extra cards, providing the volume needed to find win-conditions.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Provides **Science**, **Earth**, and **Building** tags.
   - **Science:** Triggers its own effect and helps unlock high-tier requirements like *AI Central*.
   - **Earth:** Synergies with *Earth Office* for discounts and *Cartel* for production.
   - **Building:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it an extremely cheap engine starter.
3. **Self-Triggering:** The card triggers on its own tag. When you play Olympus Conference, you add one resource immediately. You only need one more Science tag to draw your first free card.
4. **Vitor Synergy:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **"Charging" Period:** It takes two Science tags to get one card draw (1 to add, 1 to remove). If you have zero other Science tags in hand, the 13 MC (cost + draft) is a very slow investment.
- **Olympus vs. Mars University:** If you have both, the community debate favors playing **Olympus first** to grow your hand size. However, if your hand is full of "garbage," play *Mars University* first to filter into better tags.
- **Action Economy:** Unlike blue action cards, Olympus triggers passively. This makes it more efficient than *Restricted Area* or *Development Center* because it doesn't consume one of your two actions per turn.

### Tips
- **CEO's Favorite Combo:** Use **CEO's Favorite Project** to add a resource to Olympus Conference. This allows you to draw a card on your very next Science play without needing to "waste" a tag on the initial add-resource step.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for any engine-building strategy. High priority in Generations 1-4. Skip only if you are strictly Pursuing a "Rush" strategy and Oxygen/Temperature are already near the max.
