# Project: Mass Converter (#105)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Power
- **Requirements:** 5 Science Tags
- **Effect:** -2 MC discount on all Space cards you play.
- **Production:** Increase your Energy production 6 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Powerhouse)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / THE ULTIMATE PAYOFF**

Mass Converter is arguably the most efficient production card in the game. While its 5-science requirement is steep, the massive infrastructure boost and permanent discount it provides make it a "must-draft" for any engine-building strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Efficiency:** For only 8 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 6 energy production and a permanent discount. The community value of this output is ~50 MC, representing a massive 400%+ return on investment.
2. **Physics Complex God-Tier Combo:** Mass Converter provides the exact 6 energy needed to fuel **Physics Complex** every generation, creating a 2 VP point-engine for zero net MC cost.
3. **Space Engine Enabler:** The -2 MC discount stacks with *Space Station*, *Quantum Extractor*, and *Research Outpost*. In the late game, this makes expensive Jovian multipliers or event cards nearly free.
4. **Colonies Expansion Synergy:** In games with *Colonies*, the 6 energy allows for multiple trade fleet activations or powering high-energy colony cards without needing to buy Standard Project power.
5. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides a **Science** and a **Power** tag.
   - **Science:** Helps reach the 7-tag requirement for *Antigravity Technology*.
   - **Power:** contributes to the *Energizer* milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** Do not hold this in your opening hand if you have zero science tags. It will clog your hand and waste 3 MC of draft capital. It is a mid-game card that requires a clear path to 5 science tags (e.g., via *Research* or *AI Central*).
- **Accidental Terraforming:** If you are winning via a slow engine, be careful not to let the 6 energy convert to heat and speed up the game end. Use the energy for card-based actions rather than TR if you need more generations to outscale opponents.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent has 3+ science tags, you **must** cut Mass Converter. Allowing them to land 6 free energy and a space discount is often a game-ending advantage.

**Recommendation:** A top-priority draft if you have any science production. Priority 1 for anyone building a science or space engine. Skip only if you are strictly pursuing a "Rush" strategy with zero interest in long-term infrastructure.
