# Project: Vesta Shipyard (#045)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Engine Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR JOVIANS**

Vesta Shipyard is a versatile infrastructure card that provides a unique blend of production, points, and a high-value tag. While its raw efficiency is average, its role as a "cheap Jovian tag" makes it a cornerstone for establishing early-game momentum in industrial or Jovian-heavy builds.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Requirement Enabler:** It is one of the most affordable ways to get a Jovian tag in the early game. This is critical for satisfying the requirements of powerful cards like **Beam from a Thorium Asteroid** or **Miranda Resort** without waiting for the late-game point multipliers.
2. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This is an ideal way to convert starting metal into a permanent industrial engine and a guaranteed point.
3. **Multiplier Catalyst:** If you have Jovian multipliers (e.g., *Water Import from Europa*), the value of this card jumps from 1 VP to 2-4+ VP, making the 18 MC investment (+3 draft) extremely efficient.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** The premier pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive income and provides the titanium production and Jovian tag needed for their engine.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to liquidate their starting metal into a permanent production lead.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon.
   - **Milestone Support:** Contributes toward the **Rim Settler** and **Spacefarer** milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** For pure titanium production, **Titanium Mine** (7 MC) is significantly more efficient. Only play Vesta Shipyard if you need the VP or the Jovian tag to hit a specific threshold.
- **Amortization:** It takes ~5 generations for the titanium production alone to break even. If played after Generation 6, the production will not pay off, and you are better off spending the 15 MC on direct points.

### Tips
- **Unity Party (Turmoil):** Prioritize playing this when the **Unity** party is ruling to maximize the value of the Titanium spent to buy it.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Saturn Systems**, **Phobolog**, or anyone pursuing a Jovian engine. Skip early if you only need production and have better options. Use late primarily as a point-slam for multipliers.
