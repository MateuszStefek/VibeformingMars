# Project: Release of Inert Gases (#036)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 14 MC
- **Tags:** Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The "Safe" Points)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Release of Inert Gases is a simple, low-impact card often considered a "trap" due to its high cost relative to its effect. However, its lack of requirements and event status make it a valuable tool for specific milestones and solo challenges.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Unconditional TR:** It is one of the few cards that provides multiple TR with zero global requirements. Use it to "burst" your way to the 26 TR needed for the **Terraformer** milestone or the **Benefactor** award.
2. **Solo Mode MVP:** In the "TR 63" solo challenge, this card is high-priority. Any card that provides TR at a discount compared to the 16 MC Standard Project "Buffer Gas" is essential for hitting the goal.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Media Group:** Provides a 3 MC rebate, making the 2 TR effectively cost only 11 MC—a much more respectable ratio.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon (via TR).
   - **Interplanetary Cinematics:** Provides a 2 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Poor Early Tempo:** Spending 17 MC (cost + draft) in Generation 1 for only 2 MC of income is statistically inefficient. Most players prefer to invest that capital in production cards like *Acquired Company* which scale better.
- **No Tag Value:** Aside from the Event tag, the card provides zero progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Scientist* or *Ecologist*. It cannot be paid for with Steel or Titanium.
- **Turmoil Penalty:** In games with *Turmoil*, the 1 TR loss per generation makes this card's income value even worse. View it strictly as a point-slam for the final turns.

### Tips
- **Late-Game Cash Dump:** In the final generation, if you have 14 MC left and no way to build a greenery or city, this card provides **2 VP** for 14 MC. It is better than doing nothing, but almost any other scoring card is superior.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Keep only if you are playing **Solo Mode**, racing for the **Terraformer** milestone, or have massive event rebates. skip early to preserve capital for engine building.
