# Project: Optimal Aerobraking (#111)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play a Space Event (a card with both a Space tag and a Red Event border), you gain 3 MC and 3 Heat.


## Strategic Analysis (The Heat Shield)
**Status: S-TIER / BEST SPACE ENGINE REBATE**

Optimal Aerobraking is a high-efficiency engine card that provides double resources (MC and Heat) for playing Space Events. It is a cornerstone for both "Rush" and "Space Engine" strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled ROI:** With a total cost of 10 MC (cost + draft), it pays for itself in just **two triggers** (6 MC + 6 Heat). 8 Heat is worth 1 TR, making each trigger worth ~6 MC in total value.
2. **Economic Liquidity:** Like *Media Group*, it provides MC *during* your turn, allowing for more cards to be played in the same generation.
3. **Terraforming Acceleration:** The 3 Heat per trigger is a significant boost toward the temperature track. It helps secure heat-related thresholds and ending the game before opponents can stabilize their engines.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Media Group:** The "Ultimate Cashback" combo. Together, they provide 6 MC and 3 heat per Space Event, making high-impact cards like *Giant Ice Asteroid* or *Deimos Down* incredibly cheap.
   - **Interplanetary Cinematics (IC):** Adds an additional 2 MC rebate to the events.
   - **Point Luna:** Provides card draw on the Space tag, helping you find more events to trigger the effect.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows you to pay for the Aerobraking and the subsequent events with Titanium, while receiving MC back.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Scope Limitation:** Remember that it *only* triggers on **Space Events**. It does not trigger on blue space buildings (like *Space Elevator*) or non-space red events (like *Sabotage*).
- **Draft Timing:** Best taken in Generations 1-5. In later generations, only draft it if you have at least two Space Events in hand or ready to be drawn.

### Tips
- **Milestone Engine:** Use the heat generated to claim the *Thermalist* milestone. In the digital version, be aware of bugs that might trigger it more often, but in standard play, prioritize it whenever you have Titanium production or Space-heavy hand.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for almost every strategy. It provides the perfect blend of economic rebate and terraforming progress. Priority 1 if *Media Group* is also in play.
