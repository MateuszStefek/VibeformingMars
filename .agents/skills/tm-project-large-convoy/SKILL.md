# Project: Large Convoy (#143)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 36 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place 1 Ocean tile. Draw 2 cards. Add 4 Animals to any Animal card.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Game-Ending Point Bomb)
**Status: S-TIER VP SOURCE / THE PREMIER TITANIUM DUMP**

Large Convoy is the second most expensive card in the base game, but its sheer point density and resource conversion make it one of the most powerful tools in any engine. It is the gold standard for late-game "point-slams."

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional VP Swing:** When combined with a 1 VP/animal card (like *Birds*, *Fish*, or *Predators*), Large Convoy creates a massive **7 VP swing** (2 base + 4 animals + 1 TR from the ocean). This is the largest single-action point gain in the game outside of Jovian multipliers.
2. **Titanium Conversion:** As a Space card, the entire 36 MC cost can be paid with Titanium. This makes it the premier way for titanium-rich players or corporations like **Phobolog** to liquidate their metals into final victory points.
3. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Provides **Earth**, **Space**, and **Event** tags.
   - **Rebates:** Triggers maximum rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) and *Optimal Aerobraking* (+3 MC, +3 Heat).
   - **Earth Synergy:** Triggers *Point Luna* for an additional card draw and contributes to the *Legend* milestone.
4. **Early Game Utility:** While usually a finisher, it is a valid "panic play" in Generations 1-3 if you desperately need card draw or the ocean bonus to unlock a requirement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Buffer:** Do not play this if you don't have an animal card to receive the resources, unless you are in the final turn and need the 2 VP/Ocean to win. The 4 animals represent 2-4 VP of the card's value.
- **Ocean Bottleneck:** Monitor the ocean track. If 9 oceans are already placed, you lose the 1 TR and the placement bonus, though the card is usually still worth playing for the points and draw.
- **Upfront Cost:** 39 MC (cost + draft) is a massive hit to your tempo. Ensure you have the budget or titanium production to support it without stalling your engine.

### Tips
- **Speculative Hold:** In the 2024-2025 meta, players often hold Large Convoy from Generation 1. Even if you don't have an animal card yet, the potential for a 7-point swing is so high that it is worth the 3 MC to "deny" the card to opponents.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for almost any strategy. Priority 1 for titanium-heavy engines or anyone with a 1:1 VP animal card. Skip only if you are in an extreme "Rush" game and cannot afford the high cost.
