# Project: Worms (#130)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** 4% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step for every 2 Microbe tags you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Scaler)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL POWERHOUSE**

Worms is a high-ceiling production card that scales directly with your specialization in microbe-based engines. While its raw efficiency can be low, it serves as a powerful "bridge" card for players specializing in biological strategies.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling Potential:** For only 8 MC (+3 to draft), it can provide 2-3+ plant production in the mid-game. If you have 5 microbe tags (including Worms), you gain **+3 plant production** for a total investment of 11 MC—a significantly better ratio than *Heather* or *Lichen*.
2. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides a Microbe tag that triggers these passive point/resource generators immediately.
   - **Insects / NRA:** Provides the plant production needed to hit the requirements for these high-tier biological payoffs.
   - **Venus Next:** The expansion adds many high-quality microbe tags, making Worms much easier to scale than in the Base Game.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The 4% Oxygen requirement is a significant bottleneck. In many games, you may have the microbe tags ready by Generation 2, but must wait until Generation 4 or 5 to play Worms, reducing its long-term production value.
- **Tag Scarcity:** To be efficient, you need at least **3 microbe tags** (for +2 production). If you only have Worms itself, you are paying 11 MC for 0 plant production. **Do not buy this unless you have a path to 3+ microbe tags.**
- **Comparison to Insects:** *Insects* is generally superior because it scales per **1** plant tag, while Worms scales per **2** microbe tags. Microbe tags are generally harder to accumulate in the Base Game.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Worms at 2% Oxygen. This allows you to start your plant engine 1-2 generations early, potentially securing the **Gardener** milestone before your opponents can react.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft in Generations 2-5 if you are Pursuing a microbe or science strategy. Essential for **Splice**. Skip early if you have zero other microbe tags.
