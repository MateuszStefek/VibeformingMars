# Project: Cartels (#042)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for each Earth tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Earth Tag Payoff)
**Status: S-TIER / SCALING INCOME ENGINE**

Cartels is a high-potential economy card that rewards players for specializing in Earth tags. It can provide the single largest MC production jump of any low-cost card if timed correctly.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** For only 8 MC (+3 to draft), it can easily provide 5+ MC production in an Earth-focused build. At +4 production, it pays for itself in under 3 generations.
2. **Low Entry Barrier:** Unlike many high-production cards, it has no requirements, making it playable in Generation 1.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Teractor:** The ultimate pairing. Teractor starts with an Earth tag and provides a discount, often making Cartels a +2 or +3 production play on the very first turn.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers a card draw on play and benefits from the Earth-heavy strategy required to maximize Cartels.
   - **Earth Office / Earth Catapult:** Reduces the cost of the "enabler" Earth tags, making the Cartels payout much larger for a lower total investment.
   - **Banker Milestone:** Frequently the card that secures the Banker milestone (highest MC production) in the early-to-mid game.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **The Waiting Game:** A common mistake is holding Cartels too long in hopes of getting "one more Earth tag." If you have 3 Earth tags (including Cartels) by Generation 2 or 3, play it. The compounding interest of the early cash is usually better than waiting for a +6 production in Generation 5.
- **Tag Dependency:** Do not buy this if you have no other Earth tags and no path to get them. In a vacuum, +1 production for 11 MC is terrible.

### Tips
- **Stealth Payout:** If you are in a draft and see Cartels, look at your opponents. If an opponent has *Point Luna* or *Teractor*, you must hate-draft this card. Letting an Earth-based corp get Cartels is often equivalent to handing them the game's economic lead.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" if you have at least 1 other Earth tag in your opening hand. High priority for any Earth-focused engine. Best played between Generations 1 and 4.
