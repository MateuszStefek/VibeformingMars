# Project: Lava Flows (#150)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 2 steps. Place the Lava Flows tile on any area reserved for a Volcano (Tharsis Tholus, Ascraeus Mons, Pavonis Mons, or Arsia Mons). If those are all taken, place it anywhere on Mars.


## Strategic Analysis (The Threshold Sniper)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Lava Flows is a high-efficiency terraforming card used primarily for control and "sniping" global bonuses. While it provides no engine value, its ability to move the temperature track twice in a single action makes it a potent competitive tool.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 18 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 2 TR and a board tile. This 21 MC total investment is significantly cheaper than the 28 MC required for two Standard Project temperature raises.
2. **Threshold Sniping:** Its primary use is to "jump" over an opponent and claim a temperature bonus (like the Heat Production at -24°C or the Ocean at 0°C) before their heat engine can pay off.
3. **Map-Specific Powerhouse:**
   - **Elysium Map:** If placed on **Olympus Mons**, the tile grants a **3-card draw** bonus, making the card arguably the best value play on that map.
   - **Hellas Map:** No volcanic restrictions exist on Hellas. You can place the tile **anywhere** (except oceans), making it a powerful tool for blocking opponents or "teleporting" to remote bonus spots.
4. **Solo Mode Essential:** Temperature is often the hardest track to max out in Solo play. Lava Flows is a high-priority "Always-Keep" to ensure you hit the time limit.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Tags:** The card has zero tags, meaning it provides zero synergy for *Science* requirements or milestones.
- **"Locked Out" Risk:** On Tharsis or Elysium, if all volcano spots are already occupied by cities or greeneries, you must place it according to normal rules. Savvy opponents will sometimes "snipe" the final volcano spot specifically to prevent you from playing this card for its placement bonus.
- **Opportunity Cost:** Tying up 21 MC early for pure TR can stall your engine. It is best used as a mid-to-late game tool for points and board control.

### Tips
- **Mining Guild Trigger:** Use the placement bonus (often steel or titanium) to trigger the *Mining Guild* corporation's ability, turning a one-time TR boost into a permanent production increase.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft for most engine-builders. High priority for **Helion** or **Ecoline** on Elysium. Essential for **Solo Mode**. Skip early if you need to prioritize income production.
