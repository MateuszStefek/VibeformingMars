# Skill: TM Rules

This skill provides a comprehensive reference for the rules of Terraforming Mars, covering the Base Game and the Prelude expansion.

## 1. Game Flow & Phases

Each round is called a **Generation**. A generation consists of four phases:

### Phase 1: Player Order Phase
- The first player marker moves one step clockwise.
- The generation marker is moved up one step.

### Phase 2: Research Phase
- Each player draws 4 cards and decides which to keep.
- Each card kept costs **3 MegaCredits (MC)**.
- *Note: In the initial setup (Gen 0), players follow the "Initial Research Phase" rules (see below).*

### Phase 3: Action Phase
- Players take turns performing **1 or 2 actions**, or **passing**.
- If a player passes, they can take no more actions for the rest of the phase.
- The phase ends when everyone has passed.
- **Valid Actions:**
  - Play a card from hand.
  - Use a Standard Project.
  - Claim a Milestone.
  - Fund an Award.
  - Use an Action on a blue card.
  - Convert 8 Plants into a Greenery tile (increases Oxygen 1 step).
  - Convert 8 Heat into a Temperature increase (1 step).

### Phase 4: Production Phase
- All players perform production simultaneously:
  1. **Energy to Heat:** All remaining Energy is converted into Heat.
  2. **Resource Generation:** Players gain resources based on their production levels and Terraform Rating (TR).
     - **MC:** TR + MC Production. (MC Production can be negative, but TR + MC Prod cannot be lower than 0).
     - **Other Resources:** Gain resources equal to your production level.

## 2. Initial Research Phase (Setup)

### 1. Dealing the Cards
Each player is dealt:
- **2 Corporations**
- **4 Prelude Cards** (if playing with Preludes)
- **10 Project Cards**

### 2. Selection
Players simultaneously choose:
- **Exactly 1 Corporation**.
- **Exactly 2 Prelude Cards**.
- **Any number of Project Cards** to keep (0 to 10).

### 3. Costs & Timing
- **Costs:** Each project card kept costs **3 MegaCredits (MC)**.
- **Sequence of Setup:**
  1. **Selection:** Players simultaneously choose 1 Corporation, 2 Preludes, and any number of Project cards.
  2. **Corporation Action:** Players reveal their Corporation and receive all starting resources and production.
  3. **Research Payment:** Players pay 3 MC for each Project card they decided to keep. This is paid from the Corporation's starting capital.
  4. **Prelude Phase:** Players play their 2 Prelude cards in any order. All effects, costs (e.g., Business Empire's -6 MC), and production bonuses are applied immediately.
  5. **Action Phase:** The first generation's Action Phase begins.

---

## 3. Global Parameters & Terraforming Rating (TR)

Terraforming Mars ends when three global parameters reach their goal:
- **Oxygen:** 14%
- **Temperature:** +8 °C
- **Oceans:** 9 Ocean tiles placed.

**Terraform Rating (TR):**
- Every time you increase a global parameter, you increase your TR by 1 step.
- TR acts as both your minimum MC income and your base victory points at the end of the game.

---

## 4. Tile Placement Rules

- **Greenery:** Must be placed adjacent to another tile you own if possible. If no tiles are owned or no adjacent spots are available, it can be placed anywhere.
- **City:** Cannot be placed adjacent to another city tile (except for specific cards like Noctis City or Urbanized Area).
- **Ocean:** Can only be placed on areas reserved for oceans (blue borders).
- **Placement Bonuses:** When placing a tile, you immediately receive the resources or card draws printed on the hex.

---

## 5. End of Game Scoring

The game ends at the end of the generation when all three global parameters are maxed. One final plant-to-greenery conversion round occurs in player order.
- **Final Score = TR + Awards + Milestones + Map Tiles (Greenery/City) + Card VPs.**
  - **Awards:** 1st place = 5VP, 2nd place = 2VP (except in 2-player games).
  - **Milestones:** 5VP each.
  - **Greeneries:** 1VP each.
  - **Cities:** 1VP for every adjacent greenery tile (regardless of who owns it).
