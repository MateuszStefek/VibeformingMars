# Terraforming Mars: Rules & Setup

## Game Variants

### 1. Corporate Era
The Corporate Era is a variant included in the base game that adds strategic complexity. It includes 71 project cards and 2 corporations (**Saturn Systems** and **Phobolog**).
- **Starting Production:** When playing with Corporate Era, all players start with **0 production** of all resources (unless modified by their corporation).
- **Setup:** Add all cards with the Corporate Era icon to the deck.

### 2. Basic (Standard) Game
Used primarily for learning or faster games.
- **Starting Production:** All players start with **1 production** of every resource.
- **Setup:** Remove all Corporate Era cards (marked with the icon) from the deck. The Beginner Corporation is often used for new players.

## Initial Research Phase (Setup)
When playing with the Prelude expansion, the following steps are performed at the start of the game to determine your starting hand and resources. Preludes are a special category of cards played after your corporation to provide an immediate economic or terraforming boost.

### 1. Dealing the Cards
Each player is dealt:
- **2 Corporations** (from the Corporation deck)
- **4 Prelude Cards** (from the Prelude deck)
- **10 Project Cards** (from the Project deck)

### 2. Selection
Players simultaneously examine their cards and must choose:
- **Exactly 1 Corporation** to play.
- **Exactly 2 Prelude Cards** to keep.
- **Any number of Project Cards** to keep (0 to 10).

### 3. Costs
- **Corporation:** Free to play.
- **Prelude Cards:** Free to play.
- **Project Cards:** Each project card kept costs **3 MegaCredits (MC)**. These are paid for using your corporation's starting capital. Buying no projects is a valid strategy to maximize starting liquid MC.

### 4. Sequence of Play
1.  **Discard** all unselected corporations, preludes, and projects.
2.  **Pay** for the projects you kept.
3.  **Play your Corporation** and receive starting resources, production, and any immediate effects.
4.  **Play your 2 Preludes** in any order. These are played after the corporation but before the first generation's action phase. All effects and production bonuses from Preludes are applied immediately.

---

## General Rules
- **TR (Terraform Rating):** Your starting TR is always 20 (unless specified otherwise by a variant or corporation).
- **Tags:** Prelude cards have tags that can trigger corporation abilities or fulfill requirements for future project cards.
- **Action Phase:** Once setup is complete, the first generation begins.
