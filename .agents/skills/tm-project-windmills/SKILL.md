# Project: Windmills (#194)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** 7% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Filler)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "STEEL DUMP"**

Windmills is a low-tier infrastructure card often used to convert late-game building resources into points. While its production is modest, its combination of tags and victory points makes it a stable, if unexciting, choice for the final stages of the match.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Steel Sink Points:** As a Building card, the 6 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the final turns, this is an extrêmement efficient way to convert 3 surplus metal into 1 VP and energy production.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Building:** Contributes toward the **Builder** milestone or **Contractor** award.
   - **Power:** Helps hit requirements for high-tier cards like *Mass Converter*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. Vitor receives a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon, making Windmills cost only **3 MC** (+ draft).
   - **Requirement Bridge:** A cheap way to reach the 6-power requirement for the **Energizer** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** 7% Oxygen is a mid-to-late game requirement. **Never keep this in your opening hand.** It will tie up 3 MC of draft capital for several generations. Only draft it once Oxygen is at 5-6%.
- **Late Game Waste:** While 1 VP for 9 MC (cost + draft) is efficient, energy production is usually worthless in the final two generations. Only play late if you need the points or the tags for a milestone.
- **Better Alternatives:** Early game energy should be solved by more efficient cards like *Power Plant* or *Sponsors*. Windmills is a "cleanup" card for when you missed the early industrial engine.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Windmills at 5% Oxygen. This allows you to fix your energy infrastructure a generation early, potentially enabling a high-VP city or trade action that decides the match.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft in Generations 4-7. Keep only if you are playing **Vitor**, have a surplus of Steel, or need a cheap tag for a milestone. Skip early to preserve tempo.
