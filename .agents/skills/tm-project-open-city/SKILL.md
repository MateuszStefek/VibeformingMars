# Project: Open City (#109)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 23 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** 12% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 4 steps. Gain 2 Plants. Place a City tile.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Steel Dump)
**Status: S-TIER PAYOFF / A-TIER LATE-GAME CITY**

Open City is widely considered one of the most efficient late-game city cards. It serves as the ultimate tool for converting excess building resources into points and board dominance during the final generations.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Steel Sink:** As a Building card, the 23 MC cost can be paid entirely with Steel. This allows you to convert surplus metal into 1 VP (base) + adjacency bonuses (typically 2-4 VP) for zero liquid MC.
2. **Strictly Better than Standard Project:** Even without steel, Open City is superior to a 25 MC Standard Project city. It provides **1 VP**, **2 Plants** (often securing a final greenery), and **+4 MC production** for a lower base cost.
3. **Board Control:** Because it hits the table late (12% Oxygen), it is perfect for "stealing" greeneries that opponents have placed but not yet protected with their own cities.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Tharsis Republic:** Triggers their passive income and secures board control.
   - **Rover Construction / Pets:** Triggers "whenever a city is placed" effects for a final resource burst.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Increases the plant gain to 3, further accelerating your final greenery.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** 12% Oxygen is a very late-game requirement. **Never keep this in your starting hand.** It will tie up 3 MC of draft capital for almost the entire game.
- **Income Decay:** The +4 MC production is usually negligible by the time 12% Oxygen is reached. Do not value the income highly; view it primarily as a point and plant card.
- **Energy Cost:** Remember to account for the 1 energy production penalty.

### Tips
- **Mayor Warning:** Do not draft Open City expecting to win the **Mayor** milestone. Cheaper cities like *Research Outpost* or *Cupola City* will almost always decide that race long before the Oxygen track hits 12%.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-10). Essential for players with excess Steel or those holding *Rover Construction*. Skip early to preserve tempo.
