# Project: Space Elevator (#013)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 27 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 1 Steel to gain 5 MegaCredits.
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Steel Laundering Engine)
**Status: A-TIER / PREMIUM HYBRID ENGINE**

Space Elevator is a high-cost, high-reward card that bridges the gap between building-heavy and space-heavy strategies. It provides valuable production, points, and a repeatable way to convert rigid resources (Steel) into flexible currency (MC).

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Resource Flexibility:** The action converts 1 Steel (worth 2 MC) into 5 MC. This 3 MC net gain per generation is one of the best "repeatable production" boosts in the game.
2. **Hybrid Tags:** Having both Space and Building tags makes it incredibly flexible to pay for (using either Steel or Titanium) and helps toward multiple milestones like *Diversifier* or *Spacefarer*.
3. **Built-in Value:** It provides 1 Titanium production (~10 MC value) and 2 VP (~10 MC value). To break even on the remaining 10 MC of investment, you only need to use the action 4 times.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Interplanetary Cinematics (IC) / Mining Guild:** Excellent for corporations that start with massive steel reserves or production.
   - **Manutech:** Each use of the action provides an immediate MC boost for Manutech's passive.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the action slightly less efficient (as Steel is now worth 3 MC), but the Titanium production becomes worth more.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Upfront Cost:** 30 MC (27 + 3 to draft) is a massive hit to your early-game economy. Do not play this in Generation 1 if it prevents you from buying other production cards. It is often best played in Generations 3-4 once your steel engine is established.
- **Late Game Decay:** In the final 2 generations, it is strictly a 2 VP card for 30 MC—a very poor investment.

### Tips
- **Titanium Sink:** If you have high Titanium production but no Space cards to spend it on, use your Titanium to pay for the Space Elevator. This "launders" your Titanium into Steel-to-MC conversion, giving you the liquid cash needed for standard projects or greeneries.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for Steel-heavy corporations or players with Titanium surplus. Best played in the early-to-mid game (Generations 1-4). Skip if you have zero steel production.
