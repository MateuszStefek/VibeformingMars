# Project: Water Import from Europa (#012)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 25 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 12 MC to place 1 ocean tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP for each Jovian tag you have.


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Multiplier)
**Status: S-TIER VP SOURCE / SITUATIONAL ACTION**

Water Import from Europa is one of the most powerful point-scoring cards in the game. It belongs to the elite group of Jovian multipliers that can single-handedly win the game for an engine-building player.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **End-Game Point Bomb:** In a dedicated Jovian strategy, this card is frequently worth **8-12 VP**. Its ceiling is higher than almost any other non-multiplier card.
2. **Space Tag Synergy:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium, making it a perfect "VP dump" for your accumulated resources in the final generation.
3. **Titanium Sink Action:** While the 12 MC action is expensive, it is a valuable way to convert excess Titanium into TR and placement bonuses if you have no more space cards to play.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** Provides a Jovian tag that boosts income early while setting up a massive VP multiplier for the late game.
   - **Phobolog / Advanced Alloys:** If titanium is worth 4 MC, the ocean action costs only 3 Titanium, making it an incredibly efficient way to finish the ocean track.
   - **Arctic Algae / Lakefront Resorts:** These cards add immediate plant or MC rebates to the ocean action, improving its efficiency.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Stealth Timing:** **Do not play this early.** Playing a Jovian multiplier in Generations 1-5 tells the entire table you are going for Jovians. This will lead to every opponent "hate-drafting" every Jovian tag away from you. Hold it in your hand until the final or semi-final generation.
- **Action vs. Engine:** The action (placing oceans) accelerates the end of the game. If you are building a slow Jovian engine, you usually want the game to last *longer*. Only use the action if you are titanium-rich and need the TR/bonuses to secure a win.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If you are not the Jovian player, you **must** cut this card from the draft. Letting an opponent stack multiple multipliers (*Water Import*, *Ganymede*, *Terraforming Ganymede*) is the most common way to lose a long-form game.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" if you have even one other Jovian tag or high titanium production. High priority for end-game points. Use the action only as a resource sink.
