# Project: Lightning Harvest (#046)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Power
- **Requirements:** 3 Science Tags
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Energy production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Tempo Card)
**Status: C-TIER VALUE / SITUATIONAL UTILITY**

Lightning Harvest is a high-efficiency but awkward card. While it provides a diverse set of benefits for a low cost, its requirement often pushes its play into the mid-game, where small production boosts are less impactful.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Mathematical Efficiency:** For a total investment of 11 MC (8 cost + 3 to draft), you receive ~17 MC of value (7 for energy, 5 for MC production, 5 for VP). It is statistically more efficient than almost any other single-step production card.
2. **AI Central Support:** Its primary competitive use is providing the 1 energy needed to power **AI Central** once you hit the 3-science requirement. It allows you to "turn on" your draw engine for a very low net cost.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. Vitor receives a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon, making Lightning Harvest effectively cost only 5 MC.
   - **Thorgate:** Benefits from the Power tag discount.
   - **Colonies:** A cheap way to reach the 3-energy threshold for trading if you already have a science-heavy engine.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Clashing:** To get the full benefit of MC production, you want to play it early. However, 3 science tags usually aren't met until Generation 4 or 5. By that time, +1 MC production is a negligible "bonus" rather than a core engine piece.
- **Science Diversion:** Do not go out of your way to find science tags just to play Lightning Harvest. Only draft it if you are already pursuing a science engine (*Mars University*, *Olympus Conference*) and need the cheap energy/VP.
- **Opportunity Cost:** In the late game, 11 MC for 1 VP is a standard rate. Only play then if you have the tags and need to spend excess cash.

### Tips
- **Milestone Padding:** Use the Power tag to reach the 6-power requirement for the **Energizer** milestone or to hit the 8-building requirement for **Builder** (wait, it has no building tag).

**Recommendation:** A mid-to-low priority draft. Keep only if you are playing **Vitor**, **Thorgate**, or have an early path to 3 science tags (e.g., via *Research*). Skip early if you need to prioritize pure income production.
