# Project: Phobos Space Haven (#021)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 25 MC
- **Tags:** Space, City
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step. Place a City tile on the reserved Phobos area.
- **VP:** 3


## Strategic Analysis (The Titanium Dump)
**Status: B-TIER / LATE-GAME POINT SINK**

Phobos Space Haven is a high-cost orbital city that serves primarily as a way to convert excess Titanium into Victory Points. While its early-game production is mediocre, its "surprise city" potential and flat VP make it a valuable tactical tool.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Titanium Conversion:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. In the final generations, this is often the most efficient way to spend accumulated metal for a 3 VP gain.
2. **Surprise Milestone:** Since Phobos is a city tile that doesn't compete for space on Mars, it is a perfect "third city" to snatch the **Mayor** milestone from opponents in the mid-to-late game.
3. **Flat Victory Points:** 3 VP is a solid guaranteed return, especially for a card that can be paid for with non-MC resources.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog / Advanced Alloys:** Makes the card significantly cheaper and the Titanium production more valuable.
   - **Tharsis Republic:** Triggers Tharsis's passive income increase upon placement, even though it is not on Mars.
   - **Immigrant City / Rover Construction:** Triggers "whenever a city is played" effects.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Adjacency Bonuses:** Unlike cities on Mars, Phobos **does not** gain 1 VP per adjacent greenery. Its point value is fixed at 3 VP.
- **Early Game Inefficiency:** Playing it in Generations 1-3 for the production is generally a mistake. You are paying ~18 MC (after valuing production) for 3 VP too early, stalling your economic growth.
- **Space Discount Dependency:** Without Titanium production or cards like *Space Station*, the 28 MC (cost + draft) is often too steep compared to more focused engine cards.

### Tips
- **Mayor Sniping:** Hold Phobos Space Haven in your hand if you see multiple players racing for the *Mayor* milestone. Use it on your turn to jump from 2 cities to 3, claiming the 5 points before they can.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft for Titanium-heavy engines. High priority if racing for the *Mayor* milestone. Best used as a point dump in the final 2 generations. Skip early unless playing Phobolog.
