# Project: Managed Ecosystems (#037)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Plant, Animal, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play a Plant, Animal, or Microbe tag (including the ones on this card), add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 2 Animals on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Payoff)
**Status: S-TIER BIO COMPONENT / A-TIER VP SOURCE**

Managed Ecosystems is one of the most efficient point-scoring cards in the game. It bridges the gap between different biological tags, providing a massive point swing for a very low upfront cost.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unbeatable Efficiency:** For 9 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a card that triggers on all three bio-tags. In a dedicated build, this card can easily accumulate 4-8 animals, yielding a ~3 MC per VP ratio—one of the best in the deck.
2. **Immediate "Self-Trigger":** The card triggers on its own three tags, providing 1.5 animals (0.75 VP) immediately upon play. If you have even two other bio-tags in hand, it becomes point-profitable the same turn it is played.
3. **The "Bio-Trinity" Core:** It forms a devastating engine when paired with **Ecological Zone** and **Decomposers**. A single bio-tag can trigger all three, generating multiple resources and points simultaneously.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides three different tags (Plant, Animal, Microbe) toward the **Ecologist** and **Diversifier** milestones. On the **Elysium** map, this card is often the decisive factor in winning the Ecologist race.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Increases the initial animal gain, accelerating the point generation.
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for animals to bypass the dependency on tag-playing.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Timing:** Draft it early, but only play it once you have 2-3 other bio-tags in hand or a clear path to them. Playing it with zero follow-up bio-cards is a waste of 12 MC.
- **Microbe Diversion:** Unlike *Ecological Zone*, this card triggers on **Microbes**. This makes it valuable for players focusing on microbe engines who might otherwise skip animal-based points.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent is collecting bio-tags, you **must** cut this card. The point swing from a Managed Ecosystem-fueled bio-engine can be 10+ VP over the course of the game.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" for any engine-building strategy. High priority for bio-engineers. Aim to play between Generations 3 and 5 once your income is stable.
