# Corporation: Manutech

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Venus Next
- **Starting MC:** 35
- **Tags:** Building
- **Starting Production:** 1 Steel
- **Effect:** Whenever you increase a production (including starting production), you also gain that many resources of that type immediately.

## Strategic Analysis (The Exponential Engine)
**Status: S-TIER / ELITE**

Manutech is one of the most explosive corporations in the game. By gaining resources the moment production is increased, it effectively "rebates" its investments, allowing for massive Gen 1 plays.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unmatched Momentum:** Playing a card that gives +3 MC production yields 3 MC instantly. This "cashback" often allows Manutech to play one or two extra cards every generation compared to other corps.
2. **Prelude Synergy:** Manutech is arguably the best corp for production-heavy Preludes. *Acquired Company* (as a card) or Preludes like *Allied Bank* become significantly more powerful.
3. **Steel Starting Value:** Starting with 1 steel production means starting with 1 physical steel, plus the building tag, facilitating early construction.

### Synergies:
- **Production Preludes:** *Mohole*, *Acquired Space Agency*, *Sponsors* (as a card) are all improved.
- **Building Tags:** Leverages its tag for cards like *Contractor* or *Industrial Center*.
- **Standard Projects:** Even Standard Projects trigger the rebate, making "Power Plant" only effectively cost 8 MC (11 - 3 for the energy gained).

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Maximize Gen 1:** Your goal is to dump your entire hand in Gen 1. Use the instant resources to chain cards together.
- **Production over TR:** Early on, prioritize production increases over TR increases, as TR does not trigger the Manutech ability (unless it's a specific production increase).

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Pick" if you have production-heavy Preludes. One of the strongest corporations in the competitive meta.
