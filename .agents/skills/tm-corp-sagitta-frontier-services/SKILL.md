# Corporation: Sagitta Frontier Services

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude 2
- **Starting MC:** 31
- **Starting Production:** +1 Energy, +2 MC production
- **Tags:** None
- **Ability (Effect):** Gain 4 MC whenever you play a card with no tags (including this corporation). Gain 1 MC whenever you play a card with exactly one tag.
- **First Action:** Draw a card with no tags.

## Strategic Analysis (The Tagless Titan)
**Status: HIGH TIER**

Sagitta Frontier Services is a powerhouse for players who know how to exploit "filler" cards and events. By providing massive rebates on cards that are usually cheap or tagless, it accelerates the early game to a blistering pace.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Event Synergy:** Most events have only one or zero tags. Sagitta turns every Event card into a high-value tempo play.
2. **Standard Tech Synergy:** Standard Technology (the project) becomes even more efficient when your "filler" cards are providing constant MC rebates.
3. **Consistency:** The first action ensures you start with a potentially high-value tagless card, reducing the impact of a poor opening hand.

### Synergies:
- **Standard Technology:** Maximizes the value of tagless/single-tag project plays.
- **Interplanetary Cinematics:** If playing with multiple corporations or drafts, Sagitta loves event-heavy strategies.
- **Earth Tags:** Many Earth tags are single-tag cards that build your economy while Sagitta keeps your MC pool liquid.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Rebate Management:** Prioritize tagless cards early to keep your MC pool high. Even a "weak" tagless card can be worth playing just for the 4 MC rebate (effectively a -4 cost).
- **Milestone Sniping:** Use the high liquid capital to secure milestones like 'Generalist' or 'Economist' early.

**Recommendation:** Elite tier. Best picked when your starting hand has 2-3 strong tagless events or efficient single-tag economic cards.
