# Project: Power Plant (#141)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Surgical Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Power Plant is the "workhorse" of early-game infrastructure. While unexciting, its high efficiency and low cost make it one of the most reliable ways to solve energy requirements without over-investing in production.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 4 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 1 energy production. This 7 MC total is significantly cheaper than the 11 MC Standard Project, providing a surgical way to fix your energy math.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, effectively making it a "free" way to gain energy for metal-heavy corporations.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking requirements for cards like *Power Supply Consortium* or *AI Central*.
3. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Power Plant is the premier "filler" card to reach the 3-energy requirement for trading with colonies like *Luna* or *Pluto*. 
4. **Early Hand Utility:** A high-priority keep if you are holding **AI Central** or **Development Center**, as it provides half of the energy needed for their actions for a negligible cost.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Impact:** +1 Energy production is a minor boost. If you already have 3+ energy, the 4 MC is better spent on income or terraforming. 
- **Late Game irrelevance:** Energy production has zero value in the final generations (unless temperature is maxed). Only draft late if you need the Building tag for the **Builder** milestone or the Power tag for **Diversifier**.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Power Plant specifically to hit the 6-power requirement for the **Energizer** milestone on a budget. Since it is so cheap, it is the most efficient way to close the gap in a tight race.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-3 if you have energy-dependent cards or are playing with *Colonies*. Excellent opening hand card. Skip late.
