# Project: Great Escarpment Consortium (#030)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** 1 Steel Production
- **Effect:** Increase your Steel production 1 step. Decrease any player's Steel production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Steel Thief)
**Status: S-TIER IN 2-PLAYER / B-TIER GENERAL**

Great Escarpment Consortium is a highly efficient disruption card. Like its Jovian cousin (*Asteroid Mining Consortium*), it provides a massive 2-production swing in your favor, stalling an opponent's engine while accelerating your own building potential.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unbeatable Efficiency:** For only 6 MC (+3 to draft), you get 1 steel production and deny an opponent 1. In a standard game, this is a ~20 MC resource swing for a total investment of 9 MC.
2. **Synergy Breaking:** Highly effective against corporations like **Interplanetary Cinematics** or players utilizing **Space Elevator**. Stealing 1 steel production can force them to spend "real" MC for their building projects, significantly slowing their momentum.
3. **Milestone Control:** Frequently used to steal the lead for the *Miner* or *Industrialist* milestones/awards in the mid-to-late game.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate MC gain for Manutech when the production increases.
   - **Mining Guild:** Further boosts their already high steel yields.
   - **Steel Preludes:** If you start with steel production from a Prelude, this card becomes a high-priority "Gen 1" play.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Bottleneck:** You **must** already have 1 steel production to play this. If you don't have it in your opening setup, you may find yourself holding this card until Generation 4 or 5, reducing its overall payoff.
- **Multplayer Dilution:** In 4-5 player games, attacking one player benefits everyone else at the table who wasn't involved. Use it selectively on the leader or a player directly competing with you for a milestone.
- **Ceres Presence:** If the **Ceres** colony is in play and easily accessible, the value of stealing 1 steel production drops, as players can simply trade for massive stacks of metal.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** In 2-player games, this is a mandatory hate-draft if you have steel production and your opponent doesn't. Allowing them to flip your lead into their advantage is a common reason for mid-game stalls.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" if you have a path to 1 steel production. High priority in Generations 1-3. Focus your disruption on the player with the most building-tag-dependent engine.
