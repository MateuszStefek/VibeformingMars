# Corporation: Interplanetary Cinematics (IC)

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 30
- **Starting Steel:** 20
- **Ability:** Whenever you play an event card (red), you get 2 MC back.

## Strategic Analysis (The High-Roll Specialist)
**Status: B-TIER**

IC is a corporation built for explosive starts and event-heavy strategies. While its starting MC is low, its massive stock of steel effectively gives it a much larger functional budget if it has building tags in hand.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Functional Budget:** With 20 steel worth 40 MC, IC effectively starts with 70 MC. This is the highest functional budget in the game, but it *must* be spent on building tags.
2. **Event Rebates:** The 2 MC rebate on red cards adds up over the game, especially when combined with other event-synergy cards.
3. **Space Event Synergy:** IC is excellent at playing big space events that also have building tags (though rare) or simply using its cash for events and steel for infrastructure.

### Synergies:
- **Advanced Alloys:** Makes your 20 starting steel worth 60 MC total. This is a game-changing synergy for IC.
- **Media Group:** Adds another 3 MC rebate to events, bringing the total rebate to 5 MC per event played.
- **Optimal Aerobraking:** Adds heat and plants to every space event, creating a powerful engine.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Avoid the Steel Trap:** The most common mistake with IC is ending the game with unused steel. You must draft building tags aggressively to convert your starting resources into production or points.
- **Gen 1 Snowball:** Use your steel to build production (e.g., 'Mine', 'Strip Mine') so you can save your MC for events and card draw.

**Recommendation:** High priority if your starting hand has 2+ strong building tags and at least one high-impact event card.
