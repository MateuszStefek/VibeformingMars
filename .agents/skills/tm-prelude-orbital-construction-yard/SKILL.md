# Prelude: Orbital Construction Yard

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step. Gain 4 Titanium.
- **Tag:** Space.

## Strategic Analysis (The Titanium Jumpstart)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR PHOBOLOG**

Orbital Construction Yard (OCY) is a situational infrastructure card that excels in establishing early-game momentum for space-heavy strategies. While its raw efficiency is average, its role as a "titanium enabler" makes it a top-tier tool for corporations looking to liquidate metal into long-term production.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Immediate Engine Jumpstart:** The 4 starting titanium (worth 12 MC) allows you to play high-cost, no-requirement space cards in Generation 1 that would otherwise be unplayable.
   - **Targets:** Enables Gen 1 *Earth Elevator*, *Io Mining Industries*, or *Space Hotels*.
2. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog:** The absolute best case. Phobolog's higher titanium value makes the 4 starting metal worth **16 MC**, pushing the Prelude's total value to ~28 MC—one of the highest in the deck.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Significantly increases the value of both the production and the starting titanium.
   - **Saturn Systems:** The Space tag provides an immediate +1 MC production boost for the corporation on top of the titanium production.
3. **Milestone Support:** Provides a requirement-free Space tag toward the **Space Baron** and **Spacefarer** milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Titanium production is only useful if you draw Space tags. If your starting hand is full of building or bio tags, OCY is an inefficient start compared to a pure MC production card.
- **Asteroid Mining Consortium (AMC) Risk:** Since OCY provides exactly 1 titanium production, an opponent playing *AMC* in Generation 1 or 2 can completely wipe out your production, effectively neutralizing your Prelude. **Always pair with another titanium source** to protect your engine.
- **Titanium Sink Requirement:** Only take OCY if you have a high-cost space card ready to play in Gen 1. If the metal sits in your inventory, you are losing out on the compound growth of MC production.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the Space tag on OCY to hit the requirements for powerful mid-game cards like **Quantum Extractor** or **Mass Converter** a generation early, catching opponents off guard.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Phobolog** or **Saturn Systems**. High priority if your opening hand contains a 20+ MC space project. Skip early if you lack space synergies or are Pursuing a biological engine.
