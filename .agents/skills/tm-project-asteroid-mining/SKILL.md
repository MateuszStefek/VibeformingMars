# Project: Asteroid Mining (#036)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 30 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 2 steps.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Engine Finisher)
**Status: A-TIER FOR JOVIANS / C-TIER EARLY GAME**

Asteroid Mining is a heavy space card that is often a "trap" for newer players but a cornerstone for Jovian strategies. While it offers significant production and points, its high upfront cost makes it a risky early-game investment.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Jovian Multiplier Catalyst:** The Jovian tag is the card's most valuable asset. If you have even one Jovian multiplier (e.g., *Water Import from Europa*), this card's value jumps from 2 VP to 3-5+ VP.
2. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This makes it an ideal way to "launder" accumulated titanium into guaranteed points and future production in the mid-game.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** High priority. Triggers their passive income and provides the titanium production they need for a space-heavy game.
   - **Phobolog:** One of the few corporations that can justify playing this in Generation 1-2 to convert their starting titanium into long-term engine power.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the production significantly more valuable.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** 33 MC (cost + draft) is a massive early-game hit. You could play three smaller production cards for the same price. Unless you are Phobolog, playing this in Generation 1 will often stall your economy for several turns.
- **Late Game Decay:** If played in the final 2 generations, you are paying 30 MC for 2 VP + whatever your Jovian multipliers add. This is only efficient if you have high Titanium production and no other space targets.

### Tips
- **ROI Math:** The card takes ~5.5 generations to break even on titanium alone. If the game is likely to end in under 6 generations (common in high-rush games), pass this card unless you need the Jovian tag.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for Saturn Systems or Jovian-heavy engines. High priority for titanium-rich players in the mid-game. Skip early unless playing Phobolog.
