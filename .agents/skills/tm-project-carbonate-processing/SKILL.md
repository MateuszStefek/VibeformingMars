# Project: Carbonate Processing (#043)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Heat production 3 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Heat Converter)
**Status: B-TIER FOR RUSH / C-TIER IN COLONIES**

Carbonate Processing is a high-efficiency heat card that is strictly limited by your energy infrastructure. It is a premier tool for players looking to accelerate the temperature track early, but it has lost some value in the modern, energy-hungry meta.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Heat Yield:** For only 6 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 3 heat production. If you have "free" energy from Preludes or your Corporation, the 9 MC total investment is the most efficient way to generate heat in the early game.
2. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card, it can be paid for entirely with Steel. This allows you to convert excess building resources into terraforming progress without spending liquid MC.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Helion:** The ultimate pairing. Since Helion can use Heat as MC, this effectively becomes a +3 MC production card for only 6 MC—an unbeatable return on investment.
   - **Ecoline:** Helps rush the temperature to hit plant-based thresholds (-6°C or 0°C) before engine-builders can stabilize.
   - **Thermalist Award:** A core card for securing the heat-based award.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Colonies Scarcity:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is at a premium for trading. Sacrificing 1 energy production for heat often delays your ability to trade for more impactful resources (like cards or titanium), which is usually a stronger long-term play.
- **Hidden Cost:** If you have to use a 11 MC Standard Project to get the energy production, the card's total cost jumps to 20 MC. In this scenario, *Mohole Area* is almost always superior as it provides more heat and a placement bonus.
- **Breakpoint Inefficiency:** 3 heat production is slightly awkward, as you need 8 heat to raise the temperature. Pair this with another 1-step heat card (like *Lichen*) to hit the 4-heat per generation mark.

### Tips
- **Milestone Support:** Use the low cost (6 MC) and Building tag to quickly hit the 8-building requirement for the **Builder** milestone if you are in a tight race.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft for **Helion** or dedicated **Rush** strategies. High priority if you have excess Energy or Steel in Generation 1-3. Skip if playing with *Colonies* or if you need your energy for card-draw engines.
