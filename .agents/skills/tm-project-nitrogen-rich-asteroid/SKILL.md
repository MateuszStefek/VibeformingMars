# Project: Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (#037)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 31 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 2 steps. Raise temperature 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step. If you have 3 or more Plant tags, increase your Plant production an additional 3 steps (Total +4).


## Strategic Analysis (The Game-Defining Engine)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / TOP 5 LATE-GAME EVENTS**

Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA) is widely considered one of the strongest cards in *Terraforming Mars*. It provides a massive, unconditional TR boost while offering a transformative production jump if you specialize in biological tags.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Production Spike:** If the 3-plant tag requirement is met, NRA provides **+4 plant production**. This is the single largest production jump on a non-scaling card, often providing enough volume to place a greenery every 2 generations.
2. **Immediate TR Density:** Provides 3 TR instantly (2 from effect + 1 from temperature), increasing your income and score immediately.
3. **Space Tag Utility:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This makes it a premier "dump" for corporations like **Phobolog** to jumpstart their terraforming engine.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Insects:** Provides the massive plant production needed to hit the high-tier biological requirements.
   - **Media Group / Optimal Aerobraking:** Triggers significant resource/MC rebates for space events.
   - **Ecoline:** The ultimate pairing. NRA turns Ecoline's already efficient greenery engine into an unstoppable board-dominating machine.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Buffer:** **Do not play this without the 3-plant tag bonus** unless you are in the final turn and need the TR to win. Playing NRA for only +1 plant production is a massive waste of 34 MC.
- **Over-Investing in Bad Tags:** Do not play "junk" plant cards (like *Micro Mills*) specifically to unlock NRA. NRA is a payoff for a bio-engine you were already building.
- **Early Hand-Clog:** Keeping NRA in your opening hand ties up 3 MC of draft capital for several generations. Only hold it if you have a clear path to 3 plant tags via Preludes or your corporation.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** If you have 2 plant tags and need 1 more, it is often worth using a Standard Project or a cheap plant event to hit the threshold and drop NRA immediately. The compounding value of the +4 production is worth the extra upfront investment.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" if you have a path to 3 plant tags. Priority 1 in drafts for bio-engineers. Essential for titanium-rich players in the mid-game. Skip late only if temperature is maxed and you cannot hit the production bonus.
