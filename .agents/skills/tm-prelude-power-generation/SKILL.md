# Prelude: Power Generation

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 3 steps. Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step.
- **Tags:** Power.

## Strategic Analysis (The Trading Anchor)
**Status: S-TIER IN COLONIES / A-TIER UTILITY**

Power Generation is one of the most impactful infrastructure Preludes in the game. While it carries a minor economic penalty, its massive energy yield makes it a premier tool for establishing a dominant trading economy or powering a card-draw engine from the very first generation.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **The "Magic Number" for Trading:** In the 2024-2025 meta, this card is prized primarily for the 3 energy production. It provides the exact amount needed to power a full trade fleet (3 energy), allowing you to begin trading with high-value moons like *Luna* or *Pluto* as early as Generation 2 for zero net MC overhead.
2. **Exceptional Yield:** 3 Energy production for only -1 MC production is a ~21 MC net value jump in Generation 1. This ensures you have the industrial base to fund any future power-hungry cards without relying on Standard Projects.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Poseidon:** The ultimate pairing. Poseidon thrives on early trading to snowball its ability, and Power Generation guarantees a Gen 1 trade.
   - **AI Central / Development Center:** Provides the energy needed to "turn on" these draw engines immediately upon play.
   - **Thorgate:** Benefits from the Power tag discount and their natural tendency to accumulate Power tags for milestones.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides 3 of the 6 steps needed for the **Energizer** milestone and contributes toward the **Diversifier** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Decay:** Decreasing your MC production in Generation 1 effectively costs you ~10 MC over the course of the game. Only take this if you have a clear plan to utilize the energy (trading or card draw) immediately. If the energy simply converts to heat, you are better off with a pure income Prelude.
- **Without Colonies:** If you are not playing with the *Colonies* expansion, the value of Power Generation drops. Unless you have high-requirement energy cards (like *Strip Mine*) or are racing for a milestone, it may be too slow for a "Rush" strategy.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Power tag and energy boost to hit the threshold for **Power Supply Consortium** in Generation 1. Stealing an opponent's energy while establishing your own trade fleet is one of the strongest "industrial" openings in the game.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" if playing with *Colonies*. High priority if holding *AI Central* or *Physics Complex*. skip only if your corporation already has high energy production and you need to prioritize pure income.
