# Project: Viral Enhancers (#167)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play a Plant, Microbe, or Animal tag (including this one), you may either gain 1 Plant or add 1 resource to that card (Microbe or Animal).


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Anchor)
**Status: S-TIER / BEST BIO-ENGINE ENABLER**

Viral Enhancers is the "grease" that makes biological strategies viable. It provides immediate resource generation and massive long-term value for a very low upfront cost.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Immediate ROI:** The card triggers on its own Microbe tag, providing 1 plant immediately. This effectively reduces its cost to 6 MC (+3 to draft).
2. **The "Bio-Trinity":** It forms the core of the legendary biological engine when paired with **Ecological Zone** and **Decomposers**. A single bio-tag can trigger all three, generating multiple resources and points simultaneously.
3. **Double Value Tags:** It provides a **Science tag** (crucial for requirements like *AI Central*) and a **Microbe tag** (for *Insects* or *Advanced Ecosystems*).
4. **Flexible Payout:** You can choose between a plant (for immediate terraforming) or an animal/microbe (for long-term VP). 
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Arklight / Splice:** These corporations turn the constant resource gain of Viral Enhancers into a game-winning economic or point advantage.
   - **1 VP/Animal Cards:** Makes cards like *Birds* or *Fish* worth an extra 1 VP immediately upon being played.
   - **Advanced Ecosystems:** Provides 3 plants when played, as it has all three bio-tags.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Blind Resource Placement:** Do not always default to adding an animal/microbe. If gaining 1 plant allows you to complete a greenery this turn, it is almost always better due to the TR increase and board control.
- **Timing:** Draft it early, but only play it once you have 2-3 other bio-tags in hand or a clear path to them. Playing it with zero follow-up bio-cards is a waste of 12 MC.

### Tips
- **Hate Drafting:** If an opponent is collecting plant/animal tags, you must cut this card from the draft. The point swing from a Viral Enhancer-fueled bio-engine can be 10+ VP over the course of the game.

**Recommendation:** A "First-Pick" draft priority for any engine-building strategy. Essential for bio-focused decks. High priority for early-to-mid game investment.
