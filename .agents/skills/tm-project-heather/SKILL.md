# Project: Heather (#024)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -14°C
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step. Gain 1 Plant.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Bloomer)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / CHEAP SYNERGY TAG**

Heather is a simple, low-cost plant card that is highly dependent on the pace of the temperature track. While its production is basic, its low cost makes it an excellent "filler" for biological engines.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Low Cost Synergy:** For only 6 MC (+3 to draft), it provides a valuable Plant tag. It is one of the cheapest enablers for high-tier cards like **Insects** or **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)**.
2. **Immediate Plant Bonus:** The 1 plant gain frequently acts as the "8th plant" needed to convert a greenery in the mid-game, allowing you to secure a TR and 1-2 VP before an opponent can drop a meteor.
3. **Bio-Trinity Support:** Triggers the point/resource engines of *Ecological Zone*, *Viral Enhancers*, and *Decomposers* for a very small investment.
4. **Solo Mode Essential:** In solo play, Heather is high-priority as every point of plant production counts toward reaching the Oxygen goal in a tight timeframe.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -14°C requirement is relatively steep. If the temperature track is lagging (common in engine-heavy games), Heather will sit in your hand for several generations, losing its long-term production value.
- **Lichen Comparison:** Compared to *Lichen* (-24°C), Heather arrives 5 steps later. If you have to choose between them, *Lichen* is almost always superior for pure production.
- **Slow Payout:** In 4-5 player games, the game may end only 3-4 generations after -14°C is reached, meaning Heather might only produce 4-5 total plants—less than the 8 needed for a greenery.

### Tips
- **"First you heat her, then you Heather":** Do not hold this card from Generation 1 unless you have a specific plan to raise the temperature rapidly or are playing *Ecoline*. It is a better mid-game draft once the temperature has hit at least -20°C.

**Recommendation:** A solid "filler" draft in Generations 3-5. High priority if you are playing a bio-engine or need the tag for *Insects*. Skip if the temperature is not moving.
