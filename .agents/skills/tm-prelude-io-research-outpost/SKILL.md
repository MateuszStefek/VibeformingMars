# Prelude: Io Research Outpost

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step. Draw 1 card.
- **Tags:** Science, Jovian.

## Strategic Analysis (The Dual Tag Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER ENGINE PIECE / S-TIER SYNERGY START**

Io Research Outpost is a high-utility Prelude prized for its unique combination of two of the most valuable tags in the game. While its resource yield is modest, its role as a "requirement faster" makes it an essential tool for specialized engine builds.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Jovian** tag in Generation 1.
   - **Science:** Critical for hitting requirements for *AI Central* or *Mars University* immediately.
   - **Jovian:** Essential for establishing a Jovian strategy early, enabling cards like *Miranda Resort* or *Rim Settler* milestone.
2. **Card Neutrality:** The 1-card draw ensures you don't lose hand parity, potentially finding the very card you want to spend your newly gained titanium on.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** The ultimate pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive income and provides the titanium production and Jovian tag needed for their engine.
   - **Point Luna:** The Science and Jovian tags can trigger card draw depending on your other synergies, making the card effectively a "draw 2" start.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to liquidate their starting metal into a permanent industrial lead.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Titanium production is only useful if you draw Space tags. If your starting hand is full of building or bio tags, Io Research Outpost is an inefficient start compared to a pure MC production card.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** Community analysis values this Prelude at only **~17 MC** (10 for production, 3-4 for the card, 3-4 for tags), which is slightly below the standard 21 MC benchmark. Only pick it if the tags specifically enable a high-impact card in your hand.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the tags to hit the 3-science requirement for **AI Central**. Most opponents expect a science engine to take 3-4 generations to build; the Outpost allows you to drop the AI in Gen 1 or 2, which is often a game-winning advantage.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Saturn Systems**, **Phobolog**, or anyone Pursuing a science-heavy engine. Excellent as a "fixer" for a hand with high-tier requirements. Skip early if you lack space synergies.
