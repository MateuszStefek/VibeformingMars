# Project: Strip Mine (#092)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 25 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 2 steps. Increase your Steel production 2 steps. Increase your Titanium production 1 step. Raise Oxygen 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Industrial Heavyweight)
**Status: A-TIER OPENER / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Strip Mine is one of the most efficient early-game production cards, but it carries a high opportunity cost. It provides a massive jump in industrial power and terraforming progress, making it a "must-pick" for players who can solve its energy requirement quickly.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Resource Bundle:** +2 Steel and +1 Titanium production is worth ~7 MC per generation. Combined with 2 TR (from Oxygen), the card provides **~9 MC of value per generation**. If played in Generation 1 or 2, it will return over 100 MC of value by the end of the game.
2. **Double Oxygen Bump:** Providing 2 Oxygen steps in a single action is highly valuable for "sniping" temperature bonuses or reaching high-tier requirements early.
3. **Steel Sink Catalyst:** As a Building card, the 25 MC cost can be paid with Steel. This allows corporations like **Interplanetary Cinematics** to "flip" their starting resources into permanent engine power.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Mining Guild:** Triggers the corporation's passive twice, providing an additional 2 steel production for an unbeatable industrial lead.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the 3 steps of metal production worth significantly more.
   - **Solo Mode:** Essential for maxing the Oxygen track quickly while establishing the economy needed for final parameters.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Bottleneck:** -2 Energy production is a heavy hit. **Never play this using Standard Projects** to get the energy; the ~22 MC added cost makes the ROI too slow. Only play if you have "free" energy from Preludes or cheap cards like *Fueled Generators*.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is needed for trading. Tying up 2 energy production in Generation 1 can cripple your ability to trade for better cards or resources.
- **Timing:** Its value drops significantly after Generation 3. It takes roughly 5 generations of production just to break even on the ~42 MC effective cost.

### Tips
- **Requirement Buffer:** Use the 2 Oxygen steps to jump over opponents and reach the -24°C Heat Production bonus or the -20°C Ocean bonus before they can react.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" if you have a path to 2 energy production in Generation 1-2. High priority for **Mining Guild** or **IC**. Skip after Generation 4.
