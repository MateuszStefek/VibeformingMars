# Project: Fish (#158)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** Min +2°C
- **Effect:** Decrease any player's Plant production 1 step.
- **Action:** Add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Animal on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Aquatic Predator)
**Status: A-TIER VP SOURCE / SITUATIONAL OFFENSE**

Fish is a premier late-game point accumulator. Along with *Birds* and *Small Animals*, it belongs to the elite "1:1 Club" where each resource is worth a full victory point. Its reliance on temperature makes it more predictable and often easier to play than Oxygen-dependent animal cards.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **High Efficiency:** At 9 MC (+3 to draft), it costs only 12 MC. If played early enough to take the action 3 times, you are paying **4 MC per VP**, which is better than the community benchmark (~7 MC/VP).
2. **Predictable Timing:** Temperature usually rises faster than Oxygen in many metas. Fish often hits the table in Generations 6-7, giving you 3-4 generations of natural growth.
3. **Targeted Disruption:** Like *Birds*, Fish allows you to decrease an opponent's plant production. This is a powerful counter against **Ecoline** or anyone building a greenery engine.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** The ultimate multiplier. Trading with Miranda allows you to "cheat" multiple animals onto Fish in a single generation, potentially netting 8-12+ VP.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate animal (1 VP) upon play, effectively reducing the "payback" time by one generation.
   - **Large Convoy / Imported Nitrogen:** High-impact events to dump animals onto Fish for instant point bombs.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Mandatory Production Hit:** You **must** decrease someone's plant production to play the card. If all opponents are at 0, you must hit yourself. Check the board before drafting.
- **Temperature Barrier:** +2°C is a mid-to-late game requirement. Do not keep this in your opening hand unless you have the **Ecology Experts** Prelude, which allows you to play it in Generation 1 for a massive 10+ VP engine.

### Tips
- **Fish vs. Birds:** In most games, Fish is considered slightly superior to *Birds* because its requirement (+2°C) is typically met earlier than 13% Oxygen. Draft Fish over Birds if you have to choose between them and temperature is moving steadily.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game. Essential for bio-engine strategies or if playing with *Miranda*. Skip only if the temperature track is completely stalled.
