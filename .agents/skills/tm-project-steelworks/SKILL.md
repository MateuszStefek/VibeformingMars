# Project: Steelworks (#064)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 4 Energy to gain 2 Steel and raise Oxygen 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Premier Bumper)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Steelworks is widely considered the best of the "energy-to-oxygen" industrial cards. It provides a powerful way to move the oxygen track while generating building resources, making it a cornerstone for "Rush" strategies and metal-heavy engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Oxygen Control:** Provides a reliable way to raise Oxygen without relying on biological production. Use it to "sniping" oxygen thresholds or to force the game to end before engine-building opponents can stabilize.
2. **Resource Multiplier:** Unlike *Ironworks* or *Ore Processor*, Steelworks provides **2 Steel**. This 4 MC equivalent value (or 6 MC with *Advanced Alloys*) makes the action much more efficient for players with building-tag "sinks" in hand.
3. **Steel Sink Catalyst:** As a Building card, the 15 MC cost can be paid entirely with **Steel**. This allows you to convert mineral wealth into immediate TR and future steel production without spending liquid cash.
4. **Solo Mode Essential:** Consistently rated as S-tier in Solo play. It removes the RNG dependency on drawing plant cards and ensures you hit the mandatory Oxygen target in time.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High Energy Overhead:** Requiring 4 energy *every generation* is a massive economic hit (~28 MC value). If you don't have "free" energy from high-tier science cards like *Mass Converter*, the opportunity cost of Steelworks is often too high.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** You must use the action at least **4 times** to break even compared to simply buying Standard Project greeneries.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is at a premium for trading. 4 energy spent here is 4 energy not spent trading for high-value resources at a colony.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Blocking:** Use the oxygen steps from Steelworks to claim the 8% Oxygen temperature bonus or the final TR bonus before an opponent can finish their greenery conversion.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in multiplayer if you have 4+ energy production and a surplus of Steel. Priority 1 in **Solo Mode**. Skip late if Oxygen is already nearing the max.
