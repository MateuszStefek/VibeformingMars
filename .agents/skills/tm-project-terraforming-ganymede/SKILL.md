# Project: Terraforming Ganymede (#049)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 33 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 1 step for each Jovian tag you have (including this one).
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Game-Ender)
**Status: S-TIER VP MULTIPLIER / THE ULTIMATE TITANIUM DUMP**

Terraforming Ganymede is the crown jewel of the Jovian strategy. It is arguably the most powerful "point-slam" card in the game, providing a massive swing in both TR and victory points that can single-handedly secure a win in long-form matches.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exponential Scoring:** Unlike static point cards, TG's value scales linearly with your specialization. In a dedicated build (using cards like *Io Mining*, *Miranda Resort*, and *Saturn Systems*), this card is frequently worth **8-12+ total points** (TR + VP).
2. **Titanium Liquidation:** As a Space card, the entire 33 MC cost can be paid with Titanium. This is the premier way for titanium-rich players or corporations like **Phobolog** to "wash" their metal into final victory points.
3. **Immediate TR Density:** Provides a sudden surge in TR, which is vital for winning the **Benefactor** award or reaching the 26 TR needed for the **Terraformer** milestone in the final turn.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** The ultimate pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive and provides a massive TR boost that complements their engine.
   - **Other Multipliers:** Stacks with *Water Import from Europa* and *Ganymede Colony* for a "Wombo Combo" that can yield 30+ points in a single generation.
   - **Wild Tags:** If you have Wild tags (e.g., from *Research*), they count toward the TG payout, further increasing its efficiency.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Stealth Timing:** **Never play this early.** Playing TG in Generations 1-5 is a massive mistake. It alerts the entire table to your Jovian strategy, ensuring you will be "hate-drafted" for the rest of the game. Hold it in your hand until the very last generation.
- **Vacuum Value:** In a vacuum (zero other Jovian tags), 36 MC (cost + draft) for 1 TR and 2 VP is one of the worst ratios in the game. Only keep this if you have a path to at least 4 total Jovian tags.
- **Economic Sacrifice:** 33 MC is a massive hit to your budget. Do not play this if it prevents you from established more efficient production early in the game.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If you are not the Jovian player, you **must** cut this card from the draft. Allowing an opponent to land TG is the most common reason for a sudden 15-point swing that flips the leaderboard in the final round.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Must-Draft" for any strategy. Priority 1 in drafts for titanium-rich players or Jovian specialists. Skip early only if you have zero Jovian potential and are pursuing a pure "Rush" strategy.
