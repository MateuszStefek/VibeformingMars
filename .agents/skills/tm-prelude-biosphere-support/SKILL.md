# Prelude: Biosphere Support

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Plant production 2 steps.
- **Tag:** Plant.

## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Specialist Pivot)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR ECOLINE**

Biosphere Support is widely considered one of the weakest Prelude cards due to its immediate negative impact on your economy. While it jumpstarts a biological engine, the loss of liquid income early in the game is a major disadvantage that only high-tier synergies can overcome.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **Ecoline Powerhouse:** The absolute best case. Combined with Ecoline's starting production and reduced greenery cost, Biosphere Support allows you to place a forest every 2 generations, creating an immediate and overwhelming board lead.
2. **Plant Tag Catalyst:** The most valuable part of the card for engine builders. It provides 1 of the 3 tags needed to trigger **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or to satisfy the scaling requirement for **Insects** in Generation 1.
3. **Milestone Racing:** On the **Tharsis map**, it is an essential tool for securing the **Gardener** milestone (2 greeneries) early by ensuring a consistent supply of plants.
4. **Requirement Bypassing:** Helps reach the plant production requirements for high-tier cards if you have requirement-free tags ready to follow up.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Negative Snowball:** Losing 1 MC production in Generation 1 effectively costs you ~10 MC over the course of the game. For many corporations, this economic hit prevents them from established more efficient industrial engines.
- **Predator Magnet:** A +2 plant production jump in Generation 1 makes you the #1 target for **Asteroids** and **Virus**. Without **Protected Habitats**, you may find your resources destroyed before you can ever convert them to TR.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** 2 plant production takes 4 generations to produce a single greenery. If the game ends in Generation 8 (common in high-player games), the card only yields 2 greeneries for a high economic price.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Biosphere Support specifically to enable **NRA** or **Insects**. The production is secondary to the tag; the primary goal is triggering a much more powerful payoff later in the game.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are playing **Ecoline**, holding **NRA**, or are desperate for a Plant tag to hit a specific milestone. Skip early in favor of pure economic production. slightly more viable in Solo mode.
