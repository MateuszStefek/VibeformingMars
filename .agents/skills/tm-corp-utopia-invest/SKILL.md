# Corporation: Utopia Invest

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Turmoil
- **Starting MC:** 40
- **Tags:** Building
- **Starting Production:** 1 Steel, 1 Titanium
- **Action:** Decrease any production 1 step to gain 4 resources of that kind immediately.

## Strategic Analysis (The Strategic Sacrificer)
**Status: B-TIER / BURST**

Utopia Invest is the master of the "Last-Second Snipe." It allows players to trade long-term growth (production) for immediate impact (resources), making it an elite choice for winning milestones and awards.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Burst Resource Gain:** Need 4 titanium to finish a *Jupiter* card? Sacrifice 1 titanium production and you have it. This allows for incredible flexibility in completing high-value plays.
2. **Milestone Sniper:** Utopia is one of the best at stealing 'Mayor', 'Builder', or 'Gardener' milestones by suddenly having the resources needed to finish them.
3. **Solid Starting Tags:** Starting with both steel and titanium production and a building tag provides a very flexible infrastructure.

### Synergies:
- **Production-Boosting Cards:** Cards that give multiple production steps (*Mohole Area*) are great because you can "spend" one of those steps for an immediate 4-resource burst.
- **Energy Strategies:** Sacrifice energy production for immediate energy to power a blue card action *this* turn.
- **Awards:** Highly effective at securing 'Miner' or 'Thermalist' awards in the final generation.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The 4x Rule:** Only use the action if the 4 resources you gain allow you to play a card *now* that is worth more than the lost production over the rest of the game.
- **Generation 1 Burst:** Use your starting steel/titanium production to gain 4 resources in Gen 1 to jumpstart your engine.

**Recommendation:** A strong, high-skill-cap corporation. Excellent for players who can calculate the break-even point of production vs. immediate resources.
