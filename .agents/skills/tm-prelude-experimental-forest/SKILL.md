# Prelude: Experimental Forest

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Reveal cards from the deck until you reveal 2 Plant tags. Take those into your hand and discard the rest. Place a Greenery tile. Raise Oxygen 1 step.
- **Tag:** Plant.

## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Launcher)
**Status: S-TIER / THE PREMIER BOARD-CONTROL PRELUDE**

Experimental Forest is widely considered one of the most powerful Preludes in the game. It provides a dense bundle of TR, board presence, and hand quality that can define the entire trajectory of a match from the very first generation.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Board Impact:** A greenery tile in Generation 1 is worth ~23 MC of value. It grants immediate TR (increasing your income), secures a high-value board spot (like a "2 titanium" or "3 plant" bonus), and puts you halfway to the **Gardener** milestone immediately.
2. **Precision Card Advantage:** Drawing 2 guaranteed **Plant tags** allows you to skip the RNG of the opening hand and establish a biological engine with near certainty. This frequently enables high-tier payoffs like **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or **Insects** by Generation 2 or 3.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Ecoline:** The "God Tier" start. Ecoline can often drop a *second* greenery in Gen 1 after playing this card, creating an overwhelming board lead.
   - **Arctic Algae:** If played after or alongside, the greenery placement provides the plants needed for further snowballing.
   - **Ecological Zone:** Triggers the plant tag and greenery placement for immediate animals/points.
4. **Deck Thinning:** In high-player games, revealing cards until 2 plant tags are found "thins" the deck, ensuring you see more diverse cards in later generations.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Heat Risk:** If you have zero heat production, your greeneries are vulnerable to opponent's asteroids for more turns. Only take if you have a path to raising the temperature to protect your forest clusters.
- **Milestone Race:** Do not take Experimental Forest if an opponent is also playing a greenery-heavy corporation (like *Ecoline*) and has higher turn priority, as they may "block" your expansion path before you can fully utilize the anchor.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use the immediate greenery placement to take the highest-value placement bonus on the board (e.g., the 3-plant spot on Elysium). This denial is often as valuable as the TR itself.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" for bio-engineers or anyone Pursuing the **Gardener** milestone. Essential for **Ecoline** or **Tharsis Republic**. Skip only if you are strictly Pursuing a "Space Engine" and have zero interest in the board game.
