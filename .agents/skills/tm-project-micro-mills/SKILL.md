# Project: Micro-Mills (#053)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 3 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Correction Tool)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "MATH FIXER"**

Micro-Mills is one of the most polarizing cards in the game. While often dismissed as unexciting, its value lies in its extremely low cost and its ability to "fix" the heat math for terraforming rushers.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **The "Math Fixer":** Because heat raises temperature in increments of 8, having 7 or 15 heat production is a "tempo loss." Micro-Mills is a very cheap (3+3 MC) way to hit the 4 or 8 production threshold, ensuring you get your next temperature raise a full generation earlier.
2. **Cheapest Production:** At only 3 MC (+3 to draft), it is the most affordable way to increase any production step. This makes it a high-priority "panic buy" if you are racing for the **Generalist** milestone (requires production in all 6 areas).
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Community Services:** Since it has no tags, it effectively becomes an income card (+1 MC production) when this synergy is active.
   - **Administrator Award:** Contributes toward the award for most cards with no tags.
   - **Tempo Stalling:** Like other cheap cards, it can be used as a "pass action" to see an opponent's move before committing your main budget.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Impact:** +1 Heat production provides very little value over the course of a 10-generation game (~1.25 TR). Unless you have a specific synergy or a "math fix" reason, the 6 MC investment is usually better spent on card draw or economic production.
- **Drafting Waste:** **Never draft this in Generations 1-3** unless you strictly need it for *Generalist*. Holding it in your hand wastes 3 MC of draft capital that could have gone toward an S-tier engine piece.
- **No Tag Value:** Provides zero progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Ecologist* or *Builder*.

### Tips
- **Free Play Only:** The 2024-2025 meta consensus is that you should **only play this card if you draw it for free.** Spending 3 MC to buy it in a draft is rarely worth the opportunity cost.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Keep only if you are racing for **Generalist**, have "no-tag" synergies, or draw it for free in the first half of the game. Skip in all other scenarios.
