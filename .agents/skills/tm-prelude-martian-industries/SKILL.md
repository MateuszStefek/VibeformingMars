# Prelude: Martian Industries

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Steel production 1 step. Gain 6 MegaCredits (MC).
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Fixer)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "FIXER" CARD**

Martian Industries is a versatile infrastructure card that provides a balanced foundation for early-game industrial and engine building. While it lacks the raw economic power of top-tier Preludes, its ability to provide three distinct resources and a valuable tag makes it a solid "worker" choice for specific hand requirements.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **Specific Requirement Bridging:** If your starting hand contains a high-value card requiring exactly 1 energy (e.g., *Electro Catapult* or *Water Splitting Plant*), Martian Industries is a perfect "Generation 1" enabler.
2. **The "Robotic Workforce" Target:** Provides a Building-tagged production box. Copying Martian Industries with *Robotic Workforce* in Generation 1 or 2 is a high-efficiency move that provides a total of +2 Energy and +2 Steel production for only 9 MC.
3. **Colonies Enabler:** Helps hit the 3-energy threshold for trading in the *Colonies* expansion, especially when paired with corporations like **Thorgate** or other energy Preludes.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a **Building tag** and **Steel production**, contributing to the *Builder* and *Industrialist* milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Lack of Focus:** Compared to *Business Empire* (+6 MC prod) or *Mining Operations* (+2 Steel prod), Martian Industries spreads its value thin. 1 energy and 1 steel production are often not enough to define a winning strategy on their own.
- **Low Liquidity:** The 6 MC cash gain is the lowest in the deck for an economic Prelude. Ensure your starting budget can handle the cost of your primary projects without needing more cash.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you only need metal production, *Metals Company* or *Galilean Mining* are generally more efficient. Only take Martian Industries if you have a specific use for the energy.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Martian Industries as a cheap Building tag enabler. It hits the requirements for **Research Outpost** or triggers building discounts, allowing you to establishment your industrial base a generation early.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft if your starting hand has an energy requirement or if you hold *Robotic Workforce*. Skip early if you need to prioritize pure income production or if playing a bio-engine strategy.
