# Project: Martian Zoo (#182)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Animal, Building
- **Requirements:** 2 City Tiles in play (by any player).
- **Effect:** When you play an Earth tag, add 1 Animal to this card.
- **Action:** Gain 1 MegaCredit (MC) for each Animal on this card.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Earth Engine Payoff)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Martian Zoo is a high-potential income engine that rewards players for specializing in Earth tags. While its city requirement can be a bottleneck, its ability to generate scalable income makes it a premier tool for establishing a dominant economy in the mid-to-late game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Scalable Income:** Unlike flat production cards, Martian Zoo's income grows with every Earth tag you play. In a dedicated Earth build (using cards like *Sponsors*, *Acquired Company*, or *Earth Catapult*), it can easily provide 5-8+ MC per generation.
2. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both an **Animal** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Animal:** Triggers *Ecological Zone* and can act as a "decoy" for predators.
   - **Building:** Can be paid for entirely with Steel, making it a very cheap engine starter.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** The ultimate multiplier. Trading with Miranda allows you to dump extra animals onto the Zoo, bypassing the dependency on Earth tags and creating a massive cash pump.
   - **Viron:** Allows you to use the action twice per turn, effectively doubling your animal-based income.
   - **Meat Industry:** Grants 2 MC every time an animal is added to the card, effectively providing a 2 MC rebate on every Earth tag you play.
4. **Solo Mode Powerhouse:** Essential for hit the economic thresholds needed to max all parameters in time.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **City Requirement:** The 2-city requirement can delay the card until Generation 3-5. Do not hold this in your starting hand if you anticipate a slow "Rush" game without cities. Look for opponents playing **Tharsis Republic** to meet the requirement for you.
- **Timing:** Its value is strictly tied to how many Earth tags you have left to play. **Never buy this in the final two generations** unless you strictly need the 1 VP or the Building tag for a milestone.
- **Action Economy:** Like all action cards, it consumes one of your two actions per turn. In the early game, ensure the MC gain is worth more than a more impactful terraforming or card-draw move.

### Tips
- **Predator Buffer:** Since Martian Zoo provides a flat 1 VP regardless of the number of animals, it is a safe place to store bio-resources. If an opponent plays *Predators*, they might target your Zoo animals to slow your economy, which is less painful than losing VP-bearing animals on *Birds* or *Fish*.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-4 if you have Earth synergies or are playing with *Miranda*. Skip early if cities are not on the board. Priority 1 for **Viron** or **Solo Mode**.
