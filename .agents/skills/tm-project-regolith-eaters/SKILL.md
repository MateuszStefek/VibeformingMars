# Project: Regolith Eaters (#033)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Either add 1 Microbe to this card, or remove 2 Microbes from this card to raise Oxygen 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Oxygen Thief)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Regolith Eaters is a foundational terraforming engine that converts time (microbe accumulation) into Oxygen progress. While slow on its own, it serves as a premier tool for "sniping" oxygen thresholds and is a cornerstone for science-based biological strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Oxygen Control:** Provides a reliable way to raise Oxygen without placing a greenery. Use it to "steal" the 8% Oxygen temperature bonus or other thresholds before a plant-heavy opponent can reach them with their greenery conversion.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Microbe** tag.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University*.
   - **Microbe:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Insects*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Extreme-Cold Fungus (ECF):** The "God Tier" combo. ECF adds 2 microbes per generation, allowing you to **raise Oxygen every single generation**, potentially ending the game 3-4 generations earlier.
   - **Enceladus (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for microbes to bypass the slow 2-turn buildup.
   - **Solo Mode Essential:** Oxygen is often the hardest parameter to reach in Solo play; Regolith Eaters provides a steady, non-random way to ensure the track is maxed.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** Without boosters, it takes 2 generations to gain 1 Oxygen step. In a fast-paced "Rush" game, the 16 MC investment (cost + draft) may never trigger more than once, making it statistically inferior to immediate terraforming events.
- **Early Economic Hit:** Spending 16 MC in Generation 1-2 for slow terraforming progress will stall your economy. It is often best played as a mid-game enabler for Science requirements.
- **Ants Predator:** **Ants** can steal the microbes from Regolith Eaters. If an opponent has Ants, prioritize raising the Oxygen immediately rather than hoarding microbes.

### Tips
- **Ecoline Counter:** Use Regolith Eaters to max out the Oxygen track early. This makes Ecoline's plant production less valuable for TR, forcing them to spend their resources on point-scoring greeneries rather than income-generating ones.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you have microbe boosters or need a cheap Science tag. Priority 1 in **Solo Mode**. Skip late if Oxygen is already nearing the max.
