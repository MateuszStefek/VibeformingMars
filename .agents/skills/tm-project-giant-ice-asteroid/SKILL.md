# Project: Giant Ice Asteroid (#008)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 36 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 2 steps. Place 2 ocean tiles. Remove up to 6 plants from any player.
- **VP:** 0 (but provides 4 TR immediately)


## Strategic Analysis (The Game-Ender)
**Status: S-TIER / BEST TERRAFORMING EVENT**

Giant Ice Asteroid (GIA) is widely considered one of the most powerful and versatile cards in the game. It combines high-impact terraforming with aggressive plant denial.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Immediate TR Spike:** Provides 4 TR instantly (2 from temperature, 2 from oceans), which increases your income for the rest of the game.
2. **Aggressive Denial:** Removing 6 plants can single-handedly ruin an opponent's plan to convert a greenery, potentially denying them 2 VP (greenery + city bonus) and a TR.
3. **Space Tag Efficiency:** Can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This makes it a perfect "dump" for players with high Titanium production but low MC.
4. **Ocean Placement:** Allows you to grab two high-value placement bonuses or card-draw spots on the map, and set up "ocean walking" for future greeneries.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "Golden Combo." The two oceans trigger Arctic Algae for 4 plants, which often allows you to place a greenery in the same turn.
   - **Media Group / Optimal Aerobraking:** Significant MC/resource rebates on a high-cost event.
   - **Credicor:** Triggers the 4 MC rebate for high-cost cards.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Economy Timing:** Do not spend your entire Generation 1 budget on GIA if it prevents you from building an engine. It is a "Rush" card; playing it too early without a follow-up can leave you with high TR but no infrastructure to win the late game.
- **Titanium Stockpile:** Best played when you have excess titanium. Using 39 MC of "pure cash" is expensive unless it claims a critical Milestone or global threshold.

### Tips
- **Sniper Usage:** Use GIA to hit the +2°C or 0°C temperature thresholds to claim the "Heat Production" or "Ocean" bonuses before an opponent can. The plant destruction should always target the player closest to 8 plants.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft pick. Almost always worth the 3 MC to hold. Essential for "Rush" strategies and Titanium-heavy engines.
