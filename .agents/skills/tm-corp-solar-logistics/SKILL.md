# Corporation: Solar Logistics

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 40 MC
- **Ability:** Earth tag discount; card draw on space events.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: A-TIER / COMBO**

Highly efficient card-cycling and discount engine.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Focus on 'Space Hotel' or 'Banker'. Board geography is secondary to space card efficiency.
### Hellas
Prime map for 'Rim Settler' milestone. The ocean placement bonuses are great for titanium.
### Elysium
Target the 'Celebrity' award. High-value cards are your primary scoring engine.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Solar Logistics's unique mechanics.
