# Prelude: Huge Asteroid

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 3 steps. Pay 5 MC.
- **Tag:** None.

## Strategic Analysis (The Game Pacer)
**Status: S-TIER RUSH TOOL / A-TIER FOR HELION**

Huge Asteroid is the most powerful terraforming Prelude in the game. It allows a single player to fundamentally shift the game clock by jumping the temperature track before the first turn, forcing engine-builders to accelerate their plans or risk losing to a rapid game-end.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 3 TR you gain is worth ~24 MC in score and income. Even after paying the 5 MC penalty, the card provides **~19 MC of immediate value**, which is close to the Prelude benchmark while also advancing the game end.
2. **Threshold Sniping:** Pushing the temperature 3 steps immediately often hits the first threshold bonus (-24°C Heat Production) for free. This adds an additional ~7 MC of value to the card, making the total return one of the highest in the deck.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Helion:** The absolute best case. Helion can use the instant heat production as MC, effectively neutralizing the card's 5 MC penalty while gaining 3 TR.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Jumpstarting the temperature allows you to play high-value plant cards (like *Lichen* or *Grass*) 2-3 generations earlier than normal.
   - **UNMI:** Ensures you can use your corporation action (boost TR) in Generation 1 without needing to play a standard project.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Liquid Cash Loss:** You **must** pay 5 MC to play this card. If your corporation starts with low cash (e.g., *Phobolog*, *Valley Trust*), the 5 MC hit can prevent you from playing your best Generation 1 card.
- **2-Player Paradox:** In 1v1 games, rushing the game end is only beneficial if you have a clear lead. If you are building a slow engine yourself, Huge Asteroid may accidentally help your opponent win faster.
- **Zero Tags:** Aside from the TR, the card provides zero progress toward milestones like *Builder* or *Ecologist*.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Production:** Use Huge Asteroid to "lock out" engine-builders. By ending the temperature track early, you reduce the number of generations your opponents have to draw cards and accumulate income, often deciding the match before their engine matures.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Helion**, **UNMI**, or anyone pursuing a "Rush" strategy. Essential in **Solo Mode**. Skip early if you are Pursuing a long-term science engine and need every credit for card-draw infrastructure.
