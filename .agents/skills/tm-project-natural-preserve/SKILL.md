# Project: Natural Preserve (#044)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building
- **Requirements:** Max 4% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Place the Natural Preserve tile next to no other tile.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Strategic Anchor)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER OPENING HAND**

Natural Preserve is a high-utility hybrid card that combines board presence, economic scaling, and critical requirement enablers. Its ability to "teleport" to a remote map location makes it a premier tool for securing high-value bonuses early in the game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Map Teleportation:** The requirement to place it "next to no other tile" allows you to establishment a presence in a remote, lucrative area of the board (like the 3-plant spot on Elysium) without needing to "walk" your tiles there.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University* without draining liquid MC.
   - **Building:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making the 1 VP and 1 production effectively "free" for metal-heavy corporations.
3. **Milestone Powerhouse:** Contributes toward the **Scientist**, **Builder**, and **Landlord** milestones/awards simultaneously.
4. **Defensive Blocking:** Use the tile to occupy a high-value city spot far from other players, preventing them from grouping their cities for adjacency bonuses.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The "Max 4% Oxygen" limit is extremely strict. In high-player games or games with oxygen-rushing Preludes, this card can become a "dead draw" as early as Generation 2. **Play it immediately** in Generation 1 if possible.
- **Placement Logic:** Do not place the Preserve in an area where you cannot easily build out from it. Its value is highest when it serves as a second "anchor" for your future greenery chain.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Strategic players often use Natural Preserve as their "first" science tag. Since it is so cheap and provides a board presence, it is a low-risk way to start building toward high-tier science payoffs.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" in your starting hand if Oxygen is low. High priority for **Mining Guild** or anyone pursuing a science-heavy engine. Skip only if Oxygen is already at 3-4% and you have no way to play it this turn.
