# Map: Hellas

## Key Features & Placement
- **South Pole:** Contains the Hellas Sea and grants significant heat bonuses. The bottom two rows are highly relevant for the Polar Explorer milestone.
- **South Pole Special Hex:** Grants an **Ocean** as a placement bonus (Great Aquifer effect).
- **Bonuses:** Rich in Heat and Titanium in the southern hemisphere.

## Milestones
- **Diversifier:** 8 different tags in play. Rewards a broad "toolbox" strategy.
- **Tactician:** 5 cards with requirements in play. Strong for Inventrix.
- **Polar Explorer:** 3 tiles on the bottom 2 rows. A fast placement race.
- **Energizer:** 6 energy production. High priority for ThorGate.
- **Rim Settler:** 3 Jovian tags. Makes Jovian strategies (Saturn Systems) extremely potent.

## Awards
- **Cultivator:** Most greenery tiles.
- **Magnate:** Most automated (green) cards.
- **Space Baron:** Most space tags. Phobolog's best friend.
- **Excentric:** Most resources on cards.
- **Contractor:** Most building tags.

## Strategy Tips (Community Consensus)
- **Jovian Meta:** Because of **Rim Settler** and **Space Baron**, any Jovian multiplier strategy is significantly stronger on Hellas than on other maps.
- **The South Pole Race:** The South Pole placement bonus is one of the most powerful on any map. Rushing to place a tile there can give you a "free" ocean and TR.
- **Heat Rushing:** Plentiful heat bonuses make temperature the fastest parameter to max out. High heat production is more valuable here.
- **Diversifier Timing:** Keep track of your tags! This is often the first milestone claimed if someone draws a varied starting hand.
