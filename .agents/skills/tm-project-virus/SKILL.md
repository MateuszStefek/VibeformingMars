# Project: Virus (#050)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 1 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Remove up to 2 Animals or up to 5 Plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Momentum Killer)
**Status: S-TIER DISRUPTION / THE BIO-ENGINE COUNTER**

Virus is the most efficient offensive card in the base game. For a negligible cost, it provides a massive "take-that" effect that can ruin an opponent's plant engine or destroy several generations of animal accumulation.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Denial Efficiency:** For a total investment of 4 MC (+3 draft), you destroy ~10-15 MC worth of an opponent's resources (5 plants or 2 animals). This is a massive "tempo swing" that can stall an opponent's forest placement by multiple generations.
2. **Early Game Lethality:** Using Virus in Generations 2-4 to strip 5 plants from a player with low production (like *Tharsis* or *Credicor*) can prevent them from ever gaining a foothold in the greenery game.
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both a **Microbe** and an **Event** tag for a near-zero cost.
   - **Microbe Tag:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers* (gain a plant), *Decomposers*, and *Splice*.
   - **Event Tag:** Triggers rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) and *Interplanetary Cinematics* (+2 MC). With these active, Virus becomes **profit-generating** to play.
4. **Endgame Point-Snipe:** In the final round, removing 2 animals from a 1 VP/animal card is a direct 2-point swing, often deciding the match in tight games.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Protected Habitats Counter:** If an opponent has **Protected Habitats**, you **cannot** remove their resources. This makes Virus a "dead" card for attacking that player, though you can still play it for the tag triggers and rebates.
- **Multplayer Dilution:** In 4-5 player games, attacking one player only helps you and the other players who weren't hit. Use it selectively on the clear leader or a dedicated biological rusher.
- **Timing:** Ensure your opponent actually has the resources to remove. Check player boards before committing your draft action.

### Tips
- **Milestone Sabotage:** Use Virus to drop an opponent's plant or microbe count just enough to prevent them from claiming the **Gardener** or **Ecologist** milestones on their turn.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in 2-player games. High priority for anyone with *Media Group* or *Viral Enhancers*. Skip only in Solo mode or if opponents have *Protected Habitats*.
