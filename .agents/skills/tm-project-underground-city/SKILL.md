# Project: Underground City (#068)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 2 steps. Increase your Steel production 2 steps. Place a City tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent Greenery.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Pivot)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "STEEL DUMP"**

Underground City is generally considered a low-tier city card due to its high opportunity cost and inefficient production trade-off. While it provides a city placement and long-term resources, its massive energy requirement makes it difficult to justify in most competitive builds.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Steel Sink for Board Presence:** As a Building card, the 18 MC cost can be paid entirely with Steel. This allows you to convert surplus metal into 2 steel production and a city placement without spending liquid MC.
2. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both a **Building** and a **City** tag with zero requirements. 
   - **Building:** Contributes toward the **Builder** milestone or **Contractor** award.
   - **City:** Essential for securing the **Mayor** milestone in the mid-to-late game.
3. **Tharsis Republic Synergy:** Triggers the corporation's passive income and secures board control, though it is still less efficient than *Cupola City* or *Research Outpost*.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Bottleneck:** Decreasing your energy production by 2 steps is a massive hit. In the *Colonies* meta, this energy is almost always better spent on trading for high-value moon resources rather than 2 steel production.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At a total investment of ~35 MC (18 cost + 3 draft + 14 value of 2 energy), you are paying a huge premium for a city. For comparison, *Noctis City* provides better income for only 1 energy production.
- **Early Game Stalling:** **Never play this early** unless you have "free" energy from Preludes you have no other use for. Tying up your energy engine in Generations 1-4 will stall your ability to trade or build cities.

### Tips
- **Late-Game Point Dump:** In the final turn, if you have 10+ steel and 2 "useless" energy production (because temperature is maxed), Underground City is a viable way to grab 2-3 final victory points and adjacency bonuses.

**Recommendation:** Almost always an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are **Tharsis Republic**, have a massive energy surplus, or need a city for a milestone race and have no other options. Skip early to preserve trading capacity.
