# Project: Restricted Area (#121)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 2 MC to draw 1 card.
- **Effect:** Place a Restricted Area tile on an area with no other tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Precision Engine)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / TOP-TIER DRAW SOURCE**

Restricted Area is one of the most powerful and versatile engine-building cards in *Terraforming Mars*. It provides a unique combination of card advantage, a critical requirement enabler, and high-impact board control.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Source of Card Advantage:** For a total investment of 14 MC (+3 draft), you gain a repeatable draw for only 2 MC. This is the cheapest card draw in the game (excluding *AI Central* setup). In high-level play, "seeing more cards" is the most consistent way to find winning synergies.
2. **Science Tag enabler:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for unlocking high-tier science cards like *AI Central* or *Antigravity Technology*.
3. **Board Control & Blocking:** The tile can be placed anywhere on Mars (except reserved spots). Use it to occupy high-value spots near an opponent's city to deny them adjacency bonuses, or to "sniping" a 2-titanium/2-card bonus spot early.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes toward the **Scientist**, **Ecologist**, and **Landlord** milestones simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While powerful, it provides zero immediate production. If you are in Generation 1-2 and have to choose between Restricted Area and a card like *Acquired Company*, the income is usually better unless you have a path to a science engine.
- **Action Economy:** Like all action cards, it consumes one of your two actions per turn. In the final generations, ensure you use the action *first* to give yourself the best chance of drawing a playable game-ender.
- **Rush Vulnerability:** If opponents are rushing the game to end in 7-8 generations, the card draw might not have time to pay off. Its value is highest in 2-player or engine-heavy 3-player games.

### Tips
- **Stealth Timing:** Hold Restricted Area in your hand until you have the 2 MC to spare for the first draw. Playing it early with zero credits to use the action "telegraphes" your science engine, encouraging opponents to hate-draft science tags away from you.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" in almost any opening hand for engine-building strategies. Priority 1 in drafts for Generations 1-4. High priority for **Mining Guild** or anyone Pursuing **AI Central**. Skip only in extreme rush scenarios.
