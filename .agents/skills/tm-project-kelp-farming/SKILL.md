# Project: Kelp Farming (#055)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 17 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** 6 Ocean Tiles
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 3 steps. Gain 2 Plants.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Powerhouse)
**Status: S-TIER PLANT PRODUCTION / A-TIER PAYOFF**

Kelp Farming is arguably the best plant production card in the game. It provides a massive boost to both your economy and your biological engine, making it a "must-draft" card for almost any strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Yield:** For 17 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 5 total production steps (2 MC, 3 Plant) and 1 VP. The community value of this bundle is ~45 MC, representing one of the highest ROIs in the deck.
2. **Instant Greenery Catalyst:** The 2 instant plants often provide the exact amount needed to complete a greenery conversion in the same turn, securing a TR and board presence immediately.
3. **The "Ecology Experts" Dream:** Pairing Kelp Farming with the **Ecology Experts** Prelude allows you to play it in Generation 1, ignoring the ocean requirement. This is widely considered the strongest possible opening in *Terraforming Mars*.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Insects / NRA:** Provides the Plant tag and the high production volume needed to trigger these top-tier payoffs.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants a total of 3 plants immediately upon play.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 6-ocean requirement is steep. In slow 2-player games, this card can be locked in your hand for more than half the match. Monitor the ocean track closely before drafting.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** A jump to +3 plant production makes you the #1 target for **Birds**, **Fish**, and **Virus**. Ensure you have a plan to convert your plants to greeneries immediately or pair it with **Protected Habitats**.

### Tips
- **Ocean Forcing:** If you have Kelp Farming in hand, it is often worth using a Standard Project or a cheap ocean event (like *Comet*) specifically to hit the 6-ocean threshold 1 generation earlier. The compounding value of the +3 plant production is worth the extra upfront investment.

**Recommendation:** A "Do Not Pass" draft priority. High priority for **Ecoline** or anyone pursuing the **Gardener** milestone. Priority 1 if holding *Ecology Experts*. Skip only if oceans are completely stalled and the game is ending.
