# Project: Harvest (#112)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** 4% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Economic Pivot)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / MID-GAME ENABLER**

Harvest is a high-efficiency production card that bridges the gap between your economic engine and your biological engine. While its Oxygen requirement limits its early-game use, its dual production boost makes it a solid performer in the mid-game.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Yield:** For 12 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 4 total production steps (2 MC, 2 Plant). The community value of this production bundle is ~30 MC, representing a massive discount if played as soon as the requirement is met.
2. **Double Production:** Getting 2 steps of plant production in one action allows you to bypass the slow "drip" of single-step cards and start placing greeneries every 4 generations.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides a Plant tag that triggers these bio-engines immediately.
   - **Insects / NRA:** Helps reach the production or tag requirements for high-tier biological payoffs.
   - **Robotic Workforce:** A premier target for Robotic Workforce. Copying Harvest provides another +2/+2 production boost for only 9 MC, effectively winning the resource race.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The 4% Oxygen requirement can arrive late in slow games. Do not hold this card from Generation 1 unless you have a path to rapid Oxygen growth or are playing as **Ecoline**.
- **Amortization:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 6. Only draft late if you strictly need the Plant tag or the final 2 VP from the resulting greenery.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** A sudden spike to +2 plant production will draw the attention of players holding *Birds* or *Virus*. Be ready to convert your plants to greeneries immediately.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** If Oxygen is at 3%, do not wait until the next generation to play Harvest. Use an "Oxygen Bumping" card or standard project to hit 4% this turn and drop Harvest immediately. The extra generation of production is often worth more than the 14 MC spent on the bump.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 3-5. Essential for bio-engine strategies or anyone holding *Robotic Workforce*. Skip late if your engine is already over-optimized.
