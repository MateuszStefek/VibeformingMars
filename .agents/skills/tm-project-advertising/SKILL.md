# Project: Advertising (X13)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Promo
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Effect:** When you play a card with a basic cost of 20 M€ or more, increase your M€ production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Big Ticket Rebate)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / LATE-GAME SCALER**

Advertising is a low-cost Earth card that rewards players for playing high-cost "big ticket" cards. While it doesn't provide immediate production, it can turn expensive mid-to-late game plays into permanent economic growth.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Low Investment:** At only 4 MC, it is very easy to slip into a generation without disrupting other plans.
2. **Earth Tag Synergy:** Triggers Earth-tag bonuses (e.g., 'Teractor', 'Cartels', 'Miranda Resort') and helps toward milestones like 'Benefactor'.
3. **Passive Scaling:** In a typical game, you will likely play several cards costing 20+ MC (e.g., 'Earth Catapult', 'AI Central', 'Space Elevator', 'Io Mining'). Advertising makes these already powerful cards even better.

### Synergies:
- **Teractor:** The 3 MC discount makes Advertising cost only 1 MC, a near-instant keep.
- **Earth Engine:** Works perfectly with 'Cartels' and other Earth-tag-dependent production.
- **Big Payoffs:** Synergizes with any strategy that involves playing expensive space or infrastructure cards.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Timing:** Playing this too late in the game (Gen 8+) might not yield enough production to pay for itself.
- **Hand Composition:** If your hand is full of cheap 5-15 MC cards, Advertising is a dead draw.

### Tips
- **Keep it in Mind:** If you see Advertising early and have at least two 20+ MC cards in your starting hand or planned strategy, it is an easy draft.

**Recommendation:** A solid economic filler for Earth strategies or anyone planning to play heavy-hitting cards. High priority for **Teractor** or **Point Luna**.
