# Project: Caretaker Coyotes (#053)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 2 Animals on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement-Free Animal)
**Status: C-TIER / SITUATIONAL UTILITY**

Caretaker Coyotes is a low-tier animal card that is valued primarily for its lack of requirements. While its direct point generation is slow, it serves as an early-game enabler for biological engines.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Early Animal Tag:** Most animal cards require high Oxygen or Temperature. Coyotes can be played in Generation 1, providing an immediate Animal tag to satisfy requirements for cards like **Advanced Ecosystems** or to trigger **Decomposers** and **Ecological Zone** early.
2. **Requirement-Free VP:** If drawn for free or discounted, it provides a steady (if slow) trickle of points without needing any global parameters to be met.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Large Convoy / Imported Hydrogen:** These events allow you to add 2 animals to Coyotes, instantly netting you 1 VP.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Ecological Zone:** Triggers the animal gain on the Zone immediately.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Efficiency:** At 10 MC (+3 to draft) for 0.5 VP per generation, it takes **4 generations** just to reach a 2 VP return. Compared to *Birds* or *Fish* (1 VP per animal), Coyotes is significantly less efficient.
- **Action Cost:** Like all action cards, it consumes one of your two actions per turn. In the early game, that action is often better spent on production or terraforming.
- **Opportunistic Only:** **Do not keep this in your starting hand** unless you already hold *Ecological Zone* or *Advanced Ecosystems* and need the tag to make them work.

### Tips
- **Decoy Strategy:** If you have a high-value animal card (like *Birds* with 5+ points), playing Coyotes can sometimes act as a "decoy" for opponent's *Predators* or *Virus* attacks, although savvy opponents will usually target the higher VP card anyway.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft. Only keep if you need an immediate Animal tag for a high-tier bio-engine. Best used as a mid-to-late game point filler if you have excess cash. Skip early in favor of production.
