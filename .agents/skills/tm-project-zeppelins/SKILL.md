# Project: Zeppelins (#134)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** 5% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for each City tile on Mars.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Board Passive)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER IN 4-5 PLAYER GAMES**

Zeppelins is a high-ceiling economic card that rewards you for a crowded board. While its oxygen requirement pushes it out of the very early game, its ability to generate scalable income from both your own and opponents' city placements makes it a powerful mid-game tool.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** It provides +1 MC production for *every* city on Mars. In high-player games (4-5 players), it is common to see 8-12 cities, yielding a massive income boost for a total investment of only 16 MC (+3 to draft).
2. **Tharsis Republic Synergy:** The absolute best case. If you or your opponent are playing Tharsis, cities will hit the board rapidly, making Zeppelins an essential income engine.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Administrator Award:** Since it has no tags, it contributes toward the award for the most "tagless" cards.
   - **Immigrant City / Pets:** Forms a "City Engine" where every city placed grants you MC, production, and animals simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The 5% Oxygen requirement can delay the card until Generation 3-5. Do not hold this from Generation 1 unless you anticipate a city-heavy game.
- **2-Player Inefficiency:** In 1v1 games, cities are often sparse. If fewer than 4 cities are placed after you play the card, it statistically loses value compared to flat production cards.
- **No Tag Value:** Aside from the VP, the card provides zero progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Scientist* or *Builder*.

### Tips
- **Stealth Timing:** Like *Immigrant City*, playing Zeppelins early can discourage opponents from building cities. However, because it provides a VP and has an Oxygen requirement, it is often seen as less "hostile" and more of a standard mid-game point-filler.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in 4-5 player games or if playing with **Tharsis Republic**. High priority for **Vitor**. Skip in slow 2-player games or if Oxygen is already nearing the max.
