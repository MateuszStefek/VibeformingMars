# Corporation: Polyphemos

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Colonies
- **Starting MC:** 50
- **Starting Production:** 5 MC, 5 Titanium
- **Effect:** Buying cards to your hand (including the starting hand) costs 5 MC each instead of 3 MC.

## Strategic Analysis (The Heavyweight)
**Status: D-TIER / HIGH RISK**

Polyphemos is a corporation defined by its massive handicap. While its starting production is the highest in the game, the 5 MC card cost is a brutal penalty that can stall your engine if you cannot find a way to draw cards for free.

### Why it is a risky play:
1. **The Card Tax:** Paying 5 MC to draft a card means you are effectively paying a 66% premium on every card you see. This makes "bad" hands catastrophic.
2. **Titanium Reliance:** With 5 titanium production, you are forced into playing space cards. If you don't draw space cards, your primary production is wasted.
3. **Pluto Dependency:** In the Colonies expansion, Polyphemos *needs* the Pluto colony to stay competitive. Free card draw is the only way to bypass the 5 MC tax.

### Synergies:
- **Pluto Colony:** Your lifeline. Trading here is non-negotiable.
- **Card Draw Blue Cards:** *AI Central*, *Mars University*, or *Point Luna* (via Merger) are critical.
- **High-Value Space Cards:** *Earth Catapult* is almost mandatory to offset the increased costs.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Be Selective:** You cannot afford to keep "maybe" cards. Only keep cards that are essential to your win condition or provide immediate, massive ROI.
- **Standard Projects:** Because your card costs are so high, Standard Projects (like "Power Plant" or "Aquifer Pumping") are relatively more efficient for you than for other players.

**Recommendation:** Avoid unless you have *Earth Catapult* and *Sponsors* in your opening hand and Pluto is a colony. Even then, it is a uphill battle.
