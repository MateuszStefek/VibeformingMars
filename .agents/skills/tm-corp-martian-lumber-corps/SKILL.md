# Corporation: Martian Lumber Corps

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 40 MC, 1 Plant Prod
- **Ability:** Use Plants as 3 MC for Building tags.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: A-TIER / HYBRID**

Unique ability to turn biological success into industrial expansion.

## Map Analysis

King of 'Gardener'. High value on the 2-plant placement spots.

South Pole heat can be a distraction; stay focused on the 'Cultivator' award.
### Elysium
The 'Ecologist' milestone is a must-have. Use the 3-plant spot to jumpstart your engine.
