# Project: Tectonic Stress Power (#145)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** 2 Science Tags.
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 3 steps.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Industrial Engine Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Tectonic Stress Power is a high-efficiency infrastructure card that provides a massive jump in industrial power. While its science requirement limits its early-game use, its role as a "trading fleet enabler" and its steel-payable cost make it a premier tool for mid-game stabilization.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Trade Fleet" Enabler:** 3 Energy production is the "magic number" for a trade fleet. Tectonic Stress Power allows you to start trading with moons like *Luna* or *Ceres* for zero liquid MC overhead once played, which is a critical mid-game priority in the *Colonies* meta.
2. **Steel Sink Efficiency:** As a Building card, the 18 MC cost can be paid entirely with **Steel**. This allows you to convert rigid mineral wealth into flexible energy production and points without spending your liquid MC.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking requirements for high-tier cards like *Power Supply Consortium* or *AI Central*.
   - **Building Tag:** Contributes toward the **Builder** milestone or **Contractor** award.
4. **Vitor Synergy:** As a card with a VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate, making the card effectively cost only 15 MC.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 2-science requirement can arrive surprisingly late in slow games. Do not hold this from Generation 1 unless you have a path to rapid science tags (e.g., via *Research* or *Valley Trust*).
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you only need 1 or 2 energy, cheaper cards like *Geothermal Power* or *Power Plant* are generally superior as they lack the requirement bottleneck.
- **Late Game Waste:** Energy production has zero value in the final generations (unless temperature is maxed). Only draft late if you strictly need the tags for a milestone or the 1 VP to win.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Tectonic Stress Power with only 1 other Science tag. This allows you to fix your energy infrastructure a generation early, which is often enough to secure the **Energizer** milestone.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 3-6 if you have science synergies or are playing with *Colonies*. Excellent as a steel dump to fix energy infrastructure. Skip early if you lack science tags.
