# Project: Martian Survey (P38)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Event
- **Requirements:** Max 4% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Draw 2 cards.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Early Dig)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "PANIC" DRAW**

Martian Survey is a situational early-game card used primarily to dig for an engine when your starting hand is weak. While it provides immediate card advantage and a point, its restrictive requirement and relatively high cost for the early game make it a low-priority draft in most scenarios.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **The "Panic Button":** If you have zero production or playable cards in Generation 1, Martian Survey allows you to "dig" 2 cards deeper into the deck to find a viable strategy before the Oxygen limit is reached.
2. **Synergies:**
   - **Media Group:** The premier synergy. Provides a 3 MC rebate, making the 2-card draw effectively cost only 6 MC (+ draft), which is very efficient.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon.
   - **Mars University / Olympus Conference:** The Science tag triggers these draw engines, effectively drawing you **three cards** for one action.
3. **Milestone Support:** One of the cheapest ways to add an "Event" tag toward the **Legend** milestone (Elysium map).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The "Max 4% Oxygen" limit is extremely strict. In games with high player counts or oxygen-heavy Preludes, this card can become "dead" as early as Generation 2.
- **Economic Drag:** Spending 12 MC (card + draft) in Generation 1 for random draws provides zero economic growth. That capital is almost always better spent on a 10-12 MC production card.
- **Not a Space Card:** Despite the theme, it lacks a Space tag and **cannot** be paid for with Titanium, limiting its flexibility for corporations like Phobolog.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** If you are playing as **Inventrix** or have **Special Design**, you can extend the Oxygen window to 6%, allowing you to play the Survey in the mid-game once your economy is more stable.

**Recommendation:** Generally a "Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are playing **Vitor**, have **Media Group**, or are desperate to find a win-condition in a weak opening hand. Skip early if you have solid production options.
