# Project: Eos Chasma Survey (#161)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Building, Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -12°C
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Gain 3 Plants. Add 1 Animal to any Animal card.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Stabilizer)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / MID-GAME VALUE**

Eos Chasma Survey is a high-utility hybrid card that offers immediate resources, income, and points. While its requirements limit its early-game use, its role as a "steel dump" and biological engine catalyst makes it a solid performer in the mid-game.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Steel Sink Efficiency:** As a Building card, the 16 MC cost can be paid entirely with Steel. This makes it an excellent way to convert late-game building resources into liquid income, points, and plants.
2. **The "Bio-Trinity" Support:** Provides a **Plant tag** and an **Animal resource**.
   - **Animal Synergy:** If you have a card where 1 Animal = 1 VP (e.g., *Birds*, *Fish*), this card is effectively worth **2 VP** (1 base + 1 from the resource).
   - **Plant Synergy:** The 3 instant plants are often the catalyst for a greenery conversion, adding another 1 VP + 1 TR to the card's value.
3. **Requirement Bridging:** Helps reach high-tier requirements for cards like **Insects** or **Advanced Ecosystems** by providing a relatively cheap Plant tag.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -12°C requirement means you cannot play this in the very early game. Do not hold it in your starting hand unless you have significant heat production or are playing a very slow engine game.
- **ROI Calculation:** Without an animal card to receive the bonus resource, or without a steel surplus, the card's efficiency drops. It is a "good" card that becomes "great" only with synergies.
- **Late-Game Production:** Like all income cards, the +2 MC production is negligible in the final two generations. Only play it late for the points and the plants.

### Tips
- **Final Greenery Snipe:** Use Eos Chasma in the final generation. The 3 plants can often be the exact amount needed to complete a greenery conversion, representing a 2-3 point swing in the final round.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 4-7. High priority if you have excess Steel or a 1:1 VP animal card. Skip early if you need to prioritize engine building.
