# Project: Bribed Committee (#112)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 2 steps.
- **VP:** -2


## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Accelerator)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER OPENER**

Bribed Committee is a high-tempo early-game card that "trades" late-game victory points for immediate economic growth. While the -2 VP penalty seems steep, its impact on your income and its ability to secure early milestones makes it a premier tool for establishing dominance in the first generations.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Early Income:** For only 7 MC (+3 draft), you gain 2 TR, which translates to **+2 MC production**. If played in Generation 1 or 2, those 2 extra credits per turn provide a massive cumulative economic boost (~16-20 MC over the game), far exceeding the card's cost.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both an **Earth** and an **Event** tag for a very low cost.
   - **Earth Tag:** Triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and benefits from *Teractor* or *Earth Office* discounts.
   - **Event Tag:** Triggers rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) and *Interplanetary Cinematics* (+2 MC). With these active, the card can effectively be **profit-generating** to play.
3. **Milestone Support:** One of the cheapest ways to add an "Event" tag toward the **Legend** milestone (Elysium map) or to hit the 26 TR requirement for the **Terraformer** milestone.
4. **UNMI Synergy:** The cheapest way to trigger UNMI's corporation ability (+1 TR when your TR increases) in the very first generations.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Late Game Inefficiency:** In the final 2 generations, the 2 TR gain will not provide enough income to justify the -2 VP penalty. Only play late if you strictly need the TR to win the **Benefactor** award or hit a 26 TR milestone.
- **Victory Point Sensitivity:** In a very tight, high-VP engine game, that -2 can be the difference between winning and losing. Only take the hit if the income gain enables at least 3+ points worth of cards later.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use Bribed Committee as your "first" Earth tag. It provides a tag and production to help you reach the requirements for more powerful, restricted cards like *Cartels* or *Earth Office*.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in Generations 1-3 for any corporation, especially those with Earth synergies. skip after Generation 5 unless needed for a specific milestone.
