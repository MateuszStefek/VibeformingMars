# Project: Sulphur Exports (#114)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 19 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Mid-Game Income Stabilizer)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER SYNERGY**

Sulphur Exports is a reliable, mid-tier economic card. While its raw efficiency is slightly lower than early-game staples like *Sponsors*, its combination of high-value tags and unconditional income makes it a flexible tool for establishing mid-game momentum.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unconditional Income:** No temperature or oxygen requirements mean it can be played at any time. This ensures a steady return on investment, especially if drawn in the first half of the match.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Space** and an **Earth** tag.
   - **Space:** Can be paid for with Titanium, allowing you to convert rigid mineral wealth into flexible liquid income.
   - **Earth:** Triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and *Cartel* for further production increases.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna:** The premier pairing. Triggers a card draw upon play, significantly improving the card's efficiency.
   - **Teractor:** Reduces the cost to 16 MC (+3 draft), making the break-even point just over 6 generations.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes paying for this card with Titanium much more efficient.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At a total cost of 22 MC (card + draft) for 3 MC production, you are paying **~7.3 MC per production step**. This is well above the community benchmark of ~5 MC per step. Only play this if you have the tags or titanium production to justify the premium.
- **Timing Cutoff:** Playing Sulphur Exports after Generation 6 is usually poor, as the income will not recoup the 22 MC cost before the game ends.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you have better production cards (like *Acquired Company*), prioritize them first. Sulphur Exports is often the "backup" choice or a way to spend excess titanium.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Sulphur Exports as a dual tag enabler. It hits the requirements for **Earth Office** and **Quantum Extractor** simultaneously, accelerating your engine rollout by a full generation.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-4 if you have Titanium or Earth synergies. Excellent opening hand card for **Phobolog** or **Point Luna**. Skip in the late game.
