# Project: Noctis City (#017)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps. Place a City tile on the reserved Noctis area (ignores normal adjacency rules).
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent Greenery.


## Strategic Analysis (The Surprise City)
**Status: S-TIER ON THARSIS / B-TIER GENERAL**

Noctis City is a uniquely powerful card on the original Tharsis map. Its ability to ignore placement restrictions allows for high-value "point-stealing" and uncontested city triangles.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Rule-Breaking Placement:** On Tharsis, it *must* be placed on its reserved spot, but it *ignores* the rule that cities cannot be placed next to each other. This allows you to "leech" points from opponents' greeneries even if they have already surrounded the area with cities.
2. **Safe Triangles:** Because opponents are often wary of building near the Noctis reserve, you can safely plan your own greeneries around that spot, knowing you can drop the city there later to secure the points.
3. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card with no global requirements, it is a perfect way to spend excess steel in the late game to grab 3-5 VP from surrounding greeneries.
4. **Economic Boost:** +3 MC production for 18 MC is a solid mid-game investment.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Tharsis Republic:** A core card for Tharsis, triggering their passive MC production and securing board control early.
   - **Land Claim:** While you cannot claim the Noctis spot itself, you can claim the spots *around* it to force opponents to build greeneries in your future city's territory.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Telegraphing:** Playing Noctis City in Generation 1-2 on Tharsis tells everyone exactly where you will be building. This may encourage opponents to build away from you, reducing the city's point potential.
- **Other Maps (Hellas/Elysium):** On maps without a reserved Noctis spot, this card functions as a standard city with normal placement restrictions. In these cases, it is slightly overcosted compared to *Cupola City*.
- **Energy Cost:** Remember to account for the 1 energy production (~7 MC value).

### Tips
- **Late-Game Snipe:** Hold Noctis City until the final or semi-final generation on Tharsis. Use it to occupy a spot that an opponent thought was "safe" from city point-leeching.
- **Milestone Securing:** Provides a City tile and Building tag for the *Mayor* and *Builder* milestones without needing Oxygen or Temperature thresholds.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" on the Tharsis map, especially for Tharsis Republic. High priority for steel-heavy engines. On other maps, treat it as a reliable but standard city card.
