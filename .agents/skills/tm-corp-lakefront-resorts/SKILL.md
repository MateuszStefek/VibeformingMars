# Corporation: Lakefront Resorts

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Turmoil
- **Starting MC:** 54
- **Tags:** Building
- **Effect:** Whenever an ocean is placed, increase your MegaCredit production 1 step.
- **Effect:** Your bonus for placing adjacent to an ocean is 3 MC instead of 2 MC.

## Strategic Analysis (The Ocean Specialist)
**Status: A-TIER / ECONOMIC**

Lakefront Resorts is a premier economic corporation that thrives on the most common terraforming action: placing oceans. It provides high liquid cash and a scaling income that makes it a top choice for players who want to dominate the board.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Passive Income:** There are 9 oceans in every game. That's +9 MC production that Lakefront will almost certainly get, regardless of who places them.
2. **Superior Adjacency:** Increasing the ocean adjacency bonus from 2 to 3 MC makes every tile you place next to an ocean significantly more efficient.
3. **High Starting Capital:** 54 MC is one of the highest starting totals, allowing for a very explosive Gen 1.

### Synergies:
- **Ocean-Related Cards:** *Arctic Algae*, *Lake Marineris*, or any card that places multiple oceans.
- **Titanium Synergy:** Many ocean cards are space-tagged, allowing Lakefront to use titanium effectively.
- **Tharsis Republic:** Excels on maps with high ocean density or specific ocean bonuses.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Ideal for 'Mayor'. The dense ocean clusters are perfect for maximizing city/greenery points.
### Hellas
Placement bonuses in the south are key for early economy.
### Elysium
The 'Estate Dealer' award is highly competitive; secure ocean adjacencies early.

### Tips
- **Ocean Rushing:** Don't wait for others to place oceans. Place them yourself early to get your production up as quickly as possible. The sooner you get that +9 MC production, the more dominant your economy will be.
- **Strategic Placement:** Prioritize placing your cities and greenery next to as many oceans as possible to maximize the 3 MC bonus.

**Recommendation:** A very strong, reliable pick. Excellent if you have ocean-placing cards or if you want a high-starting-cash game.
