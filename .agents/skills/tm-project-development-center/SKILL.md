# Project: Development Center (#014)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 1 Energy to draw 1 card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Stable Engine)
**Status: B-TIER / CONSISTENT DRAW SOURCE**

Development Center is a reliable, mid-tier card draw engine. It provides the consistency needed for long-form games but requires a significant upfront investment in both MegaCredits and Energy.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Steady Card Flow:** Drawing 1 extra card per generation allows you to find synergies and global parameter bonuses more reliably.
2. **Science Tag Enabler:** It provides a Science tag with no requirements, making it a critical "bridge" to high-tier science cards like *AI Central* or *Antigravity Technology*.
3. **Building Tag:** Can be paid for with Steel, making it an excellent way to convert excess building resources into card draw.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Colonies Expansion:** If you have surplus energy from trading or specific colonies, the action cost becomes effectively "free."
   - **Mars University:** Use the cards drawn by the Center as "fuel" for Mars University's filtering ability.
   - **Mining Guild / IC:** Corporations with high steel production can drop this card without spending liquid MC.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Total Setup Cost:** Between the card cost, draft, and the energy production required, you are investing ~25 MC. It takes 5-7 generations of use to recoup this value in cards. **Do not play this after Generation 6** unless you strictly need the Science tag.
- **Energy Vulnerability:** If your energy production is stolen or reduced by an opponent's card (e.g., *Power Consortium*), the Development Center becomes useless until you can replace the power.
- **Efficiency Comparison:** *AI Central* (2 cards for 1 energy) and *Restricted Area* (Draw for 2 MC + placement bonus) are generally considered more efficient if you can meet their requirements.

### Tips
- **Steel Conversion:** View the Development Center primarily as a way to turn Steel into "potential points" (cards). If you are drowning in Steel but have no cards to spend it on, the Center is your best out.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-4. Prioritize if you need a Science tag or have excess Steel/Energy. Skip in the late game.
