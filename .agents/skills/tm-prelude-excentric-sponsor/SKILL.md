# Prelude: Excentric Sponsor

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Play one card from your hand, reducing its cost by 25 MegaCredits (MC).
- **Requirements:** You must still meet all other requirements for the card (tags, global parameters, energy production).

## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Rocket)
**Status: S-TIER ENABLER / SITUATIONAL POWERHOUSE**

Excentric Sponsor is one of the most impactful "Turn 0" cards in the game. It allows you to drop a massive, high-cost project card before the first generation even begins, effectively preserving your entire starting budget for additional projects in Generation 1.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Tempo:** By playing a card for free (or near-free), you gain its production and effects immediately. This allows you to snowball your economy faster than opponents who must spend their entire Gen 1 budget on a single big card.
2. **Above-Average Value:** A 25 MC discount is one of the highest raw values provided by any Prelude. Since the community benchmark for a Prelude is ~21 MC, using this on any card costing **22 MC or more** is an immediate efficiency win.
3. **The "Dream" Pairings:**
   - **Earth Catapult:** The ultimate combo. Drop the game's strongest engine for free (or 1 MC) before the first turn, ensuring every subsequent card is discounted.
   - **Io Mining Industries:** Jumpstart a massive Titanium and MC production engine for zero liquid MC.
   - **Strip Mine:** Establish a dominant industrial base early (ensure your other Prelude or corporation provides the 2 energy production needed).
   - **Deimos Down / Giant Ice Asteroid:** Start the game with an aggressive terraforming swing, potentially crippling an opponent's plant engine before they can act.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Requirement Bypassing:** Unlike *Ecology Experts*, you **must** meet all requirements for the card. You cannot use this to "cheat out" a card that requires oxygen or temperature if the tracks aren't ready.
- **Whiff Risk:** If your starting hand only contains cheap cards (under 15 MC), Excentric Sponsor is a waste of a Prelude slot. Its effective value drops below the 21 MC benchmark, and you are better off with a pure production card.
- **Early Economic Hit:** If you use the discount on a card that provides no production (e.g., a pure point card), you may find yourself "rich" in points but "poor" in engine power compared to opponents who took income Preludes.

### Tips
- **1st Player Advantage:** If you are the first player, use Excentric Sponsor to snag high-value ocean or city spots (via the played card) before anyone else has a chance to play a single action.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" if you have a high-cost (21+ MC), requirement-free card in your starting hand. Priority 1 for **Earth Catapult** or **Io Mining**. Skip if your hand is full of low-cost or high-requirement cards.
