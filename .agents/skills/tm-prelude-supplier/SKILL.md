# Prelude: Supplier

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 2 steps. Gain 4 Steel.
- **Tag:** Power.

## Strategic Analysis (The Trading Anchor)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Supplier is a high-efficiency infrastructure card that serves as a premier tool for establishing a dominant trading economy. While its production is modest, its role as a "trade setup" card and its steel-payable cost make it a staple for engine builders.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Colonies Synergy:** The primary reason to take Supplier. 2 Energy production is 2/3 of the energy needed for a trade fleet. Playing this in Generation 1 allows you to hit the 3-energy threshold (with just one more power card or Prelude) to begin trading for high-value moon resources immediately.
2. **Immediate Engine Jumpstart:** The 4 starting steel (worth 8 MC) allows you to play high-cost building projects like **Research Outpost** or **Space Elevator** in Generation 1 for zero net liquid MC.
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both an **Energy step** and a **Power tag**.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking requirements for high-tier cards like *Power Supply Consortium* or triggering *Energizer* milestone.
   - **Building Resource:** Fuels metal-heavy corporations like **Mining Guild** or **Interplanetary Cinematics**.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes 2 steps toward the **Energizer** milestone and contributes to the **Industrialist** award.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Provides zero MC production. If your starting hand is expensive and lacks building tags, Supplier will not help you established your economic snowball in Generation 1.
- **Without Colonies:** If you are not playing with the *Colonies* expansion, the value of energy production drops. Unless you have an energy-hungry card (like *Strip Mine*) or are racing for the *Energizer* milestone, pure income Preludes are strictly superior.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Power tag and energy boost to hit the threshold for **Power Supply Consortium** in Generation 1. Stealing an opponent's energy while establishing your own industrial base is one of the strongest "industrial" openings in the game.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" if playing with *Colonies*. High priority if your starting hand has high-cost building targets or energy requirements. skip early if you have zero use for energy and need to prioritize pure income.
