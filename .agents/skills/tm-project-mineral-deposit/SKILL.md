# Project: Mineral Deposit (#031)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 5 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Gain 5 Steel.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Converter)
**Status: B-TIER EFFICIENCY / TACTICAL UTILITY**

Mineral Deposit is a simple, high-efficiency resource card. It serves as a "clean" way to convert MegaCredits into building resources at a profit, while also providing tactical advantages for milestones and events.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Direct Profit:** The card costs 5 MC (+3 to draft) and provides 5 Steel, which is worth 10 MC for building tags. This yields a net profit of **+2 MC** for a minimal investment.
2. **Instant Liquidity:** If drawn for free (via card effects or placement bonuses), it is a pure **+5 MC profit**.
3. **Event Rebates:** As a Red (Event) card, it triggers rebates from **Media Group** (+3 MC) and **Interplanetary Cinematics** (+2 MC), potentially making the card cost-neutral or even cash-positive.
4. **Milestone Control:** Provides an "instant" 5 Steel that can be used in the final generation to steal the **Miner** or **Industrialist** awards from an opponent.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the 5 Steel worth 15 MC, significantly increasing the card's ROI.
   - **Building-Heavy Hand:** Excellent for funding expensive early-game buildings like *Space Elevator* or *Electro Catapult* when you lack long-term steel production.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While profitable, the 3 MC draft cost can sometimes be better spent on long-term production. Only draft if you have building tags in hand or are racing for a milestone.
- **Late-Game Excess:** If you already have 20+ steel and no building tags to spend it on, Mineral Deposit is a waste of a draft action.
- **Tag Absence:** The card has a Power tag, which is useful for *Diversifier*, but lacks a Building tag itself.

### Tips
- **Surprise Award Swing:** Save this card in your hand for the very last turn of the game. If the *Miner* award is close, dropping an extra 5 Steel can catch your opponents off guard and secure the 5 VP.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft. Always worth keeping if you have any building synergies or need a cheap tag for *Diversifier*. High priority if *Media Group* or *Advanced Alloys* is in play.
