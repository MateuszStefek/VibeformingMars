# Project: Miranda Resort (#063)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for each Earth tag you have.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian-Earth Bridge)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER FOR JOVIANS**

Miranda Resort is a versatile hybrid card that rewards players for diversifying their engine. While its income potential depends on your Earth tags, its role as a "cheap Jovian tag" makes it a cornerstone for late-game point multipliers.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Jovian Multiplier Catalyst:** The Jovian tag is the card's most valuable asset. If you have Jovian multipliers (e.g., *Water Import from Europa*), this card's value jumps from 1 VP to 2-4+ VP, making the 15 MC investment (+3 draft) exceptionally efficient.
2. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This is an ideal way to convert excess titanium into permanent income and points.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna / Teractor:** These corporations start with Earth tags and naturally accumulate more. Miranda Resort can easily provide +4-5 MC production in these builds, rivaling the efficiency of *Acquired Company*.
   - **Saturn Systems:** High priority. Triggers their passive income and provides the Jovian tag they need for their endgame scoring.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes to the **Rim Settler** milestone (3 Jovian tags) and the **Banker** award.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Buffer:** Do not play this in Generation 1 if you have zero Earth tags. You are paying 15 MC for 1 VP and zero income. Wait until you have at least 2 Earth tags to ensure the production is worth the capital.
- **Amortization:** Like all income cards, its value drops after Generation 5. Only draft late if you strictly need the Jovian tag for a multiplier payoff.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent is playing **Point Luna** or **Saturn Systems**, do not pass them Miranda Resort. The combination of income scaling and a Jovian tag is too synergistic to let an engine player have uncontested.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Saturn Systems** or anyone with 2+ Earth tags. Excellent mid-game filler for Jovian-heavy engines. Skip early if you lack Earth tags.
