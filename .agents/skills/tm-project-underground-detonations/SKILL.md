# Project: Underground Detonations

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 10 MC to increase your Heat production by 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Meme Card)
**Status: STRATEGICALLY WEAK / MEME CARD**

Underground Detonations is widely considered one of the least efficient cards in the game.

### Why it is usually a bad play:
1. **Extremely Low Efficiency:** You pay 6 MC to play the card, then 10 MC per use. Total cost for the first 2 heat production is 16 MC. For comparison, a standard project to increase heat production is often more efficient or provides better immediate value.
2. **Action Cost:** It consumes an action that could be used for more impactful plays.
3. **Better Alternatives:** Most other heat-production cards or energy-to-heat engines provide significantly better ROI.

### When to play it (Rare Situations):
- You have an massive surplus of MC and Steel, and absolutely no other way to increase your Heat production or TR.
- You are playing for the "Meme Value" or attempting a challenge run.
- You need one more Building tag to claim a Milestone (like Builder) and this is the only building card in hand.

**Recommendation:** Almost always discard this card during the research phase.
