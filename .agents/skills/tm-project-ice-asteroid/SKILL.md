# Project: Ice Asteroid (#071)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 23 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place 2 Ocean tiles.


## Strategic Analysis (The Mid-Tier Terraformer)
**Status: B-TIER EFFICIENCY / S-TIER WITH ARCTIC ALGAE**

Ice Asteroid is a straightforward, high-efficiency terraforming card. It provides a solid discount on ocean placement and serves as a premier tool for players looking to accelerate the game's end while gaining a foothold on the board.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost Efficiency:** At 23 MC (+3 to draft), you are paying **13 MC per ocean**. While more expensive than *Glaciers* (12 MC), it is still a significant discount over the 18 MC Standard Project ocean.
2. **Double Placement Bonus:** Placing two oceans simultaneously allows you to "chain" high-value placement bonuses (e.g., getting 4 plants or 2 titanium and a card) in one move, often providing the resources needed for a follow-up play.
3. **Space Tag Utility:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This makes it an excellent way for players with high titanium production (like Phobolog) to convert metal into TR and board presence.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "God Tier" combo. Playing Ice Asteroid while Arctic Algae is active nets you **4 plants** immediately, which is often enough to complete a greenery conversion in the same turn.
   - **Credicor:** Since the card costs 23 MC, it triggers Credicor's 4 MC rebate, making the 2 TR and 2 tiles exceptionally cheap.
   - **Optimal Aerobraking / Media Group:** Triggers significant resource/MC rebates for space events.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While efficient, it provides zero long-term production. If you are in Generation 1-2 and have to choose between Ice Asteroid and a card like *Acquired Company*, the long-term production is usually better unless you need the TR to hit a requirement.
- **Ocean Cap:** Monitor the ocean track. In high-player games, oceans are often the first parameter to finish. Do not draft this late if only 1 ocean spot remains.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Blocking:** Use Ice Asteroid to occupy high-value spots near an opponent's city to deny them the 2 MC adjacency bonus and to secure the most lucrative placement spots for yourself.
- **Titanium Sink:** If the game is ending and you have 20+ titanium but zero other space cards, Ice Asteroid is the most efficient way to "dump" that metal into final victory points (via TR).

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for any strategy. High priority for **Rush** players or anyone holding *Arctic Algae* or *Credicor*. Skip only if your engine is already over-optimized and you need card draw instead of TR.
