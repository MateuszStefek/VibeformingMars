# Project: Designers Microbes (#118)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 2 steps. Increase your Oxygen production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Industrial Hybrid)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER ENGINE PIECE**

Designers Microbes is a high-utility hybrid card that offers two distinct types of production. While its cost is moderate, its combination of tags and reliable output makes it a foundational piece for science-based biological engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Rare Oxygen Production:** Oxygen production is one of the hardest stats to find on a card without high requirements. Designers Microbes provides 1 oxygen step from Generation 1, accelerating your TR and pushing the game toward the high-tier animal cards.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Microbe** tag. 
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* and *Mars University*.
   - **Microbe:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Insects*.
3. **Versatile Production:** The 2 Heat production adds a steady trickle of terraforming progress, helping hit temperature thresholds while you focus on your biological engine.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Splice Tactical Genomics:** Triggers Splice's ability for an immediate benefit.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate plant when played, effectively reducing the card's cost.
   - **Ecologist / Scientist Award:** Contributes to two high-value endgame scoring categories.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Upfront Cost:** 18 MC (cost + draft) is a significant early-game investment. If playing this prevents you from establishing basic MC production, it might be too slow for high-player count games.
- **Oxygen Threshold:** While it has no requirements, its value drops if Oxygen is already maxed. **Play it early** to maximize the TR gain from the production.

### Tips
- **Requirement Buffer:** Use Designers Microbes as your "first" microbe card. It provides a tag and production to help you reach the requirements for more powerful, restricted microbe cards like *Nitrite Reducing Bacteria*.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for Science-heavy or bio-focused builds. Excellent opening hand card if your corporation or Preludes provide a starting budget boost. Skip only if you are committed to a pure "Space Rush" with zero interest in biological engines.
