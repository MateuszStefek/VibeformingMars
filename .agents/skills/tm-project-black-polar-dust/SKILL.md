# Project: Black Polar Dust (#031)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Heat production 3 steps. Place 1 Ocean tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Rush Engine)
**Status: C-TIER FOR ENGINES / A-TIER FOR RUSH & HELION**

Black Polar Dust is a highly polarizing tempo card. It "trades" long-term liquid currency (MC) for immediate terraforming progress and high-value heat production. While engine-builders often avoid it, it is a premier tool for players looking to end the game quickly.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **The "Rusher's Pivot":** In a "Rush" strategy, Heat production is more valuable than MC production because it forces the game to end before engine-heavy opponents can stabilize. At 15 MC (+3 to draft), it is roughly the same price as a Standard Project ocean, but you gain 3 Heat production for the price of 1 TR's worth of income.
2. **Immediate TR & Bonus:** Placing the ocean grants 1 TR immediately. This effectively reduces the production penalty from -2 MC to -1 MC per generation in the short term. Use the placement to grab high-value 2-card or 4-MC bonuses.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Helion:** The premier synergy. Since Helion can use Heat as MC, the "downside" is completely mitigated. You gain +1 net production (3 Heat vs. 2 MC loss) while getting an ocean on the board.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost on the ocean placement.
   - **Solo Mode Essential:** High priority in Solo play to ensure the temperature track doesn't stall while simultaneously clearing an ocean requirement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Engine Decay:** If you are playing a 10-12 generation engine game, the -2 MC production will cost you ~20-24 MC over the course of the game. For non-rushers, this is almost always a net negative.
- **No Tags:** The card has zero tags, meaning it provides no synergy for *Science* requirements, *Diversifier*, or *Ecologist* milestones.

### Tips
- **Temperature Threshold Sniping:** Use the 3 heat production to hit the -24°C or -20°C thresholds faster than your opponents, securing the bonus heat production or ocean placement for yourself.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Helion** or dedicated **Rush** strategies. Excellent in Solo mode. Generally an "Easy Pass" for engine-builders or long-form 2-player games.
