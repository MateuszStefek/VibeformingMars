# Corporation: Saturn Systems

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Starting MC:** 42
- **Starting Titanium Production:** 1
- **Starting Tags:** 1 Jovian Tag
- **Ability:** Whenever *any* Jovian tag is played (including yours), increase your MC production 1 step.

## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Specialist)
**Status: B/A-TIER (Expansion Dependent)**

Saturn Systems is a snowballing economic powerhouse that benefits from every Jovian card played at the table. It thrives in high-player games and expansions like Colonies where Jovian tags are more prevalent.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Passive Income:** In a 4-5 player game, other players will likely play Jovian tags for their own engines, inadvertently boosting your income.
2. **Titanium Synergy:** Starting with titanium production and 42 MC allows for a strong Gen 1 setup.
3. **Colonies Domination:** Saturn Systems is arguably S-Tier in the Colonies expansion, where Titan and Io provide massive fuel for its engine.

### Synergies:
- **Jovian Multipliers:** Cards like 'Terraforming Ganymede', 'Io Mining Industries', and 'Water Import from Europa' are game-winners for Saturn Systems.
- **Titanium Engines:** Focus on titanium production first to afford the expensive Jovian cards later.
- **Media Archives:** If many Jovian tags are played, this card can provide a massive one-time cash infusion.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Focus on 'Space Hotel' or 'Banker'. Board geography is secondary to space card efficiency.
### Hellas
Prime map for 'Rim Settler' milestone. The ocean placement bonuses are great for titanium.
### Elysium
Target the 'Celebrity' award. High-value cards are your primary scoring engine.

### Tips
- **Avoid the Jovian Trap:** Don't force expensive Jovian cards in Gen 1 just because of your ability. Build a solid economic base first.
- **TR Rushing:** If you can't find Jovian cards, use your titanium engine to rush terraforming via space events.

**Recommendation:** A top pick in 4-5 player games or when playing with the Colonies expansion. High priority if you have 'Io Mining Industries' or strong titanium production in your starting hand.
