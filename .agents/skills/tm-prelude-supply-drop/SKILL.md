# Prelude: Supply Drop

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Gain 3 Titanium. Gain 8 Steel. Gain 3 Plants.
- **Tags:** None.

## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Booster)
**Status: A-TIER / THE PREMIER EARLY TEMPO PRELUDE**

Supply Drop is one of the most powerful non-production Preludes in the game. It provides a massive injection of resources in Generation 0, allowing for explosive starts that can define the entire game. While it offers zero long-term production, its immediate impact often outweighs years of slow growth.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Immediate Value:** The card provides **~31 MC worth of resources** (3 Ti = 9 MC, 8 Steel = 16 MC, 3 Plants = 6 MC). This is significantly higher than the Prelude average of ~21 MC, making it an immediate efficiency gain.
2. **Generation 1 Power Plays:** The 8 Steel and 3 Titanium allow you to play high-cost, no-requirement cards in Generation 1 for zero net liquid MC.
   - **Targets:** Enables Gen 1 *Space Elevator*, *Electro Catapult*, or *Mohole Area* while preserving your starting cash for income cards.
3. **Instant Greenery Catalyst:** The 3 starting plants often provide the exact "missing piece" needed to trigger a greenery conversion in Generation 1 or 2, securing a TR and board presence immediately.
4. **Milestone Control:** Essential for racing for the **Builder** or **Generalist** milestones by allowing you to dump resources into multiple project tracks simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Production:** Supply Drop provides zero long-term growth. If your starting hand is full of cards you cannot play immediately (due to requirements), the resources will sit in your inventory, losing their snowball potential compared to an income-based Prelude.
- **Plant Vulnerability:** In 3+ player games, the 3 plants you gain are a primary target for early-game "plant-killing" events (*Asteroids*, *Virus*). Only take this if you can convert the plants within 1-2 generations.
- **Zero Tags:** Lacks any tags, providing no progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Ecologist* or *Scientist*.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** Only take Supply Drop if you have **at least two cards** in your starting hand that can utilize the metal immediately. Its power is in the "now"; if you have to wait for the late game to use the resources, pick a production Prelude instead.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" if your hand contains expensive building or space cards. Essential for **Mining Guild**, **Ecoline**, or anyone Pursuing an aggressive early board presence. Excellent in Solo mode.
