# Project: Tardigrades (#049)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Add 1 Microbe to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 4 Microbes on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Filler)
**Status: D-TIER VP SOURCE / B-TIER SYNERGY ENABLER**

Tardigrades is widely considered one of the weakest point-scoring cards in the game. While it provides a path to victory points, its extremely slow pace and low density make it a low-priority draft in almost all scenarios unless specific synergies are present.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Early Microbe Tag:** Most microbe cards have requirements. Tardigrades can be played in Generation 1, providing an immediate Microbe tag to satisfy requirements for cards like **Advanced Ecosystems** or to trigger **Decomposers**.
2. **Tempo Stalling:** Like other cheap action cards, it provides a valuable "pass" action to wait out opponents' moves without ending your turn.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Enceladus (Colonies):** The only way to make the card a viable point source. Trading with Enceladus allows you to dump massive amounts of microbes onto the card, potentially yielding 5-10+ VP.
   - **Vitor:** The 0 cost (after the rebate) makes it a "free" tag and potential VP.
   - **Splice:** Provides a cheap way to trigger the corporation's ability for a minor benefit.
   - **Topsoil Contract:** Allows you to remove a microbe to gain 1 MC, turning it into a slow but steady income source.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Point Density:** 1 VP for every 4 generations of actions is a terrible return. You need **8 generations** just to reach a 2 VP efficiency mark. In most games, you are better off spending the 7 MC (cost + draft) on production.
- **Action Cost:** Consumes one of your two actions per turn for a very small benefit. In the early game, this action is almost always better spent on terraforming or card draw.
- **Opportunity Cost:** **Never keep this in your opening hand** (unless playing for a specific milestone). The 3 MC draft cost is a significant drag on your early economy.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the Microbe tag on Tardigrades to reach the **Ecologist** milestone on a budget. The points are a secondary benefit; the primary value is securing the 5 points from the milestone before your opponents.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are **Vitor**, have **Enceladus** in play, or are desperate for a cheap Microbe tag to hit a milestone. Skip early in favor of production.
