# Corporation: UNMI (United Nations Mars Initiative)

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 40
- **Ability:** Action: If you raised your TR this generation, you may pay 3 MC to raise your TR one additional step.

## Strategic Analysis (The Terraforming Rusher)
**Status: D/F-TIER / HIGH SKILL**

UNMI is widely considered one of the most difficult corporations to play effectively. Its ability rewards pure terraforming, but it requires a consistent source of TR increases and a solid economy to sustain its 3 MC per generation cost.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **TR Rushing:** UNMI can reach high TR levels faster than any other corporation if it can consistently raise TR every generation.
2. **Solo Mode Specialist:** UNMI is S-Tier in Solo mode, where reaching 63 TR is the primary objective.
3. **End-Game Points:** High TR translates directly to victory points and income, giving UNMI a strong late-game position if it survives the early game.

### Synergies:
- **Oxygen Bumpers:** Cards like 'Ironworks', 'Steelworks', or 'Water Splitting Plant' provide recurring TR increases, triggering UNMI's ability every generation.
- **Great Dam / Mohole Lake:** Large TR jumps that allow UNMI to leverage its ability.
- **Economic Preludes:** UNMI needs income cards in Gen 1 to afford both its cards and its 3 MC ability.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Skip Gen 1-2:** Do not feel obligated to use the corporation ability in the first two generations if you need that 3 MC for production cards. Building an economy is more important than 1 TR early on.
- **Find Recurring TR:** You MUST find a way to raise TR every turn without spending a fortune on standard projects. Cards that raise TR via energy or heat are your best friends.
- **Milestone Race:** A strong candidate for 'Terraformer'.

**Recommendation:** Only for experienced players who enjoy the challenge of a pure terraforming rush. High priority if you have recurring TR sources in your starting hand.
