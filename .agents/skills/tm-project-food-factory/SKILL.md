# Project: Food Factory (#162)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Plant production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 4 steps.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Pivot)
**Status: D-TIER ECONOMY / SITUATIONAL "LEMONS TO LEMONADE" CARD**

Food Factory is generally considered a low-tier card due to the high value of plant production. However, it serves as a valuable "emergency exit" for strategies where biological production has stalled or is under threat.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Conversion of "Dead" Production:** If you have 1 step of plant production from a weak Prelude (e.g., *Biolabs*) but no other plant cards in hand, Food Factory allows you to convert that inefficient stat into flexible liquid MC.
2. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card, it can be paid for with Steel. This makes it a decent way to convert surplus metal into 1 VP and a solid income boost in the mid-game.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate 4 MC gain upon placement.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Banker Award:** A cheap way (+4 production for 12 MC) to steal the Banker award in the final turns.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **The "Plant Value" Problem:** 1 Plant production is community-valued at ~10-12 MC. Combined with the 15 MC total cost (card + draft), you are effectively spending **25+ MC** of value to get **4 MC production**. 
- **Payback Period:** It takes roughly **7 generations** to break even on this investment. In a standard 10-generation game, playing this after Generation 3 is almost always a net loss compared to simply keeping the plant production and eventually getting a TR from a greenery.
- **Milestone Competition:** While it provides a Building tag, the loss of plant production often prevents you from competing for the *Gardener* or *Ecologist* milestones.

### Tips
- **Predator Protection:** If an opponent has *Predators* or *Ants* and is aggressively targeting your plants, play Food Factory to "save" your value by converting it into MC, which cannot be stolen or destroyed.

**Recommendation:** Generally a "Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you have excess Steel, no path to a greenery engine, or need an emergency boost for the **Banker** award. Priority 1 is to find a better use for your plant production.
