# Project: Greenhouses (#045)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Plant, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Gain 1 Plant for each City tile in play.


## Strategic Analysis (The Mid-Game Catalyst)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN 4-5 PLAYER GAMES**

Greenhouses is a high-ceiling resource card that is strictly limited by the board state. While it provides zero "engine" value, its ability to provide a massive injection of plants for a very low cost makes it a premier tool for "sniping" greeneries in the mid-to-late game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 6 MC (+3 to draft), it can easily provide 5-8+ plants in the late game. This is by far the most MC-efficient way to gain plants, often providing a "free" greenery for the price of a standard project city's adjacency bonus.
2. **Instant Conversion:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Greenhouses is prized as a "one-action greenery." Playing it when 8 cities are on the board instantly grants you a greenery, TR, and VP, often catching opponents off guard who thought you were generations away from a conversion.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Plant** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Plant Tag:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Ecological Zone*.
   - **Building Tag:** Can be paid for with Steel, potentially making the card effectively "free" in terms of liquid MC.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes to the **Gardener** and **Builder** milestones simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Stalling:** **Never keep this in your opening hand.** It is a "dead card" for several generations until cities hit the board. You are better off spending the 3 MC draft capital on immediate production.
- **2-Player Inefficiency:** In 1v1 games, cities are often sparse. If there are only 2 cities on the board in Generation 8, Greenhouses is statistically worse than simply buying 1 plant production.

### Tips
- **Plant Destruction Buffer:** Wait until an opponent has used their Asteroids or predators before playing Greenhouses. Since the plants are gained in one action, you can convert them to a greenery in your very next action, leaving zero window for an opponent to destroy your resources.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 5-8). Essential for anyone racing for the **Gardener** milestone or building a bio-engine. Skip in the early game.
