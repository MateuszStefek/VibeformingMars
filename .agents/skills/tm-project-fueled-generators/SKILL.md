# Project: Fueled Generators (#057)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 1 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Energy production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tactical Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Fueled Generators is a low-cost, high-tempo card that "trades" future liquidity for immediate infrastructure. While it has a negative economic modifier, its extremely low upfront cost makes it an essential tool for unlocking high-impact engines in the early game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Dirt-Cheap" Enabler:** At only 1 MC (+3 to draft), it is the most affordable way to get 1 energy production. This is critical for immediately "turning on" high-tier cards like **AI Central**, **Electro Catapult**, or **Development Center** without waiting to afford a more expensive power project.
2. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Fueled Generators is highly valued for jumpstarting trade. Since trading requires 3 Energy, this card provides a very cheap final step to reach that threshold, often allowing you to trade with moons like *Luna* or *Pluto* a full generation earlier.
3. **Steel Sink / Building Tag:** As a Building card, it can be paid for with Steel. It is also the cheapest way to add a tag toward the **Builder** milestone or to trigger building-related discounts.
4. **Milestone Padding:** Use it to reach the 6-energy production requirement for the **Energizer** milestone on a budget.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Decay:** Decreasing your MC production in Generation 1-2 can be painful if you don't have a plan to utilize the energy immediately. Only play this if the energy yield provides more than 1 MC of value per generation (e.g., via card draw or trading).
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you only need 1 energy and have the budget, a 4 MC *Power Plant* is generally superior as it lacks the -1 MC production penalty.
- **Late Game irrelevance:** In the final 2 generations, the energy production is useless unless you are racing for a milestone. The -1 MC production is irrelevant, but the card provides zero points.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Efficiency:** High-level players often value tempo (playing a card *now*) over mathematical efficiency (playing a better card *later*). Fueled Generators is the king of early-game tempo.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" if you are holding *AI Central* or playing with *Colonies*. High priority for early-game infrastructure. Skip if you have zero immediate use for the energy.
