# Project: Nuclear Power (#046)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Energy production 3 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Trading Jumpstart)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Nuclear Power is a high-efficiency infrastructure card that "trades" long-term liquid income for immediate industrial power. In the *Colonies* era, it is one of the most important early-game cards for establishing a dominant trading economy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Trade Fleet" setup:** Trading with a colony requires exactly 3 Energy. Nuclear Power provides this in a single card for only 10 MC (+3 draft), allowing you to begin trading with high-value moons like *Luna* or *Pluto* as early as Generation 2.
2. **Exceptional Yield:** 3 Energy production for 10 MC is a ~3.3 MC per energy ratio, which is the best standalone efficiency in the game. Even with the -2 MC production penalty, the card pays for itself in ~6 generations if you utilize the energy for trading or card draw.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it an excellent way to convert mineral wealth into energy without spending liquid cash.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking requirements for cards like *Power Supply Consortium* or *AI Central*.
4. **Physics Complex Synergy:** Provides half the energy needed to power *Physics Complex* for a very low net cost.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Deficit:** Losing 2 MC production in Generation 1-2 is a heavy price. Do not play this early unless you have an immediate use for the energy (trading or a blue action card). Tying up your cash flow for "empty" energy production will stall your engine.
- **Hidden Cost:** If you don't have building resources or discounts, the "true" cost of the card (including lost production) is closer to 30 MC over a full game. Only play if the energy's output (resources from trading/cards) is worth more than that.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Power tag and the 3-energy boost to reach the 6-energy requirement for the **Energizer** milestone on a budget.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" if playing with *Colonies* or holding *AI Central*. High priority for steel-heavy engines. Skip if you have zero use for energy and are purely building a cash-based engine.
