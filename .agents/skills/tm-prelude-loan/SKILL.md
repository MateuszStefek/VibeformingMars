# Prelude: Loan

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Gain 30 MC. Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps.
- **Tags:** None.

## Strategic Analysis (The High-Risk Jumpstart)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "POWER-SPIKE" TOOL**

Loan is widely considered one of the weakest economic Preludes due to its heavy long-term penalty. While it provides the largest immediate cash injection in the deck, the loss of income early in the game is a major disadvantage that only an "explosive" Generation 1 start can justify.

### Why it is a good pick (rarely):
1. **The "God-Hand" Enabler:** Its primary use is funding a high-impact Generation 1 play that you otherwise couldn't afford. The goal is to recoup the -2 MC production loss through the immediate benefits of that card.
   - **AI Central:** Requires 21 MC plus Science tags and Energy. Loan provides the raw cash to bridge the gap, potentially drawing +2 cards per generation starting from Turn 1.
   - **Strip Mine:** Establishing this massive industrial engine in Gen 1 can easily overcome the Loan's penalty by Gen 3 or 4.
   - **Soletta / Io Mining:** High-cost cards that define your entire game strategy.
2. **Early Milestone Sniper:** Use the 30 MC to fund a rapid expansion that secures a contested milestone (like *Builder* or *Generalist*) by Generation 2 or 3.
3. **Phobolog Synergy:** Often the only way for the titanium-rich but cash-poor corporation to actually play their expensive starting hand.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Negative Snowball:** Over a standard 10-generation game, Loan costs you **20 MC** in lost production. Combined with the 30 MC gain, it only provides a net benefit of **10 MC**. For comparison, **Donation** gives 21 MC with zero penalty, making it strictly better in almost every scenario.
- **Payback Period:** Loan takes roughly **4.5 generations** to break even compared to taking a neutral start. If the cards you play with the 30 MC don't generate significant points or production immediately, you will fall behind the curve.
- **Late Game Waste:** Never pick Loan if your hand is full of low-cost or high-requirement cards you cannot play yet. The -2 production will cripple your ability to buy cards in future research phases.

### Tips
- **Robinson Industries Counter:** Use Loan to intentionally drop your MC production to hit a specific threshold for **Robinson Industries** or to contribute toward the **Benefactor** award while gaining a cash boost.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Avoid." Only pick if you have a specific, game-defining card in hand (AI Central, Strip Mine, Earth Catapult) that requires exactly this much cash to play Gen 1. skip in favor of *Donation* or *Allied Bank* whenever possible.
