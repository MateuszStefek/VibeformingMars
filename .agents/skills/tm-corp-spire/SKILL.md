# Corporation: Spire

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude 2
- **Starting MC:** 50
- **Tags:** City, Earth
- **Ability (Effect):** When you play a card with at least 2 tags (including this corp), add 1 Science resource here. Science resources on Spire can be used as 2 MC each for Standard Projects.
- **First Action:** Draw 4 cards, then discard 3.

## Strategic Analysis (The Consistency King)
**Status: MID-HIGH TIER**

Spire is built for reliability and high-value card filtering. Its ability to convert tag density into Standard Project discounts makes it a formidable engine for both board control and terraforming.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Incredible Filter:** Drawing 4 and discarding 3 in Gen 1 is one of the strongest consistency actions in the game, allowing Spire to find its core engine pieces immediately.
2. **Standard Project Discount:** By accumulating science resources, Spire can drop Oceans or Cities at a massive discount, making "Greenery-City" combos much cheaper.
3. **High Starting Capital:** 50 MC is a very comfortable starting pool, allowing for a strong Gen 1 development.

### Synergies:
- **Science Cards:** Science cards often have multiple tags (Science + something else), triggering Spire's resource accumulation quickly.
- **Standard Technology:** Synergizes with the focus on Standard Projects.
- **Multi-tag Cards:** Any card with 2+ tags is high priority.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The 2 MC Resource:** Remember that each resource is worth 2 MC for Standard Projects. Accumulate them aggressively through cheap 2-tag cards to make your late-game cities or oceans nearly free.
- **Science Requirement Buff:** Spire excels in games where science requirements are high, as it naturally gravitates toward those cards.

**Recommendation:** Consistency king. Pick if you need to find specific combo pieces early or if you plan to dominate the board via Standard Projects.
