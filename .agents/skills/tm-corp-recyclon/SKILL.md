# Corporation: Recyclon

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 38 MC, 1 Steel Prod
- **Ability:** Building tags generate microbes that convert to Plant production.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: B-TIER / CONVERSION**

Strongest pivot from industrial steel into a late-game plant engine.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Key for 'Builder' milestone. Claim the steel placement spots early to snowball production.
### Hellas
Extremely strong map due to the abundance of steel placement bonuses in the south.
### Elysium
Watch out for 'Industrialist' award. Placement bonuses are more spread out, so card draw is more vital.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Recyclon's unique mechanics.
