# Prelude: Mohole Excavation

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Steel production 1 step. Increase your Heat production 2 steps. Gain 2 Heat.
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Dual Hybrid)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "FIXER" CARD**

Mohole Excavation is a mid-to-low tier Prelude that provides a fragmented set of resources. While it offers a balanced foundation for both industrial and terraforming strategies, its low yield in any one category often makes it a second-choice pick compared to more focused Preludes.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **Steel Sink Catalyst:** Provides a Building tag and +1 Steel production. This is useful for satisfying requirements for cards like **Research Outpost** or establishing a base for metal-heavy corporations like **Mining Guild**.
2. **Helion Synergy:** Since Helion can use heat as money, this Prelude effectively provides +2 MC and +1 Steel production, while also contributing to their terraforming engine.
3. **The "Robotic Workforce" Target:** Provides a Building-tagged production box. While not as efficient as copying the standard *Mohole*, it is a decent fallback for *Robotic Workforce* if you need a mix of industrial and heat production.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes toward the **Generalist** milestone (requires production in all 6 areas) and the **Builder** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Lack of Focus:** Compared to *Mohole* (+3 Heat prod) or *Mining Operations* (+2 Steel prod), Mohole Excavation spreads its value too thin. 2 heat production is too slow to hit the "8 heat" threshold for a temperature bump without additional help.
- **Low Liquidity:** Provides zero MC production or significant cash gain. If your starting hand is expensive, Mohole Excavation will not help you established your economic engine in Generation 1.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Only take Mohole Excavation if it perfectly bridges the gap for a specific card in your hand (e.g., you have *Electro Catapult* and need the steel production, or you have *Trees* and need the heat to push the track).

**Recommendation:** A low-to-mid priority draft. High priority for **Helion** or anyone Pursuing the **Generalist** milestone. Skip early in favor of more focused economic or terraforming Preludes. Excellent in Solo mode.
