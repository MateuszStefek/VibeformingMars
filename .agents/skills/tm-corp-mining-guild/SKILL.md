# Corporation: Mining Guild

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 30
- **Starting Steel:** 5
- **Starting Steel Production:** 1
- **Ability:** Whenever you place a tile on a placement bonus of steel or titanium, increase your steel production 1 step.

## Strategic Analysis (The Map Specialist)
**Status: C/D-TIER / SPECIALIZED**

Mining Guild is a highly situational corporation that depends heavily on the map and the availability of building tags. While its ability can generate massive steel production, it often struggles to spend that steel effectively.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Steel Engine:** On maps like Hellas or specific areas of the base map, Mining Guild can reach 5+ steel production very quickly.
2. **Board Presence:** The drive to claim metal-producing spots often leads to a strong board presence.
3. **Synergy with Building Tags:** If you have a hand full of expensive building tags, Mining Guild can play them for "free" using accumulated steel.

### Synergies:
- **Advanced Alloys:** Makes your high steel production even more valuable (3 MC per steel).
- **Mining Rights / Mining Area:** These cards are essential for triggering the corporation's ability and securing more metal spots.
- **Card Draw Engines:** Crucial to find enough building tags to spend the steel you produce.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Key for 'Builder' milestone. Claim the steel placement spots early to snowball production.
### Hellas
Extremely strong map due to the abundance of steel placement bonuses in the south.
### Elysium
Watch out for 'Industrialist' award. Placement bonuses are more spread out, so card draw is more vital.

### Tips
- **Map Dependency:** Check the map before picking. If the metal spots are sparse or easily blocked, pick another corporation.
- **Don't Force It:** Don't waste MC on bad tiles just to get steel production. The steel is only useful if you have cards to spend it on.
- **Milestone Race:** Strong contender for 'Miner' or 'Builder'.

**Recommendation:** Only pick Mining Guild on metal-rich maps (like Hellas) and when you have a strong starting hand of building tags and/or card draw.
