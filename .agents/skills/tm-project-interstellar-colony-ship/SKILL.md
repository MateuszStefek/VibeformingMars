# Project: Interstellar Colony Ship (#146)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 24 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Space, Event
- **Requirements:** 5 Science Tags
- **VP:** 4


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Finisher)
**Status: A-TIER LATE-GAME VP / S-TIER TITANIUM DUMP**

Interstellar Colony Ship is a high-cost point-slam card designed for science-heavy engines. While its raw efficiency is average, its ability to convert accumulated titanium and event rebates into pure victory points makes it a premier tool for winning long-form games.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Titanium Conversion:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. In the final generations, this is often the most efficient way to "dump" accumulated metal into victory points.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Provides **Earth**, **Space**, and **Event** tags.
   - **Space/Event Rebates:** Triggers massive rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) and *Optimal Aerobraking* (+3 MC, +3 Heat), potentially making the card cost only 18 MC for 4 VP.
   - **Earth Tag:** Triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate and contributes to the *Legend* or *Terran* milestones.
3. **High VP Density:** 4 VP on a single card is a massive swing. If played with stacking discounts, the cost-per-VP ratio can drop below 3 MC, which is unbeatable for a non-multiplier card.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 5 Science tag requirement is strict. Do not hold this in your hand unless you are already sitting on 3+ science tags or have a clear path to hitting the threshold (e.g., via *Research* or *AI Central*).
- **Early Speculation:** **Never keep this in your opening hand.** Tying up 3 MC for a card you cannot play until the very end of the game is an opportunity cost that will stall your economic development.
- **Vacuum Efficiency:** Without discounts or titanium, paying 27 MC (cost + draft) for 4 VP is poor value (~6.75 MC/VP). Only draft if you have the engine to support it.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent has *Anti-Gravity Technology* or a massive space engine, you **must** cut this card. Allowing a player with infinite resources to land a "free" 4 VP is often the difference between 1st and 2nd place.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 6-9) for science or space specialists. Essential for players with *Media Group*. Skip early to preserve capital for infrastructure.
