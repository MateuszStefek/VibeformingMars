# Project: Designed Microorganisms (#162)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Microbe
- **Requirements:** Max -14°C
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Starter)
**Status: A-TIER OPENER / S-TIER FOR ECOLINE**

Designed Microorganisms is one of the most efficient early-game plant production cards. While its strict temperature requirement makes it a "use it or lose it" card, its role as a double-production jump and a dual-tag catalyst makes it a foundational piece for any biological strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 16 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 2 steps of plant production. This 9.5 MC per production ratio is significantly better than *Heather* or *Adapted Lichen*, allowing you to establish a greenery engine twice as fast.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Microbe** tag with no Oxygen or Temperature minimums.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University*.
   - **Microbe:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Insects*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Ecoline:** The premier pairing. Combined with Ecoline's innate ability, this card allows for a greenery every 3.5 generations from a single action.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate plant (or microbe) when played, effectively reducing the card's cost and accelerating your first greenery.
   - **Splice Tactical Genomics:** Triggers Splice's corporation ability for an immediate resource or card advantage.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The "Max -14°C" limit is extremely strict. In high-player games or games with heat-heavy Preludes, this card can become a "dead draw" as early as Generation 3 or 4. **Play it immediately** if you are anywhere near the threshold.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** A sudden spike to +2 plant production will draw the attention of players holding *Birds* or *Virus*. Be ready to convert your plants to greeneries immediately.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the Science tag on Designed Microorganisms to hit the 3-tag threshold for **AI Central**. The production is a primary benefit, but the tag utility ensures your draw engine stays active.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in your starting hand if you have a path to biological synergies or need Science tags. Priority 1 in drafts for Generations 1-2. skip if temperature is already at -16°C and you have zero other microbe cards.
