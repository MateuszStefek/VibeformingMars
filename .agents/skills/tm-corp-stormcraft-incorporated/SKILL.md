# Corporation: Stormcraft Incorporated

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Colonies
- **Starting MC:** 48
- **Tags:** Jovian
- **Action:** Add 1 floater to any card.
- **Effect:** Floaters on this card may be used as 2 Heat each.

## Strategic Analysis (The Confused Hybrid)
**Status: F-TIER / WEAK**

Stormcraft Incorporated is widely regarded as one of the weakest corporations in the game. Its mechanics are disjointed, attempting to link floater accumulation with heat production, but the conversion rate and impact are simply too low.

### Why it struggles:
1. **Inefficient Action:** Spending an action to add a floater that is worth 2 heat is equivalent to 1/4th of a temperature step. This is significantly slower than almost any other terraforming strategy.
2. **Lack of Identity:** It has a Jovian tag but no Jovian synergy. it has floaters but no way to use them for anything but heat.
3. **Slow ROI:** To raise the temperature once, you need 4 floaters (4 generations of actions). Helion or even basic heat production cards are vastly superior.

### Synergies:
- **Jovian Multipliers:** The starting Jovian tag is its only real saving grace, helping with cards like *Water to Venus* or *Jovian Lanterns*.
- **Titan Colony:** Can provide extra floaters, but you're better off putting them on a card that actually does something useful.
- **Careful Aiming:** If you are *exactly* 2 heat short of a temperature bump, the ability can be useful, but this is a rare occurrence.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Ignore the Ability:** In many games, you are better off playing Stormcraft as a "blank" corp with a Jovian tag and 48 MC. Don't waste your actions on floaters unless you have literally nothing else to do.
- **Pivot to Jovians:** Lean hard into the Jovian tag. It's your only path to victory.

**Recommendation:** Do not pick unless your other option is even worse or you have an incredible Jovian-starting hand.
