# Project: Satellites (#175)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for each Space tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Space Economy)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER AWARD SNIPER**

Satellites is a high-ceiling economic card that rewards players for specializing in space-based infrastructure. While its early-game production is mediocre, its ability to scale with your tableau makes it a premier tool for mid-game stabilization and late-game award sniping.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** It provides +1 MC production for *every* Space tag you own. In a dedicated build (using cards like *Space Station*, *Quantum Extractor*, or *Io Mining*), this card can easily provide +5-8 MC production, making it more efficient than almost any other single-step production card.
2. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This is the premier way to "launder" accumulated metal into permanent income if you lack high-VP space events.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna:** The ultimate pairing. Triggers a card draw upon play and scales with the space-heavy deck Point Luna naturally builds.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to jumpstart their income using starting titanium without draining liquid MC.
   - **Banker Award:** Frequently used in the mid-to-late game to suddenly boost your income by 5-10 steps, often catching opponents off guard and securing the 5 VP.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Event Tags Don't Count:** A common mistake is counting **Space Events** (Red cards). Space tags on events only count *while they are being played*; they do not stay in your tableau and do not contribute to the Satellites trigger.
- **Early Game Deficit:** If played with only 1 or 2 other Space tags, the 13 MC investment (cost + draft) takes 6-7 generations to break even. Only play early if you have a clear path to 4+ Space tags total.
- **Late Game Decay:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 6. Only draft late if you strictly need it to win the **Banker** award.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Space tag on Satellites to hit the requirements for powerful mid-game cards like **Quantum Extractor** or **Mass Converter**. The tag is often as valuable as the production in the first few generations.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-4 if you have space synergies. Essential for **Point Luna** or **Phobolog**. Skip early if you have zero Space tags or if you need to prioritize bio-engines.
