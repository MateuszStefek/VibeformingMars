# Project: Colonizer Training Camp (#037)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Building
- **Requirements:** Max 5% Oxygen
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Bridge)
**Status: A-TIER ON HELLAS / B-TIER GENERAL**

Colonizer Training Camp is a high-utility "early VP" card. While it provides no production, its rare combination of tags and efficient point ratio makes it a cornerstone for specific milestones and Jovian strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Hellas Map" Powerhouse:** On the Hellas board, this card is arguably S-Tier. It contributes to multiple milestones and awards:
   - **Rim Settler:** One of the cheapest ways to get a Jovian tag early.
   - **Diversifier:** Provides a Building tag and a Jovian tag toward the 8-tag requirement.
   - **Tactician:** An easy-to-play requirement card to hit the 5-card threshold.
2. **Mathematical Efficiency:** At 8 MC (+3 to draft), it provides 2 VP. This 5.5 MC/VP ratio is excellent, especially for a card that can be played entirely with Steel.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** Triggers the corporation's passive income gain (+1 MC production) for only 8 MC.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate, making the card effectively cost only 5 MC for 2 VP.
   - **Steel Sink:** Since it has a Building tag, it is a perfect way to convert excess early-game steel into guaranteed points.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Threshold:** The "Max 5%" Oxygen requirement is a strict limit. In high-player games (4-5 players) or games with Oxygen-rushing Preludes, this card can become a "dead draw" as early as Generation 3.
- **Telegraphing Jovians:** Playing this early signals to the table that you are pursuing a Jovian engine, which may lead to opponents hate-drafting Jovian multipliers away from you.
- **Opportunity Cost:** Tying up 11 MC (card + draft) in Generation 1 for 2 VP provides zero economic growth. Only play it in Gen 1 if you are Saturn Systems or are racing for a specific milestone.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Adaptation Technology** or **Inventrix** to extend the Oxygen window for this card, allowing you to drop 2 VP in the mid-game after your economy is established.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" on Hellas or if playing Saturn Systems/Vitor. High priority for steel-heavy engines. Skip if Oxygen is already nearing 5% and you have no way to bypass requirements.
