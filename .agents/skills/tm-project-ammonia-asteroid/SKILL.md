# Project: Ammonia Asteroid (#131)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 26 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 3 steps. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Add 2 Microbes to any Microbe card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Bundle)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL POWERHOUSE**

Ammonia Asteroid is a high-cost hybrid card that offers a diverse set of production and resource boosts. While its raw efficiency is only average, its ability to jumpstart multiple engine tracks at once makes it a valuable tool for versatile engine-builders.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Diverse Production:** +3 Heat and +1 Plant production is worth ~29 MC of value. At a total investment of 29 MC (cost + draft), the card is "fairly priced" even before accounting for the microbes and event status.
2. **Microbe Injection:** Adding 2 microbes to another card is a powerful way to accelerate engines like **GHG Producing Bacteria** or **Regolith Eaters**, potentially gaining a TR or an Oxygen step immediately.
3. **Space Tag Utility:** Can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This makes it an ideal "dump" for titanium-rich players to establish long-term bio and heat production without spending liquid MC.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking / Media Group:** Triggers significant rebates, making the card effectively cost only 20 MC—a massive bargain for the yield.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to convert starting metal into a permanent industrial engine.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the titanium conversion even more efficient.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Gen 1 Bottleneck:** To get the full value of the microbes, you **must** have another microbe card already on the board. Playing Ammonia Asteroid in Generation 1 without a microbe Prelude or card is a waste of 6 MC worth of resources.
- **Payback Period:** Since it provides zero immediate TR, it takes 4-5 generations of production to break even on the investment. Do not play this after Generation 5.
- **Low Impact Rush:** In a high-player "Rush" game, the slow drip of heat and plants may arrive too late to be meaningful.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the microbes to hit the 3-microbe threshold for **Nitrite Reducing Bacteria** or other high-tier requirements a generation early, catching opponents off guard.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-3 if you have titanium production or an active microbe card. skip late if your engine is already stable. High priority if holding *Optimal Aerobraking*.
