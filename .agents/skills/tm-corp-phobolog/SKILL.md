# Corporation: Phobolog

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 23
- **Starting Titanium:** 10
- **Ability:** Your titanium is worth 1 MC extra (4 MC total).

## Strategic Analysis (The Space Juggernaut)
**Status: B/C-TIER / HIGH VARIANCE**

Phobolog is the ultimate "all-in" corporation for space-tag strategies. Its low starting MC is compensated by a massive value in titanium, which it must spend efficiently on high-impact space cards to stay competitive.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Titanium Value:** Titanium worth 4 MC (or 5 MC with Advanced Alloys) is incredibly efficient. Phobolog can play massive cards like 'Deimos Down' or 'Giant Ice Asteroid' with very little liquid cash.
2. **Space Engine:** Phobolog starts with a clear direction: find space tags. This focus can lead to powerful synergies with science and Jovian tags.
3. **Board Swings:** Using its titanium for massive "red" events allows Phobolog to make huge board swings that disrupt opponents.

### Synergies:
- **Advanced Alloys:** The holy grail for Phobolog. Makes titanium worth 5 MC and steel worth 3 MC.
- **Space Tags:** Any card with a space tag is effectively discounted for Phobolog.
- **Titan / Io Colonies:** Establishing more titanium production early is critical to keep the engine running.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Focus on 'Space Hotel' or 'Banker'. Board geography is secondary to space card efficiency.
### Hellas
Prime map for 'Rim Settler' milestone. The ocean placement bonuses are great for titanium.
### Elysium
Target the 'Celebrity' award. High-value cards are your primary scoring engine.

### Tips
- **Gen 1 Priority:** You *must* establish more titanium production or find a high-impact space card to play in Generation 1. If you sit on your 10 starting titanium, you are effectively playing with 23 MC.
- **Titanium Trap:** Don't draft bad space cards just because you have titanium. Sometimes it's better to wait for a better card or use the titanium for standard project-like effects from cards.

**Recommendation:** High priority if you have 'Advanced Alloys' or 'Deimos Down' in your starting hand. Avoid if you have no strong space tags.
