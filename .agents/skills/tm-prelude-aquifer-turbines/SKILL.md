# Prelude: Aquifer Turbines

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Place 1 Ocean tile. Increase your Energy production 2 steps.
- **Cost:** Pay 3 MC.
- **Tag:** Power.

## Strategic Analysis (The Trading Jumpstart)
**Status: S-TIER IN COLONIES / A-TIER UTILITY**

Aquifer Turbines is a high-efficiency utility card that excels in establishing an industrial and terraforming foundation in Generation 1. While it carries a small upfront cost, its role as a "trading fleet enabler" makes it a top-tier choice in many expansions.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, this card is prized primarily for the 2 energy production. It provides 2/3 of the energy needed to power a full trade fleet (3 energy), allowing you to begin trading with moons like *Luna* or *Pluto* as early as Generation 2 without relying on Standard Projects.
2. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 3 MC, you gain ~14 MC worth of energy production and ~18 MC worth of ocean placement (1 TR + placement bonus). This ~32 MC value for a 3 MC cost is one of the highest ROIs in the Prelude deck.
3. **Map Advantage:** Placing an ocean in Generation 1 allows you to "sniping" high-value placement bonuses (like the 2-card spot on Tharsis or the 3-plant spot on Elysium) before other players can react.
4. **Tag Catalyst:** Provides a **Power tag**, which is essential for unlocking high-tier requirements like *Power Supply Consortium* or triggering *Energizer* milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Liquid Cash Loss:** You **must** pay 3 MC to play this card. In Generation 1, that 3 MC is often the difference between playing a critical engine card or not. Ensure your starting budget can handle the cost.
- **Without Colonies:** If you are not playing with the *Colonies* expansion, the value of energy production drops. Unless you have an energy-hungry card (like *Strip Mine*) or are racing for the *Energizer* milestone, pure income Preludes like *Allied Bank* may be superior.

### Tips
- **Ocean Forcing:** Use the immediate ocean placement to meet the requirement for **Arctic Algae** or **Kelp Farming** in Generation 1. This allows you to start your biological engine multiple turns earlier than normal.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" if playing with *Colonies*. High priority if you have energy-dependent cards or high-value ocean bonuses to claim. Skip only if you are desperately short on liquid MC in Gen 1.
