# Corporation: ThorGate

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 48
- **Starting Energy Production:** 1
- **Starting Tags:** 1 Power Tag
- **Ability:** When you play a card with a Power tag or the Standard Project 'Power Plant', you pay 3 MC less for it.

## Strategic Analysis (The Energy Specialist)
**Status: D/C-TIER / SITUATIONAL**

ThorGate is a specialized corporation that focuses on energy production and infrastructure. While generally considered one of the weaker base game corporations, it becomes significantly more viable with expansions like Colonies.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Early Energy Engine:** Starting with 1 energy production and a discount on power tags allows ThorGate to establish a powerful energy base very cheaply.
2. **Colonies Synergy:** In games with 'Colonies', energy is a premium resource for trading. ThorGate's ability to generate energy cheaply makes it a top-tier trader.
3. **Cheap Terraforming:** With 'Standard Technology', the 'Power Plant' standard project costs only 5 MC for ThorGate, allowing for efficient energy-to-oxygen conversion cards.

### Synergies:
- **Colonies (Trading):** The primary reason to pick ThorGate. 3 energy per generation allows for consistent trading.
- **Standard Technology:** Makes the already discounted 'Power Plant' standard project incredibly efficient.
- **Oxygen Bumpers:** Cards like 'Steelworks' or 'Ironworks' that consume energy to raise Oxygen and TR.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Don't Overproduce Energy:** Energy is only useful if you have a way to spend it (Trading, Oxygen production, or Heat). Overproducing energy without a plan is a waste of MC.
- **Milestone Race:** Can sometimes sneak into 'Generalist' if they can branch out from their energy base quickly.

**Recommendation:** Only pick ThorGate if playing with the 'Colonies' expansion or if your starting hand has 3+ strong power-related cards.
