# Project: Cloud Seeding (#004)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** 3 Ocean Tiles
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 2 steps. Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Decrease any player's Heat production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Awkward Producer)
**Status: C-TIER / SITUATIONAL PLANT SOURCE**

Cloud Seeding is a controversial card that offers plant production at a significant cost to your other resources. Its restrictive requirements and lack of tags make it a difficult fit for most engines.

### Why it is usually a "Skip":
1. **Restrictive Requirement:** The 3-ocean requirement often prevents this card from being played in the very early game, which is when +2 plant production is most valuable for long-term growth.
2. **Economic Penalty:** Decreasing your own MC production by 1 step is a steep price, especially early on.
3. **No Tags:** The lack of a Plant or Bio tag means it doesn't trigger synergies with *Viral Enhancers*, *Ecological Zone*, or *Decomposers*, making it much less efficient than other bio-cards.
4. **Heat Requirement Bug:** You **cannot** play this card unless some player (including yourself) has at least 1 heat production to remove. In 2-player games, this can render the card unplayable for several generations.

### When to Play It:
- **Solo Mode:** Essential for hitting the Oxygen requirement if you haven't found better plant production cards like *Insects* or *Arctic Algae*.
- **Desperation Plants:** If you are playing a city-heavy game and desperately need plants to surround your cities in the mid-to-late game.
- **Heat Engine Disruption:** If an opponent (e.g., *Helion*) is building a massive heat engine, removing 1 production can slightly stall their temperature TR, though this is rarely game-winning.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Cost vs. Value:** Over an 8-generation game, Cloud Seeding costs ~18 MC total (card + play + lost production). This produces ~2 greeneries. A standard project greenery costs 23 MC. While technically a "discount," the slow rollout and lost MC flexibility often make it a net negative compared to simply saving cash for more impactful cards.

### Tips
- **Don't Draft for Tags:** If you are drafting for the "Ecologist" or "Scientist" milestones, remember this card provides **zero** tags. Pass it in favor of cards with actual synergy.

**Recommendation:** Generally a "Hard Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are in a solo game or have zero other options for plant production by Generation 4. Priority 1 is to find *Insects* or *Arctic Algae* instead.
