# Project: Domed Crater (#016)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 24 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** Max 7% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps. Gain 3 Plants. Place a City tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent Greenery.


## Strategic Analysis (The Plant-City Hybrid)
**Status: A-TIER FOR BIO-STRATEGIES / B-TIER GENERAL**

Domed Crater is one of the most efficient city-placement cards in the game due to its immediate plant injection. It effectively bridges the gap between board presence and biological production.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Instant Greenery Potential:** The 3 plants are the card's standout feature. If you have 5 plants in hand (or 4 on specific maps), playing Domed Crater allows for an immediate greenery conversion, securing 2 VP (greenery + city) in a single action.
2. **Economic Efficiency:** +3 MC production is a solid boost for a city card.
3. **Building Tag:** Can be paid for entirely with Steel. This makes it significantly cheaper than a 25 MC Standard Project city for players with metal production.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a City tile and a Building tag for the *Mayor* and *Builder* milestones.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Tharsis Republic:** Synergizes with their city placement bonuses and production gains.
   - **Ecoline:** The 3-plant boost is massive for Ecoline's reduced greenery cost.
   - **Media Group:** If you are also playing *Land Claim* or other board events, this fits into a board-control strategy.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Limit:** The "Max 7%" Oxygen requirement is a strict cutoff. If opponents are "rushing" Oxygen (common in 4-5 player games), this card can become unplayable by Generation 4 or 5.
- **Hidden Energy Cost:** You must account for the 1 energy production (~7 MC value). If you don't have energy to spare, the "true" cost of the card is closer to 34 MC, which is more expensive than a standard project city.
- **Colonies Tradeoff:** In games with *Colonies*, the 1 energy production is often more valuable for trading than for a city action.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Sniping:** Use the Domed Crater tile to block an opponent's greenery path or to occupy a high-value bonus spot (like 2 cards or 2 titanium) before the Oxygen limit is reached.
- **Plant Protection:** Play it to convert plants into a greenery immediately if you suspect an opponent is holding an *Asteroid* card.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" for Ecoline or Tharsis. High priority for any player with a steel engine or a mid-game plant strategy. Prioritize playing it in Generations 2-4 before the Oxygen threshold is met.
