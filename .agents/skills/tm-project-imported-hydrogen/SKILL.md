# Project: Imported Hydrogen (#019)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Earth, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Gain either 3 Plants, or 3 Microbes to another card, or 2 Animals to another card. Place 1 Ocean tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Versatile Catalyst)
**Status: A-TIER CATALYST / PREMIUM OCEAN EVENT**

Imported Hydrogen is a highly flexible utility card that bridges the gap between board presence, biological engines, and resource management. It is strictly better than the "Aquifer" standard project and offers multiple paths to victory points.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Flexibility:** The choice between plants, microbes, or animals allows the card to adapt to your current engine needs. 
   - **Early Game:** Use the 3 Microbes to jumpstart an oxygen or heat producer like *GHG Producing Bacteria*.
   - **Late Game:** Use the 2 Animals to secure 1-2 VP on cards like *Birds* or *Fish*.
2. **Space & Earth Tags:** Having both tags makes it incredibly efficient to pay for (using Titanium) and helps toward multiple milestones like *Diversifier*, *Spacefarer*, and *Terran*.
3. **Economic Rebates:** As a Space Event, it triggers massive rebates from *Optimal Aerobraking* and *Media Group*, often making the card effectively free.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** If you have Arctic Algae, placing the ocean grants you 2 additional plants, bringing the total gain to 5 plants in one action.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for cards with VP icons.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers an immediate card draw from the Earth tag.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Placement bonuses:** Do not just place the ocean anywhere. Prioritize "2 Plant" or "2 Card" spots to maximize the ROI of the 16 MC investment.
- **Animal Timing:** Only choose the animal option if you have an active animal card that can hold them. Adding 2 animals to a card that scores 1 VP per 2 animals is a 1-point swing on top of the TR from the ocean.

### Tips
- **The "Order of Operations":** You can choose the order of effects. Place the ocean first if the placement bonus gives you the plants needed to hit a greenery threshold, then take the 3 plants from the card effect to complete the conversion.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft pick. Almost always worth the 3 MC to hold. Excellent for any strategy that utilizes Space tags or biological engines. Use it early for momentum or late for a point-bomb.
