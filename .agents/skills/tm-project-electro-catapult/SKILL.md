# Project: Electro Catapult (#069)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 17 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 1 Energy and either 1 Plant or 1 Steel to gain 7 MegaCredits.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Liquidity Engine)
**Status: A-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER FOR VIRON**

Electro Catapult is one of the most efficient economic conversion cards in the game. It allows you to "launder" rigid or low-value resources (Steel/Plants) into flexible MegaCredits, providing the liquidity needed to fund high-impact card chains.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Net Gain:** For a total investment of ~24 MC (17 cost + 7 energy production), you gain a repeatable action worth 5 MC net (7 MC gain minus the 2 MC value of the resource spent). This is one of the highest repeatable MC production boosts in the game.
2. **Resource Flexibility:** Unlike *Space Elevator*, which is limited to steel, the Catapult can spend **plants**. This is critical in the early game when you may have "junk" plants from placement bonuses but lack a path to a greenery.
3. **Tempo Stalling:** Like other action cards, the Catapult provides a valuable "pass" action to wait out opponents' moves without ending your turn.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Viron:** The absolute best candidate for Viron's double action, netting 14 MC per generation (minus energy/resources).
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate and provides a "free" VP.
   - **Manutech:** Each use of the action triggers Manutech's passive MC gain.
   - **Mining Guild:** Excellent for corporations with massive steel yields they cannot otherwise spend.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Bottleneck:** Do not play this if you are already energy-starved. The energy cost is often the true limiting factor.
- **Building Starvation:** Be careful not to spend steel that you need to play critical building-tag cards like *Research Outpost*. Only use the Catapult to "liquidate" surplus metal.
- **Timing:** Its value drops significantly after Generation 5. It takes roughly 5 generations of use to break even on the 24 MC setup cost.

### Tips
- **Plant Protection:** Use the Catapult to spend plants if you suspect an opponent is holding an *Asteroid* or *Birds*. Converting plants into MC protects your value from being destroyed.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-3. Essential for engine-building strategies. Priority 1 for **Viron** or **Mining Guild**. Skip in the late game.
