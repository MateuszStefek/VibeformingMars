# Project: Earth Office (#107)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 1 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** You pay 3 MC less for any Earth card you play (including events).


## Strategic Analysis (The Earth Engine Anchor)
**Status: S-TIER OPENER / TOP 5 ENGINE PIECE**

Earth Office is arguably the most efficient discount card in the game. With a negligible upfront cost, it provides a massive cumulative economic advantage that can define the pace of an entire match.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled ROI:** For only 1 MC (+3 to draft), it pays for itself after playing just **two** subsequent Earth tags. Given that Earth tags include some of the best economic and point-scoring cards, this threshold is trivial to reach.
2. **Tempo Catalyst:** Reducing the cost of Earth-tagged events (like *Imported Hydrogen* or *Land Claim*) often makes them effectively "free" when combined with other rebates, allowing you to chain multiple high-impact plays in a single turn.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna:** The "Holy Grail" combo. Earth Office fuels the draw engine while making the drawn cards cheaper to play.
   - **Teractor:** Stacks with the corporation's innate discount for a total of -6 MC on Earth tags, making many production cards (like *Sponsors*) free.
   - **Earth Catapult:** Together, they provide a -5 MC discount on Earth cards, essentially making the "Earth Engine" unstoppable.
   - **Cartels:** Helps drop the "primer" tags needed to maximize the *Cartels* payout for a much lower investment.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Late Game Decay:** While it's an "auto-keep" early, its value drops significantly in the final two generations. Only draft it late if you have a specific high-cost Earth tag (like *Terraforming Ganymede*) to drop or need the tag for a *Cartels* trigger.
- **Over-Speculation:** A common community meme is playing Earth Office early and "never seeing another Earth tag again." Despite the statistical variance, the low 4 MC total investment makes this a risk always worth taking.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent is playing **Point Luna** or **Teractor**, you **must** cut this card from the draft. Allowing an Earth-based corp to land Earth Office is one of the most common ways to lose an engine race.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" in almost any opening hand. Priority 1 in drafts for Generations 1-4. High priority for anyone building an Earth-focused engine. Skip only in the very final turns.
