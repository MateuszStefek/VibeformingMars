# Corporation: Poseidon

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Colonies
- **Starting MC:** 45
- **First Action:** Place a colony for free.
- **Effect:** Whenever a colony is placed (by anyone), increase your MC production 1 step.

## Strategic Analysis (The Colony Overlord)
**Status: S-TIER (BANNED IN SOME TOURNAMENTS) / BROKEN**

Poseidon is widely considered the most powerful corporation in the Colonies expansion. It benefits passively from every other player's progression, creating an income curve that is almost impossible to keep up with.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Passive Income Explosion:** In a 4-player game, there are often 8-10 colonies placed. That's +10 MC production for free, on top of the starting resources.
2. **Free Colony Start:** Starting with a free colony (worth 17 MC) allows Poseidon to secure a prime spot on *Luna* or *Ceres* immediately.
3. **Kingmaking Resistance:** Even if opponents try to "starve" Poseidon by not building colonies, they are handicapping their own engines more than Poseidon's.

### Synergies:
- **Luna / Ceres / Pluto:** Getting a free presence on these high-value colonies sets the tone for the game.
- **Colony Cards:** Cards that allow you to place extra colonies (*Space Port Colony*, *Research Colony*) further boost your production.
- **Trading Engine:** Use your massive income to fund a dominant trading fleet.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The Luna Grab:** In 99% of games, your free colony should go on *Luna*. The extra MC production and trade income make you unstoppable.
- **Force the Meta:** Build your own colonies early to force opponents to build theirs to keep up, which only serves to make you richer.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Pick" if not banned. The strongest economic engine in the expansion.
