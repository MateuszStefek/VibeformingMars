# Project: White Dome (#022)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Building, Plant, Animal
- **Requirements:** Greens are ruling **OR** you have at least 2 delegates in the Green party.
- **Effect:** Place a Greenery tile. Raise Oxygen 1 step.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent City.


## Strategic Analysis (The Political Greenery)
**Status: S-TIER IN TURMOIL / A-TIER BIO-ENGINE**

White Dome (also known as Wildlife Dome in some editions) is a high-value card that leverages the *Turmoil* expansion's political mechanics to provide a discounted greenery and high-value tags.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Discounted Terraforming:** A standard greenery project costs 23 MC. White Dome provides a greenery for 15 MC, and if the **Greens** are in power, you receive an additional 4 MC rebate, bringing the effective cost down to **11 MC**.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** The combination of **Building**, **Plant**, and **Animal** tags is exceptional.
   - **Building:** Can be paid for with Steel.
   - **Animal:** Rare early-game animal tag that enables high-tier cards like *Ecological Zone* or *Large Convoy* much earlier.
   - **Plant:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and contributes to the *Gardener* milestone.
3. **Milestone Powerhouse:** Helps secure the *Gardener* (greenery + tag), *Builder* (building tag), and *Ecologist* (animal + plant tags) awards/milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Political Dependency:** You must monitor the Green party. If they are not ruling and you have no delegates, the card is dead. Use your lobbyists to ensure you can play it during the mid-game bio-push.
- **Oxygen Threshold:** Like all greeneries, its value is tied to Oxygen. If Oxygen is already maxed, the card's value is significantly lower (only for VP and tags).

### Tips
- **Steel Dump:** This is one of the best ways to "convert" Steel into a greenery. If you have steel production, White Dome is a 100% draft pick.
- **Animal Buffer:** Even if you aren't going for a greenery strategy, playing White Dome early purely for the Animal tag can be worth it to unlock a 1 VP/Animal card that draws you cards later.

**Recommendation:** A "Snap-Pick" if you have any steel production or are playing a Turmoil-heavy game. High priority for bio-engineers. Best played when Greens are ruling or likely to rule.
