# Project: Io Mining Industries (#092)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 41 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 2 steps. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps.
- **VP:** 1 VP for each Jovian tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Engine King)
**Status: S-TIER OPENER / S-TIER VP MULTIPLIER**

Io Mining Industries is one of the most powerful and expensive engine cards in the game. It uniquely combines massive early-game production with a late-game VP multiplier, making it a "must-pick" for specialized space and Jovian strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Production Yield:** +2 Titanium and +2 MC production is worth ~10-12 MC of value per generation. If played in Generation 1-2, it will return over 100 MC of value by the end of the game, plus its victory points.
2. **Exponential VP Ceiling:** As a Jovian multiplier, its point value scales with your specialization. In a dedicated Jovian build, this card can easily be worth **6-10 VP**, making it one of the most efficient "point-bombs" in the deck.
3. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This is the premier way for corporations like **Phobolog** to "flip" their starting resources into a permanent industrial empire.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** The ultimate pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive income and provides the resources needed for their space-heavy game.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the 2 titanium production worth ~8 MC per generation, significantly increasing the card's ROI.
   - **Eccentric Sponsor (Prelude):** Allows you to drop this card for only 16 MC, arguably the strongest opening sequence in the game.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Upfront Cost:** 44 MC (cost + draft) is a massive hit to your tempo. Playing this in Gen 1 can leave you with zero cash to buy card draw or requirements. Ensure your starting budget or Preludes can support the hit.
- **Stealth Timing:** Unlike other multipliers, the production on Io Mining is so high that you **must play it early** to see the ROI. However, this alerts the table to your Jovian strategy, leading to aggressive "hate-drafting" of subsequent Jovian tags.
- **Titanium Sinks:** Having +2 titanium production is only useful if you keep finding Space cards. Ensure you have a card-draw engine (like *AI Central*) to feed your titanium stockpile.

### Tips
- **Unity Party (Turmoil):** Prioritize playing this when the **Unity** party is ruling or about to rule to maximize the value of the Titanium you spend to buy it and the production you gain from it.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" for Phobolog or Saturn Systems. Priority 1 in drafts for Generations 1-3. Best used as a foundation for a long-form engine game. Skip only in the final 3 generations unless it yields 5+ VP immediately.
