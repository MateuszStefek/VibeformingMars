# Project: Farming (#056)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min +4°C
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 2 steps. Gain 2 Plants.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Harvest)
**Status: A-TIER PAYOFF / B-TIER TIMING**

Farming is a high-value production card that serves as a premier late-game "pivot." While its requirement is one of the highest in the game, the sheer volume of resources and points it provides makes it a staple for finishing biological engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Diverse Output:** Getting MC production, Plant production, instant plants, AND 2 flat VP on one card is exceptional value. It pushes your TR, your final greenery count, and your endgame score simultaneously.
2. **Plant Tag Synergy:** Provides a valuable Plant tag for late-game multipliers like **Herbivores** or requirements for **Advanced Ecosystems**.
3. **The "Ecology Experts" Dream:** If you have the *Ecology Experts* Prelude, playing Farming in Generation 1 is arguably the strongest opening in the game, providing a massive economy and plant lead that is difficult for opponents to overcome.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants a total of 3 plants immediately upon play.
   - **Decomposers / Ecological Zone:** Triggers multiple resource/VP gains from the tag.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The +4°C requirement is extremely steep. In many games, the temperature only reaches this point in the final 2-3 generations. **Do not keep this in your opening hand** unless you have a path to rapid heat or the *Ecology Experts* Prelude.
- **Payback Period:** Since it is played late, the +2 MC and +2 Plant production will only trigger a few times. Its value shifts from "engine building" to "resource sniping" for the final greeneries.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Adaptation Technology** or **Inventrix** to play Farming at 0°C or +2°C. This allows the production to pay off for 1-2 extra generations, which is often enough to secure a victory.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-9). High priority if you have requirement-bypassing effects. Skip early unless you are *Ecoline* or have *Ecology Experts*.
