# Project: Herbivores (#147)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** 8% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease any player's Plant production 1 step. Gain 1 Animal for each Greenery tile you place (including this one). 1 Animal on play.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 2 Animals on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Board-Scoring Engine)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR ECOLINE**

Herbivores is a high-ceiling animal card that rewards players for dominating the map with greeneries. While it is often overshadowed by the "Bio-Trinity" (*Ecological Zone*, *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*), it serves as a powerful "hidden" point modifier for board-heavy strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Board-Driven VP:** Unlike most animals that require an action, Herbivores scales passively with your greenery placement. If you place 5 greeneries after playing this card, it provides a total of 6 animals (3 VP).
2. **Defensive Disruption:** Reducing an opponent's plant production by 1 step is a solid way to slow down a competing bio-engine or to delay a greenery rush.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Ecoline:** The premier pairing. Ecoline's high greenery output turns Herbivores into a 4-6+ VP powerhouse.
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for animals to bypass the dependency on greenery placement.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Greens Party (Turmoil):** The rebate on greenery placements makes the Herbivore point-gain even more efficient.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The 8% Oxygen requirement is a significant bottleneck. In games where Oxygen is slow, Herbivores can sit in your hand for several generations, losing its point-accumulating potential.
- **Low Standalone Efficiency:** At 12 MC (+3 to draft) for 0.5 VP per greenery, you need to place at least **4 greeneries** to break even on efficiency (2 VP for 15 MC). If the board is already crowded or Oxygen is nearing 14%, skip this card.
- **Plant Production Hit:** You **must** decrease someone's plant production. If everyone is at 0, you must hit yourself.

### Tips
- **Hidden Points:** In the final generations, players often miscalculate an opponent's score when Herbivores is on the board. Use your excess cash on standard project greeneries to trigger "surprise" points that can decide a close match.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Ecoline** or anyone with 4+ plant production. Mid-priority for mid-to-late game board control. Skip if Oxygen is already at 12% or if you are ignoring the greenery game.
