# Project: Solar Wind Power (#077)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Power, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step. Gain 2 Titanium.


## Strategic Analysis (The Ultimate Engine Starter)
**Status: S-TIER UTILITY / TOP 10 EARLY-GAME CARDS**

Solar Wind Power is arguably the most efficient "utility" card in the game. While its direct yield is modest, its unique combination of three high-value tags and immediate resource rebate makes it an essential tool for establishing early-game momentum and unlocking high-tier requirements.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Net Cost:** For a total investment of 14 MC (11 cost + 3 draft), you receive 1 Energy production (~7 MC value) and 2 Titanium (6 MC value). This makes the card effectively **cost only 1 MC** for a permanent production boost and three tags.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Provides **Science**, **Power**, and **Space** tags—the three most important tags for engine building.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University*.
   - **Power:** Helps reach the threshold for *Power Supply Consortium* or *Energizer* milestone.
   - **Space:** Can be paid for with Titanium and benefits from space discounts (*Space Station*).
3. **Colonies Enabler:** In the 2024-2025 meta, it is a premier early-game pick to reach the 3-energy requirement for trading with moons like *Luna* or *Pluto*.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to convert starting titanium into energy and future science potential for zero liquid MC.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes the 2 titanium gain worth 8 MC, turning the card into a **profit** on the turn it is played.
   - **Vitor:** As a non-negative VP-related potential card (Wait, it has no VP icon).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Standalone Impact:** +1 Energy production is a minor boost. If you already have stable energy and zero science/space cards in hand, the 11 MC is better spent on income.
- **Late Game Decay:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 5. Only draft late if you strictly need one of the tags to hit a specific requirement or milestone.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Solar Wind Power specifically to hit the 3-science requirement for **AI Central**. Most opponents do not expect a player to land a science tag, energy production, and titanium in a single action, making it the perfect "stealth" engine starter.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in almost any opening hand. Priority 1 in drafts for Generations 1-3. Essential for **Phobolog**, **Thorgate**, or anyone pursuing a science-heavy engine. Skip only in the very final turns.
