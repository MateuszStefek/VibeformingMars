# Project: Small Asteroid (#135)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 1 step. Remove up to 2 plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Surgical Disruption)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / A-TIER FOR TITANIUM ENGINES**

Small Asteroid is a foundational terraforming card that provides high efficiency and tactical disruption. While not as game-breaking as its larger counterparts, its low cost and ability to "snipe" opponents' plants make it a staple in competitive play.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Direct Efficiency:** For only 10 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 1 TR (from temperature) and a tactical advantage. This is strictly better than the 14 MC Standard Project "Asteroid" raise.
2. **Titanium Sink:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This makes it an excellent way for players with high titanium production (like Phobolog) to convert metal into terraforming progress without draining their liquid MC.
3. **Plant Denial:** Removing 2 plants is often enough to delay an opponent's greenery conversion by an entire generation, especially in the early-to-mid game. This can deny them a milestone (like *Gardener*) or a critical placement bonus.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking:** The premier synergy. Provides 3 MC and 3 Heat, making the card effectively cost only 4 MC—an unbeatable ROI for 1 TR and a disruption effect.
   - **Media Group:** Provides a 3 MC rebate.
   - **Legend Milestone:** One of the cheapest ways to add an "Event" tag toward the 5-event requirement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Impact:** While efficient, it only moves the temperature by 1 step. In the late game, it is a point-filler, not a game-changer. Only draft late if you have excess titanium or need the event tag.
- **Timing:** Most effective in Generations 1-4 when opponents are just starting their biological engines and plant removal has the most impact.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use the 1-step temperature jump to secure a threshold bonus (like the +1 Heat Production bonus at -24°C) before an opponent can reach it.
- **Defensive Blocking:** If an opponent is "walking" toward a high-value spot with greeneries, use Small Asteroid to knock them back 2 plants, buying yourself the generation needed to build there first.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft for any strategy. High priority if you have Titanium production or *Optimal Aerobraking* in play. Use it early for momentum or late to secure a milestone.
