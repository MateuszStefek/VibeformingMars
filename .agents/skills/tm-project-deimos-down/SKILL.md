# Project: Deimos Down (#028)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 31 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 3 steps. Gain 4 Steel. Remove up to 8 plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Relationship Destroyer)
**Status: S-TIER / PREMIER AGGRESSIVE TERRAFORMING**

Deimos Down is one of the most high-impact red cards in the game. It provides a massive temperature jump, immediate resources, and the game's most devastating plant destruction.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Massive TR Jump:** Raising temperature 3 steps provides 3 TR and moves the global parameter significantly toward the end-game.
2. **Resource Rebate:** The 4 Steel (worth 8 MC) reduces the effective cost of the card to 23 MC (+3 to draft).
3. **Plant Nuclear Option:** Removing 8 plants is a game-swinging action. It typically denies an opponent a greenery, representing a 2-4 point swing (VP from the greenery + city adjacency bonuses).
4. **Titanium Dump:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium, making it an excellent way to convert space production into terraforming progress and board control.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking:** Provides 3 MC and 3 Heat on top of the card's effects.
   - **Media Group:** Provides a 3 MC rebate.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows Phobolog to dominate the temperature track early using their starting titanium.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Over-Investment in Temp:** If the temperature track is already near its limit, the value of Deimos Down drops. Ensure you aren't "wasting" temperature steps.
- **Plant Denial Timing:** The plant removal is most effective against players who are hoarding plants to place multiple greeneries. If no one has 4+ plants, you lose a large portion of the card's tactical value.

### Tips
- **Threshold Sniping:** Use the 3-step jump to jump over an opponent and claim the +2°C (Heat Production) or 0°C (Ocean) bonuses. 
- **The "Relationship Down" Nickname:** Be aware that in multiplayer games, playing this on someone will often draw their ire for the rest of the game. Use it strategically on the leader.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in almost any scenario. High priority for titanium-heavy engines and terraforming rush strategies. Lowers the viability of purely biological engines for your opponents.
