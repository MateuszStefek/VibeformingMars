# Project: Standard Technology (#071)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you pay for a Standard Project (except *Power Plant*), you gain 3 MegaCredits (MC) back.


## Strategic Analysis (The Standard Project Disruptor)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Standard Technology is a high-efficiency utility card that fundamentally changes how you view the board. By providing a permanent rebate on Standard Projects, it transforms "emergency" moves into viable strategic alternatives and serves as a premier tool for science-heavy engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional ROI:** For a total investment of 9 MC (+3 draft), the card pays for itself after just **3 activations**. Given that Standard Projects (SPs) include placing oceans, greeneries, and cities, this is an incredibly low threshold for a permanent discount.
2. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, this card is significantly buffed by the *Colonies* expansion. Building a **Colony** is considered a Standard Project, meaning this card provides a 3 MC discount on every trade setup.
3. **Science Tag Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for unlocking high-tier draw engines like **AI Central** or **Mars University** for a very low cost.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Credicor:** The "God Tier" combo. Credicor's innate 4 MC rebate stacks with Standard Technology, allowing you to place greeneries for 16 MC and cities for 18 MC—a massive discount that makes a "Standard Project Rush" a winning strategy.
   - **Poseidon:** Essential for Poseidon's colony-heavy strategy, turning their primary production engine into a profit-generating machine.
   - **Tharsis Republic:** Synergies with SP cities to claim the board early and secure the **Mayor** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Card Draw Paradox:** If you have a powerful card-draw engine (e.g., *AI Central*), you will rarely need to use Standard Projects. In this scenario, Standard Technology is only worth the 9 MC for the Science tag and shouldn't be a draft priority.
- **Power Plant Exception:** Remember that the rebate **does not apply** to the *Power Plant* Standard Project.
- **Timing:** Its value is strictly tied to how many SPs you intend to do. **Never draft late** unless you strictly need the Science tag to hit a requirement.

### Tips
- **Pacing Tool:** Use Standard Technology to end the game 1-2 generations early. If you are ahead on points, the rebate makes spamming SP oceans and temperature moves much more affordable, allowing you to "lock out" engine-builders before their setups can mature.

**Recommendation:** A top-priority draft for **Solo Mode** (arguably the best card in the game). High priority for **Credicor**, **Poseidon**, or anyone pursuing a science-heavy engine. Skip if you already have massive card draw.
