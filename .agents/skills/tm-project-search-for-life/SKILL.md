# Project: Search for Life (#005)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 3 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** Max 10% Oxygen
- **Action:** Spend 1 MC to reveal the top card of the deck. If it is a Microbe tag, add a resource to this card.
- **VP:** 3 if this card has 1 or more resources.


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Key)
**Status: C-TIER ACTION / S-TIER ENABLER**

Search for Life is a polarizing card. While its repeatable action is statistically a poor "gamble," its low cost and Science tag make it an essential tool for unlocking high-tier requirements.

### Why it is a top-tier play (The Tag):
1. **Cheapest Science Key:** For only 3 MC (+3 to draft), it is one of the most affordable ways to hit the 3 Science tag requirement for **AI Central** or the high requirements for *Mars University* and *Olympus Conference*.
2. **Award Strategy:** High value for securing the **Scientist** award on a budget.
3. **Requirement Buffer:** Allows you to "bypass" higher-cost science cards in your hand by providing a quick, easy tag to meet requirements.

### The Action (The Gamble):
- **Inefficiency:** Microbe tags make up only ~7-8% of the deck. You will likely spend 10-15 MC over several generations before finding life. 3 VP for 15+ MC is a poor return.
- **When to Use:**
  - **Tempo Stalling:** Use the action as your first move to "pass time" and see what opponents do before committing to a larger play.
  - **The "Final 1 MC":** If you are in the final generation and have 1 MC left with no other usage, it's a valid "hail mary" for 3 points.
  - **Stop at One:** Once you find a resource, the card is worth 3 VP. Additional resources provide **zero** extra value.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Action Distraction:** Do not spend 1 MC every turn in the early game. That money is far better spent on production or card draw. The action is a late-game point-filler, not an engine.
- **Oxygen Limit:** Ensure you play it before Oxygen hits 10%. If you miss this window, the card becomes a dead draw.

### Tips
- **Guaranteed Life:** If you have cards like *L1 Trade Terminal* (which can add resources to any card), you can skip the gamble and guarantee the 3 VP on Search for Life.

**Recommendation:** Draft and play strictly for the Science tag to unlock *AI Central* or other science-heavy cards. Ignore the action unless stalling for tempo or spending leftover change in the final round.
