# Project: Medical Lab (#099)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step for every 2 Building tags you have (including this one).
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Scalable Economy)
**Status: B-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER WITH ROBOTIC WORKFORCE**

Medical Lab is a high-ceiling economic card that rewards players for specializing in infrastructure. While its early-game production is mediocre, its ability to scale with your board state makes it a premier tool for mid-game stabilization and late-game point padding.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** It provides scalable income that grows as you play more building tags. In a dedicated build, it can easily provide +4-6 MC production, making it more efficient than almost any other single-step production card.
2. **The "Robotic Workforce" Power Play:** The premier target for Robotic Workforce. Copying Medical Lab in the late game can provide a massive second cash injection for only 9 MC, effectively winning the economic race.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University* without draining liquid MC.
   - **Building:** Can be paid for entirely with **Steel**, making it an excellent way to convert metal into TR, income, and points.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes to the **Builder**, **Scientist**, and **Banker** milestones simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Inefficiency:** Playing Medical Lab in Generation 1 yields only +1 MC production. Tying up 16 MC (cost + draft) for such a low return will stall your engine. It is best played in Generations 4-6 once you have accumulated 4-6 building tags.
- **Expansion Dilution:** In games with all expansions, the density of building tags in the deck is lower. You may find it harder to hit high production numbers than in Base-only games.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use Medical Lab as a "free" science tag to hit requirements for card-draw engines. Since you can pay with steel, it preserves your liquid MC for the high-impact cards you draw.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 3-5). High priority for anyone holding *Robotic Workforce* or building a steel-heavy engine. Skip early to preserve tempo.
