# Project: Towing a Comet (#134)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 20 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise Oxygen 1 step. Place 1 Ocean tile. Gain 2 Plants.


## Strategic Analysis (The Foundation Terraformer)
**Status: A-TIER EFFICIENCY / PREMIER RUSH EVENT**

Towing a Comet is one of the most efficient terraforming cards in the game. It provides a dense bundle of TR, board presence, and biological resources for a relatively low cost, making it a staple for both "Rush" strategies and bio-engine catalysts.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost-to-TR Efficiency:** For 20 MC (+3 to draft), you gain **2 TR** (Oxygen + Ocean) and a placement bonus. This is strictly superior to Standard Project costs, and the 2 bonus plants bring the card's effective value to ~25 MC.
2. **Space Tag Utility:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This is a premier way for corporations like **Phobolog** to convert their starting metal into immediate TR and plant momentum.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "God Tier" combo. Gain 2 plants from the card plus 2 plants from the ocean placement, putting you halfway to a greenery for zero net biological investment.
   - **Optimal Aerobraking / Media Group:** Triggers significant rebates, making the 2 TR and 2 plants effectively cost only ~11 MC.
   - **Ecoline:** The 2 plants are vital for Ecoline's reduced greenery cost, often allowing for a forest placement 1-2 generations early.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Threshold:** While it has no requirement, its value drops if Oxygen is already maxed. **Play it early** to maximize the income gain from the TR.
- **Ocean Cap:** Monitor the ocean track. If all 9 oceans are already placed, you lose 1 TR and the placement bonus, making the card much less efficient.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** Gaining 2 plants can put you near the 8-plant threshold, making you a target for *Birds* or *Virus*. Be ready to convert your plants to greeneries immediately.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use the ocean placement to "swipe" a high-value bonus spot (like 2 titanium or 2 cards) before an opponent can react. The combination of TR and the placement bonus often makes the card effectively free.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-4 for any strategy. Essential for **Rush** players or anyone holding *Arctic Algae*. Skip late if Oxygen and Oceans are already mostly maxed.
