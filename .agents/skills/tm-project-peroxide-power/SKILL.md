# Project: Peroxide Power (#026)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Energy production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Trading Enabler)
**Status: A-TIER IN COLONIES / B-TIER GENERAL**

Peroxide Power is a high-utility engine card that "borrows" from your future economy to provide immediate infrastructure. While it has a negative production modifier, its low cost and high energy yield make it a premier tool for specific expansions and card-heavy builds.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Colonies Synergy:** Trading requires 3 Energy. Peroxide Power provides 2 of those steps for only 7 MC (+3 to draft). In the 2024-2025 meta, this is the most efficient way to jumpstart early-game trading with high-value colonies like **Luna** or **Pluto**.
2. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card, it can be paid for with Steel. Using metal to gain energy production effectively makes the setup "liquid-cash free," allowing you to preserve MC for non-building tags.
3. **Engine Foundation:** It perfectly fuels repeatable actions like **Electro Catapult** or **Ironworks**, or card-draw engines like **Development Center**.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Power tag and a Building tag, contributing to *Diversifier* and *Builder*.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Decay:** Decreasing your MC production in Generation 1-2 can be painful. Only play this early if you have a clear plan to utilize the 2 energy immediately (e.g., trading or card draw).
- **Single Energy Needs:** If you only need 1 energy (e.g., for a city or a cheap oxygen card), *Power Plant* or *Fueled Generators* are better because they lack the MC production penalty.
- **Late Game:** In the final 2 generations, the energy production rarely has time to convert to useful heat or power an engine. The -1 MC production is irrelevant, but the card is still a poor investment compared to direct TR or VP.

### Tips
- **Trading ROI:** The ROI from early trading (especially for cards or MC) far outweighs the loss of 1 MC production per generation. Draft this aggressively in any game with the *Colonies* expansion.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" if you are playing with *Colonies* or have any steel production. High priority for early-game infrastructure. Skip if you have no immediate use for the energy.
