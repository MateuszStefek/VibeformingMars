# Corporation: Inventrix

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 45
- **Starting Ability:** Draw 3 cards immediately.
- **Ongoing Ability:** You may ignore up to 2 steps of any global parameter requirement (Oxygen, Temperature, Oceans, Venus).

## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Cheater)
**Status: B/C-TIER / SPECIALIZED**

Inventrix is a high-variance corporation that thrives in fast-paced games. Its ability to "cheat" requirements allows it to play powerful mid-to-late game cards much earlier than opponents.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Early Power Plays:** Inventrix can play 'Fish' at 0% Oxygen, 'Kelp Farming' at 2% Oxygen, or 'Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid' at 7% Oxygen. This grants them a massive head start on production and points.
2. **Fast Games:** Inventrix benefits from games that end in Generation 8 or 9, where their early-game requirement cheating has the most impact.
3. **Card Advantage:** Starting with 3 extra cards gives Inventrix more options to find those crucial requirement-heavy cards.

### Synergies:
- **Venus Next:** Inventrix is exceptionally strong with Venus cards, which often have high requirement thresholds.
- **Ecological Zone / Decomposers:** Cheating out early animal or microbe cards to start accumulating points early.
- **NRA (Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid):** The ultimate target for Inventrix to play early.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Milestone Rush:** The extra 3 cards in Gen 1 make Inventrix a strong contender for the 'Planner' milestone.
- **Tempo is Key:** If you don't have cards that benefit from requirement cheating in your starting hand or draft, Inventrix becomes a weak corporation. It relies entirely on its ability to play specific cards early.

**Recommendation:** Pick Inventrix if your starting hand contains high-impact cards with mid-range requirements (e.g., Kelp Farming, Fish, Birds).
