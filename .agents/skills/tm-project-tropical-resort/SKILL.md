# Project: Tropical Resort (#098)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Heat production 2 steps. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Pivot)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "STEEL DUMP"**

Tropical Resort is a high-cost hybrid card used primarily to pivot from a terraforming engine into a pure point-scoring economy. While it offers a solid victory point yield, its high opportunity cost and negative production modifier make it a niche choice in most engine builds.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Steel Sink for VP:** As a Building card, the 13 MC cost can be paid entirely with Steel. In the final generations, this is an extrêmement efficient way to convert surplus metal into 2 VP and a minor income boost.
2. **The "Banker" Sniper:** Use the +3 MC production in the penultimate or final generation to steal the **Banker** award. While the production itself won't pay off, the 5 VP from the award makes the card highly efficient.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. As a card with a VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate cash gain for increasing production steps.
   - **Cheung Shing Mars:** Benefits from the Building tag discount and fits their industrial playstyle.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Heat Penalty:** Decreasing your heat production by 2 steps early in the game is a massive hit to your terraforming potential. It effectively costs you 0.25 TR per generation. **Never play this in Generations 1-3** unless you have "free" heat production from Preludes you have no intention of using.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At 16 MC total (cost + draft), it takes roughly 6 generations for the income alone to break even. Compared to *Acquired Company* (+3 production for 13 MC), Tropical Resort is strictly worse for economy.
- **Late Game irrelevance:** Aside from the VP and award-sniping, the production is useless in the final turns. Only draft if you have the Steel to spare.

### Tips
- **Final Turn "Clean-up":** Wait until the final round when Temperature is already maxed. The "decrease heat production" penalty is now zero, turning the card into a clean 2 VP and 3 income for a very low net cost.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft for most players. High priority if playing **Vitor** or if you have a massive Steel surplus in the endgame. Skip early to preserve terraforming momentum.
