# Corporation: Cheung Shing Mars

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Starting MC:** 44
- **Tags:** Building
- **Starting Production:** 3 MC
- **Effect:** You pay 2 MC less for any card with a Building tag.

## Strategic Analysis (The Reliable Contractor)
**Status: B-TIER / SOLID**

Cheung Shing Mars is the epitome of consistency. It provides a healthy starting income and a passive discount that stays relevant throughout the entire game, especially in building-heavy engines.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Strong Early Economy:** Starting with +3 MC production (equivalent to a free *Sponsors*) gives Cheung Shing a very stable floor.
2. **Tag Synergy:** The Building tag discount (-2 MC) is one of the most frequently triggered discounts in the game, as Building tags are ubiquitous.
3. **Versatility:** Unlike specialized corps, Cheung Shing can pivot into almost any strategy, though it naturally leans toward ground games and infrastructure.

### Synergies:
- **Building-Heavy Cards:** *Industrial Center*, *Research Outpost*, and expensive space-building hybrids.
- **Advanced Alloys:** Further improves the value of the building-centric strategy.
- **Contractor Prelude:** A perfect opening that doubles down on the building discount.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Don't Over-specialize:** While the building discount is great, don't ignore powerful space or event cards just because they aren't buildings. Use your stable income to fund a balanced engine.
- **Milestone Snipe:** With your efficient building costs, you are often a strong contender for 'Builder' or 'Architect' milestones.

**Recommendation:** A safe, high-floor pick. Choose if your starting hand has 2-3 high-value building cards or if you want a low-variance game.
