# Project: Business Contacts (#149)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Look at the top 4 cards of the deck. Take 2 of them into your hand and discard the other 2.


## Strategic Analysis (The Precision Dig)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR POINT LUNA**

Business Contacts is one of the most efficient card-draw tools in the game. Unlike raw draw engines, it offers a "mini-draft" that significantly improves the quality of your hand, making it a premier tool for finding win-conditions in the mid-to-late game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Quality:** Seeing 4 cards and picking 2 is statistically superior to drawing 2 cards blindly. It allows you to filter out "junk" and find the specific tags or multipliers needed for your current engine.
2. **Immediate Payout:** For a total investment of 10 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 2 high-quality cards. This is a very efficient "burst" draw that can jumpstart a stalled engine or secure a final point-slam.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both an **Earth** and an **Event** tag for a low cost.
   - **Earth Tag:** Triggers *Point Luna* for a 3rd card draw and benefits from *Earth Office* or *Teractor* discounts.
   - **Event Tag:** Triggers rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) or *Interplanetary Cinematics* (+2 MC). With these active, the card can net you **high-quality draws for as low as 1-2 MC**.
4. **Information Denial:** In high-level play, discarding 2 cards from the top of the deck ensures your opponents will never see them, potentially burying their missing engine pieces.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Budget:** Spending 10 MC in Generation 1-2 for random cards (even high-quality ones) provides zero economic growth. That capital is almost always better spent on production. It is a mid-game tool for finding the "next step" of your engine.
- **Speculation Risk:** **Do not hold this early** unless you are playing Point Luna or have massive rebates. Tying up 3 MC of draft capital for a card you might "whiff" on is an opportunity cost.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Business Contacts as a cheap Earth tag to hit the requirements for **Earth Office** or to boost your **Cartels** payout. The draws are a primary benefit, but the tag utility stabilizes your rollout.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 3-7 for any strategy. Essential for **Point Luna** or anyone holding **Media Group**. Skip early if you need to prioritize immediate income production.
