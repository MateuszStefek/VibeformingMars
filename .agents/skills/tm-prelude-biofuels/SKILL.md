# Prelude: Biofuels

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Gain 2 Plants.
- **Tag:** Microbe.

## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Fixer)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "FIXER" CARD**

Biofuels is a mid-to-low tier Prelude that provides a diverse but fragmented set of resources. While it lacks the raw economic power of S-tier Preludes, it serves as a valuable tool for "fixing" an awkward starting hand by providing exactly the energy or biological foundation needed to jumpstart an engine.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **Specific Requirement Bridging:** If your starting hand contains a high-value card requiring exactly 1 energy (e.g., *Electro Catapult* or *Water Splitting Plant*), Biofuels is a perfect "Generation 1" enabler.
2. **Bio-Engine Catalyst:** For biological specialists (like **Ecoline**), the plant production and 2 bonus plants are highly valuable. It can help secure a Generation 1 forest placement, which is often enough to claim the **Gardener** milestone.
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both an **Energy step** and a **Microbe tag**.
   - **Energy:** Contributes toward the 3-energy threshold for trading in the *Colonies* expansion.
   - **Microbe:** Triggers early-game bio-synergies like *Viral Enhancers* or *Decomposers*.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Snowball Potential:** Compared to Preludes that provide MC production (like *Allied Bank*), Biofuels offers very slow economic growth. 1 plant production takes roughly 6 generations to produce a single greenery.
- **Plant Vulnerability:** In 3+ player games, the 2 plants you gain are highly likely to be destroyed by early-game asteroids before you can convert them. Only take this if you are first or second in turn order.
- **Solo Mode Advantage:** Biofuels is significantly stronger in Solo play, where plant destruction is non-existent and the energy production is vital for early trading with colonies without spending liquid MC.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Microbe tag on Biofuels to reach the requirement for **Advanced Ecosystems** or other high-tier bio-cards earlier than your opponents expect.

**Recommendation:** An "Emergency" pick. Only take if your other Prelude options provide zero synergy or if you are 100% committed to a bio-engine strategy. Skip early in favor of pure economic production. Excellent in Solo mode.
