# Project: Symbiotic Fungus (#081)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** Min -14°C
- **Action:** Add 1 Microbe to another card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Force Multiplier)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER ENGINE CATALYST**

Symbiotic Fungus is a high-efficiency engine card that serves as a "buffer" for powerful microbe-based strategies. Its value is strictly tied to the presence of high-tier "sinks" that convert microbes into points or terraforming progress.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional ROI:** For only 4 MC (+3 to draft), it provides a repeatable action that effectively doubles the speed of another card. If used on a **1 VP/2 Microbe** card (like *Ants* or *Nitrite Reducing Bacteria*), it generates **0.5 VP every turn** for zero net cost.
2. **Acceleration:** The most common high-level use is to speed up terraforming microbes like **Regolith Eaters** or **GHG Producing Bacteria**, allowing you to raise Oxygen or Temperature twice as fast as your opponents expect.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Extreme-Cold Fungus (ECF):** The "Infinite Loop" combo. ECF adds a microbe to Symbiotic Fungus, and Symbiotic Fungus adds one to your primary sink, allowing for explosive growth in a single generation.
   - **Ants:** Ensures a steady supply of microbes for your predator to "eat," turning a disruptive card into a consistent point engine.
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides the Microbe tag needed to trigger these passive point generators for a very low cost.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -14°C requirement is relatively steep. In very slow games, the Fungus can sit in your hand for half the match. Do not hold from Generation 1 unless you have a path to rapid heat or the *Inventrix* corporation.
- **Dependency:** The card provides **zero value** without another microbe card to receive the resources. **Never play this card first.** Wait until your primary engine piece is on the board.
- **Ants Predator:** If an opponent has **Ants**, they can steal the microbes added by Symbiotic Fungus before you can use them. Prioritize cashing out your primary engine immediately after taking the Fungus action.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** to drop Symbiotic Fungus at -18°C. This allows you to jumpstart your microbe engine 1-2 generations early, which is often enough to secure the **Ecologist** milestone.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you have any microbe-based TR or VP engines. Excellent mid-game filler for bio-strategies. Skip early if temperature is not moving or if you lack a primary microbe sink.
