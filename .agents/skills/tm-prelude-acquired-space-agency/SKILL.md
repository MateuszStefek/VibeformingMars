# Prelude: Acquired Space Agency

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Gain 6 Titanium. Reveal cards from the deck until you reveal 2 Space tags. Take those 2 cards into your hand and discard the rest.
- **Tags:** None.

## Strategic Analysis (The Precision Engine)
**Status: A-TIER ENGINE / S-TIER FOR TITANIUM CORPS**

Acquired Space Agency (ASA) is a high-utility Prelude that removes the randomness of initial card draws by "digging" for specific tags. It provides the perfect combination of resources and specialized options to establish a dominant space-based strategy from Generation 1.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Precision Card Advantage:** Unlike random draws, ASA guarantees you find **2 Space tags**. In the 2024-2025 meta, this is exceptionally valuable for ensuring you find the high-impact events or Jovian multipliers needed for a late-game win.
2. **Immediate Liquidity:** The 6 starting titanium is worth **18 MC**. This ensures you can actually *play* the expensive space cards you just drew on the very first turn.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog:** The premier pairing. The 6 titanium is worth **24 MC** for Phobolog, making the Prelude's total value ~33 MC—one of the highest in the deck.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Significantly increases the value of the 6 starting titanium.
   - **Saturn Systems:** Guaranteed Jovian potential (as many space tags are also Jovian).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Information Leak:** You **must reveal** the cards you draw (and those you discard) to your opponents. If you draw a game-ending Jovian multiplier, expect opponents to "hate-draft" Jovian tags away from you for the rest of the match.
- **Lack of Production:** ASA provides zero long-term production. It is a "tempo" and "hand quality" card. If you don't find a production-based space card (e.g., *Io Mining*), you may fall behind in the mid-game economy.
- **Zero Tags:** The Prelude itself has no tags, providing no progress toward milestones like *Space Manager*.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** Use the 6 titanium immediately to play the best space card you drew. Establishing an early industrial lead or terraforming engine is often more valuable than "hoarding" the metal for a later multiplier.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" for Phobolog or if your starting hand lacks a clear direction. High priority for any space-heavy strategy. Skip only if you are strictly Pursuing a biological engine.
