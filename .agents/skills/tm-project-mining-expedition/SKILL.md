# Project: Mining Expedition (#032)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise Oxygen 1 step. Gain 2 Steel. Remove up to 2 plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Surprise Oxygen Bump)
**Status: B-TIER TEMPO / SITUATIONAL SNIPER**

Mining Expedition is a highly tactical event card that excels in "sniping" global parameter bonuses and disrupting biological engines. While its raw efficiency is average, its ability to move the oxygen track without a greenery makes it a unique competitive tool.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Bonus Sniping:** It is one of the few ways to raise Oxygen without placing a greenery. Use it to "steal" the 8% Oxygen temperature bonus or other thresholds before an opponent can reach them with their plant conversion.
2. **Economic Rebate:** The 2 Steel (worth 4-6 MC) reduces the effective cost of the TR jump. As an Event card, it triggers rebates from **Media Group** (+3 MC) and **Interplanetary Cinematics** (+2 MC), making it incredibly efficient in the right engine.
3. **Disruption:** Removing 2 plants is a focused attack on plant-heavy players (like *Ecoline*). It can prevent them from reaching the 8-plant threshold, protecting your board position and delaying their score.
4. **Requirement Bridge:** A cheap way to push Oxygen to 11% or 13% to unlock powerful mid-game cards like *Predators* or *Birds* if the track is moving too slowly.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Steel Sink:** If you have zero building tags in hand and no steel production, the 2 Steel gain is much less valuable. The card's ROI drops significantly without a way to spend the metal.
- **Engine Delay:** If you are playing a long-term engine strategy, accelerating the Oxygen track is counter-productive. Only play this if you are pursuing a "Rush" strategy or need to secure a specific milestone.

### Tips
- **Surprise Greenery Prevention:** Wait until an opponent has exactly 7 or 8 plants. Playing *Mining Expedition* to drop them to 5 or 6 can delay their greenery by multiple generations, a massive swing in competitive matches.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft for terraforming-heavy strategies. High priority if *Media Group* or *Advanced Alloys* is in play. Use it tactically to claim bonuses or stall opponents.
