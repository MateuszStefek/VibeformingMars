# Corporation: Nirgal Enterprises

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude 2
- **Starting MC:** 30
- **Starting Production:** +1 Energy, +1 Plant, +1 Steel production
- **Tags:** Power, Plant, Building
- **Ability (Effect):** Awards and Milestones always cost 0 MC for you.

## Strategic Analysis (The Milestone Sniper)
**Status: LOW TIER (Map Dependent)**

Nirgal Enterprises is a specialized corporation that trades starting capital for the ability to dominate the claim phase. By making Milestones and Awards free, it effectively "starts" with a 40-50 MC advantage if it can claim all of its spots.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Milestone Efficiency:** In a competitive 2025 meta, milestones are the most efficient points in the game. Nirgal gets them for free, allowing it to spend all its MC on its engine.
2. **Diverse Production:** Starting with Energy, Plant, and Steel production provides a balanced foundation for many strategies.
3. **Triple Tags:** Satisfies early requirements for 'Building', 'Power', and 'Plant' cards.

### Synergies:
- **Hellas/Elysium Maps:** These maps have more accessible milestones that Nirgal can "snipe" before opponents can afford them.
- **Tharsis Map:** Good for 'Mayor' or 'Gardener' milestones.
- **Building Strategies:** High steel production and building tags favor board-heavy play.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Tempo is Life:** You have low starting MC. You must play very efficiently to reach milestone requirements before opponents.
- **Award Denial:** Even if you aren't winning an award, the fact that it's free means you can fund it just to prevent an opponent from getting the points or to force them to fight for it.

**Recommendation:** Low tier but high fun. Only pick if you are confident you can claim at least 2 milestones and 1 award. Map knowledge is critical.
