# Project: Industrial Microbes (#127)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Building, Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Steel production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Slot-Efficient Producer)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Industrial Microbes is a reliable infrastructure card that offers "two cards in one." While statistically slightly less efficient than playing separate power and mining cards, its combination of tags and card-slot efficiency makes it a valuable tool for stabilizing your early-game engine.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Infrastructure Foundation:** Provides the 1 Energy and 1 Steel production needed for almost any building-heavy or trading strategy. 
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** The rare combination of **Building** and **Microbe** tags is exceptionally valuable for milestones and requirements.
   - **Microbe Tag:** Helps hit requirements for high-VP cards like *Advanced Ecosystems* without needing global parameters (O2/Temp) to be met.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it a great way to "recycle" building resources into energy for trading or card draw.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Electro Catapult:** The perfect setup. Provides the exact energy and steel production required to fuel the Catapult's lucrative action every generation.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate plant or microbe upon play, effectively reducing the card's net cost.
   - **Ecologist / Builder Milestones:** Contributes toward two high-value endgame scoring categories.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** A *Power Plant* (7 MC) and a *Mine* (7 MC) cost 14 MC total. Industrial Microbes costs 15 MC total (+3 draft). You are paying a 1 MC premium for "slot efficiency." Only draft this if you are short on card draw or need the specific tags.
- **Late Game Decay:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 5. Only draft late if you strictly need the tags for a milestone or to hit a bio-requirement.

### Tips
- **Trading Jumpstart:** In games with *Colonies*, use Industrial Microbes as your first energy step. The +1 steel production ensures you have the resources to keep building your trade fleet without stalling your liquid MC.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-3. High priority for **Solo Mode** or anyone holding **Electro Catapult**. Skip in the late game.
