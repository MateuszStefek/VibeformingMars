# Project: Ganymede Colony (#081)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 20 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space, City
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place a City tile on the reserved area for Ganymede Colony.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Jovian tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Jovian Multiplier)
**Status: S-TIER VP SOURCE / LATE-GAME FINISHER**

Ganymede Colony is one of the most powerful point-scoring cards in the game. It is part of the elite "Jovian Multiplier" group that can single-handedly decide the winner of a long-form engine game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Point Ceiling:** In a dedicated Jovian build, this card is frequently worth **6-10+ VP**. Its value scales linearly with your specialization, making it much more efficient than static point cards.
2. **Titanium Dump:** As a Space card, it can be paid for entirely with Titanium. This is the premier way to convert accumulated space resources into raw points in the final generation.
3. **Surprise Milestone Catalyst:** Although the city is off-Mars, it still contributes to the **Mayor** milestone (owning 3 cities) and the **Rim Settler** milestone (owning 3 Jovian tags). 
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Saturn Systems:** Provides an immediate income boost on top of the points.
   - **Jovian Multipliers:** Stacks with *Water Import from Europa* and *Terraforming Ganymede* for exponential point growth.
   - **Space Discounts:** Cards like *Space Station* or *Earth Catapult* make this point-bomb even cheaper to drop.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Stealth Timing:** **Do not play this early.** Playing a Jovian multiplier in the mid-game alerts every opponent to your strategy, leading to aggressive "hate-drafting" of every Jovian tag. Hold it in your hand until the very last generation.
- **Vacuum Value:** In a vacuum (zero other Jovian tags), 23 MC (card + draft) for 1 VP and a city is terrible. Only keep this if you have a path to at least 3-4 Jovian tags total.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If you are not the Jovian player, you **must** cut this card from the draft. Allowing a Jovian specialist to land their second or third multiplier is the most common reason for an unexpected 20-point swing in the final round.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" for any Jovian-heavy strategy. High priority for titanium-rich players in the final 2 generations. Skip early unless you need the city for a milestone race.
