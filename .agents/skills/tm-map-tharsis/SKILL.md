# Map: Tharsis (Base Map)

## Key Features & Placement
- **Equatorial Ocean Belt:** Oceans are largely placed in the center, providing a clear path for chaining greeneries and cities.
- **Noctis City:** A reserved placement spot for the Noctis City card. No other tile can be placed here unless the card is played.

## Milestones
- **Terraformer:** 35 TR. High priority for UNMI or TR-rush strategies.
- **Mayor:** 3 cities. The "must-have" for Tharsis Republic.
- **Gardener:** 3 greeneries. Usually the fastest milestone on this map; highly competitive.
- **Builder:** 8 building tags. Favors Mining Guild and IC.
- **Planner:** 16 cards in hand. A good fallback if your engine involves heavy draw but slow play.

## Awards
- **Landlord:** Most tiles on the board. Ground-game dominance.
- **Banker:** Most MC production. Long-term engine building.
- **Scientist:** Most science tags. Science-heavy engines.
- **Thermalist:** Most heat resources. Late-game conversion or hoarding.
- **Miner:** Most steel/titanium resources. Resource hoarding.

## Strategy Tips (Community Consensus)
- **The "Walk":** Chaining greeneries from the equator outward is the standard "efficient" path.
- **City Blocking:** Use your cities to block opponents from high-bonus spots near the oceans.
- **Ecoline Power:** This map is arguably the best for Ecoline due to the Gardener milestone and central plant bonuses.
- **Noctis Strategy:** If you have the Noctis City card, it's a guaranteed safe spot that can't be stolen.
