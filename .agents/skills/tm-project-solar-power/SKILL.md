# Project: Solar Power (#113)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Stable Filler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "CLEAN-UP" CARD**

Solar Power is a simple, high-efficiency hybrid card that offers both immediate points and long-term production. While it provides a low yield, its combination of tags and victory points makes it a stable choice for rounding out your mid-game engine.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency with Steel:** As a Building card, the 11 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the mid-to-late game, this is an excellent way to convert surplus metal into 1 TR (via the TR-equivalent value of 1 VP) and energy production.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Contributes toward the **Builder** milestone or **Contractor** award.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking requirements for high-tier cards like *AI Central*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. Vitor receives a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon, making Solar Power effectively cost only 8 MC.
   - **Thorgate:** Benefits from the Power tag discount.
   - **Colonies:** A cheap way to reach the 3-energy trade threshold if you are already sitting on 2 energy production.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Liquid MC Inefficiency:** If you have to pay 14 MC in pure cash (+3 draft), the card is statistically "overpriced" compared to a 7 MC *Power Plant* or a 14 MC *Geothermal Power*. Only draft early if you have a specific need for the Power tag or points.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you already have stable energy and zero building tags in hand, the 11 MC is better spent on establishing your income or terraforming progress.
- **Late Game Waste:** While 1 VP for 14 MC is a standard late-game rate, many other cards provide better point-to-MC density in the final generations.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Solar Power as your "third" power tag to hit the threshold for **Mass Converter**. The 1 VP is a nice bonus that helps offset the cost of fixing your infrastructure.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 2-5. High priority for **Vitor**, **Thorgate**, or anyone with a steel surplus. Skip early if you need to prioritize pure income production.
