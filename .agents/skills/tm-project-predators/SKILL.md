# Project: Predators (#024)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 14 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** 11% Oxygen
- **Action:** Remove 1 Animal from any card (including this one) and add it to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Animal on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Point Swinger)
**Status: S-TIER IN 2-PLAYER / A-TIER DISRUPTION**

Predators is one of the most high-impact point-scoring cards in the game. It belongs to the elite "1:1 Club" where each resource is worth a full victory point, but its ability to steal resources from opponents makes it a devastating offensive tool in competitive play.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Point Swing:** In 1v1 games, stealing an animal from an opponent is a **2-point swing** (they lose 1 VP, you gain 1 VP). This makes it the ultimate counter-pick to animal engines like *Birds* or *Fish*.
2. **High VP Density:** Even if no opponents play animals, Predators can cannibalize your own lower-value animals (like *Livestock*) to convert them into 1:1 points, or simply generate 1 VP per turn via its own action.
3. **Draft Priority:** It is a high-priority "hate-draft." Even if you don't intend to play it, keeping it out of the game prevents an opponent from using it to destroy your own point engine.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for animals directly onto Predators, potentially netting 8-12+ VP and bypassing the "once per turn" limit.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate animal (1 VP) upon play, reducing the "payback" time.
   - **Large Convoy / Imported Nitrogen:** High-impact events to dump animals onto Predators for instant point bombs.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The 11% Oxygen requirement is a significant late-game bottleneck. In slow games, Predators can sit in your hand for most of the match. **Do not hold it from Generation 1** unless you have an oxygen-rushing strategy.
- **Countered by Protected Habitats:** If an opponent has **Protected Habitats**, you **cannot** steal their animals. This drops the card's value from an S-tier swinger to a standard B-tier engine.

### Tips
- **Baiting with Pets:** If you are afraid of being targeted by Predators, play **Pets** first. Since animals cannot be removed from Pets (per official rules), it protects your animal tag synergies without giving the Predator player a target.
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Predators at 9% Oxygen. This allows you to start stealing from opponents before they can finish their own animal engines.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in 2-player games. High priority in the mid-to-late game for any strategy. skip early unless you possess requirement-bypassing cards.
