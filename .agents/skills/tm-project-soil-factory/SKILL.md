# Project: Soil Factory (#053)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Pivot)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "STEEL DUMP"**

Soil Factory is generally considered a low-tier card due to its high opportunity cost and inefficient production trade-off. While it provides a rare source of plant production, its lack of a Plant tag and its energy requirement make it a difficult fit for most engines.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Steel Sink for Points:** As a Building card, the 9 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the mid-to-late game, this is a viable way to convert surplus metal into 1 VP and a minor plant boost.
2. **Milestone Support:** Provides a cheap Building tag for the **Builder** milestone or the **Contractor** award.
3. **Requirement Bridging:** Helps reach the plant production requirements for cards like *Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)* if you haven't drawn better alternatives like *Lichen* or *Bushes*.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At a total investment of ~19 MC (9 cost + 3 draft + 7 value of 1 energy), you receive only 1 plant production and 1 VP. This is significantly worse than *Lichen* (9 MC total) which provides the same production and a valuable **Plant tag**.
- **Energy Scarcity:** Decreasing your energy production by 1 step is a heavy price, especially in the *Colonies* meta where energy is needed for trading.
- **Late Game Waste:** Playing Soil Factory after Generation 7 is statistically poor, as the plant production won't have time to yield even half a greenery.
- **No Plant Tag:** Lacks the Plant tag, meaning it provides zero synergy for bio-engines or multipliers.

### Tips
- **Final Greenery Push:** Only play Soil Factory if the +1 plant production is the exact amount needed to reach 8 plants in the final generation, allowing you to secure a 2-point greenery for 9 MC.

**Recommendation:** Almost always an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are desperate for a Building tag or have massive Steel production with no other outlets. Skip early to preserve trading capacity.
