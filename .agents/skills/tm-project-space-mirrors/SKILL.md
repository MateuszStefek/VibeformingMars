# Project: Space Mirrors (#161)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 3 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 7 MegaCredits (MC) to increase your Energy production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Emergency Power Button)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / A-TIER IN COLONIES**

Space Mirrors is a situational utility card used primarily to "fix" energy infrastructure on a budget. While its raw efficiency is poor, its ability to provide a repeatable way to "buy" energy production makes it a vital tool for certain expansions and tag-heavy builds.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Colonies Enabler:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Space Mirrors is valued primarily for its reliability in games with *Colonies*. If you haven't drawn efficient power cards, this acts as an "emergency button" to reach the 3-energy threshold required for trading.
2. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Space** tag for only 3 MC (+3 draft).
   - **Space:** Can be paid for with Titanium and benefits from space discounts (*Space Station*).
   - **Power:** Helps hit requirements for high-tier cards like *Power Supply Consortium*.
3. **Budget Thorgate:** For corporations with high Titanium production but low MC, Space Mirrors allows you to "launder" your metal into permanent energy production.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes toward the **Space Manager**, **Energizer**, and **Diversifier** milestones simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Poor Raw Math:** 13 MC total (draft + play + first use) for 1 energy production is worse than the 11 MC Standard Project. It only becomes "efficient" if you use the action **3 or more times**. 
- **Tempo Loss:** Spending an action and 7 MC every turn to build a slow power engine is often a mistake. It is usually better to find a single card that provides 2-3 energy production in one go.
- **Action Economy:** Like all action cards, it consumes one of your two actions per turn.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use Space Mirrors as a cheap "filler" tag to hit the 2-space tag requirement for **Mass Converter**. The energy action is a secondary benefit that stabilizes your engine while you hunt for win conditions.

**Recommendation:** A mid-to-high priority draft if playing with *Colonies*. High priority for **Phobolog** or anyone needing a cheap Space/Power tag. Skip early in Base-only games if you have better production options.
