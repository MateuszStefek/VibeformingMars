# Corporation: Vitor

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Starting MC:** 45
- **Tags:** Earth
- **First Action:** Fund an Award for free.
- **Effect:** Whenever you play a card with a non-negative VP icon (including this corp card), gain 3 MC.

## Strategic Analysis (The Victory Point Engine)
**Status: S-TIER / ELITE**

Vitor is an economic powerhouse that turns Victory Points—usually a late-game concern—into immediate early-game capital. By getting a 3 MC rebate on cards that also give points, Vitor scales its economy while simultaneously building a point lead.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Free Award:** Funding an award for free is worth 8, 14, or 20 MC depending on when you do it. More importantly, it allows you to lock in an award you are likely to win before opponents can react.
2. **Passive Rebate:** A 3 MC rebate on VP cards effectively makes many high-value cards free or extremely cheap to play.
3. **High Liquid Cash:** Starting with 45 MC plus a likely 3 MC rebate on the first turn gives Vitor a very strong opening.

### Synergies:
- **Point-Heavy Cards:** *Dust Seals*, *Immigration Shuttles*, and most Jovian cards.
- **Earth Tag Synergy:** As an Earth corp, it benefits from the standard Earth engine (*Cartel*, *Point Luna*).
- **Media Group:** If you play event cards with VP icons, the rebates stack for incredible efficiency.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Strong for 'Terraformer'. High starting capital allows for early milestone locking.
### Hellas
Good for 'Diversifier' if you can branch into different productions early.
### Elysium
High synergy with 'Legend' milestone due to frequent event plays.

### Tips
- **Early Awards:** Don't wait. Fund the award in Gen 1 that best matches your starting hand or Preludes. This prevents others from taking it and puts pressure on the table.
- **Rebate Hunting:** In drafts, a card with a VP icon that you wouldn't normally play might become a "keep" simply because it costs 3 MC less for you.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier corporation. Excellent in all player counts. Pick if you want to combine scoring with a high-velocity economy.
