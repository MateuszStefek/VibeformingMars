# Project: Geothermal Power (#115)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Reliable Workhorse)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / EARLY-GAME STAPLE**

Geothermal Power is a high-efficiency energy card that serves as the "older brother" to the basic *Power Plant*. It provides a stable base for energy-heavy engines and is one of the most reliable ways to fix your infrastructure early.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 11 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 2 energy production. This 7 MC per energy ratio is the community benchmark for "fair value," making it significantly cheaper than using two Standard Project Power Plants.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Power** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Power Tag:** Essential for unlocking high-tier requirements like *Power Supply Consortium* or *AI Central*.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it effectively "free" for metal-heavy corporations.
3. **Colonies Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Geothermal Power is a premier early pick because it provides 2/3 of the energy needed to power a trade ship, which is a critical early-game priority.
4. **Engine Enabler:** The perfect setup for high-requirement energy cards like **Strip Mine**, **Water Splitting Plant**, or **Physics Complex**.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Waste:** If you already have 4+ energy production and no immediate use for more, the 11 MC is better spent on production or terraforming. Energy has no value if it simply converts to heat in an engine game.
- **Late Game Decay:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 5. Only draft late if you need the tags for the **Builder** or **Diversifier** milestones.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bridging:** Use Geothermal Power to hit the 6-energy requirement for the **Energizer** milestone on a budget. It is more slot-efficient than basic power cards, as it provides two steps in one action.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-3 if you have energy-dependent cards or are playing with *Colonies*. Skip if you already have stable energy or are in the final turns.
