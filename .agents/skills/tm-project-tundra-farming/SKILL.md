# Project: Tundra Farming (#077)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -6°C
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Gain 1 Plant.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Filler)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "POINT-SLAM"**

Tundra Farming is a mid-tier hybrid card that suffers from late-game timing. While its stat bundle is diverse, the steep temperature requirement often prevents its production from paying off, making it primarily a tool for final-round point optimization.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Mathematical Efficiency:** At 16 MC (+3 to draft) for 2 VP, you are paying **9.5 MC per VP**. This is a solid ratio for a non-multiplier card, especially when you factor in the minor production boost and the Plant tag.
2. **Synergy Enabler:** Provides a Plant tag in the late game to trigger multipliers like **Herbivores** or requirements for **Advanced Ecosystems**.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants a total of 2 plants immediately upon play.
   - **Decomposers / Ecological Zone:** Triggers multiple resource/VP gains from the tag.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **The "Too Late" Problem:** The -6°C requirement is its greatest weakness. In fast-paced games, the match often ends just 1-2 generations after this threshold is met, meaning the +2 MC and +1 Plant production will never recoup the 19 MC investment.
- **Comparison to Alternatives:** **Trees** (-4°C) is generally considered superior because it provides +3 Plant production for a lower cost (13 MC), while **Kelp Farming** (6 Oceans) provides massive production for a similar stage of the game.
- **Early speculative risk:** **Never hold this from Generation 1** unless you are playing **Ecoline** or have **Inventrix**. The opportunity cost of the 3 MC draft capital is too high for a card that will sit in your hand for 80% of the game.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Tundra Farming at -10°C. This allows the production to pay off for 1-2 extra generations, turning a mediocre "point dump" into a viable economic bridge.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 6-8). High priority if you strictly need a Plant tag or a cheap 2-VP swing. Skip early to preserve tempo.
