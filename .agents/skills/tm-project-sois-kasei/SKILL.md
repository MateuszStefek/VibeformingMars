# Project: Sois-Kasei (Promo Card)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Promo
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Gain 2 Plants. Place 1 Ocean tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Booster)
**Status: A-TIER RUSH TOOL / B-TIER GENERAL**

Sois-Kasei is one of the most efficient early-game cards for players looking to accelerate the game's pace. It provides a unique blend of terraforming progress, income, and biological resources for a very low upfront cost.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 8 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a card worth **~23 MC of value**:
   - **Ocean (1 TR + Tile):** worth ~14-18 MC.
   - **MC Production:** worth ~4.5 MC in the early game.
   - **2 Plants:** worth ~4 MC toward a greenery.
   This ~200% return on investment is nearly unbeatable for a requirement-free card.
2. **Instant Greenery Catalyst:** The 2 plants are often the "missing piece" needed to trigger a greenery conversion. In a "Rush" strategy, this allows you to place a forest a full generation earlier than your opponents expect.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "God Tier" combo. Gaining 2 plants from the card plus 2 plants from the ocean placement puts you exactly halfway to a greenery for only 8 MC.
   - **UNMI:** The cheapest way to trigger UNMI's corporation ability (+1 TR when your TR increases) while also fixing your income.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost upon placement.
4. **Milestone Support:** One of the most efficient ways to reach the 26 TR needed for the **Terraformer** milestone or the **Gardener** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Tags:** The card provides zero progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Scientist* or *Builder*. If you are racing for tag-based awards, the 11 MC might be better spent elsewhere.
- **Ocean Cap:** Monitor the ocean track. In high-player games, oceans are often the first parameter to finish. Do not draft this late if only 1 ocean spot remains.
- **Low Scaling:** Unlike engine-building cards, Sois-Kasei provides a one-time boost that doesn't scale. It is a "tempo" card rather than an "engine" piece.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use the ocean placement to "swipe" a high-value bonus spot (like 2 titanium or 2 cards) before an opponent can react. The combination of income and the bonus often makes the card effectively free.

**Recommendation:** A top-priority draft in Generations 1-3 for any strategy. Essential for **Rush** players or anyone holding *Arctic Algae*. Skip late if oceans are already mostly placed.
