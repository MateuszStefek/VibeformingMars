# Project: Mining Rights (#034)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place a Mining Rights tile on an area with a Steel or Titanium placement bonus. Increase the production of that resource (Steel or Titanium) 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Global Mine)
**Status: S-TIER OPENING / VERSATILE UTILITY**

Mining Rights is a high-tier economic card that combines resource production with board presence. Unlike its cheaper sibling (*Mining Area*), Mining Rights has no placement restrictions, making it an ideal tool for claiming high-value bonuses anywhere on Mars.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrestricted Placement:** You can place the tile on *any* valid metal bonus spot on the board. This allows you to "jump" to remote areas to grab high-value bonuses (like the double Titanium/Plant spots) or to secure a strategic foothold for future cities.
2. **Net Efficiency:** While it costs 9 MC (+3 to draft), the immediate placement bonus (usually 1 Titanium or 2 Steel, worth 3-4 MC) reduces the effective cost of the production to ~8 MC. This is strictly better than playing a standard *Mine* card.
3. **Mining Guild Catalyst:** Triggers **Mining Guild's** ability to gain an additional steel production, providing a massive 2-production swing for only 9 MC.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Building tag and a special tile, contributing to *Builder* and *Landlord*.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Metal Bonus Only:** You **must** place the tile on a spot that provides a Steel or Titanium bonus. You cannot place it on a generic spot or an ocean reserve.
- **Titanium vs. Steel:** Always prioritize Titanium spots. Titanium production is worth ~3 MC per generation, making it a better long-term investment than Steel.
- **Early Game Priority:** Its value is highest in Generations 1-2. After Generation 4, the ROI drops, and you should only draft it if you need the Building tag or tile placement for a specific milestone.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use Mining Rights to take a high-value Titanium spot that an opponent is clearly "walking" toward with their greeneries. Denial of a placement bonus can be as impactful as the production itself.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" in almost any opening hand. High priority for metal-heavy engines or players pursuing the *Builder* milestone. Priority 1 if playing *Mining Guild*.
