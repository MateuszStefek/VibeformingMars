# Corporation: Tempest Consultancy

## Card Data

- **Expansion:** Moon
- **Cost:** 0 (Corporation)
- **Tags:** Moon
- **Starting MegaCredits:** 37
- **First Action:** Place 2 delegates in one party.
- **Action:** Place 1 delegate in any party for every 5 Moon tags you have (max 3).
- **Effect:** When your delegate becomes the chairman, increase your TR 1 step.
- **Card Number:** MC2

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: COMPLETED**

Tempest Consultancy is a powerful political corporation that bridges the gap between the Moon expansion and the Turmoil expansion. It leverages Moon tags to dominate the Committee for Mars without the usual financial overhead of buying delegates.

### Key Strengths
- **Political Dominance:** The ability to place up to 3 delegates per turn for free (once you have 15 Moon tags) is an enormous value engine, saving up to 15 MC per generation.
- **TR Engine:** The effect pairs perfectly with its action, making it much easier to cycle delegates into the Chairman position for consistent TR gains.
- **Early Foothold:** Starting with 2 delegates in one party allows you to immediately compete for Party Leader or influence the first global event.

### Weaknesses
- **Low Starting MC:** At 37 MC, it has one of the lower starting budgets, requiring careful management of early-game purchases.
- **Tag Dependency:** Its main action is useless until you have at least 5 Moon tags, making early Moon card draws critical.
- **Turmoil Complexity:** Its value is entirely tied to the Turmoil mechanics; in games without Turmoil, this corporation's primary strengths are neutralized (though usually, it wouldn't be played without it).

### Tips
- **Prioritize Moon Tags:** Even cheap, low-impact Moon cards are valuable early on just to hit the 5/10/15 thresholds for the corporation's action.
- **Chairman Rotation:** Use your free delegates to ensure you become the Chairman as often as possible to trigger the TR bonus.
- **Party Control:** Aim for parties whose bonuses complement a Moon-tag strategy (e.g., those providing discounts or bonuses for Space/Science tags, which often overlap with Moon cards).
