# Project: Fusion Power (#061)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 14 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Power
- **Requirements:** 2 Power Tags.
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 3 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Mid-Game Energy Spike)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN COLONIES**

Fusion Power is one of the most cost-efficient infrastructure cards in the game. While it requires an existing industrial base, its massive yield makes it a premier tool for powering late-game cities, trading fleets, and card draw engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 14 MC (+3 to draft), you gain **3 steps of energy production**. This 5.6 MC per energy ratio is the best in the game for a standalone project, far surpassing the 11 MC Standard Project Power Plant.
2. **Colonies Synergy:** The premier enabler for trading. 3 Energy is exactly what is needed to fund a trade fleet. Fusion Power allows you to start trading with multiple moons (like *Luna* or *Ceres*) for zero liquid MC overhead once played.
3. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Power** tag.
   - **Science:** Essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Antigravity Technology*.
   - **Power:** Helps hit requirements for cards like *Power Supply Consortium*.
4. **Energy "Sink" Foundation:** Provides the exact energy needed to fuel powerful actions like **Physics Complex** or high-requirement cards like **Strip Mine**.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** You **must** already have 2 Power tags in play. This prevents the card from being an early-game opener unless you are playing **Thorgate** or have a Power-heavy starting setup.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If your energy needs are already met, the 17 MC investment is better spent on income or points. Energy only has value if you have a card to spend it on; otherwise, it is just "delayed heat."

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use a **Wild Tag** (from Preludes or Robinson Industries) to drop Fusion Power with only 1 other Power tag. This allows you to jumpstart your trading economy a full generation earlier than your opponents expect.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you are playing with *Colonies* or building a science engine. Skip early if you have zero Power tags. skip late if your energy engine is already stable.
