# Project: Rad-Suits (#186)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** 2 City Tiles in play (any player).
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Stable Filler)
**Status: D-TIER ECONOMY / S-TIER FOR VITOR**

Rad-Suits is widely considered one of the weakest cards in the base game due to its inefficient production ratio and restrictive requirements. While it provides a guaranteed point, its lack of tags and slow return on investment make it a low-priority draft in almost all scenarios.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Efficiency for Vitor:** The premier synergy. As **Vitor**, you receive a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon. This makes Rad-Suits effectively cost only **3 MC** (+3 draft) for +1 production and 1 VP—an excellent 6 MC total investment for early-game stabilization.
2. **Solo Mode Utility:** In Solo play, the game starts with two neutral cities already on the board. This makes Rad-Suits playable in Generation 1, providing a small but reliable income boost from the very start.
3. **Administrator Award:** Since it has no tags, it contributes toward the award for the most "tagless" cards.
4. **Milestone Padding:** Use it to reach the 26 TR needed for the **Terraformer** milestone (as the 1 VP is effectively a permanent TR for scoring purposes).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Poor Amortization:** At a total cost of 9 MC (cost + draft) for 1 MC production, you are paying **9 MC per production step**. This is nearly double the cost of S-tier cards like *Acquired Company*.
- **City Barrier:** In 2-player games or slow "Rush" matches, hitting 2 cities can take until Generation 4 or 5. By then, +1 MC production is a negligible bonus that won't have time to pay off.
- **No Tag Value:** Lacks any tags, providing zero synergy for *Science* requirements or milestones.
- **Better Alternatives:** *Sponsors* costs the same (6 MC) but provides double the income and a useful Earth tag without any requirements.

### Tips
- **Free Play Only:** The 2024-2025 meta consensus is that you should **only play this card if you draw it for free.** Spending a draft action to buy Rad-Suits is almost always an opportunity cost that slows down your engine.

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Keep only if you are **Vitor**, playing in **Solo Mode**, or are desperate for the **Administrator** award. Skip early in favor of more efficient production.
