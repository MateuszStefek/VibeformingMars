# Project: Physics Complex (#208)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 6 Energy to add 1 Science resource to this card.
- **VP:** 2 VP per Science resource on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Point Engine)
**Status: A-TIER PAYOFF / S-TIER WITH VIRON**

Physics Complex is one of the most powerful point-generating engines in the game. It allows you to convert surplus energy directly into high-density victory points, making it a premier tool for winning long-form engine games.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled VP Density:** At 2 VP per resource, each activation is equivalent to a temperature raise or a greenery in terms of points, but without needing board space or moving global parameters.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Science** and a **Building** tag.
   - **Science:** Helps reach the threshold for *Antigravity Technology* or triggers *Mars University*.
   - **Building:** Can be paid for with Steel, making the card effectively "free" to put into play.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mass Converter / Quantum Extractor:** The perfect enablers. They provide the large chunks of energy (6 and 4 respectively) needed to fuel the Complex every generation.
   - **Viron:** The "God-Tier" combo. Viron allows you to use the action twice per generation. If you have 12 energy production, you generate **4 VP every turn**, which is often enough to win the game single-handedly.
   - **CEO's Favorite Project:** Adding a resource to Physics Complex for free is worth **2 VP**, making it the best target in the game for that event.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Bottleneck:** 6 Energy is a massive requirement. **Do not hold this early** unless you have an energy-heavy corporation or Prelude. Tying up your energy engine in Generations 1-3 will prevent you from trading or building cities.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** You need at least 3 activations (6 VP total) to justify the 15 MC investment and the opportunity cost of the energy (which could have been heat/TR). This typically requires playing the card by Generation 5-6.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is needed for trading. Only use Physics Complex if you have energy left over *after* your trading fleets are deployed.

### Tips
- **Stealth Point-Slam:** Hold Physics Complex in your hand until you have the energy production to use it immediately. Playing it early with zero energy "telegraphes" your point-engine to opponents, who will then hate-draft energy and science cards away from you.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 4-6) if you have 4+ energy production. Skip early unless you are **Viron**. Skip late if you cannot activate it at least twice.
