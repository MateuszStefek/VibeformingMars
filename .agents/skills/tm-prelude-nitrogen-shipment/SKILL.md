# Prelude: Nitrogen Shipment

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 1 step. Gain 5 MC.


## Strategic Analysis (The Generalist Filler)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "FIXER" CARD**

Nitrogen Shipment is widely considered one of the weakest Preludes in the deck. While its on-paper value is mathematically average, its fragmented benefits often fail to provide the "explosive" start needed to define a winning strategy in Generation 1.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **The "Budget" Fixer:** If you need exactly 5-8 MC to afford a game-changing Generation 1 card (like *AI Central* or *Earth Catapult*) and your other Preludes provide zero cash, Nitrogen Shipment acts as a vital economic bridge.
2. **Ecoline Catalyst:** For biological specialists (like **Ecoline**), the extra plant production and TR are slightly more valuable, helping secure a Generation 1 forest placement.
3. **Milestone Rushing:** The +1 TR can be the difference between hitting 26 TR for the **Terraformer** milestone or the **Benefactor** award in a tight race.
4. **Generalist Support:** Contributes toward the **Generalist** milestone (requires production in all 6 areas) for a very low opportunity cost.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Lack of Focus:** The primary criticism is that it provides "a bit of everything but not enough of anything." 1 plant production is too slow to impact the game, and 1 MC production is a negligible boost compared to S-tier Preludes like *Allied Bank* (+4 production).
- **No Tags:** Lacks any tags (Science, Earth, etc.), meaning it provides zero synergy for requirement-filling or milestone progress.
- **Plant Vulnerability:** Having exactly 1 plant production often makes you a target for early-game asteroids, potentially losing your resources before you can ever use them.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** Only take Nitrogen Shipment if your hand is already "near-perfect" and you just need a small resource boost to make it work. In all other scenarios, prioritize Preludes that provide focused, scalable production (MC or Metals).

**Recommendation:** Generally an "Emergency" pick. Only take if your other Prelude options are unplayable or if you are 100% committed to a bio-engine strategy. skip early in favor of more specialized production engines.
