# Prelude: Business Empire

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 6 steps. Pay 6 MC.
- **Tag:** Earth.

## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Juggernaut)
**Status: S-TIER / THE ULTIMATE ENGINE STARTER**

Business Empire is the most powerful long-term economic Prelude in the game. It provides a massive income boost from the very first generation, allowing you to fund an expensive industrial or scientific engine that outscales all opponents.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For a net cost of only 6 MC, you receive 6 MC production. In a standard 10-generation game, this yields a **60 MC net profit**—the highest economic return of any Prelude card.
2. **The "Specialist" Milestone:** Provides 6 steps toward the **Specialist** milestone (highest production), making it the easiest way to secure the 5 points in Generation 1 or 2.
3. **Earth Tag Synergy:** The Earth tag is a premier asset.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers a free card draw immediately upon play.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Helps satisfy the "Earth tag" requirement for powerful cards like *Cartels* or *Earth Office* in the very first generation.
4. **Manutech:** The ultimate combo. Manutech receives 6 MC immediately upon playing this card, effectively making the "cost" zero and granting +6 production for free.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Gen 1 Liquidity Crisis:** You **must** pay 6 MC to play this card. If your corporation starts with low cash (e.g., *Phobolog*, *Inventrix*), the 6 MC hit can prevent you from playing your best Generation 1 card. Ensure your starting budget can handle the investment.
- **Payback Period:** While more efficient than *Allied Bank*, it doesn't surpass it in total cumulative MC until **Generation 6**. In high-player games (4-5 players) where the game ends by Gen 7 or 8, the immediate 9 MC advantage of *Allied Bank* may be superior for early tempo.
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent has Point Luna, never pass them Business Empire. The combination of card draw and massive income is often game-ending.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Production:** Only take Business Empire if you can afford to play at least one other income or production card in Generation 1. If playing this card leaves you with zero liquid cash, you may "stall" for several turns, losing the advantage you gained from the production.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" for any long-form or engine-building strategy. Priority 1 for **Manutech**, **Point Luna**, or **Vitor**. skip only if you are desperately short on liquid cash in Gen 1.
