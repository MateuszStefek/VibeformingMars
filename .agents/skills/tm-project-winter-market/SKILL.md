# Project: Winter Market (Promo Card)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Promo
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** 2 City Tiles in play (any player).
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Stable Efficiency)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR VITOR**

Winter Market is a high-efficiency income card used primarily to stabilize the early-to-mid game economy. While it lacks tags, its low cost and guaranteed point return make it a staple "filler" choice for players specializing in liquid MC strategies.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional ROI:** For only 9 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 2 MC production and 1 VP. This 12 MC total investment pays for itself in just **4 generations** (accounting for the point's value), making it one of the most efficient early-game income boosts.
2. **Synergies:**
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. Vitor receives a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon, making Winter Market cost only **9 MC** (+ draft) for +2 production—the same efficiency as *Sponsors* but with an extra point.
   - **Administrator Award:** Since it has no tags, it contributes toward the award for the most "tagless" cards.
   - **Tharsis Republic:** Cities hit the board earlier, making Winter Market viable as early as Generation 2-3.
3. **Surprise Point-Slam:** In the final generations, it serves as a cheap 12 MC point dump with a minor income bonus, helping you spend every last credit.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **City Requirement:** In 2-player games or slow "Rush" matches, the 2-city requirement can be surprisingly difficult to hit before Generation 4. Do not hold this from Generation 1 unless cities are guaranteed (e.g., via Tharsis or high-city Preludes).
- **No Tag Value:** Aside from the VP, the card provides zero progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Scientist* or *Builder*.
- **Dilution:** Drawing this instead of a high-impact space or science card in the mid-game can be unexciting. View it as a reliable "worker" card rather than an engine starter.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Winter Market with 0 or 1 cities on the board. This allows you to establish your income lead a generation early, which is often enough to secure the **Banker** award.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-4. High priority for **Vitor** or anyone playing a city-heavy board game. Skip late if you need to prioritize high-VP space events.
