# Project: Magnetic Field Dome (#054)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 5 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Filler)
**Status: D-TIER ENGINE / SITUATIONAL FINAL POINT DUMP**

Magnetic Field Dome is generally considered a low-tier card due to its high opportunity cost and inefficient production trade-off. However, it serves as a valuable tool for "washing" surplus resources into final victory points in the endgame.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Steel Sink for TR:** As a Building card, the 5 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the final turn, this is a very cheap way to convert surplus metal into 1 TR (1 VP).
2. **Greenery Math:** If you are exactly 1 plant production away from reaching 8 plants in the final generation, the +1 plant production on this card can effectively "buy" you a 2-VP greenery for only 5 MC.
3. **Requirement Bridging:** Provides a cheap Building tag for hitting the 8-building threshold for the **Builder** milestone or for triggering building discounts.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Scarcity:** Decreasing your energy production by 2 steps is a significant hit. In the *Colonies* meta, this energy is almost always better spent on trading for high-value resources rather than a single TR.
- **Early Game Inefficiency:** **Never play this early.** Spending 8 MC (cost + draft) for 1 plant production and 1 TR is mathematically inferior to almost any other production card. Tying up your energy engine in Generations 1-5 will stall your ability to trade or build cities.
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you have to spend 11 MC on a Standard Project Power Plant to replace the energy lost to the Dome, the "true" cost of the card jumps to ~19 MC, making it a terrible investment.

### Tips
- **Award Stealing:** Use the +1 TR to sneak ahead of an opponent in the final round of the **Terraformer** or **Benefactor** awards. Since it is so cheap, it is hard for opponents to predict your final TR score.

**Recommendation:** Almost always an "Easy Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you are in the final generation and have excess Steel and Energy to "convert" into a point. Skip early to preserve trading capacity.
