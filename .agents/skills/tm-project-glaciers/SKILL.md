# Project: Glaciers (#144)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** None (Event)
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place 2 Ocean tiles.


## Strategic Analysis (The Ocean Economy)
**Status: B-TIER EFFICIENCY / S-TIER WITH ARCTIC ALGAE**

Glaciers is one of the most efficient terraforming cards in the game. It provides a massive discount on ocean placement and serves as a premier tool for both "Rush" strategies and biological engine catalysts.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost Efficiency:** At 12 MC (+3 to draft), you are paying **7.5 MC per ocean**. This is a massive savings compared to the 18 MC Standard Project project. It is arguably the most efficient way to gain 2 TR and placement bonuses in a single action.
2. **Double Placement Bonus:** Placing two oceans simultaneously allows you to "chain" bonuses (e.g., getting 4 plants or 2 titanium and a card) in one move, often providing the resources needed for a follow-up play.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "God Tier" combo. Playing Glaciers while Arctic Algae is active nets you **4 plants** immediately, which is often enough to complete a greenery conversion in the same turn.
   - **Media Group:** As an Event card, it triggers the 3 MC rebate, making the 2 oceans cost only 9 MC total—an unbeatable ROI.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost upon placement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While efficient, Glaciers provides zero long-term production. If you are in Generation 1 and have to choose between Glaciers and a card like *Acquired Company*, the long-term production is usually better unless you need the TR to hit a specific requirement.
- **Ocean Cap:** Monitor the ocean track. In high-player games, oceans can disappear quickly. Do not draft this late if only 1 ocean spot remains.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Blocking:** Use Glaciers to occupy high-value spots near an opponent's city to deny them the 2 MC adjacency bonus and to secure the most lucrative placement spots for yourself.
- **The "Order of Operations":** If you have *Arctic Algae*, ensure you gain the 4 plants *before* finishing your turn. This prevents opponents from using an Asteroid to destroy your newly gained resources before you can convert them.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for any strategy. High priority for **Rush** players or anyone holding *Arctic Algae*. Skip only if your engine is already over-optimized and you need card draw instead of TR.
