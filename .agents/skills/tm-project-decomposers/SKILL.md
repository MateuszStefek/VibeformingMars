# Project: Decomposers (#131)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 5 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** 3% Oxygen
- **Effect:** When you play a Plant, Microbe, or Animal tag (including this one), add 1 Microbe to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 3 Microbes on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Catalyst)
**Status: A-TIER SIDE-ENGINE / CORE BIO COMPONENT**

Decomposers is a high-efficiency passive point generator. While it requires a specialized deck to reach its full potential, its extremely low cost and self-triggering ability make it a staple for any player pursuing biological strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost-to-Points:** For only 5 MC (+3 to draft), it provides a point engine that typically yields 2-4 VP. This ~3 MC/VP ratio is far superior to standard game-end point cards.
2. **Self-Triggering:** The card triggers on its own Microbe tag, giving you 1 microbe immediately. You only need 2 more bio-tags (Plant, Microbe, or Animal) to hit your first 1 VP.
3. **The Bio-Trinity synergy:** It forms a devastating point engine when paired with **Ecological Zone** and **Advanced Ecosystems**. In this setup, every bio-tag played generates multiple resources and points across three different cards.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Provides an immediate microbe or plant when Decomposers is played.
   - **Enceladus (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for microbes, potentially adding 1 VP every 1-2 generations.
   - **Extreme-Cold Fungus:** Can be used to "feed" microbes into Decomposers every generation.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Bottleneck:** The 3% Oxygen requirement is low, but in slow games, it might not be met until Generation 3-4. Do not hold this card if your hand has zero other bio-tags.
- **Dilution in All-Expansions:** In games with all expansions enabled, the density of bio-tags in the deck is lower. You may find it harder to trigger Decomposers naturally than in Base-only games.
- **Late Game Decay:** Playing this in the final 2 generations is rarely worth the investment unless you already have a hand full of bio-tags ready to drop.

### Tips
- **Delayed Playing:** If you have 3 bio-tags in hand but Oxygen is at 2%, wait one turn for someone else to raise the Oxygen rather than "wasting" the tags before Decomposers is on the board.
- **Ants Counter:** Be aware that **Ants** can steal microbes from Decomposers. If an opponent has Ants, prioritize spending your bio-resources elsewhere or use Decomposers as a decoy.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you have even one other bio-tag. Essential for *Ecoline* or *Splice*. Skip only in dedicated "Rush" strategies with zero biological focus.
