# Project: Sponsors (#035)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Baseline)
**Status: B-TIER FOUNDATION / S-TIER SYNERGY**

Sponsors is the gold standard for early-game economy in *Terraforming Mars*. While it is a "simple" card, it serves as the benchmark for efficiency against which all other MC production cards are measured.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Mathematical Efficiency:** The card costs 6 MC (+3 to draft) for 2 MC production. This yields a 5-generation break-even point. If played in Generation 1-2, it is a guaranteed profit.
2. **Earth Tag Catalyst:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Sponsors is valued more for its tag than its production. It is a vital enabler for high-value cards like *Cartels* or *Earth Catapult*.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna:** The premier pairing. Triggers a card draw upon play, effectively making the card "free" in terms of card advantage.
   - **Teractor:** Reduces the cost to 0 MC (paying only the 3 MC draft cost), making it one of the strongest Gen 1 openers in the game.
   - **Earth Office:** Reduces the cost to 3 MC, significantly shortening the break-even period.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for cards with non-negative VP icons.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Payback Period:** If you are in Generation 6 of a 10-generation game, Sponsors will only return 8 MC for a 9 MC investment. **Do not play this after Generation 4-5** unless you strictly need the Earth tag for a milestone or synergy trigger.
- **Player Count Scaling:** In 4-5 player games where the game ends faster (Gen 7-8), the value of Sponsors drops significantly compared to 2-player games (Gen 10-12).

### Tips
- **The "Sponsors Rule":** Always use Sponsors to evaluate other production cards. If a card is less efficient than Sponsors (e.g., +1 production for 7 MC), it is generally considered unplayable unless it provides a critical tag or milestone boost.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" in any opening hand if you have Earth synergies or are playing a 2-player game. High priority in Generations 1-3. Skip in the late game.
