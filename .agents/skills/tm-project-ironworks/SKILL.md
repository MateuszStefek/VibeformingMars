# Project: Ironworks (#141)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 4 Energy to gain 1 Steel and raise Oxygen 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Oxygen Bumper)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Ironworks is a situational "industrial" card that converts surplus power into terraforming progress and building resources. While often considered bulky in multiplayer, its ability to move the oxygen track without relying on plants makes it a vital tool for certain strategies.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Oxygen Control:** Provides a reliable way to raise Oxygen that doesn't depend on board state or biological production. Use it to "sniping" oxygen thresholds (like the 8% temperature bonus) or to hasten the game's end to deny engine-builders more generations.
2. **Solo Mode Essential:** In Solo play, Ironworks is top-tier. Since you must max out all parameters to win, having an industrial way to "buy" Oxygen steps allows you to focus your early game on economy and temperature.
3. **Steel Sink / Generation:** The 1 Steel per generation adds up, especially if you have high-value building sinks like **Electro Catapult** or **Space Elevator**.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Building tag toward the **Builder** milestone and the TR from Oxygen contributes to the **Terraformer** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High Energy Overhead:** Requiring 4 energy *every generation* is a massive economic hit (~28 MC worth of production). If you don't have "free" energy from Preludes or high-tier science cards like *Mass Converter*, the opportunity cost of Ironworks is often too high.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** You must use the action at least **4 times** to see a net profit over Standard Projecting a greenery (which provides the TR + board presence + points).
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is at a premium for trading. Spending 4 energy on 1 TR and 1 steel is almost always inferior to trading with a colony for cards or titanium.

### Tips
- **Late Game Pivot:** Only play Ironworks if you have 4+ energy production and zero other use for it. In the final generations, if your trading fleets are done, Ironworks provides a way to squeeze out a final TR and 1-2 steel for final points.

**Recommendation:** A mid-to-low priority draft in multiplayer. High priority in **Solo Mode** or if you have a massive energy surplus (6+ production). Skip if playing with *Colonies* unless your energy engine is already over-producing.
