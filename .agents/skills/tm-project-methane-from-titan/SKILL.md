# Project: Methane from Titan (#018)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 28 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian, Space
- **Requirements:** 2% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 2 steps.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Dual Producer)
**Status: S-TIER IF EARLY / B-TIER GENERAL**

Methane from Titan is a powerful hybrid card that offers two distinct types of production and direct victory points. While its cost is high, its multi-faceted value makes it a top-tier engine piece if played early.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Diverse Production:** Getting both Heat and Plant production on a single card is rare and highly efficient for pushing multiple global parameters.
2. **Space Tag Utility:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. This makes it an ideal "dump" for corporations like **Phobolog** to jumpstart their terraforming engine.
3. **Jovian Tag synergy:** Provides a Jovian tag, which is essential for late-game point multipliers and milestones like *Rim Settler*.
4. **Immediate VP:** The flat 2 VP reduces the card's effective cost, as you are buying both production and end-game points simultaneously.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Inventrix:** Allows you to play the card immediately (ignoring the 2% Oxygen requirement), maximizing the number of generations you receive the production.
   - **Saturn Systems:** The Jovian tag provides an immediate income boost on top of the card's effects.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Makes paying for this 28 MC card with Titanium significantly easier.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Bottleneck:** If you don't have Preludes or cards to raise Oxygen early, Methane from Titan can sit in your hand for several generations, losing its production value.
- **Internal Anti-Synergy:** The card pushes the game end (via terraforming) but also provides a Jovian tag (which usually wants a long game). If you play this, commit to the terraforming rush to maximize the production value.
- **Late Game Inefficiency:** Playing this after Generation 5-6 is generally poor unless you strictly need the Jovian tag or the 2 VP to win.

### Tips
- **Titanium Conversion:** View this card as the best way to "convert" Titanium into plant production. Most other plant cards require liquid MC; Methane from Titan lets you build a greenery engine using your space-based economy.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" if you have any path to 2% Oxygen and high Titanium production or a strong early-game budget. Priority 1 for any Jovian-heavy strategy. Best played in Generations 1-3.
