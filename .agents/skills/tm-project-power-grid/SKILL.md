# Project: Power Grid (#027)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** Power
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step for each Power tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Energy Scaler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN ENERGY-HEAVY ENGINES**

Power Grid is a high-ceiling production card that scales directly with your industrial infrastructure. While expensive, its ability to provide a massive jump in energy in a single action makes it a premier tool for unlocking high-tier energy-dependent engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** It provides +1 Energy production for *every* Power tag you own. In a dedicated build (using cards like *Solar Power*, *Wave Power*, or *Geothermal Power*), this card can easily provide 5-10+ energy production, far exceeding the efficiency of any other power card.
2. **Instant "Physics Complex" Starter:** Perfect for immediately "turning on" high-requirement energy cards like **Physics Complex** (6 energy), **Strip Mine**, or **Water Splitting Plant** in a single generation.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Power Infrastructure:** The "Ultimate Industrial" combo. Convert your massive energy yield from Power Grid directly into MC every generation to fund your late-game projects.
   - **Thorgate:** Benefits from the corporation's Power tag discount and their natural tendency to accumulate Power tags.
   - **Colonies Expansion:** Provides the massive energy required to trade with multiple high-value colonies (like *Luna* or *Ceres*) simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Upfront Cost:** 21 MC (cost + draft) is a heavy investment. If you only have 1 or 2 Power tags, this card is statistically worse than simply using Standard Project Power Plants. Only play it once you have at least 3 relevant tags.
- **Energy Waste:** If you don't have a specific "sink" for the energy (like card draw, trading, or points), the surplus energy will simply convert to heat, which may end the game faster than your engine can support.
- **No Building Tag:** Unlike many other power cards, Power Grid **cannot** be paid for with Steel. This makes it a significant drain on your liquid MC budget.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Power tag on Power Grid to hit the requirement for **Power Supply Consortium** or other tag-based cards. The tag is often as valuable as the production in tight milestone races.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 4-6). Essential for anyone building a science or energy engine. Skip early if you have zero Power tags or if you need to prioritize income production.
