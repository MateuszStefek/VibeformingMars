# Project: Research (#027)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Draw 2 cards.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Catalyst)
**Status: S-TIER ENABLER / HIGH VELOCITY ENGINE**

Research is one of the most critical engine-building cards in the game. While it provides immediate card draw and points, its true power lies in its unique **double Science tags**, which act as a "fast-track" for the game's most powerful requirements.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Requirement Fulfillment:** Research provides 2 Science tags for only 11 MC (+3 to draft). This makes it the single most efficient way to hit the 3-tag threshold for **AI Central** or the steep 7-tag requirement for **Antigravity Technology**.
2. **Card Velocity:** Drawing 2 cards helps you dig for win conditions, while the 1 VP reduces the card's effective cost.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University:** A legendary combo. Pair Research with Mars University to discard the cards you just drew for even more draws, potentially seeing 4-5 cards in one action.
   - **Olympus Conference:** Triggers the Olympus draw engine multiple times.
   - **Scientist Award:** Provides a massive boost toward securing the Scientist milestone or award.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Efficiency:** In isolation (without Science synergies), paying 14 MC for 2 cards and 1 VP is statistically poor. If you aren't building a Science engine or racing for a requirement, *Technology Demonstration* is better.
- **Opportunity Cost:** Do not spend your last 14 MC on Research in Generation 1 if it prevents you from building basic MC production. It is a "velocity" card; it works best when you already have the income to play the cards you draw.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent is sitting on 1-2 Science tags, you **must** cut Research from the draft. Allowing them to land 2 tags in one action can jumpstart their AI Central engine and end the game in their favor.
- **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, Research triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate, making it an incredibly efficient play.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for any engine-building strategy. High priority for early-to-mid game play. Priority 1 for anyone pursuing *AI Central*.
