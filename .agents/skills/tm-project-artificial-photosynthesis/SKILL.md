# Project: Artificial Photosynthesis (#115)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase either your Plant production 1 step or your Energy production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Bridge)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER ENGINE ENABLER**

Artificial Photosynthesis is a high-utility hybrid card that serves as a premier tool for "fixing" your engine. While statistically average, its combination of a requirement-free Science tag and flexible production makes it a staple for established science strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Energy" Rule:** In the 2024-2025 meta, you should **almost always choose the 2 Energy production**. For only 12 MC (+3 draft), you are receiving energy at the benchmark 7.5 MC/step ratio, while also gaining a high-value tag.
2. **Science Tag Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for unlocking high-tier draw engines like **AI Central** (which also requires the 1 energy this card provides) or **Mars University**.
3. **Colonies Synergy:** In games with *Colonies*, the 2 energy production is exactly what is needed to power a trade ship. Playing this in Generation 1-2 allows you to start trading immediately without relying on Standard Projects.
4. **Late-Game Flexibility:** In the final generation, if you are exactly 1 plant production away from reaching 8 plants for a greenery, you can pivot to the plant option to secure a 2-VP swing.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Plant Option Inefficiency:** Choosing 1 plant production for 15 MC is statistically poor value. Only choose this if it immediately yields a greenery or if your engine is already drowning in energy.
- **No Building Tag:** Unlike *Geothermal Power*, this card **cannot** be paid for with Steel. This makes it a significant drain on your liquid MC budget in the early game.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Artificial Photosynthesis specifically to hit the 3-science requirement for **AI Central**. The ability to gain the tag and the energy to power the AI in a single card is one of the most efficient "rollouts" in the game.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you are pursuing a Science strategy or playing with *Colonies*. Skip early if you have zero science potential and need to prioritize pure income production.
