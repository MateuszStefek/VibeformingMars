# Project: Lake Marineris (#043)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** Min 0°C
- **Effect:** Place 2 Ocean tiles.
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Point Slam)
**Status: A-TIER PAYOFF / SITUATIONAL "CLEAN-UP" CARD**

Lake Marineris is one of the most point-efficient cards in the game, providing a massive swing in TR, VP, and board presence for a relatively low cost. While its high temperature requirement makes it a late-game card, its sheer efficiency makes it a "must-draft" in the final stages of the match.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Point Density:** For 18 MC (+3 to draft), you gain **4 total VP** (2 base + 2 TR from oceans) plus placement bonuses. This 5.25 MC/VP ratio is elite, making it one of the strongest "point-slams" available for non-engine players.
2. **Double Placement Bonus:** Placing two oceans simultaneously allows you to claim high-value bonuses (e.g., 2 cards or 2 titanium) in one action, often providing the resources needed for one last game-ending play.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The "God Tier" combo. Net 4 plants immediately, which often secures one final greenery conversion.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides a significant boost to MC production if played before the final generation.
   - **Elysium Map:** Essential for securing the *Estate Dealer* award (tiles next to oceans).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Ocean Race Risk:** In many competitive games, oceans are maxed out *before* the temperature reaches 0°C. If 8 or 9 oceans are already on the board, the card's efficiency drops significantly. Discard if you see oceans filling up too quickly.
- **Timing:** **Never keep this in your opening hand** unless you have **Inventrix** or **Adaptation Technology**. Tying up 3 MC for a card you cannot play until the final round is an opportunity cost that will stall your engine.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Strategic players often use **Inventrix** specifically to drop Lake Marineris at -4°C. This allows you to snag the final high-value ocean spots before your opponents can reach 0°C.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-9). High priority if oceans are still available and temperature is rising. Essential for anyone holding *Arctic Algae*. Skip early to preserve engine capital.
