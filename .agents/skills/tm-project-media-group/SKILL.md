# Project: Media Group (#109)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play an Event (Red card), you gain 3 MC.


## Strategic Analysis (The Event Engine)
**Status: S-TIER / ESSENTIAL REBATE ENGINE**

Media Group is one of the most efficient "liquidity" cards in the game. It transforms the one-time impact of red cards into a sustainable economic engine by providing immediate cash rebates.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Incredibly Low Break-Even:** At a total investment of 9 MC (cost + draft), it only takes **3 events** to break even. Given that events make up nearly 20% of the deck, this is a very easy threshold to cross.
2. **Immediate Liquidity:** Unlike production cards that pay out at the end of the generation, Media Group provides cash *during* your turn, allowing you to chain multiple cards together in a single generation.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Interplanetary Cinematics (IC):** The premier pairing. Combined with IC's innate rebate, you get 5 MC back for every event.
   - **Optimal Aerobraking:** The "Ultimate Cashback" combo. Space events provide 6 MC and 3 heat, often making them effectively free or even profitable.
   - **Earth Engine:** As an Earth tag, it triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and benefits from *Earth Office* or *Teractor* discounts.
   - **Cheap Events:** Cards like *CEO's Favorite Project*, *Land Claim*, or *Sabotage* become net-positive or free tools for disruption and terraforming.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Timing:** Generally, a mid-game draft of Media Group is fine if you have 3+ events in hand. However, do not prioritize it in the final generation unless you are about to dump a massive hand of events.
- **Stealth Draft:** In high-level play, if you reveal Media Group too early, opponents will actively hate-draft red cards to starve your engine. Consider holding it until you have a few events ready to play in sequence.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** In a tight race for the *Terraformer* or *Gardener* milestones, Media Group can provide the 3-9 MC "swing" needed to afford that one extra standard project or card that secures the win.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in Generations 1-5. Provides excellent economic flexibility for any strategy that utilizes event cards (Space or Board-heavy).
