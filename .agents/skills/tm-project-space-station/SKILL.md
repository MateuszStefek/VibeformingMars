# Project: Space Station (#025)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** When you play a Space card, you pay 2 MC less for it.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Space Engine Anchor)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / HIGH-VALUE UTILITY**

Space Station is one of the most efficient discount cards in the game. While its upfront cost is moderate, the permanent -2 MC discount on all future Space cards provides a massive cumulative advantage for any player specializing in industrial or Jovian strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional ROI:** For a total investment of 13 MC (+3 to draft), it pays for itself after playing just **5 subsequent Space tags** (saving 10 MC, with the 1 VP accounting for the remaining cost). Given that many Space cards are events or point-scoring finishers, this is a very easy threshold to cross.
2. **Titanium Stretching:** Since Space cards can be paid for with Titanium, the 2 MC discount effectively "stretches" your accumulated metal further, allowing you to fund more projects without draining your liquid MC.
3. **Requirement Enabler:** Provides a requirement-free Space tag, which is essential for hitting thresholds for high-tier cards like **Quantum Extractor** or **Mass Converter**.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking / Media Group:** Allows you to play Space Events for a massive net gain, potentially making high-impact cards like *Giant Ice Asteroid* free.
   - **Research Outpost:** Together, they provide a -3 MC discount on Space cards, making even the smallest space projects profitable.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Late Game irrelevance:** Drafting Space Station after Generation 7 is usually a mistake unless you have at least 3 high-cost Space cards ready to play. The 13 MC cost is better spent on direct terraforming in the final rounds.
- **Speculation Risk:** **Do not hold this early** if you have zero other Space cards and zero Titanium production. Only keep it if your hand or corporation (e.g., *Phobolog*, *Point Luna*) naturally supports a space strategy.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent has high Titanium production and a card-draw engine, you **must** cut Space Station. Allowing a space specialist to land this discount is often the catalyst for a game-winning point-slam in the final generation.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-4 for any strategy. Essential for **Phobolog** or **Point Luna**. Skip late unless you strictly need the tag or the 1 VP.
