# Project: AI Central (#001)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 21 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building
- **Requirements:** 3 Science Tags
- **Action:** Spend 1 Energy to draw 2 cards.
- **VP:** 0


## Strategic Analysis (The Engine King)
**Status: S-TIER / PREMIER DRAW ENGINE**

AI Central is the gold standard for card draw in *Terraforming Mars*. While its requirements are steep, its ability to provide 2 cards every generation is a game-defining advantage.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Massive Volume:** Drawing 2 extra cards per generation allows you to find synergies, Jovian multipliers, and late-game point scorers at twice the speed of your opponents.
2. **Action Efficiency:** 1 Energy for 2 cards is the best repeatable draw ratio in the game.
3. **Building Tag:** Can be paid for with Steel, making it easier to drop once the 3 Science requirements are met.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University:** The raw volume from AI Central provides the "fuel" for Mars University to filter into perfection.
   - **Science-Heavy Engine:** Naturally fits into builds targeting *Mass Converter* or *Antigravity Technology*.
   - **Thorgate:** The energy requirement is easily met by their starting production.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Economy First:** Do not rush AI Central if you cannot afford to play the cards you draw. Spending ~30+ MC (on science tags and energy) just to play AI Central in Generation 1 can stall your income growth to a fatal degree.
- **Science Keys:** Only play "low-value" science cards (like *Search for Life*) to unlock AI Central if you have a clear plan to utilize the draw immediately.
- **Late Game:** If drawn after Generation 7 (in a 10-gen game), calculate if the ~24 MC investment will draw enough high-VP cards to be worth the cost.

### Tips
- **Hate Drafting:** If an opponent is sitting on 2 Science tags and has energy production, **do not pass them AI Central.** In 2-player or 3-player games, letting one person run the AI Central engine uncontested often leads to their victory.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Keep" if you have a path to 3 Science tags and energy. Priority investment for the mid-game (Generations 3-5).
