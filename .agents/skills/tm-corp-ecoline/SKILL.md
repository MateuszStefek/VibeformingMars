# Corporation: Ecoline

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Starting MC:** 36
- **Starting Plants:** 2
- **Plant Production:** 3
- **Ability:** You only need 7 plants to place a greenery tile.

## Strategic Analysis (The Oxygen Engine)
**Status: A-TIER (Competitive) / S-TIER (Casual)**

Ecoline is the undisputed master of the board and the Oxygen track. Its ability to churn out greeneries faster and cheaper than any other corporation makes it a massive threat for board control and TR rushing.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Oxygen Rushing:** Ecoline can reliably push the Oxygen track to its limit by Generation 7-9, forcing a faster game that leaves engine-builders in the dust.
2. **Efficiency:** Saving 1 plant per greenery is more powerful than it looks, especially when combined with high plant production.
3. **Board Control:** Early greeneries secure placement bonuses (especially Oceans) and block opponents' city spots.

### Synergies:
- **Ecology Experts (Prelude):** Allows cheating out 'Kelp Farming' or 'Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid' in Gen 1, which is often game-ending for Ecoline.
- **Protected Habitats:** MANDATORY defense. Ecoline is the primary target for asteroid attacks. This card protects your 7-plant investment.
- **Arctic Algae:** Grants plants whenever an ocean is placed, further fueling the greenery engine.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
King of 'Gardener'. High value on the 2-plant placement spots.
### Hellas
South Pole heat can be a distraction; stay focused on the 'Cultivator' award.
### Elysium
The 'Ecologist' milestone is a must-have. Use the 3-plant spot to jumpstart your engine.

### Tips
- **Avoid Over-Specialization:** Don't *only* build greeneries. You need cities to capitalize on the VP from your plants.
- **Milestone Race:** Easy path to 'Gardener'.

**Recommendation:** A high-skill corporation that requires careful board placement and defense. High priority if you have plant-boosting cards or 'Protected Habitats' in your starting hand.
