# Corporation: Septem Tribus

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Turmoil
- **Starting MC:** 36
- **Tags:** Wild
- **Action:** Gain 2 MC for each party where you have at least 1 delegate.
- **Effect:** The Wild tag counts as any tag of your choice when performing an action (e.g., playing a card with a requirement).

## Strategic Analysis (The Political Mastermind)
**Status: S-TIER / ELITE**

Septem Tribus is the king of the Turmoil expansion. Its ability to generate massive income through political presence, combined with the flexibility of a Wild tag, makes it one of the most strategically deep corporations in the game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **"I Am the Senate":** Having a delegate in all 6 parties yields 12 MC per generation via the corp action. This is one of the highest-yielding actions in the game.
2. **Requirement Breaking:** The Wild tag allows Septem Tribus to play high-requirement cards (*AI Central*, *Quantum Extractor*) much earlier by pretending to have the necessary tags.
3. **Turmoil Dominance:** By design, Septem Tribus is always involved in the political phase, allowing it to reap the benefits of party bonuses and leadership more frequently.

### Synergies:
- **Viron (via Merger):** Doubling the Septem Tribus action to gain 24 MC per turn is effectively a game-over state.
- **Delegates:** Cards that provide free delegates or allow for extra placement are high priority.
- **High-Requirement Cards:** *AI Central* (needs 3 Science) becomes much easier to reach.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Spread Thin:** Early on, prioritize getting at least 1 delegate in every party rather than trying to lead one party. The 12 MC income is your primary engine.
- **Wild Tag Timing:** Remember that the Wild tag only counts during your turn. It doesn't help with passive effects or milestones unless specified.

**Recommendation:** An "Auto-Pick" if you are playing with Turmoil. One of the highest ceilings in the game.
