# Project: Insects (#064)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** 6% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your plant production 1 step for each Plant tag you have (including this one).


## Strategic Analysis (The Plant Multiplier)
**Status: S-TIER / BEST PLANT PRODUCTION SCALER**

Insects is the single most cost-efficient way to generate high plant production in the mid-to-late game. Its power scales directly with your biological engine.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unbeatable Scaling:** For only 9 MC, you gain production that typically ranges from 3-6 plants per generation. This is far superior to standard plant production cards like *Mohole Area* or *Heather*.
2. **Draft Priority:** Never pass Insects. If you see it in a draft, you either take it to fuel your own engine or "hate-draft" it to prevent an opponent from running away with the plant game.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Wild Tags:** If you have wild tags (e.g., from *Research* or certain Preludes), you can set them to Plant tags when playing Insects to maximize the gain.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Triggers off the Microbe tag and provides immediate plant resources.
   - **Decomposers:** The Microbe tag on Insects helps build the point-scoring engine on Decomposers.
   - **Ecological Zone:** Triggers the animal gain on Ecological Zone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Requirement:** The 6% Oxygen requirement means you cannot play it in the very early game. However, you should still **draft it early** and hold it.
- **Predator Awareness:** Once you play Insects and your plant production spikes, opponents will prioritize playing predators like *Birds* or *Ants* against you. Ensure you have a plan to convert plants to greeneries immediately.

### Tips
- **Insects vs. NRA:** Compared to *Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)*, Insects is more efficient for pure production. Use the MC saved (22 MC difference) to buy more plant tags to further boost the Insects trigger.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Hold" from Generation 1. Prioritize playing it as soon as the Oxygen threshold is met and you have at least 2 other plant tags.
