# Project: Asteroid (#009)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 14 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 1 step. Gain 2 Titanium. Remove up to 3 plants from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Converter)
**Status: B-TIER / SOLID TACTICAL EVENT**

Asteroid is a foundational space event that provides a balanced mix of terraforming, resource gain, and minor disruption. While not as game-breaking as its larger counterparts, its efficiency makes it a steady performer.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Resource Rebate:** The 2 Titanium (worth 6 MC) reduces the effective cost of the card to 8 MC (+3 to draft). For 11 MC total, you gain 1 TR (from temperature) and a tactical advantage.
2. **Plant Denial:** Removing 3 plants is often enough to delay an opponent's greenery by a generation, especially in the early-to-mid game when plant production is still ramping up.
3. **Space Tag Utility:** Can be paid for with Titanium, making it a low-cost way to cycle through your space-card hand or spend leftover resources.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Optimal Aerobraking:** Provides 3 MC and 3 Heat, making the card effectively cost-neutral or even profitable.
   - **Media Group:** Provides a 3 MC rebate.
   - **Phobolog:** Excellent for Phobolog to maintain temperature momentum without spending liquid MC.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Inefficient Point Scoring:** If played purely for the TR, it is less efficient than larger asteroids or engine cards. Use it when the plant removal or the titanium rebate provides a clear tactical benefit.
- **Timing:** Most effective in Generations 2-5 when opponents are just starting their biological engines.

### Tips
- **Bonus Sniping:** Use the 1-step temperature jump to secure a threshold bonus (like the +2°C heat production step) before an opponent's turn. 
- **The "3 Plant" Rule:** If an opponent has 7 plants, playing *Asteroid* to drop them to 4 is a high-value move that forces them to wait 2+ generations for a greenery.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft. Always worth keeping if you have titanium production or need to stall an opponent's plant engine. High priority if *Optimal Aerobraking* is in play.
