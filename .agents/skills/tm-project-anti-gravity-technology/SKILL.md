# Project: Anti-Gravity Technology (#153)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 14 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** 7 Science Tags.
- **Effect:** When you play a card, you pay 2 MC less for it.
- **VP:** 3


## Strategic Analysis (The Ultimate Engine Capstone)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / THE "WIN-MORE" KING**

Anti-Gravity Technology (AGT) is widely considered the strongest economic card in the late game. While its 7-science requirement is the highest in the deck, the universal -2 MC discount and high victory point yield make it a game-defining advantage for science specialists.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Universal Discount:** Unlike *Earth Office* or *Space Station*, AGT discounts **every single card** you play. In a typical engine game where you play 20+ cards after AGT, it provides a massive cumulative savings of 40+ MC.
2. **High Point Density:** 3 VP for 14 MC (+3 to draft) is a 5.6 MC/VP ratio, which is elite even without the discount. It is one of the most efficient "point-slams" in the science category.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University / Olympus Conference:** These engines help you reach the 7-tag threshold while providing the card draw needed to utilize the AGT discount.
   - **Earth Catapult / Research Outpost:** Stack the discounts. With AGT and Earth Catapult, every card you play is **4 MC cheaper**, making many low-cost cards effectively free.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon.
   - **Scientist Award:** Almost guarantees victory in the Scientist award/milestone due to the tags and points.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Obsession:** **Do not "fiend" for science tags** just to enable AGT. If you are playing "junk" science cards (like *Search for Life* or *Hackers*) and stalling your economy for 6 generations, the AGT discount won't arrive early enough to recoup the losses.
- **2-Player Dominance:** Its value is highest in 1v1 games that last 10-12 generations. In high-player count "Rush" games (ending in Gen 7-8), you may never hit the 7-tag requirement, or hit it too late to trigger the discount more than 2-3 times.
- **Hate-Drafting:** Even if you cannot play it, **never pass AGT** to a player with 3-4 science tags. Allowing a science leader to land AGT is often equivalent to handing them the game.

### Tips
- **Late-Game Point Dump:** If you hit the 7-tag requirement in the final generation, AGT is still a fantastic 3 VP for 14 MC. Don't ignore it just because you won't use the discount.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Must-Draft" for any engine-building or science-heavy strategy. Priority 1 for anyone holding *AI Central*. Skip early only if your hand has zero science potential and you are pursuing a pure "Rush" strategy.
