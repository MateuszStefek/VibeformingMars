# Prelude: Biolab

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step. Draw 3 cards.
- **Tag:** Science.

## Strategic Analysis (The Science Dig)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / SITUATIONAL "PANIC" DRAW**

Biolab is a low-tier Prelude used primarily to fix a weak starting hand or to accelerate a specialized science engine. While it provides a diverse set of benefits, the randomness of the draw and the slow pace of single-step plant production make it a rare pick in competitive play.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **The "Panic Button":** If your initial 10 cards are unplayable, Biolab allows you to "dig" 3 cards deeper into the deck in Generation 1, potentially finding the engine-starters or requirements you need to survive.
2. **Science Tag Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for unlocking high-tier draw engines like **AI Central** (3 tags) or **Mars University** on the very first turn.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **AI Central:** The premier synergy. Biolab provides both a tag and extra cards to help find the other tags needed for the AI.
   - **Electro Catapult:** The +1 Plant production provides consistent "fuel" for the Catapult's lucrative 7 MC action.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a potential VP icon (if drawn), Biolab fits into a hand-heavy strategy.
4. **Solo Mode MVP:** Significantly stronger in Solo play, where card advantage is vital and there is no "plant hate" to destroy your early production.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High RNG Risk:** Drawing 3 cards is a gamble. If you draw unplayable high-cost cards, Biolab is statistically worse than a pure income Prelude like *Allied Bank*.
- **Slow Production:** 1 plant production takes roughly 6 generations to produce a single greenery. This is too slow to impact most high-level games unless supplemented by other cards.
- **Plant Vulnerability:** Having exactly 1 plant production often makes you a target for early-game asteroids, potentially losing your resources before you can ever use them.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use the Science tag on Biolab specifically to reach the 3-tag threshold for **AI Central**. Most opponents do not expect a player to hit the requirement in Gen 1; Biolab allows you to drop the AI immediately, which is often a game-winning advantage.

**Recommendation:** Generally a "Desperation" pick. Only take if your starting hand is terrible or if you are 100% committed to an *AI Central* rush. Skip in favor of guaranteed income whenever possible. Excellent in Solo mode.
