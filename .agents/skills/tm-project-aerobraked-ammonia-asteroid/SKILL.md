# Project: Aerobraked Ammonia Asteroid (#063)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 26 MC
- **Tags:** Space, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 3 steps. Increase your Plant production 1 step. Add 2 Microbes to any card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Surge)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / SITUATIONAL RUSH TOOL**

Aerobraked Ammonia Asteroid (AAA) is a high-cost hybrid card that provides a diverse set of production and resource boosts. While its raw efficiency is only average, its role as a "space event" makes it a potent tool for players with established resource rebates and space-heavy economies.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Diverse Production Yield:** +3 Heat and +1 Plant production is worth ~29 MC of value. At a total investment of 29 MC (cost + draft), the card is "fairly priced" but requires high-value synergies to move from average to elite.
2. **Microbe Catalyst:** The 2 microbes are the "hidden" value. Use them on **Sulphur-Eating Bacteria** (worth 6-9 MC), **Psychrophiles** (worth 4 MC toward plants), or **Regolith Eaters** to bypass the slow natural accumulation.
3. **Space & Event Synergy:** 
   - **Optimal Aerobraking / Media Group:** The absolute best case. These rebates can reduce the net cost to **~17 MC**, making the yield exceptionally efficient.
   - **Titanium Sink:** Can be paid for with Titanium, allowing corporations like **Phobolog** to convert metals into a balanced terraforming and biological engine.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Space and Event tag, contributing toward the **Space Baron** and **Legend** milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Gen 1 Inefficiency:** To get the full value of the microbes, you **must** have another microbe card already on the board. Playing AAA in Generation 1 without a microbe Prelude or card wastes 6-9 MC worth of resources.
- **Payback Period:** Since it provides zero immediate TR, it takes 4-5 generations of production to break even on the investment. Do not play this after Generation 5.
- **Opportunistic Only:** **Never draft this early** unless you already hold *Optimal Aerobraking* or a high-value microbe card. The 26 MC cost is too large a hit to your tempo without guaranteed rebates.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the microbes to hit the 3-microbe threshold for **Nitrite Reducing Bacteria** or other high-tier requirements a generation early, catching opponents off guard.

**Recommendation:** A mid-to-low priority draft in Generations 1-3. High priority for **Phobolog** or anyone holding **Optimal Aerobraking**. Skip late or if you lack a primary microbe sink.
