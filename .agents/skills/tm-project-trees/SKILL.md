# Project: Trees (#060)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -4°C
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 3 steps. Gain 1 Plant.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Closer)
**Status: S-TIER PLANT PRODUCTION / A-TIER PAYOFF**

Trees is widely considered the best late-game plant production card. It provides a massive jump in resources and a guaranteed point for a very low cost, making it the premier tool for finishing biological engines and securing board dominance.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Production Density:** +3 Plant production for only 13 MC (+3 to draft) is an incredible ratio (~5.3 MC per step). In the final generations, this ensures you are placing a greenery every 2-3 turns without needing any other production.
2. **The "Ecology Experts" Dream:** Pairing Trees with the **Ecology Experts** Prelude allows you to play it in Generation 1, ignoring the temperature requirement. This is arguably the strongest plant-based opening in the game, providing a massive production lead from the very start.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Ecoline:** The premier pairing. Combined with Ecoline's reduced greenery cost, Trees provides enough volume to place a forest nearly every other turn.
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides the Plant tag needed to trigger multiple resource/VP gains for a very low cost.
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate 3-plant gain upon increasing production, effectively making the card "Gain 4 plants" on the turn it is played.
4. **Plant Protection:** The 1 instant plant often provides the exact amount needed to complete a greenery conversion in the same action, "saving" your plants from an opponent's *Asteroid* attack.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -4°C requirement is steep. In fast-paced games, the oxygen track might already be maxed by the time temperature hits -4°C. In this case, Trees is still worth playing for the raw VP and greenery points, but you lose the 1 TR from the oxygen increase.
- **Speculation Risk:** **Never hold this from Generation 1** unless you have a path to rapid heat or the *Ecology Experts* Prelude. Tying up 3 MC for a card you cannot play for 6-8 generations is a major economic drag.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Trees at -8°C. This allows you to establishment your final greenery chain a full generation early, which is often enough to secure the **Gardener** milestone.

**Recommendation:** A top-priority draft in the mid-to-late game (Generations 5-8). High priority for **Ecoline**, **Manutech**, or anyone Pursuing the **Gardener** milestone. Skip only in the final 2 generations if you already have 5+ plant production.
