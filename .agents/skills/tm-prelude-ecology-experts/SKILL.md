# Prelude: Ecology Experts

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Play one card from your hand, ignoring global requirements. (You still pay the card's MC cost).
- **Tags:** Plant, Microbe.

## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Cheater)
**Status: S-TIER / THE ULTIMATE "HIGH-ROLL" CARD**

Ecology Experts is widely considered one of the most powerful and swingy Preludes in the game. It allows you to "cheat out" game-defining cards that are normally locked behind high oxygen, temperature, or ocean requirements, potentially establishing a winning engine on the very first turn.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **The "Kelp Farming" God-Tier Combo:** The most powerful use case. Playing **Kelp Farming** (+3 Plant, +2 MC prod) or **Farming** in Generation 1 effectively doubles your early-game engine power and outscales all opponents immediately.
2. **Point Scaling with Animals:** If you have **Birds**, **Fish**, or **Penguins**, you can start accumulating 1:1 VP from the very first generation, potentially netting an extra 5-8+ VP by the end of the game compared to a normal rollout.
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both a **Plant** and a **Microbe** tag in Generation 1.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** These two tags satisfy 2/3 of the requirement for **Advanced Ecosystems** or contribute significantly toward **Insects**.
   - **Milestone Support:** Contributes 2 steps toward the **Ecologist** milestone (4 bio tags), making it an easy win on the Elysium map.
4. **Miranda (Colonies) Combo:** If trading with Miranda is possible, playing a 1:1 VP animal card in Gen 1 allows for an overwhelming "runaway leader" scenario.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Cost Discount:** You **must still pay the MC cost** of the card. If you play a 23 MC card like *Kelp Farming*, you may start the game with zero liquid cash for other projects.
- **Predator Magnet:** Cheating out high plant production or animals early makes you the #1 target for **Asteroids** and **Virus**. Ensure you have **Protected Habitats** or can convert your resources immediately.
- **Whiff Risk:** If your starting hand does not contain a high-value card with global requirements, Ecology Experts is a "dead" Prelude slot. Only take if you have a specific 5-point+ or 3-production+ target.

### Tips
- **Stealth Point-Slam:** Use Ecology Experts on **Trees** (-4°C requirement). It is cheaper than *Kelp Farming*, providing a more balanced start while still establishing a dominant +3 plant production engine in Gen 1.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" if you have a high-value bio-engine card in hand. Priority 1 for **Ecoline** or **Vitor**. Skip only if your hand lacks a requirement-locked target.
