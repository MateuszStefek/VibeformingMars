# Project: Adapted Lichen (#048)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Enabler)
**Status: C-TIER PRODUCTION / B-TIER SYNERGY**

Adapted Lichen is a foundational plant card that is often misunderstood. While its raw efficiency is low, its role as a requirement-free "Plant tag" makes it a valuable utility card for biological strategies.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **No Requirements:** It is one of the few plant production cards that can be played in Generation 1, regardless of temperature or oxygen. This allows you to start your biological engine before anyone else.
2. **Tag Efficiency:** For 9 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a Plant tag. This is the primary reason to play the card, as it helps hit the 3-tag requirement for powerhouse cards like **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or the scaling requirement for **Insects**.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Triggers their immediate resource gains or point engines upon play.
   - **Ecologist Award:** A reliable, cheap way to add to your plant tag count.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Poor ROI:** Paying 12 MC for 1 plant production is statistically inefficient. Over a 10-generation game, it will only produce 10 plants—barely more than one greenery.
- **Lichen Comparison:** Compared to standard *Lichen* (-24°C), Adapted Lichen costs 2 MC more for the exact same effect. Since -24°C is usually reached very early, the "Adapted" version is rarely worth the extra cost.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** 1 plant production makes you a target for plant-destruction cards (*Birds*, *Virus*) without providing enough volume to quickly convert to a greenery and protect your TR.

### Tips
- **Hold for Synergy:** Do not play this in Generation 1 just because you can. Hold it in your hand until you have **NRA** or **Insects** ready. Using Adapted Lichen as a "primer" tag right before playing a high-impact plant card is its most effective use.

**Recommendation:** A mid-to-low priority draft. Only keep if you are already holding high-tier plant synergy cards. Skip if you have zero bio-engine plans.
