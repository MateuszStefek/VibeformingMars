# Map: Elysium

## Key Features & Placement
- **Olympus Mons:** Located in the northwest, grants a **3-card draw** placement bonus. Usually claimed by Gen 2.
- **Northern Ocean Cluster:** All 9 ocean spots are in the north. This creates extreme competition for adjacency.
- **Southern Highlands:** High concentration of Plant and Steel bonuses.

## Milestones
- **Generalist:** 1 production in all 6 resources. Hardest to get but rewards balanced starts.
- **Specialist:** 10 production in any one resource. Helion/ThorGate/Mining Guild territory.
- **Ecologist:** 4 biological tags. Ecoline/Bio-engine focus.
- **Tycoon:** 15 project cards (blue/green) in play. Favors low-cost engines.
- **Legend:** 5 event cards (red) played. The fastest milestone; increases the value of every cheap event.

## Awards
- **Celebrity:** Most non-event cards in play with cost 20+ MC. Teractor/Vitor power-up.
- **Industrialist:** Most Steel and Energy resources. save your resources in the final gen!
- **Desert Settler:** Most tiles in the bottom 4 rows. Pivot south if the north is too crowded.
- **Estate Dealer:** Most tiles adjacent to ocean tiles. The "Ocean Walker's" award.
- **Benefactor:** Highest TR. TR-rush viability.

## Strategy Tips (Community Consensus)
- **Tharsis Republic Nerf:** Tharsis is much weaker here without Mayor/Gardener. Avoid unless you have heavy city synergy cards.
- **Event Cards:** Cheap events (like *Media Group* or *Business Contacts*) are much more valuable because they count toward **Legend**.
- **Energy Point Density:** Energy production helps with **Specialist**, and Energy resources help with **Industrialist**. This makes energy cards higher priority than usual.
- **The Olympus Mons Rush:** If you can land on Olympus Mons early, the 3 cards can jumpstart your entire game.
