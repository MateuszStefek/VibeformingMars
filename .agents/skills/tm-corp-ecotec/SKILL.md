# Corporation: Ecotec

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude 2
- **Starting MC:** 42
- **Starting Production:** +1 Plant production
- **Tags:** Plant, Microbe
- **Ability (Effect):** When you play a Plant, Microbe, or Animal tag (including those on this card), gain 1 plant OR add 1 microbe to any card.

## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Specialist)
**Status: MID TIER**

Ecotec is a "Viral Enhancers" on a corporation. It provides immediate value for any biological strategy, converting card plays into raw resources or engine components.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Built-in Viral Enhancers:** Having this ability from Gen 1 is massive for plant-heavy or microbe-heavy strategies.
2. **Flexible Bio-Support:** You can choose between raw plants for terraforming or microbes for card-based engines.
3. **Double Tags:** Starting with both Plant and Microbe tags helps satisfy requirements early.

### Synergies:
- **Decomposers / Ecological Zone:** Triggers multiple times per card play when combined with Ecotec.
- **Nitrite Reducing Bacteria / Ghg Producing Bacteria:** Ecotec can fuel these microbe-based engines instantly.
- **Ecoline (Comparative):** While Ecoline is better for raw greenery spam, Ecotec is better for a balanced engine that uses both plants and microbes.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Plant Pacing:** Use the extra plants to hit greenery thresholds before opponents can burn them.
- **Microbe Pivot:** If you find a strong microbe VP card (like 'Ants' or 'Nitrite Reducing Bacteria'), Ecotec becomes a VP machine.

**Recommendation:** Mid tier. Requires a decent biological starting hand to outshine more generic economic corporations. Best in high-player count games where biology is less contested.
