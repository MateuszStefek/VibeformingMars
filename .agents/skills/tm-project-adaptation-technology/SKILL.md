# Project: Adaptation Technology (#069)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Your requirements are 2 steps more flexible. (e.g., you can play a 10% Oxygen card at 8% Oxygen, or a Max -10°C card at -6°C).
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Cheater)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER ENGINE ENABLER**

Adaptation Technology is a high-utility "utility" card that serves as a premier tool for accelerating your engine rollout and managing late-game requirements. While expensive for its immediate impact, its ability to bypass global bottlenecks makes it a high-skill ceiling card in competitive play.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Engine Acceleration:** Bypassing 2 steps of a global parameter (Oxygen, Temperature, Venus, or Oceans) typically allows you to play a high-impact card **one generation earlier** than normal. This compounding advantage is vital for cards like *Kelp Farming* or *Birds*.
2. **Science Tag Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for unlocking high-tier draw engines like **AI Central** or **Mars University**.
3. **Max Requirement Protection:** Its most underrated use is allowing you to play cards with **maximum requirements** (e.g., *Extreme Cold Fungus* or *Deimos Down*) after the track has already passed the limit, allowing you to "hate-draft" these cards late in the game.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Inventrix:** Stacks for a total of **+/- 4 step** flexibility, allowing you to drop mid-game point bombs in the very first generations.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.
   - **Mars University:** The tag triggers a card-draw swap, potentially finding the very card you want to "cheat" onto the board.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High Upfront Cost:** 15 MC (cost + draft) for a passive effect and 1 VP is statistically poor. Only draft if you have a specific high-value requirement card in hand (like *Fish* or *Advanced Ecosystems*) that you need to play immediately.
- **Track Velocity:** If global parameters are moving quickly (3+ steps per generation), the +/- 2 effect becomes irrelevant almost instantly. It is most valuable in slow, engine-heavy games.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Adaptation Technology specifically to reach the 3-science requirement for **AI Central**. The flexibility is a bonus that ensures you can always play the high-impact cards you draw.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you are pursuing a Science strategy or holding high-tier animal cards. skip early if your hand is full of requirement-free production cards. Essential for **Inventrix**.
