# Corporation: Philares

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 47 MC, 1 Building Tag
- **Ability:** Free Gen 1 Greenery; gain resources from adjacency to opponents.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: S-TIER / AGGRESSIVE**

Thrives on board conflict. Placing next to others provides a constant stream of resources.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
King of 'Gardener'. High value on the 2-plant placement spots.
### Hellas
South Pole heat can be a distraction; stay focused on the 'Cultivator' award.
### Elysium
The 'Ecologist' milestone is a must-have. Use the 3-plant spot to jumpstart your engine.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Philares's unique mechanics.
