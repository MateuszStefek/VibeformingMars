# Prelude: Great Aquifer

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Place 2 Ocean tiles.


## Strategic Analysis (The Board Dominator)
**Status: A-TIER UTILITY / THE RUSH ENABLER**

Great Aquifer is a high-efficiency terraforming card that provides immediate board impact and TR gain. While it lacks tags for milestones, its raw value and tactical flexibility make it a staple for both "Rush" strategies and specific industrial engines.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Immediate TR & Income:** Placing 2 oceans in Generation 0 immediately increases your Terraform Rating (TR) by 2, providing a permanent +2 MC income boost from the very first turn.
2. **Placement Bonus Sniping:** The value of the oceans depends heavily on the board:
   - **Tharsis (Base Map):** Top tier. Use the oceans to immediately snag high-value bonuses like the **2-card draw** spot or the **2-titanium** spot before other players can act.
   - **Elysium:** Excellent for securing high-density bonus clusters to jumpstart your engine.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mining Guild:** The "God Tier" start. Placing oceans on steel/titanium spots triggers the Mining Guild ability immediately, granting +2 steel production before Generation 1.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides immediate income and establishes an anchor for future board scoring.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Instantly "unlocks" powerful early-game cards like **Kelp Farming**, **Algae**, or **Water-Splitting Plant**.
   - **UNMI:** Ensures you can use your corporation action (boost TR) in Generation 1 without needing to play a standard project.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Arctic Algae Anti-Synergy:** **Do not pick Great Aquifer if you have Arctic Algae in hand.** You lose the 4 plants you would have gained if the oceans were placed after the card was in play.
- **Zero Tags:** Provides no progress toward milestones like *Builder* or *Ecologist*. If you are racing for tag-based awards, your Prelude slots may be better spent elsewhere.
- **Feeding Opponents:** Be cautious if an opponent is playing a corporation that benefits from early oceans (like *Lakefront Resorts* or *Ecoline*).

### Tips
- **Tempo over Production:** Use Great Aquifer to shorten the game. Ending the ocean track early puts massive pressure on engine-builders, potentially forcing them to play suboptimal cards to keep up with the game's pace.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority "Safe" pick for any strategy. High priority for **Mining Guild**, **UNMI**, or anyone with ocean-locked cards in hand. skip if holding *Arctic Algae* or Pursuing a long-term science engine.
