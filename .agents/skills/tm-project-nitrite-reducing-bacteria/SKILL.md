# Project: Nitrite Reducing Bacteria (#156)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Comes with 3 Microbes.
- **Action:** Either add 1 Microbe to this card, or remove 3 Microbes from this card to raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Slow Engine)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / SITUATIONAL UTILITY**

Nitrite Reducing Bacteria (NRB) is a low-tier microbe card that is often considered a "trap" for newer players. While it provides a path to TR, its slow speed and high opportunity cost make it difficult to justify without significant microbe synergies.

### Why it is a good play (rarely):
1. **Immediate TR:** If played in Generation 1, you can remove the initial 3 microbes immediately to gain 1 TR and a permanent income boost. This makes the 14 MC investment (+3 draft) slightly more efficient.
2. **Requirement-Free Tag:** Provides a Microbe tag with zero global requirements, making it an easy way to satisfiy requirements for high-tier cards like **Advanced Ecosystems** or to trigger **Decomposers**.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Extreme-Cold Fungus / Symbiotic Fungus:** These cards can "feed" microbes into NRB every generation, turning it into a consistent TR-per-generation engine.
   - **Enceladus (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for microbes to bypass the slow 3-turn buildup.
   - **Splice Tactical Genomics:** Triggers Splice's resource/card advantage.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** Without boosters, it takes 3 generations to gain a single TR. In a standard game, you might only trigger it 2-3 times, making the 14 MC investment statistically inferior to basic income cards.
- **No Terraforming Progress:** Unlike *GHG Producing Bacteria* or *Regolith Eaters*, NRB raises TR but does **not** move a global parameter. This means it doesn't help end the game, which can be a disadvantage in a meta that favors rushing.
- **Ants Predator:** **Ants** can steal the microbes from NRB. Since you need to hoard 3 microbes for a payoff, an opponent with Ants can permanently shut down your NRB engine.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** Use the Microbe tag and the immediate TR gain to reach the 26 TR requirement for the **Terraformer** milestone or to secure the **Ecologist** milestone on a budget.

**Recommendation:** Generally a "Pass" in drafts. Only keep if you have a dedicated microbe-boosting card already in hand or are desperate for an early TR income boost. Skip late unless you strictly need 1 VP to win.
