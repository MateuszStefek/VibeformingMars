# Prelude: Research Network

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Draw 3 cards.
- **Tag:** Wild.

## Strategic Analysis (The Engine King)
**Status: S-TIER / THE ULTIMATE FLEXIBILITY TOOL**

Research Network is widely considered one of the strongest Preludes in the game. It provides a unique combination of hand quality, card advantage, and universal requirement-filling that makes it a top-tier choice for any strategy.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **The "Wild Tag" Advantage:** The Wild tag is the card's most valuable asset. It counts as **any tag** when you are playing a card or claiming a milestone.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Counts as a Science tag for *AI Central*, a Plant tag for *NRA*, or a Jovian tag for multipliers.
   - **Milestone Sniper:** Can provide the final tag needed for the *Scientist*, *Ecologist*, or *Rim Settler* milestones.
2. **Precision Hand Digging:** Drawing 3 random cards acts as a "mulligan" for a weak starting hand. It significantly increases your odds of finding the game-defining engine pieces (like *Earth Catapult*) that you missed in the first 10 draws.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University / Olympus Conference:** The Wild tag can be set to Science to trigger these draw engines immediately.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with potential VP-related draws, it fits into their point-heavy strategy.
   - **Robinson Industries:** The Wild tag provides maximum flexibility for the corporation's ability.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Initial Yield:** +1 MC production is a minor boost. If your starting hand is already perfect, you might prefer a Prelude that provides massive resources or TR instead.
- **RNG Dependency:** While drawing 3 cards is strong, you can still "whiff" and find zero playable cards. It is a "high-ceiling, average-floor" card.
- **Tempo over Value:** In a 4-5 player "Rush" game, the slow value of the Wild tag may be less important than the immediate TR or heat production of other Preludes.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Wild tag specifically to hit the 3-science requirement for **AI Central** in Generation 1. This is the most common reason for picking Research Network in high-level play.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" if your starting hand is mediocre or if you need exactly one more tag of any kind to enable your engine. skip only in extreme "Rush" scenarios where TR is your only priority. Priority 1 for **Valley Trust**.
