# Corporation: Aridor

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Colonies
- **Starting MC:** 40
- **First Action:** Add an additional colony tile of your choice to the game.
- **Effect:** When you play a new type of tag (excluding events), increase your MegaCredit production 1 step.

## Strategic Analysis (The Master of Diversity)
**Status: S-TIER / ELITE**

Aridor is one of the most versatile and powerful corporations in the Colonies expansion. Its ability to generate income simply by playing a diverse range of cards allows it to build a massive economy without specifically hunting for production cards.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Passive Income Scaling:** There are 10 unique tags (excluding events). Aridor can easily reach +6 or +7 MC production just by playing a standard game.
2. **Colony Manipulation:** Adding a specific colony tile allows Aridor to ensure a resource they need (like *Luna* for MC or *Pluto* for cards) is available.
3. **Gen 1 Advantage:** Aridor often starts the game with a higher effective income than almost any other corp because the first few tags played provide immediate production boosts.

### Synergies:
- **Diverse Tag Hands:** Cards with rare tags (like Bio or Jovian) become high-priority for the production boost.
- **Wild Tags:** *Septem Tribus* (via Merger) or cards with Wild tags can trigger the "new type" check more easily.
- **Colonies Expansion:** Obviously excels when more colonies are available to interact with.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Tag Tracking:** Keep a mental checklist of which tags you've played. Don't waste a "new tag" opportunity on a weak card, but also don't wait too long to get your production up.
- **The Luna Pick:** In most cases, adding *Luna* is the safest and strongest choice for your first action to ensure liquid cash is available.

**Recommendation:** An elite, "S-Tier" pick. Highly recommended in all game formats, especially when playing with multiple expansions where tag diversity is higher.
