# Project: Media Archives (#106)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Gain 1 MegaCredit (MC) for each Event card (Red card) played by all players.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Cashout)
**Status: S-TIER IN 4-5 PLAYER GAMES / B-TIER UTILITY**

Media Archives is a high-ceiling liquidity card that rewards players for a long, event-heavy match. While it provides no "engine" value, its ability to generate a massive cash injection in the final generations makes it a premier tool for funding game-ending projects.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Scaling:** It counts *all* events played by *all* players. In high-player games (4-5 players), it is common to see 20+ events by the end of the match. This turns a 8 MC investment (+3 to draft) into a **9-15+ MC pure profit**.
2. **Earth Tag Synergy:** In the 2024-2025 meta, the tag is often more valuable than the cash.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers a card draw and provides the cash needed to play the drawn card immediately.
   - **Teractor:** Reduces the cost to 5 MC, significantly lowering the break-even point.
   - **Cartels:** Contributes toward maximizing your Earth-based income engine.
3. **Surprise Liquid Cash:** In the final turn, Media Archives can provide the exact MC needed to fund a high-VP city or greenery that an opponent thought you couldn't afford, potentially deciding the match.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Speculation:** **Never keep this in your opening hand** (unless playing Point Luna). Tying up 3 MC of draft capital for a reward that only materializes in Generation 8-10 is an opportunity cost that will stall your economic growth.
- **2-Player Inefficiency:** In 1v1 games, events are much rarer. If fewer than 11 total events are played, the card statistically loses money compared to the draft and play cost.
- **Digital vs. Physical:** In physical games, calculating the event count across all player piles can be tedious. Do not let "math fatigue" prevent you from playing this card if it is profitable.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** Use the sudden cash injection from Media Archives to fund the **Banker** award in the final turn. It is one of the hardest moves for an opponent to predict and block.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-10) for any player count above 2. Essential for anyone with Earth synergies. Skip early to preserve tempo.
