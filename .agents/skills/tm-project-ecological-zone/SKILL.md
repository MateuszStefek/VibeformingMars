# Project: Ecological Zone (#160)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Bio, Animal, Plant
- **Requirements:** 1 Greenery Tile
- **Effect:** When you play a Plant or Animal tag (including this one), add an Animal to this card.
- **Action:** Place a special tile adjacent to any Greenery tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 2 Animals.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Payoff)
**Status: S-TIER / COMBO POWERHOUSE**

Ecological Zone is widely regarded as one of the most efficient point-scoring cards in the game. It bridges the gap between plant production and animal-based victory points.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unbeatable Efficiency:** For 12 MC, you get 2 Animals immediately (from its own Plant/Animal tags), a tile placement, and a repeatable VP generator.
2. **The Bio-Trinity:** It forms a devastating engine when paired with **Viral Enhancers** and **Decomposers**. In this setup, every plant/animal tag played generates multiple resources and points.
3. **Board Control:** The special tile can be placed next to *any* greenery. Use this to block opponents' city placements, steal ocean rebates, or reach lucrative areas of the map.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides three different tags (Bio, Animal, Plant) toward the *Ecologist* and *Diversifier* milestones.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Meat Industry:** Reduces the cost of the tags you're already playing and provides a 4 MC rebate on the Zone itself.
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows early trading for animals, potentially netting 5+ VP by the end of the game.
   - **Arklight:** If playing this corporation, the animal value is essentially doubled.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Timing:** You must have at least one greenery on the board (yours or an opponent's). Playing it too late in the game minimizes the number of tags you can trigger it with.
- **Microbe Diversion:** If you have to choose between a Microbe engine and an Animal engine, the Animal engine (Ecological Zone) is generally more point-efficient but harder to protect from predators.

### Tips
- **Blocking with the Tile:** Do not just place the tile for the bonus. Look for an opponent's city and use the Ecological Zone tile to occupy the most valuable adjacent spot, effectively "stealing" 1 VP from their city.

**Recommendation:** A "Snap-Pick" in the draft if you have any plant production or animal/plant tags in hand. Highly effective in the mid-game (Generations 3-6).
