# Project: Protected Valley (#030)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 23 MC
- **Tags:** Building, Plant
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Place a Greenery tile on an area reserved for Ocean (ignores normal greenery placement rules). Raise Oxygen 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Ocean-Greenery Hybrid)
**Status: A-TIER BOARD CONTROL / B-TIER ECONOMY**

Protected Valley is a unique board-control card that allows you to "cheat" a greenery onto the board in locations where plants normally cannot grow. It provides a solid mix of terraforming progress, income, and tactical placement.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Rule-Breaking Placement:** You can place the greenery on an **Ocean reserve** tile. This allows you to "walk" your way to lucrative ocean placement bonuses (like 2 titanium or 2 cards) while also securing a TR and VP from the greenery.
2. **Instant "Safe" Points:** Since the greenery is placed on an ocean spot, it is often isolated from an opponent's cities, preventing them from "leeching" points from your greenery placement.
3. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Building** and a **Plant** tag.
   - **Building:** Can be paid for entirely with Steel, making it a very efficient way to convert metal into TR and board presence.
   - **Plant:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and contributes to the *Gardener* and *Ecologist* milestones.
4. **Immediate Income:** +2 MC production helps recoup the card's cost over time, while the TR from Oxygen provides another +1 income step.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High Upfront Cost:** 26 MC (cost + draft) is a heavy investment. Do not play this in Generation 1 if it prevents you from building a more focused engine. It is often best played in Generations 3-4 to claim a critical board spot.
- **Ocean Limitation:** Once all 9 ocean spots are filled with either oceans or greeneries (via this card or *Mangrove*), the placement effect becomes impossible.
- **Oxygen Threshold:** While it has no requirement, its value drops if Oxygen is already maxed.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Blocking:** Use Protected Valley to occupy an ocean spot that an opponent was planning to take with a Standard Project ocean. This denies them the TR and placement bonus while securing your own greenery chain.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for **Ecoline** or players with high Steel production. High priority for securing board-based awards. Skip if the board is already crowded and you have no steel to spend.
