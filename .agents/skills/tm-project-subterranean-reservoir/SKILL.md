# Project: Subterranean Reservoir (#121)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** None (Event)
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Place 1 Ocean tile.


## Strategic Analysis (The Efficiency Benchmark)
**Status: B-TIER EFFICIENCY / TACTICAL STAPLE**

Subterranean Reservoir is a simple, high-efficiency terraforming card. It serves as the community benchmark for ocean pricing and acts as a premier tactical tool for securing early board presence.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Direct Efficiency:** For only 11 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 1 TR (from the ocean) and a placement bonus. This 14 MC total investment is exactly **4 MC cheaper** than the 18 MC Standard Project ocean, making it a "fair value" play in any generation.
2. **Requirement-Free Momentum:** No temperature or oxygen requirements mean it can be played in Generation 1. Use it to immediately "swipe" a high-value bonus spot (like the 2nd card draw on Tharsis) before opponents can react.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** The premier synergy. Gain 2 plants immediately, accelerating your first greenery conversion.
   - **Media Group:** As an Event card, it triggers the 3 MC rebate, making the ocean effectively cost only 8 MC (+ draft).
   - **Interplanetary Cinematics:** Provides a 2 MC rebate.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost upon placement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Impact:** While efficient, it only provides 1 TR and 1 tile. In the late game, it is a point-filler, not a game-changer.
- **Ocean Cap:** Monitor the ocean track. In high-player games, oceans are often the first parameter to finish. Do not draft this late if only 1-2 ocean spots remain.
- **Zero Engine Value:** The card provides no long-term production. If you are in Generation 1-2 and have to choose between this and a card like *Acquired Company*, the long-term production is usually better.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** Use Subterranean Reservoir to reach the "3 Ocean" or "5 Ocean" requirements for cards like **Moss** or **Algae** a generation earlier than your opponents expect.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft for any strategy. High priority for **Rush** players or anyone holding *Arctic Algae*. skip late if oceans are already mostly placed.
