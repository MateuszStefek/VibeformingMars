# Project: Security Fleet (#028)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 1 Titanium to add 1 Fighter resource to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Fighter resource on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Titanium Sink)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / SITUATIONAL POINT DUMP**

Security Fleet is a low-to-mid tier point engine that converts accumulated titanium into victory points. While its raw efficiency is average, it serves as an essential "overflow" valve for titanium-heavy engines that lack high-VP space events.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Titanium Liquidation:** In the late game, players heavily invested in titanium production (e.g., **Phobolog**) often find themselves with a surplus of resources and no space cards to spend them on. Security Fleet provides a way to convert that metal into 1:1 VP (valuing 1 titanium at ~3 MC).
2. **Surprise Point-Slam:** If you have animal-adding effects like **CEO's Favorite Project** or **Imported Nitrogen**, you can add resources to Security Fleet for free, bypassing the titanium cost and significantly improving its ROI.
3. **Space Tag Synergy:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium, making it very cheap to put into play. It also helps hit requirements for high-tier cards like *Quantum Extractor*.
4. **Award Support:** Contributes toward the **Excentric** award (most resources on cards).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** Spending titanium on Security Fleet in Generations 1-4 is usually a mistake. That same metal could have been used to fund production cards or terraforming events that "snowball" into more than 1 VP over time.
- **Low Floor:** If played in the final 2 generations, the 15 MC investment (+3 draft) will only yield 2 VP, a very poor ratio. You need to activate the action at least **5 times** to reach the community benchmark for point efficiency.
- **Vulnerability:** High titanium production makes you a target for *Asteroid Mining Consortium* or *Sabotage*, which can cripple your ability to fuel the Fleet.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Space tag on Security Fleet to reach the threshold for **Mass Converter** early. The points are a secondary benefit; the primary value is enabling your core industrial engine.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft for titanium-rich players. Excellent mid-game filler for space-heavy engines. Skip early to preserve metal for economic growth. Skip late if you cannot activate it at least 3 times.
