# Project: Plantation (#193)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** 2 Science Tags
- **Effect:** Place a Greenery tile. Raise Oxygen 1 step.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent City.


## Strategic Analysis (The Science Greenery)
**Status: A-TIER PAYOFF / B-TIER UTILITY**

Plantation is one of the most efficient ways to place a greenery tile in the mid-to-late game. While it provides no "engine" value, its role as a discounted Standard Project and its high-value tag make it a staple for science-heavy strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost Efficiency:** At 15 MC (+3 to draft), it costs **18 MC** total. This is a 5 MC discount over the 23 MC Standard Project Greenery, while also providing a valuable tag and synergy triggers.
2. **The "Surprise" Greenery:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Plantation is prized as a "burst" greenery. Because it is a card in hand, opponents often fail to account for it when racing for the **Gardener** milestone (Tharsis map) or **Ecologist** award (Elysium map).
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:**
   - **Plant Tag:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Ecological Zone*.
   - **Requirement Bridge:** Provides the Plant tag needed to hit the 3-tag requirement for **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or to boost the scaling of **Insects**.
4. **Oxygen Stealing:** Frequently used to "sniping" the temperature bonus (at 8% Oxygen) or the final TR bonus on the Oxygen track before an opponent can convert their plants.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 2-science tag requirement is the main hurdle. **Do not hold this early** unless you have a clear path to science tags (e.g., via *Research* or *Mars University*). Tying up 3 MC for a card you cannot play will stall your engine.
- **Credicor / Standard Tech:** If you are playing as **Credicor** or have **Standard Tech** in play, the discount on Standard Project greeneries makes Plantation less attractive, though the tag still provides niche value.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Sniping:** Use Plantation to take a high-value spot adjacent to multiple opponent cities. Since it is so cheap, you can often "steal" 2-3 points of city value from your competitors for a minimal investment.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 4-7) if you have the science tags. Essential for anyone racing for the **Gardener** milestone or building a bio-engine. Skip early if your science track is slow.
