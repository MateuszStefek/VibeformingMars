# Project: Equatorial Magnetizer (#015)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step to increase your TR 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Energy Sink)
**Status: C-TIER / SITUATIONAL UTILITY**

Equatorial Magnetizer is a niche card used primarily to convert surplus energy into Victory Points and income. While statistically inefficient in a vacuum, it serves as an excellent "damage control" tool for energy-heavy builds.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **TR Generation:** It provides a reliable, repeatable way to increase your Terraform Rating (TR) without depending on global parameters. This is vital for sniping the **Terraformer** milestone.
2. **Waste Management:** If you have overproduced energy to meet science requirements (e.g., for *Mass Converter*) or to fuel high-tier buildings, this card ensures that energy production isn't "wasted" as heat once its primary purpose is served.
3. **Steel Dump:** As a Building card with no requirements, it is a prime target for spending excess steel to build an end-game point engine.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Factorum (Corporation):** The premier synergy. Factorum can increase energy production as an action, creating a loop for "free" TR every generation.
   - **Viron (Corporation):** Allows you to use the action twice in the final generation for a 2 TR swing.
   - **Colonies:** A great pivot once the high-value colonies (like *Luna* or *Ceres*) are exhausted and you no longer need energy for trading.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** Energy production costs ~7 MC per step. The card costs 14 MC (cost + draft). You need to activate it **4 times** before you see a real profit over simply having MC production.
- **Early Game Stalling:** **Do not play this in Generations 1-3.** The 14 MC and 1 energy production are much better spent on establishing your income engine.
- **Standard Projects:** If you have high liquid MC, you are usually better off using a standard project to terraform, as it provides a board tile or moves a parameter closer to the bonus thresholds.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** Use the repeatable TR gain to reach 26 TR before your opponents can, securing the *Terraformer* milestone. In the digital version, use the action first in your turn to stall and see if opponents commit to a heat or oxygen push.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft. Only keep if you are playing **Factorum**, have a massive steel surplus, or have 3+ excess energy production by Generation 4. Skip if the game is likely to end in under 3 generations.
