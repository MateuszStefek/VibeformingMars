# Project: Gold Mining (#113)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Bridge)
**Status: B-TIER FOUNDATION / S-TIER SYNERGY**

Gold Mining is a reliable, mid-tier economic card. While it is less efficient for pure production than *Sponsors* or *Acquired Company*, its unconditional nature and high-value Earth tag make it a staple for establishing early-game momentum.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unconditional Income:** No temperature or oxygen requirements mean it can be played in Generation 1. This ensures a steady return on investment from the very start of the match.
2. **Earth Tag Catalyst:** In the 2024-2025 meta, Gold Mining is valued more for its tag than its production. It is a vital enabler for high-value cards like **Cartels**, **Earth Office**, or **Earth Catapult**.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Point Luna:** The premier pairing. Triggers a card draw upon play, making the 15 MC investment much more palatable.
   - **Teractor:** Reduces the cost to 9 MC (+3 to draft), making the break-even point just 6 generations.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for cards with non-negative VP icons (wait, it has no VP icon). Unlike *Research*, this card does not trigger Vitor.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** At a total cost of 15 MC (card + draft) for 2 MC production, you are paying **7.5 MC per production step**. This is well above the community benchmark of ~5 MC per step. Only play this early (Generations 1-3).
- **Opportunity Cost:** If you have better production cards (like *Acquired Company*), prioritize them first. Gold Mining is often the "backup" choice if you miss the S-tier economy cards.
- **Late Game Decay:** Playing Gold Mining after Generation 5 is usually poor, as the income will not recoup the 15 MC cost before the game ends.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bridging:** Use Gold Mining as a cheap Earth tag to hit the 2-tag requirement for **Earth Office** or to boost your **Cartels** payout. The production is a secondary benefit that stabilizes your engine while you hunt for win conditions.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 1-3. High priority for Point Luna or anyone building an Earth-focused engine. Skip in the second half of the game.
