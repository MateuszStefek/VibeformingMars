# Corporation: Arklight

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Colonies
- **Starting MC:** 45
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Starting Production:** 2 MC
- **Effect:** When you play a Plant or Animal tag, add 1 animal to this card.
- **Effect:** 1 VP per 2 animals on this card.

## Strategic Analysis (The Biological Preserve)
**Status: B-TIER / BIOLOGICAL ENGINE**

Arklight functions as a built-in *Ecological Zone* for animals. It rewards a consistent commitment to biological strategies by providing passive victory points and a solid economic floor.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Internal Scoring:** Unlike most animal cards that require you to find a "consumer" card, Arklight *is* the consumer. Every plant or animal tag you play is worth 0.5 VP.
2. **Solid Economy:** Starting with 45 MC and +2 MC production is a very respectable opening that doesn't leave you behind.
3. **Animal Tag Synergy:** Starting with an animal tag helps meet requirements for powerful cards like *Large Convoy* or *Decomposers*.

### Synergies:
- **Miranda Colony:** Absolutely essential. Trading with Miranda to add animals directly to Arklight accelerates your scoring significantly.
- **Biological Engine:** *Ecological Zone*, *Decomposers*, and *Herbivores* all trigger or benefit from the same cards Arklight wants.
- **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid:** Provides a plant tag and a Venus bump, perfect for triggering the animal addition.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **Don't Over-Draft Bio:** It's tempting to keep every plant/animal card, but remember that 0.5 VP per card is not a primary win condition. You still need a strong MC engine.
- **The Miranda Priority:** If Miranda is in play, you should be the primary player trading there.

**Recommendation:** A solid choice if your starting hand has 2+ cheap plant/animal tags and Miranda is a colony.
