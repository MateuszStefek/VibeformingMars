# Prelude: Metal-rich Asteroid

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 1 step. Gain 4 Titanium. Gain 4 Steel.
- **Tags:** None.

## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Rocket)
**Status: S-TIER / THE ULTIMATE ENABLER**

Metal-rich Asteroid is widely considered one of the most powerful Preludes in the game. It provides a massive injection of resources and a TR boost in Generation 0, allowing for explosive starts that can define the entire game.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Quantified Value:** The card provides ~30 MC worth of immediate value (1 TR/Temperature raise worth ~10-14 MC, 4 Titanium worth 12 MC, and 4 Steel worth 8 MC). This is significantly higher than the Prelude average of ~21 MC.
2. **Immediate Engine Jumpstart:** The 4 Titanium and 4 Steel allow you to play high-cost, no-requirement cards in Generation 1 that would otherwise be unplayable.
   - **Titanium:** Enables Gen 1 *Io Mining Industries*, *Earth Elevator*, or *Space Elevator*.
   - **Steel:** Enables Gen 1 *Mohole Area*, *Electro Catapult*, or *Research Outpost*.
3. **TR & Income:** The temperature raise provides 1 TR immediately, increasing your income and score from the very first turn. It also hits the first threshold bonus (-24°C Heat Production) if combined with other temperature boosts.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog:** Increases the value of the Titanium to 16 MC, making the card worth ~34 MC.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Significantly increases the utility of the 8 metal resources.
   - **UNMI:** The temperature raise provides the necessary TR to trigger UNMI's corporation ability in Generation 1.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **No Production:** Unlike *Metals Company*, this card provides zero long-term production. If you do not have high-value building or space cards in your starting hand, the metals will sit in your inventory, and you will eventually fall behind opponents with production-based Preludes.
- **Zero Tags:** Lacks any tags, providing no progress toward requirement-based milestones like *Builder* or *Ecologist*.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Value:** Only take Metal-rich Asteroid if you have **at least two cards** in your starting hand that can utilize the metals in Generation 1 or 2. If your hand is bio-heavy or event-heavy, a pure income Prelude is usually better.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" if your hand contains high-cost metal-based targets. Essential for **Phobolog**, **UNMI**, or anyone Pursuing a "Rush" strategy. Excellent in Solo mode.
