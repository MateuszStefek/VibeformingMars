# Project: Extreme-Cold Fungus (#086)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Microbe
- **Requirements:** Max -10°C
- **Action:** Either gain 1 Plant, or add 2 Microbes to any card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Engine Fuel)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE COMPONENT / B-TIER UTILITY**

Extreme-Cold Fungus (ECF) is one of the most powerful "force multipliers" in the game. While its ability to gain plants is modest, its primary value lies in its role as a "microbe battery" that can triple the speed of high-impact biological engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Microbe Yield:** Adding 2 microbes per generation is the strongest repeatable microbe injection in the game. When used on high-value "sinks" like **GHG Producing Bacteria** or **Regolith Eaters**, ECF allows you to raise Temperature or Oxygen **every single generation**.
2. **Infinite Loop Potential:** Pair ECF with **Symbiotic Fungus**. ECF adds 2 microbes to Symbiotic Fungus, and Symbiotic Fungus adds 1 back to ECF or another card, allowing you to "overcharge" a third microbe card for explosive growth.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **GHG Producing Bacteria:** The "God Tier" combo. Raise temperature 1 step every generation for only 1 energy.
   - **Sulphur-Eating Bacteria (Venus):** Converts the 2 microbes into 6-9 MC per turn, creating a massive economic engine.
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides the Microbe tag needed to trigger these passive point generators.
   - **Splice Tactical Genomics:** Triggers Splice's corporation ability for additional resources or cards.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The "Max -10°C" requirement is strict. In fast-paced games, the temperature track can finish before ECF pays off. **Play it as early as possible** (Generations 1-3).
- **Dependency:** ECF provides **zero engine value** without another microbe card to receive the resources. Only play for the "Gain 1 Plant" option as a last resort in the final turn.
- **Upfront Cost:** 16 MC (cost + draft) is a significant early-game investment. Ensure you already have a high-value microbe "sink" (like *Regolith Eaters*) before committing your budget.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to play ECF at -6°C or -4°C. This allows you to "hate-draft" the card late and use it to finish the temperature or oxygen tracks before opponents can stabilize their plant engines.

**Recommendation:** A top-priority draft if you have any microbe-based TR or VP engines. Excellent opening hand card. Skip late if temperature is already nearing -10°C. Priority 1 in **Solo Mode**.
