# Corporation: Celestic

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Venus Next
- **Starting MC:** 42
- **Tags:** Venus
- **First Action:** Reveal cards from the deck until you have 2 cards with a floater icon; take them into your hand and discard the rest.
- **Action:** Add 1 floater to any card.
- **Effect:** 1 VP per 3 floaters on this card.

## Strategic Analysis (The Floater Specialist)
**Status: B-TIER / ENGINE BUILDER**

Celestic is the premier choice for players looking to exploit the floater mechanics introduced in Venus Next and Colonies. Its ability to "dig" for floaters ensures it starts with the tools it needs.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Guaranteed Setup:** The "First Action" significantly reduces the variance of floater strategies by guaranteeing two relevant cards.
2. **Milestone Synergy:** Celestic is the frontrunner for the 'Hoverlord' milestone (owning a certain number of floaters).
3. **Internal VP Engine:** The 1 VP per 3 floaters on the corp card provides a fallback point for excess floaters if other cards are full or absent.

### Synergies:
- **Hoverlord Milestone:** Often an easy 5 points for Celestic.
- **Jovian/Venus Floater Cards:** Cards like *Corrosion Resisters*, *Dirigibles*, or *Local Shuttles* become high priority.
- **Titan Colony:** Crucial for gaining extra floaters to feed into VP-generating cards.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The One-Card Rule:** Celestic needs at least one *strong* floater-consuming card (like *Physics Complex* via some conversion or specific Venus cards) to truly excel. Without a high-value consumer, the 1/3 VP rate on the corp is too slow.
- **Tempo Management:** Don't spend every action adding floaters to your corp; ensure you are still building an economy.

**Recommendation:** High priority if 'Hoverlord' is available or if you draw powerful floater cards (e.g., *Dirigibles*) in your opening hand.
