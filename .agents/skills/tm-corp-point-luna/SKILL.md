# Corporation: Point Luna

## Corp Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Starting MC:** 38
- **Tags:** Earth, Space
- **Starting Production:** 1 Titanium
- **Effect:** When you play an Earth tag (including the ones on this card), draw 1 card.

## Strategic Analysis (The Card Draw King)
**Status: S-TIER / PREMIER**

Point Luna is widely regarded as one of the best corporations in the game. Its ability to turn Earth tags—already some of the best economic cards—into card draw creates a cycle of growth that is difficult for other corps to match.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Engine Velocity:** In Terraforming Mars, cards are the most valuable resource. Drawing a card every time you play an Earth tag allows you to see more of the deck and find your win conditions faster.
2. **Premier Tags:** Starting with both Earth and Space tags is incredible for meeting requirements and benefiting from early-game discounts or rebates.
3. **Titanium Start:** The 1 titanium production helps fund the expensive space cards that often accompany a high-draw strategy.

### Synergies:
- **Earth Catapult / Sponsors / Acquired Company:** These cards are already S-Tier; for Point Luna, they are game-winning because they also replace themselves in your hand.
- **Luna Metropolis:** A high-value Earth card that fits perfectly with the theme.
- **Cartel:** Scales beautifully with the Earth tags you'll be incentivized to play.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Focus on 'Space Hotel' or 'Banker'. Board geography is secondary to space card efficiency.
### Hellas
Prime map for 'Rim Settler' milestone. The ocean placement bonuses are great for titanium.
### Elysium
Target the 'Celebrity' award. High-value cards are your primary scoring engine.

### Tips
- **Priority 1 - Earth Tags:** In the early drafts, prioritize almost any playable Earth tag. The card draw is worth more than the marginal utility of other cards.
- **The "Sponsors" Start:** If you have *Sponsors* in your opening hand as Point Luna, your win probability increases significantly.

**Recommendation:** An "Always-Pick" unless you have a truly degenerate combo with another S-Tier corp (like Viron + AI Central).
