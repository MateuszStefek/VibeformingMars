# Prelude: Allied Bank

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 4 steps. Gain 3 MC.
- **Tag:** Earth.

## Strategic Analysis (The Economic Benchmark)
**Status: S-TIER / THE VERSATILE GOLD STANDARD**

Allied Bank is widely regarded as one of the strongest and most flexible Preludes in the game. It provides a massive economic boost in Generation 1, ensuring a stable foundation for any strategy, whether focusing on terraforming or engine building.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 4 MC production and 3 MC cash-on-hand is worth ~27 MC in Generation 1 value. This is well above the "fair value" of a Prelude (~21 MC), making it an immediate efficiency gain over almost any other economic start.
2. **The "Earth Engine" Foundation:** The Earth tag is a critical asset.
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers a free card draw immediately upon play.
   - **Requirement Enabler:** Helps satisfy the "Earth tag" requirement for powerful cards like *Cartels* or *Earth Office* in the very first generation.
3. **Universality:** Unlike specialized Preludes (e.g., those for heat or titanium), Allied Bank is never a bad choice. Every corporation benefits from increased income and immediate liquidity.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides 4 steps toward the **Specialist** milestone (highest production), making it the easiest way to secure that milestone in the early game.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Allied Bank vs. Business Empire:** While *Business Empire* (+6 production, -6 MC) has a higher ceiling, *Allied Bank* is generally superior in the 2024-2025 meta. The 9 MC net advantage in Generation 1 (3 gain vs 6 loss) is usually more valuable for getting a high-impact engine (like *AI Central*) online than the extra production.
- **Opportunity Cost:** Only skip Allied Bank if you have a "high-roll" synergy (e.g., *Ecology Experts* + *Kelp Farming*) that specifically accelerates a game-ending engine.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Production:** Use the 3 MC cash gain to fund a critical Generation 1 card like **Research Outpost** or **Acquired Company**. Allied Bank is often the "grease" that allows an expensive starting hand to function without needing to sell cards.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Pick" in almost any scenario. Priority 1 for **Point Luna** or **Vitor**. Best choice for players who want a reliable, high-income start without being locked into a specific board strategy.
