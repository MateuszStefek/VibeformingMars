# Project: Birds (#072)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 10 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** 13% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease any player's Plant production 2 steps.
- **Action:** Add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Animal on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Sky Predator)
**Status: S-TIER VP SOURCE / THE BIO-COUNTER**

Birds is one of the premier late-game point scoring cards in *Terraforming Mars*. It combines high-efficiency VP generation with a devastating offensive effect against biological engines.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled VP Density:** At 1 VP per animal, Birds is part of the elite "1:1 Club" (along with *Fish* and *Small Animals*). Its action provides 1 VP every generation for zero MC cost once played.
2. **The "Nuclear Option" against Ecoline:** Reducing any player's plant production by 2 steps is a massive blow. In competitive matches, this is often used to single-handedly shut down an opponent's greenery engine, potentially denying them 5-10 VP over the course of the game.
3. **Oxygen Dependency:** Unlike *Fish* (which depends on temperature), Birds depends on Oxygen. In 4-5 player games, Oxygen often maxes out 1-2 generations before other parameters, making Birds a more reliable and earlier point-scorer than its aquatic counterpart.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Large Convoy:** The premier combo. Adding 4 animals to Birds instantly grants 4 VP and a massive board swing.
   - **Viral Enhancers / Decomposers:** Provides immediate resource gains or microbes when the Animal tag is played.
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for animals, bypassing the "once per turn" limit and potentially netting 3-5+ extra points.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Mandatory Production Hit:** You **must** be able to decrease someone's plant production by 2 steps. If no opponent has production, you are forced to hit yourself. Always check the production board before drafting or playing Birds.
- **Timing:** 13% Oxygen is the highest requirement in the game. Do not keep this in your opening hand if you anticipate a slow, engine-heavy game where Oxygen might not hit 13% until the final generation.

### Tips
- **Speculative Hold:** In the 2024-2025 meta, players often hold Birds in their starting 10 if they have an "Oxygen Bumping" Prelude. Snatching Birds early prevents any opponent from using it as a counter-pick against your own plant engine.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in the mid-to-late game. High priority if an opponent is building a greenery engine. Priority 1 if holding *Large Convoy*. Skip only if Oxygen is stalled and the game is ending.
