# Project: Livestock (#184)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** 9% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Decrease your Plant production 1 step.
- **Action:** Add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per Animal on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Industrial Trade-off)
**Status: B-TIER VP SOURCE / SITUATIONAL UTILITY**

Livestock is a solid late-game point accumulator that unique among animals for providing economic production. While its plant production penalty can be painful for bio-engineers, its high VP density and relatively early oxygen requirement make it a staple for mid-to-late game scoring.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **High VP Efficiency:** At 1 VP per animal, Livestock is part of the elite "1:1 Club." If played in Generation 6-7 (when 9% Oxygen is typical), it can naturally generate 3-5 VP, representing an excellent return on the 16 MC investment (+3 draft).
2. **Economic Compensation:** It is one of the few point cards that actually *increases* your income. The +2 MC production helps offset the card's cost over the final rounds, even if the plant penalty hurts your greenery progress.
3. **Oxygen Advantage:** Its 9% Oxygen requirement is significantly lower than *Birds* (13%). This allows you to start your 1:1 VP engine 1-2 generations earlier than sky-based predators.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** The ultimate multiplier. Trading with Miranda allows you to dump extra animals onto Livestock, potentially netting 8-12+ VP and bypassing the "once per turn" action limit.
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants an immediate animal (1 VP) upon play, effectively reducing the "payback" time by one generation.
   - **Large Convoy / Imported Nitrogen:** High-impact events to dump animals onto Livestock for instant point bombs.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Mandatory Plant Hit:** You **must** decrease your own plant production by 1 step. If you are playing *Ecoline* or a dedicated greenery strategy, ensure the -1 production won't stall your final forest placement.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** Like all 1 VP/animal cards, Livestock is the #1 target for **Predators** or **Virus** attacks. Always convert your animals to points (via TR or game-end scoring) as soon as possible.
- **Late Game Waste:** If played in the final 2 generations, the MC production is irrelevant and the -1 plant production might prevent a final greenery. Only play late if you have animal-adding events ready.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Special Design** or **Inventrix** to drop Livestock at 7% or 8% Oxygen. This grants you an extra generation of 1:1 VP growth and secures the Animal tag for bio-synergies earlier than your opponents expect.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 4-6). Essential if playing with *Miranda* or if you have a surplus of plant production. Skip only if you are strictly racing for the *Gardener* milestone and cannot afford the production penalty.
