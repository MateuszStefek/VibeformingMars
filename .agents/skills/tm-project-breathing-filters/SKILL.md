# Project: Breathing Filters (#133)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** 7% Oxygen
- **VP:** 2


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Point Filler)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / A-TIER FOR VITOR**

Breathing Filters is a straightforward point card. While it provides no economic production or board presence, its high efficiency in converting MC to victory points makes it a valuable tool for "greedy" engine players in the final generations.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Mathematical Efficiency:** At a total cost of 11 MC (+3 to draft), it provides 2 VP. This 5.5 MC/VP ratio is better than the community benchmark (~7 MC/VP), making it an efficient way to spend excess cash at the end of the game.
2. **Vitor Synergy:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate, bringing the effective cost down to **8 MC** for 2 VP—one of the best ratios in the game.
3. **Science Tag Utility:** While played too late to satisfy early science requirements, the tag can trigger bonuses from **Mars University** (card draw) or **Olympus Conference** if they are still active in the final turns.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 7% Oxygen requirement is a strict cutoff. If you are playing a slow game where Oxygen is the last track to finish, Breathing Filters can clog your hand for several generations.
- **Early-Game Speculation:** **Do not keep this in your starting hand.** Tying up 3 MC for a card you cannot play until the mid-to-late game is an opportunity cost that prevents you from building critical income production.
- **Zero Engine Value:** The card provides no production, no board tile, and no terraforming progress. It is strictly a "point-slam" for the final round.

### Tips
- **Draft Timing:** Best drafted in the final 2-3 generations once the Oxygen track is nearing 7%. In a tight race for the **Scientist** award, this can be a cheap way to add a tag and points simultaneously.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft in the final third of the game. High priority for **Vitor** or players with significant science discounts. Skip early to preserve capital for engine building.
