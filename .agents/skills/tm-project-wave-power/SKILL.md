# Project: Wave Power (#187)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 8 MC
- **Tags:** Power
- **Requirements:** 3 Ocean Tiles.
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Stable Efficiency)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR VITOR**

Wave Power is a high-efficiency utility card that is strictly superior to the basic *Power Plant* Standard Project. While it requires a modest board setup, its combination of production and points makes it a staple for mid-game infrastructure stabilization.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 8 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 1 Energy production (~7 MC value) and 1 VP (~5 MC value). This 11 MC total investment provides **~12 MC of value**, making it one of the few cards that is effectively "free" in terms of point-to-cost ratio.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides a Power tag and a Victory Point.
   - **Vitor:** The premier synergy. Vitor receives a 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon, making Wave Power cost only **5 MC**—an unbeatable ROI for energy and a point.
   - **Requirement Bridge:** A cheap way to reach the 6-power requirement for the **Energizer** milestone.
3. **Colonies Synergy:** A cost-effective way to reach the "3 Energy" threshold for trading if you are currently sitting at 2 production.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Barrier:** The 3-ocean requirement is low, but in very slow games, it can delay the card until Generation 4 or 5. Do not hold from Generation 1 unless you have a path to early oceans.
- **Low Impact:** +1 Energy is a minor boost. If you already have stable energy and zero need for points, the 8 MC is better spent on income.
- **Late Game Waste:** While 1 VP for 11 MC is efficient, it provides zero board presence or TR. Only draft late if you have the oceans and need the final point to win.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use Wave Power specifically to hit the requirements for high-tier cards like **AI Central** (requires Power tags). The 1 VP is a nice bonus that helps offset the cost of fixing your infrastructure.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if playing **Vitor**, **Thorgate**, or with *Colonies*. Excellent as a mid-game "filler" to fix your energy math. skip early if oceans are not moving.
