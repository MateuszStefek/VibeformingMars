# Project: Ore Processor (#029)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Spend 4 Energy to gain 1 Titanium and raise Oxygen 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Titanium Bumper)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Ore Processor is a high-utility "industrial" card that converts surplus power into terraforming progress and high-value space resources. While often considered bulky in multiplayer, its ability to move the oxygen track while generating titanium makes it a premier tool for space-heavy engines and rushing strategies.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Oxygen Control:** Provides a reliable way to raise Oxygen that doesn't depend on board state or biological production. Use it to "sniping" oxygen thresholds or to hasten the game's end to deny engine-builders more generations.
2. **Titanium Generation:** Unlike other bumpers (*Steelworks*, *Ironworks*), Ore Processor provides Titanium. This is the premier way to "launder" building resources (steel) into space resources (titanium) if you have the energy production to support it.
3. **Steel Sink Catalyst:** As a Building card, the 13 MC cost can be paid entirely with **Steel**. This allows you to convert mineral wealth into immediate TR and titanium without spending liquid cash.
4. **Solo Mode MVP:** Essential for maxing out the Oxygen track in Solo play, which is often the hardest parameter to reach without lucky plant draws.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **High Energy Overhead:** Requiring 4 energy *every generation* is a massive economic hit (~28 MC value). If you don't have "free" energy from high-tier science cards like *Mass Converter*, the opportunity cost of Ore Processor is often too high.
- **Efficiency Paradox:** You must use the action at least **5 times** to see a net profit over Standard Projecting a greenery.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is at a premium for trading. 4 energy spent here is 4 energy not spent trading for cards or 2x titanium at a colony.

### Tips
- **Space Engine Feed:** If you are holding a hand full of expensive Jovian multipliers, prioritize Ore Processor over other bumpers. The 1 titanium per turn adds up, potentially saving you 9-12 MC on a game-winning card in the final generation.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in multiplayer. High priority in **Solo Mode** or if you have a massive energy surplus (6+ production). Skip if playing with *Colonies* unless your energy engine is already over-producing and you need titanium.
