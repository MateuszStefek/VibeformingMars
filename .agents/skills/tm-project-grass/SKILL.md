# Project: Grass (#023)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -16°C
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step. Gain 3 Plants.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Booster)
**Status: B-TIER / SOLID BIO-ENGINE ENABLER**

Grass is a reliable mid-tier plant card that excels in providing immediate tempo. While its production is modest, the 3 instant plants are often the catalyst for a game-winning greenery conversion.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Immediate Payout:** The 3 plants allow you to "snipe" a greenery conversion. If you have 5 plants in hand, playing Grass lets you convert to a greenery in the same action, protecting your resources from plant-destruction cards like *Asteroid* or *Birds*.
2. **Tag Synergy:** As one of the more affordable plant-tagged cards, it is a vital enabler for high-tier scaling cards like **Insects** or **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)**.
3. **TR Momentum:** In a "Rush" strategy, Grass provides a TR boost (via the greenery) and a long-term production boost for only 11 MC (+3 to draft).
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Increases the instant plant gain to 4, making greenery conversions incredibly easy.
   - **Ecological Zone / Decomposers:** Triggers their bio-resource/VP engines.
   - **Gardener Milestone:** Helps secure the milestone by providing both the greenery and the plant tag.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -16°C requirement means the card cannot be played in the very early game. If the temperature track is lagging, Grass can clog your hand. Only draft early if you have a path to rapid heat production.
- **Production Efficiency:** If you are purely looking for long-term production, *Bushes* or *Trees* (later) are more efficient. Play Grass when the *instant* plants provide a tactical advantage.

### Tips
- **Stealth Conversion:** In the 2024-2025 meta, players use Grass as a "finisher" for their plant engine. Wait until an opponent has spent their actions or their titanium (potential asteroids) before playing Grass and converting your plants to a greenery.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in Generations 2-5. High priority if you have 4-5 plants in hand or need one more plant tag for a high-tier synergy. Skip if the temperature is nowhere near -16°C.
