# Project: Asteroid Mining Consortium (#002)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Jovian
- **Requirements:** 1 Titanium Production
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step. Decrease any player's Titanium production 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Resource Thief)
**Status: S-TIER DISRUPTION / PREMIER OPENING**

Asteroid Mining Consortium (AMC) is one of the most aggressive early-game cards in *Terraforming Mars*. It provides a massive 2-production swing in your favor while securing a high-value Jovian tag.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The Economic Swing:** In a standard game, stealing 1 production is effectively a ~30 MC resource swing over 10 generations (you gain ~30 MC worth of titanium, they lose ~30 MC).
2. **Immediate Payout:** For only 13 MC (+3 to draft), you get 1 production, 1 VP, and a Jovian tag. This is incredibly efficient, especially if you can pay for it with steel or titanium.
3. **Disruption:** It is the ultimate counter to corporations like **Phobolog** or **Point Luna**. Playing AMC against them in Generation 1 can stall their entire game plan.
4. **Jovian Synergy:** The Jovian tag is vital for late-game multipliers and for claiming milestones like *Rim Settler* (Hellas map).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Constraint:** You **must** have 1 titanium production to play this. If you don't have it in your opening hand or Preludes, you must find a way to get it (e.g., *Robinson Industries* action) or pass the card.
- **Late Game Decay:** After Generation 5, the "steal" value drops significantly. In the final generations, you are paying 16 MC for 1 VP and a tag, which is rarely optimal unless it triggers a massive Jovian point bonus.
- **Titanium Usage:** Ensure you have high-value space cards (blue or red) to spend your stolen titanium on. Production is only useful if it can be turned into points or terraforming.

### Tips
- **Robinson Combo:** Use *Robinson Industries* to buy your first titanium production for 4 MC specifically to enable AMC. This 20 MC total investment (13+3+4) for a 2-step titanium production swing is one of the strongest "Gen 1" sequences possible.
- **Hate-Drafting:** In 2-player games, AMC is a mandatory hate-draft if you have titanium production and your opponent doesn't. Do not let them steal from you.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" if you have a path to 1 titanium production. High priority in Generations 1-3. Focus your disruption on the player with the highest space-card potential.
