# Prelude: Smelting Plant

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Raise Oxygen 2 steps. Gain 5 Steel.
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Industrial Rush)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN SOLO MODE**

Smelting Plant is a high-efficiency terraforming Prelude used to secure early Oxygen progress and industrial resources. While it provides zero liquid income, its combination of TR gain and steel makes it a premier tool for both "Rush" strategies and metal-heavy engines.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 2 Oxygen steps grant 2 TR immediately (worth ~16 MC in score and income), and the 5 starting steel adds another 10 MC. At a total value of **~26 MC**, it is significantly more efficient than the standard Prelude benchmark (~21 MC).
2. **Immediate Engine Jumpstart:** The 5 starting steel allows you to play high-cost building projects like **Mohole Area**, **Research Outpost**, or **Space Elevator** in Generation 1 for zero net liquid MC.
3. **Requirement Bumping:** Pushing Oxygen 2 steps immediately hits the first threshold bonus if combined with other oxygen boosts and helps reach requirements for mid-game cards like *Methane from Titan* or *Insects* 1-2 generations early.
4. **Solo Mode MVP:** Oxygen is often the hardest parameter to reach in Solo play; Smelting Plant provides a steady, non-random path to the goal while establishing an industrial base.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Liquidity:** Provides zero MC production or significant cash gain. If your starting hand is expensive, Smelting Plant will not help you establishment your economic engine in Generation 1.
- **Requirement Obsession:** Only take Smelting Plant if you have high-value building-tag "sinks" in hand. If your hand is full of space or bio cards, the steel is "dead weight" that doesn't snowball.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Savings:** Use the 2 Oxygen steps to jump over opponents and reach the 8% Oxygen temperature bonus or the final TR bonus before they can react.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-2 if you have building synergies or are Pursuing a "Rush" strategy. Essential for **Mining Guild** or **UNMI**. Priority 1 in **Solo Mode**. Skip early if you need to prioritize pure income production.
