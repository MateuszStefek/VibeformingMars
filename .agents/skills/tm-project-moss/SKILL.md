# Project: Moss (#122)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** 3 Ocean Tiles.
- **Effect:** Decrease your Plants 1 step. Increase your Plant production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Catalyst)
**Status: C-TIER PRODUCTION / B-TIER SYNERGY ENABLER**

Moss is a low-cost plant card that serves as a vital "bridge" for biological engines. While its direct efficiency is hampered by the plant loss requirement, its role as a cheap tag makes it an essential tool for reaching high-tier payoffs.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Exceptional Tag Efficiency:** For only 4 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a Plant tag. This is the most affordable way to reach the 2-tag or 3-tag requirements for **Insects** or **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)**.
2. **Predictable ROI:** At a total investment of 7 MC, it pays for itself in ~1.5 generations (once the -1 plant penalty is overcome). Since 3 oceans are usually placed by Generation 2-4, it provides steady value for the rest of the game.
3. **Bio-Trinity Support:** Triggers the point/resource engines of **Ecological Zone**, **Viral Enhancers**, and **Decomposers** for a very low upfront cost.
4. **Milestone Support:** A cost-effective way to work toward the **Ecologist** milestone (4 bio tags) or **Gardener**.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Mandatory Resource Loss:** You **must** be able to lose 1 plant to play the card. If you have 0 plants and no way to gain one during the action (e.g., via *Viral Enhancers*), you cannot play Moss.
- **Late Game Waste:** Playing Moss after Generation 7 is statistically poor, as the production won't have time to replace the 1 plant lost plus the 7 MC investment.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** 1 plant production makes you a target for plant-destruction cards (*Birds*, *Virus*) without providing enough volume to quickly convert to a greenery. Always pair Moss with other plant production.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Viral Enhancers** to play Moss even if you have 0 plants. The tag triggers the Enhancer to give you 1 plant, which is then immediately "lost" to satisfy the card's effect, making it effectively free to play.

**Recommendation:** A mid-priority draft in Generations 2-4 if you are pursuing a bio-engine or holding *NRA*. High priority for **Ecoline**. Skip late unless you strictly need the tag for a milestone.
