# Project: Investment Loan (#143)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 3 MC
- **Tags:** Earth, Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Gain 10 MegaCredits.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Booster)
**Status: B-TIER TEMPO / S-TIER IN FINAL ROUND**

Investment Loan is a flexible utility card that "borrows" from your future economy to provide immediate liquidity. While it has a negative production modifier, its low cost and high immediate payout make it an essential tool for hitting critical milestones or securing late-game victory points.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Immediate Tempo:** For a total investment of 6 MC (3 cost + 3 to draft), you gain 10 MC immediately. This net gain of **4 MC** is often the difference between playing a game-defining card (like *Earth Catapult*) now versus waiting an entire generation.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both an **Earth** and an **Event** tag.
   - **Earth Tag:** Triggers *Point Luna* for card draw and benefits from *Teractor* or *Earth Office* discounts.
   - **Event Tag:** Triggers rebates from *Media Group* (+3 MC) or *Interplanetary Cinematics* (+2 MC). With these active, the card can net you **7-9 MC** for almost zero true cost.
3. **Late-Game "Free Money":** In the final generation, the -1 MC production penalty is irrelevant as there are no more production phases. It becomes a pure +7 MC profit injection to fund one last greenery or city.
4. **Milestone Racing:** Use it to "cheat" out the final credits needed to claim a contested milestone like *Generalist* or *Builder* before an opponent.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Decay:** If played in Generation 1-2 without a clear follow-up, the -1 MC production will cost you ~10 MC over the course of the game, canceling out the initial gain. Only play early if it enables a high-impact engine piece.
- **Drafting Overhead:** Do not keep this in your hand for several generations if you don't need the liquid cash. The 3 MC draft cost is a significant drag on your early-game snowball.

### Tips
- **Robinson Industries Counter:** Use Investment Loan to intentionally drop your MC production to hit a specific threshold for **Robinson Industries** or to contribution toward the **Benefactor** award.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final round. High priority for **Point Luna** or **Media Group**. Skip early unless it secures a milestone or a top-tier engine starter.
