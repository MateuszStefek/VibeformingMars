# Project: Research Outpost (#020)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 18 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Building, City
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** You pay 1 MC less for all cards you play (including this one). Place a City tile next to no other tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent Greenery.


## Strategic Analysis (The Ultimate Engine Starter)
**Status: S-TIER / TOP 5 ENGINE PIECE**

Research Outpost is arguably the most versatile and powerful early-game card in *Terraforming Mars*. It combines long-term economic scaling with board presence and critical requirements enablers.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The Global Discount:** A universal -1 MC discount on every card is a massive cumulative advantage. While only half as strong as *Earth Catapult*, it is much cheaper and easier to play.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Provides a **Science**, **Building**, and **City** tag. This single card can hit requirements for *AI Central*, contribute to the *Builder* and *Mayor* milestones, and be paid for entirely with Steel.
3. **No Energy Infrastructure:** Unlike almost every other city in the game, Research Outpost does not require energy production. This makes it a "free" city that provides +1 MC production (from TR) and board control without any overhead.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University / Olympus Conference:** The Science tag triggers these draw engines immediately.
   - **Mining Guild:** A perfect dump for starting steel to establish a discount engine in Generation 1.
   - **Earth Catapult:** Stack the discounts for -3 MC per card, effectively making most low-cost cards free.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Placement Restriction:** You **must** place it next to no other tile. This is easy in the early game but can become impossible by Generation 7-8 on a crowded board. **Play it early** or risk it becoming a dead draw.
- **Opportunity Cost:** In Generation 1, ensure that playing the Outpost doesn't prevent you from establishing critical MC production (like *Acquired Company*). If your budget is tight, it is often better to wait until Gen 2 to drop the Outpost with Steel.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Blocking:** Use the Outpost to "claim" a remote corner of the map or a high-value bonus spot (like the 3-plant spot on Elysium) where opponents cannot easily build toward you.
- **Steel over Cash:** Always prioritize paying for the Outpost with Steel. This preserves your liquid MC for cards that cannot be discounted by metal.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Keep" in almost every opening hand. Priority 1 for any engine-building strategy. Aim to play between Generations 1 and 3.
