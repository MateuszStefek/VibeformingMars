# Project: Mohole Area (#112)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 20 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Heat production 4 steps. Place a Mohole Area tile on an area reserved for Ocean.


## Strategic Analysis (The Heat Engine Foundation)
**Status: S-TIER FOR RUSH / A-TIER FOR HELION**

Mohole Area is the most efficient heat production card in the base game. It provides a massive jumpstart to the temperature track and serves as the primary tool for players looking to accelerate the game's end.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 20 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 4 steps of heat production. This 5.75 MC per production ratio is one of the best in the game, yielding a TR every 2 generations once fully active.
2. **Steel Sink Catalyst:** As a Building card, the 20 MC cost can be paid entirely with **Steel**. This allows you to convert rigid building resources into a powerful terraforming engine without draining your liquid MC.
3. **Placement Bonus Snipe:** Must be placed on an **Ocean reserve**. This allows you to snag high-value placement bonuses (like 2 titanium or 2 cards) while also securing the temperature-production lead.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Helion:** The premier synergy. Since Helion can use Heat as MC, Mohole Area effectively becomes a +4 MC production card for only 20 MC.
   - **Robotic Workforce:** The ultimate pairing. Copying Mohole Area gives you a total of 8 heat production (1 TR every generation) for a very low combined investment.
   - **Mining Guild:** Triggers the corporation's passive and provides a Building tag for milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While efficient, it provides zero immediate points or TR. If you are in Generation 1 and have to choose between this and *Acquired Company*, the income is usually more flexible unless you are committed to a "Rush" strategy.
- **Ocean Bottleneck:** Once all 9 ocean spots are filled with actual Ocean tiles, this card **cannot** be played. Monitor the ocean track closely; if rushers are filling the board with water, discard Mohole Area.
- **Timing:** Its value drops significantly after Generation 5. Only draft late if you need the Building tag for the **Builder** milestone.

### Tips
- **Temperature Threshold Sniping:** Use the 4 heat production to hit the -24°C or -20°C thresholds faster than your opponents, securing the bonus heat production or ocean placement for yourself.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-3. Essential for **Helion** or anyone holding **Robotic Workforce**. Skip in the late game or if oceans are already mostly placed.
