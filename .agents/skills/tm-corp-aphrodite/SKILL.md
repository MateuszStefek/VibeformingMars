# Corporation: Aphrodite

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Venus Next
- **Starting MC:** 47
- **Tags:** Venus, Plant
- **Starting Production:** 1 Plant
- **Effect:** Whenever any Venus parameter is increased (by you or anyone else), gain 2 MC.

## Strategic Analysis (The Venusian Gardener)
**Status: C-TIER / SITUATIONAL**

Aphrodite is a niche corporation that attempts to bridge the gap between plant strategies and the Venus expansion. While it has a solid starting plant production, its economy is highly dependent on the pace of Venus terraforming.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Passive Income:** In games where players are actively pushing the Venus track (e.g., to reach World Government bonuses or specific card requirements), Aphrodite gains significant "free" money.
2. **Plant Synergy:** Starting with plant production and a plant tag makes it a natural candidate for *Ecological Zone*, *Decomposers*, or *Herbivores*.
3. **Venus Tag Utility:** Early access to a Venus tag helps meet requirements for powerful Venus cards like *Aphrodite* (the card) or *Stratospheric Birds*.

### Synergies:
- **Venus Cards:** Any card that increases the Venus track essentially comes with a 2 MC rebate.
- **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid:** A premier card for Aphrodite, providing plants and a Venus bump (plus a rebate).
- **Insects / Ecological Zone:** Good for leveraging the starting plant production.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The Venus Trap:** Do not force the Venus track yourself just to get the 2 MC rebate unless it also furthers your TR or VP goals. The rebate makes the track cheaper, but not necessarily "free."
- **Opponent Dependent:** Aphrodite performs better in 4-5 player games where more people are likely to touch the Venus track.

**Recommendation:** Pick if your starting hand has strong plant synergies or high-value Venus cards. Avoid if the game looks like it will be a pure heat/oxygen rush.
