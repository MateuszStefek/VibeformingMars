# Corporation: Morning Star Inc. (MSI)

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Venus Next
- **Starting MC:** 50
- **Tags:** Venus
- **First Action:** Reveal cards from the deck until you have 3 cards with a Venus tag; take them into your hand and discard the rest.
- **Effect:** Your Venus requirements for playing cards and using actions are modified by +/- 2 steps (your choice).

## Strategic Analysis (The Venusian High-Roller)
**Status: A-TIER / SPECIALIST**

Morning Star Inc. (MSI) is the definitive Venus corporation. It excels by ignoring the slow progression of the Venus track and playing high-requirement cards far earlier than anyone else.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Card Advantage:** Starting with 3 guaranteed Venus cards provides a massive head start in hand quality and options.
2. **Requirement Breaking:** The +/- 2 Venus requirement shift is exceptionally powerful. MSI can play *Venus Governor* or *Way Station* when the Venus track is still at low levels.
3. **High Starting Capital:** 50 MC is a very healthy start, allowing for a flexible opening.

### Synergies:
- **Venus Governor / Way Station:** The ultimate goals for MSI. Playing these early secures a dominant VP and income lead.
- **Stratospheric Birds:** Can be played at 10% Venus instead of 14%, getting the animal engine running early.
- **Maxwell Base:** Access to early Venus actions.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Strong for 'Terraformer'. High starting capital allows for early milestone locking.
### Hellas
Good for 'Diversifier' if you can branch into different productions early.
### Elysium
High synergy with 'Legend' milestone due to frequent event plays.

### Tips
- **Requirement Management:** Always check if you can "cheat" a card out 2 steps early. This often allows you to ignore the Venus track entirely while still playing its best cards.
- **The Venus Milestone:** MSI should almost always win the 'Venus Governor' milestone if it's in play.

**Recommendation:** Very strong if Venus Next is active. Pick if you want a reliable card-draw and high-requirement playstyle.
