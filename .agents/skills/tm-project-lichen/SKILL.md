# Project: Lichen (#140)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min -24°C
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Requirement Enabler)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER SYNERGY ENABLER**

Lichen is a simple, cost-efficient plant card that serves as a cornerstone for biological engines. While its direct production is modest, its role as a "cheap tag" makes it an essential tool for reaching high-tier requirements.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Tag Efficiency:** For only 7 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a Plant tag. This is one of the most affordable ways to reach the 3-tag requirement for **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or to boost the scaling of **Insects**.
2. **Predictable ROI:** At a total investment of 10 MC, it pays for itself in ~2.5 generations (compared to the community value of plant production). Since -24°C is usually reached by Generation 3-4, it provides steady value for the remainder of the game.
3. **Bio-Trinity Support:** Triggers the point/resource engines of **Ecological Zone**, **Viral Enhancers**, and **Decomposers** for a very low upfront cost.
4. **Milestone Support:** One of the most efficient cards for working toward the **Ecologist** milestone (4 bio tags) or **Gardener**.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The -24°C requirement is low, but in very slow games, it can still delay the card until Generation 4 or 5. Do not hold it from Generation 1 unless you have a path to rapid heat production or are playing **Ecoline**.
- **Predator Vulnerability:** 1 plant production makes you a target for plant-destruction cards (*Birds*, *Virus*) without providing enough volume to quickly convert to a greenery. Always pair Lichen with other plant production to reach the 8-plant threshold faster.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Lichen specifically as your "primer" tag for **NRA**. Most opponents expect a plant engine to take several generations to build; Lichen allows you to drop a tag and production for only 7 MC, accelerating your path to high-impact biological payoffs.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 2-4 if you are pursuing a bio-engine or holding *NRA*. High priority for **Ecoline**. Skip late if you already have 5+ plant production.
