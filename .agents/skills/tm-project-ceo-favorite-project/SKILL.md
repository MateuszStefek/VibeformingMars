# Project: CEO's Favorite Project (#088)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 1 MC
- **Tags:** Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Add 1 resource to any of your cards that has at least one resource on it already.


## Strategic Analysis (The Precision Finisher)
**Status: S-TIER VP EFFICIENCY / LATE-GAME STAPLE**

CEO's Favorite Project (CFP) is one of the most cost-effective ways to secure Victory Points in the final turns of the game. While it has zero "engine" value, its ability to bypass standard resource generation makes it a high-priority "point-slam" tool.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** The card costs only 1 MC (+3 to draft). If used on a card that grants 1 VP per resource (e.g., *Birds*, *Fish*, *Small Animals*), you are paying **4 MC for 1 VP**—nearly twice as efficient as the community benchmark.
2. **Physics Complex God-Tier Combo:** If used on *Physics Complex* (2 VP per resource), CFP yields **2 VP for 4 MC**, which is arguably the strongest point-to-cost ratio in the entire game.
3. **Event Rebates:** As a Red (Event) card, it triggers rebates from **Media Group** (+3 MC) and **Interplanetary Cinematics** (+2 MC). With these active, you can actually **gain money** while adding a victory point to your board.
4. **Milestone Padding:** Use it as a cheap way to increase your event count for the **Legend** milestone (Elysium map) or to hit requirements for playing other high-tier events.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Early Game Speculation:** **Do not keep this in your starting hand.** Spending 3 MC to hold a card that cannot be played until you have an active resource-bearing card stalls your early-game engine.
- **Search for Life Anti-Synergy:** While you *can* add a resource to *Search for Life*, it provides zero extra points. *Search for Life* grants a flat 3 VP for having *any* resources; it does not scale with additional ones.
- **Requirement Rule:** You **must** have a card that already has at least one resource on it to use CFP's primary effect. Ensure you have played your animals/microbes first.

### Tips
- **Resource Evening:** Use CFP in the final generation to "even out" cards that require two resources for a point (like *Security Fleet* or certain microbes), turning a "wasted" resource into a guaranteed point.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" in the final 2-3 generations. High priority for players with *Physics Complex* or 1:1 VP animal cards. Skip in the early game.
