# Corporation: Robinson Industries

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Starting MC:** 47
- **Action:** Spend 4 MC to increase your lowest production 1 step (if tied, you choose which).

## Strategic Analysis (The Balanced Generalist)
**Status: B-TIER / ADAPTIVE**

Robinson Industries is a unique "math" corporation. Its power lies in its ability to efficiently fill gaps in its production at a flat rate of 4 MC, which is significantly cheaper than most cards providing production.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **Production Efficiency:** 4 MC for a production increase is a bargain, especially for Heat or Energy production which usually costs 7-11 MC via cards.
2. **Milestone Synergy:** Robinson is exceptionally good at chasing 'Generalist' or 'Diversifier' milestones by ensuring all production tracks are moving.
3. **No-Card Play:** On turns where you have a bad draft, you can still spend your money effectively using the corp action.

### Synergies:
- **Generalist Milestone:** Often an easy 5 points.
- **Production Preludes:** Use Preludes to boost the productions you *don't* want to spend the action on, so you can focus the action on the high-value ones (like Titanium or MC).
- **Early Energy:** Use the action to get 1-2 energy production early to power blue cards without needing to find energy cards.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The Decay Factor:** Robinson's ability is strongest in Generations 1-4. By Gen 7, spending 4 MC for 1 production that will only pay back 3-4 times is usually a waste. Shift to playing high-VP cards late.
- **Tie-Breaking:** If multiple productions are at zero, use your choice to prioritize MC production or Titanium production first.

**Recommendation:** Pick if the 'Generalist' milestone is available or if you have a hand that needs specific productions (like Energy) to function.
