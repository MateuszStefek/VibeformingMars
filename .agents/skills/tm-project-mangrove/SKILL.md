# Project: Mangrove (#039)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 12 MC
- **Tags:** Plant
- **Requirements:** Min +4°C
- **Effect:** Place a Greenery tile on an area reserved for Ocean (ignores normal greenery placement rules). Raise Oxygen 1 step.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Ocean-Greenery Specialist)
**Status: S-TIER LATE-GAME POINT BOMB**

Mangrove is one of the most efficient point-scoring cards in the game. It provides a unique board-control advantage by allowing greeneries to "take over" ocean spots, resulting in massive victory point swings for a very low cost.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unbeatable Efficiency:** For 12 MC (+3 to draft), you receive a card worth **2 to 4+ VP**:
   - **1 VP** printed on the card.
   - **1 VP** from the Greenery tile itself.
   - **1 VP** from the TR gain of the Oxygen step.
   - **1+ VP** from adjacency to your own cities.
   This ~4 MC per point ratio is arguably the best for a non-multiplier card in the late game.
2. **Rule-Breaking Placement:** Must be placed on an **Ocean reserve** tile. This allows you to "cheat" a greenery onto the board in locations where plants normally cannot grow, potentially connecting remote city clusters or blocking an opponent's "ocean walk."
3. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** 
   - **Plant Tag:** Triggers *Viral Enhancers*, *Decomposers*, and *Ecological Zone* for even more resources/points.
   - **Milestones:** Contributes to both the **Gardener** and **Ecologist** milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Barrier:** The +4°C requirement is extremely steep. In many games, the temperature only reaches this point in the final 2 generations. **Never keep this in your opening hand** unless you have a path to rapid heat or the *Inventrix* corporation.
- **Ocean Bottleneck:** Once all 9 ocean spots are filled with actual Ocean tiles, this card **cannot** be played. Monitor the ocean track closely; if rushers are filling the board with water, discard Mangrove.
- **Oxygen Threshold:** While it has no Oxygen requirement, its value drops if Oxygen is already maxed.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Sniping:** Use Mangrove to take an ocean spot adjacent to an opponent's city. This denies them the TR and placement bonus of a Standard Project ocean while securing a point-stealing greenery for yourself.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the final third of the game (Generations 7-9). Essential for anyone with cities near ocean spots. Skip early to preserve capital for engine building.
