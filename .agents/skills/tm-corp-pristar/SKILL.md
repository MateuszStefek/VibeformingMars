# Corporation: Pristar

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Turmoil
- **Starting MC:** 53
- **Starting TR:** -2 (Starts at 18 TR)
- **Effect:** During the production phase, if you did not raise your Terraform Rating this generation, gain 6 MC and add 1 preservation resource to this card.
- **Effect:** 1 VP per preservation resource.

## Strategic Analysis (The Isolationist)
**Status: C-TIER / ENGINE BUILDER**

Pristar is the ultimate "welfare" corporation for engine builders who want to ignore terraforming. It rewards players for playing a slow, card-heavy game by providing a massive 6 MC bonus every turn they don't contribute to the global parameters.

### Why it is a viable play:
1. **The "Non-Action" Bonus:** Gain 6 MC (equivalent to 6 production) for doing nothing. This allows Pristar to build a massive card-based engine without worrying about TR.
2. **Passive VP Engine:** The preservation resources act as a slow but steady clock of victory points, rewarding longevity.
3. **High Starting Cash:** Starting with 53 MC offsets the initial TR penalty.

### Synergies:
- **Pure Engine Builds:** Cards like *AI Central*, *Earth Catapult*, and Jovian multipliers that don't increase TR.
- **Blue Card Strategies:** Focus on actions that generate resources or VPs without touching the global parameters.
- **Turmoil Expansion:** Using delegates to influence the game doesn't count as raising TR, allowing Pristar to remain active in the political meta.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.

### Tips
- **The TR Trap:** If you *must* raise TR (e.g., via a high-value card like *Large Convoy*), try to do it all in one generation to minimize the number of times you lose the 6 MC bonus.
- **Watch the Clock:** Pristar fails if the game ends too quickly. If opponents are rushing terraforming, your 6 MC bonus won't be enough to catch up.

**Recommendation:** A fun, unique choice for players who enjoy "engine-only" strategies. Risky in fast games.
