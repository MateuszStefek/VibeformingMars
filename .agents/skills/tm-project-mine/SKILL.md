# Project: Mine (#056)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Steel production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Building Block)
**Status: S-TIER OPENER / B-TIER UTILITY**

Mine is the foundational production card for building-heavy strategies. While unexciting, its high efficiency and low cost make it one of the most reliable ways to solve economic bottlenecks in the early game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For only 4 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 1 steel production (worth 2 MC per generation). This yields a **4-generation break-even point**, one of the fastest in the game.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Building tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Essential for reaching the threshold for cards like *Research Outpost* or claiming the **Builder** milestone early.
   - **Steel Production:** Fuels powerful late-game building engines like **Electro Catapult** or **Space Elevator**.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Advanced Alloys:** The "God Tier" combo. Each steel becomes worth 3 MC, making the Mine's production worth 50% more and accelerating its ROI to ~2.5 generations.
   - **Mining Guild:** Triggers the corporation's passive and contributes to their dominant industrial engine.
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate resource gain upon placement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Steel Sinks Requirement:** Steel production is only valuable if you have cards to spend it on. If your hand is full of space tags or biological engines, Mine is an inefficient use of 7 MC. **Draft only if you have high-value building targets** (Cities, infrastructure) in your hand or expected to be drawn.
- **Late Game irrelevance:** After Generation 5, the +1 steel production will rarely have time to pay off. Only draft late if you strictly need the Building tag for a milestone or award.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Mine specifically to hit the 8-building requirement for the **Builder** milestone on a budget. Since it is so cheap, it is the most efficient way to close the gap in a tight race for the 5 points.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in Generations 1-2 for any corporation, especially those with building synergies or Mining Guild. Excellent opening hand card. Skip after Generation 4.
