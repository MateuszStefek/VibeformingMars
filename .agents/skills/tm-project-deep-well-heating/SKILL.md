# Project: Deep Well Heating (#003)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 13 MC
- **Tags:** Power, Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Energy production 1 step. Raise temperature 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Dual Hybrid)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / MID-GAME BRIDGE**

Deep Well Heating is a reliable hybrid card that offers both immediate terraforming and long-term production. While not as flashy as some engine starters, its flexibility and steel-payable cost make it a solid "worker" card in many competitive builds.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **Efficiency:** For 13 MC (+3 to draft), you gain 1 Energy production and 1 TR (from the temperature step). A Standard Project (SP) for energy costs 7 MC, and an SP for temperature costs 14 MC. By playing this card, you are receiving **21 MC of value for a 16 MC investment**, a very respectable discount.
2. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card, it can be paid for with Steel. This allows you to convert excess building resources into immediate TR and energy without spending liquid MC.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **AI Central / Development Center:** Provides the 1 energy production needed to fuel these high-tier draw engines.
   - **Colonies Expansion:** Essential for hitting the 3-energy requirement for trading with high-value moons like *Luna* or *Ceres*.
   - **Mining Guild:** Triggers the corporation's passive and provides a Building tag for milestones.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Impact:** While efficient, it only moves the temperature by 1 step. In a fast-paced game, you might prefer a more impactful event like *Big Asteroid* or *Comet* to claim thresholds.
- **Energy Waste:** If you already have 3+ energy production and zero use for more, the 13 MC is better spent on direct terraforming or card draw.
- **Timing:** Best played in Generations 1-4 to maximize the energy production income. Its value drops to "D-Tier" in the final two generations.

### Tips
- **Milestone Snipe:** Use the temperature step to hit the -24°C or -20°C thresholds to claim the "Heat Production" or "Ocean" bonuses before an opponent's turn.

**Recommendation:** A solid mid-priority draft in the early game. High priority if you have a surplus of Steel or need Energy for trading/draw engines. Skip late unless you specifically need 1 TR to win.
