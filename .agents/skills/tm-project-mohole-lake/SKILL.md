# Project: Mohole Lake (#049)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 31 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Raise temperature 1 step. Place 1 Ocean tile. Gain 3 Plants.
- **Action:** Add 1 Microbe or Animal to any card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Versatile Engine)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR MINING GUILD**

Mohole Lake is a high-cost hybrid card that offers immediate terraforming, resource gain, and a repeatable engine payoff. While expensive, its role as a "steel dump" and its multi-faceted value make it a potent tool in the mid-to-late game.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Steel Sink:** As a Building card, the entire 31 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the mid-game, this is one of the best ways to convert surplus metal into 2 TR (temp + ocean), placement bonuses, and immediate points.
2. **Instant Bio-Catalyst:** The 3 instant plants often provide the exact "8th plant" needed for a greenery conversion, adding another 1 TR and 1 VP to the card's immediate value.
3. **Engine Multiplier:** The action (adding a resource to another card) is highly efficient when used on **1 VP/Animal** cards (like *Fish* or *Birds*) or high-tier microbe cards (like *GHG Bacteria*). 
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Building tag toward the **Builder** milestone and the TR from terraforming contributes to the **Terraformer** milestone.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Upfront Cost:** 34 MC (cost + draft) is a massive hit. Unless you have a surplus of steel or are playing **Credicor**, the liquid MC investment is often too high for the immediate return.
- **Hybrid Identity:** The card pushes the game end (via terraforming) while also providing an engine action (which wants a long game). If you play this, commit to the terraforming rush to maximize the production value.
- **Requirement Rule:** You **must** have a card that can receive the microbe/animal to utilize the blue action. Ensure you have played your biological enablers first.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Sniping:** Use the ocean placement to take a spot adjacent to an opponent's city, denying them the TR and placement bonus of a Standard Project ocean while securing your own resource gain.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 4-7 if you have a surplus of Steel or a 1:1 VP animal card. Skip early if you need to prioritize pure income production. Essential for **Mining Guild**.
