# Prelude: Dome Farming

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 2 steps. Increase your Plant production 1 step.
- **Tags:** Plant, Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Reliable Hybrid)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / A-TIER ENGINE ENABLER**

Dome Farming is a solid, mid-tier Prelude that provides a balanced foundation for both economic and biological strategies. While its direct yield is modest, its rare combination of tags and reliable income make it a premier tool for establishing mid-game momentum.

### Why it is a good pick:
1. **Economic Stability:** Unlike other plant-based Preludes (e.g., *Biosphere Support*), Dome Farming provides **+2 MC production** without any penalties. This ensures you have a stable liquid income base while slowly building your greenery engine.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides both a **Plant** and a **Building** tag in Generation 1.
   - **Plant Tag:** Essential for reaching the 3-tag requirement for **Nitrogen-Rich Asteroid (NRA)** or satisfying the scaling for **Insects**.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to trigger building-related discounts or contribute toward the **Builder** milestone early.
3. **The "Robotic Workforce" Power Play:** One of the best targets for *Robotic Workforce*. Copying Dome Farming in Generation 1 or 2 provides a total of +4 MC and +2 plant production for a very low combined cost.
4. **Milestone Support:** Contributes to the **Builder**, **Ecologist**, and **Specialist** milestones simultaneously.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Production Volume:** 1 plant production is too slow to impact the game on its own. Only take this if you already have other plant-boosting cards in hand or are playing as **Ecoline**.
- **Late Game Waste:** If you aren't building a biological engine, the plant production is effectively "dead weight." In a pure industrial or space engine, a pure income Prelude like *Allied Bank* is strictly superior.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Dome Farming specifically as a "bridge" card. The combination of income and the Plant tag allows you to fix your early economy while ensuring you have the enabler needed for a game-winning bio-payoff in the mid-game.

**Recommendation:** A solid "Safe" pick. High priority for **Ecoline**, **Mining Guild**, or anyone holding **Robotic Workforce**. skip early in favor of pure MC production if you have zero biological plans.
