# Prelude: Early Settlement

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Plant production 1 step. Place a City tile.
- **Tags:** Building, City.

## Strategic Analysis (The Board Presence Anchor)
**Status: D-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR THARSIS REPUBLIC**

Early Settlement is often considered a "trap" card for newer players. While an early city looks impressive, its high opportunity cost and slow biological yield make it one of the less efficient Preludes for establishing a winning economic engine.

### Why it is a good pick (situational):
1. **Tharsis Republic Synergy:** The absolute best use case. It grants Tharsis +1 MC production and 3 MC immediately upon placement. On the **Tharsis (Base) map**, it puts you 2/3 of the way toward the **Mayor Milestone** in the very first generation.
2. **High-Value Map Placements:** Use the city to immediately "snag" a lucrative board bonus before opponents can react.
   - **Hellas:** Claim the "3 Card" or "6 MC" ocean placement spot.
   - **Elysium:** Secure the "3 Plant" spot (Olympus Mons) to jumpstart a greenery engine.
3. **Double Tag Catalyst:** Provides both a **Building** and a **City** tag in Generation 1. This is essential for racing for the **Builder** milestone or contributing toward the **Landlord** award.
4. **Ecoline Support:** The plant production helps Ecoline hit the 7-plant threshold for their first greenery as early as Generation 2.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Economic Stagnation:** Preludes are meant to kickstart your production. Early Settlement provides a city (worth ~14 MC) and 1 plant production (~10 MC value), but cities produce zero resources. A Prelude that provides raw MC or metal production is usually better for early-game tempo.
- **Plant Vulnerability:** Having only 1 plant production makes you a target for early-game "plant-killing" events (*Asteroids*, *Virus*). You often lose your plants before you can ever convert them.
- **Opportunity Cost:** **Self-Sufficient Settlement** (+2 MC prod, City) is generally considered superior because it provides flexible liquid income instead of slow-burn plant production.

### Tips
- **Milestone Sniper:** Only take Early Settlement if you have a specific plan to claim a milestone (like *Mayor* or *Builder*) by Generation 3. If you can't secure the 5 points early, the long-term inefficiency of the card makes it a poor choice.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft. High priority for **Tharsis Republic** or **Ecoline**. Skip early in favor of raw production unless a critical board bonus or milestone is within reach.
