# Project: Titanium Mine (#144)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 7 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Increase your Titanium production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Space Engine Foundation)
**Status: S-TIER OPENER / A-TIER UTILITY**

Titanium Mine is the foundational production card for industrial and Jovian strategies. While unexciting, its high efficiency, low cost, and lack of requirements make it one of the most reliable ways to establish an early-game economic lead.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional ROI:** For only 7 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 1 titanium production (worth 3 MC per generation). This yields a **3.3-generation break-even point**, one of the fastest in the game. In a standard 10-generation match, playing this in Gen 1 provides a massive net profit of ~20 MC in value.
2. **Requirement Enabler:** One of the most important roles for Titanium Mine is satisfying the requirement for **Asteroid Mining Consortium** (requires 1 titanium production). Playing the Mine in Gen 1 allows you to steal an opponent's production immediately, a potentially game-winning blow.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Phobolog:** The premier pairing. Phobolog starts with high-value titanium, making Titanium Mine an immediate priority to snowball their resource advantage.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Each titanium becomes worth 4 MC, making the production worth 33% more and accelerating its ROI even further.
   - **Colonies Expansion:** Allows you to **Trade** using titanium, providing a flexible way to gain cards, energy, or other resources without spending liquid MC.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Opportunity Cost:** While it's a great card, top players will sometimes pass it in favor of **card draw** (e.g., *AI Central*) or **massive discounts** (e.g., *Earth Office*), as those have higher ceiling potential for an engine.
- **Titanium Sinks Requirement:** Titanium production is only valuable if you keep drawing Space cards. If your hand is full of building tags or bio-engines, Titanium Mine is an inefficient use of 10 MC.
- **Late Game Decay:** Like all production cards, its value drops after Generation 5. Only draft late if you strictly need the Building tag for the **Builder** milestone or the **Miner** award.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Titanium Mine to hit the requirements for powerful mid-game cards like **Mass Converter** or **Quantum Extractor**. The production is a primary benefit, but the tag utility stabilizes your engine rollout.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in Generations 1-2 for any corporation, especially those with Titanium or space synergies. Skip after Generation 4.
