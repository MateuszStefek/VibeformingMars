# Corporation: Arcadian Communities

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 40 MC, 10 Steel
- **Ability:** Reserve areas with markers; gain 3 MC when placing tiles there.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: B-TIER / BOARD CONTROL**

Excels at securing high-value spots early and leveraging steel for building infrastructure.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Key for 'Builder' milestone. Claim the steel placement spots early to snowball production.
### Hellas
Extremely strong map due to the abundance of steel placement bonuses in the south.
### Elysium
Watch out for 'Industrialist' award. Placement bonuses are more spread out, so card draw is more vital.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Arcadian Communities's unique mechanics.
