# Project: Insulation (#152)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 2 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Heat production any number of steps and increase your MegaCredit (MC) production the same number of steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Pivot)
**Status: C-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER AWARD SNIPER**

Insulation is a highly situational utility card used primarily to manipulate endgame scoring or to salvage a "dead" heat engine. While it provides no independent value, its low cost and flexible conversion make it a powerful tactical tool in the final generations.

### Why it is a good play:
1. **The "Banker" Award Swing:** The most common high-level use for Insulation. By converting high heat production into MC production in the final generation, you can often "poach" the 5 VP from the Banker award, catching opponents who thought they had a safe lead.
2. **Salvaging Dead Heat:** Once the temperature track is maxed out, heat production has zero value (unless you are *Helion*). Insulation allows you to "wash" that useless production into liquid income for the final round's city and greenery placements.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Manutech:** Triggers the corporation's passive resource gain for every step of production increased, potentially yielding a massive cash injection in a single action.
   - **Solo Mode:** Essential for pivoting from a temperature-rush build into a point-maximizing economy for the final rounds.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Efficiency Paradox:** The cost of building heat production is generally higher than the value of the MC production you get back. Never build heat production *specifically* to convert it with Insulation. It is a tool for correcting over-production, not an engine in itself.
- **Draft Timing:** **Never draft this early.** It will clog your hand and waste 3 MC of draft capital for most of the game. Only pick it up in the final 2-3 generations if you already have 4+ heat production.
- **No Tag Value:** Lacks any tags, meaning it provides zero synergy for *Science* requirements or milestones.

### Tips
- **Hidden Threat:** If you are the leader in MC production, check your opponents' heat production before funding the **Banker** award. If an opponent is holding Insulation, they can easily flip the rankings on the final turn.

**Recommendation:** An "Easy Pass" for the first two-thirds of the game. A high-priority "tech" pick in the final generation if you have excess heat production or are racing for the Banker award. Excellent in Solo mode.
