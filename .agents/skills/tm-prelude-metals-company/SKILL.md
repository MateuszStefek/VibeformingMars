# Prelude: Metals Company

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step. Increase your Steel production 1 step. Increase your Titanium production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Industrial Engine)
**Status: S-TIER / THE ULTIMATE "GENERALIST" START**

Metals Company is widely considered one of the strongest economic Preludes in the game. It provides a balanced industrial foundation for zero upfront cost, allowing you to establish a dominant lead in both building and space-based projects from the very first turn.

### Why it is a top-tier pick:
1. **Unrivaled Production Value:** Functionally equivalent to **+6 MC production** (1 MC + 2 Steel + 3 Titanium). Unlike *Business Empire*, which gives 6 production but costs 6 MC upfront, Metals Company is completely free, making it superior for your Generation 1 tempo.
2. **Exceptional Versatility:** By boosting all three economic tracks, Metals Company ensures you are ready for any cards you draw. Whether you find building tags, space tags, or events, you have the industrial base to fund them.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Advanced Alloys:** The "God Tier" combo. If your metal is worth more, the Metals Company production jumps to **8 MC equivalent per generation**, outperforming every other economy Prelude in the game.
   - **Phobolog:** Allows the corporation to jumpstart their titanium engine without draining their liquid MC.
   - **Mining Guild:** Triggers the corporation's passive and contributes to their dominant industrial engine.
   - **Generalist Milestone:** Checks off three of the six required production categories immediately, making it the premier tool for securing the 5 points in Gen 1 or 2.
4. **Colonies Synergy:** Provides the titanium production needed to **Trade** for high-value resources without needing to play space cards.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Zero Tags:** Lacks any tags, providing no progress toward milestones like *Builder* or *Ecologist*. In games with *Turmoil*, you may miss out on early Senate influence.
- **Metal Sink Requirement:** Production is only valuable if you have cards to spend it on. If your hand is exclusively focused on plants or microbes, the metals will sit in your inventory, losing their snowball potential.

### Tips
- **Tempo over Value:** Only skip Metals Company if you have a "high-roll" combo (e.g., *Ecology Experts* + *Kelp Farming*) that defines the game end faster than the production can pay off.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Pick" for any long-form or engine-building strategy. Priority 1 for **Mining Guild**, **Phobolog**, or anyone holding **Advanced Alloys**. skip only in extreme rush scenarios.
