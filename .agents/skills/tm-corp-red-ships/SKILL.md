# Corporation: Red Ships

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 40 MC
- **Ability:** MC rewards for adjacency to oceans.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: B-TIER / ECONOMY**

Requires specific board setups but provides high liquidity in the mid-game.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Ideal for 'Mayor'. The dense ocean clusters are perfect for maximizing city/greenery points.
### Hellas
Placement bonuses in the south are key for early economy.
### Elysium
The 'Estate Dealer' award is highly competitive; secure ocean adjacencies early.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Red Ships's unique mechanics.
