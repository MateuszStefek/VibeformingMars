# Project: Lagrange Observatory (#153)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Science, Space
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Draw 1 card.
- **VP:** 1


## Strategic Analysis (The Role Compression Tool)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER ENGINE ENABLER**

Lagrange Observatory is a high-utility "filler" card that excels in bridging the gap between early-game setup and late-game payoffs. Its value is primarily driven by its dual tags and its ability to "replace itself" in your hand.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Science Tag Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free Science tag. This is essential for reaching the threshold for game-defining cards like **AI Central** (3 tags) or **Antigravity Technology** (7 tags).
2. **Titanium Conversion:** As a Space card, it can be paid for with Titanium. In the late game, this is an efficient way to convert 3 titanium into 1 VP and a fresh card without losing card parity.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mars University / Olympus Conference:** The premier combo. The tag triggers these draw engines, effectively drawing you **two cards** (the card effect + the trigger) for a very low net cost.
   - **Media Group / Optimal Aerobraking:** Triggers rebates for space-based plays, potentially making the card free or even cash-positive.
   - **Vitor:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Tempo Loss:** In Generations 1-2, spending 12 MC (card + draft) for a single draw and a point is statistically "slow." Only play early if the Science tag immediately unlocks a high-value requirement card in your hand.
- **Solo Mode Inefficiency:** In Solo play, points are secondary to terraforming. Paying 12 MC for a random draw is often too expensive unless you are desperate for a Science tag to enable an oxygen/heat producer.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use Lagrange Observatory as your "third" science tag. Most opponents expect a science engine to take 4-5 generations to establish; dropping a requirement-free tag can accelerate your AI Central rollout by a full generation, catching the table off guard.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft for any engine-building or science-heavy strategy. Excellent as a mid-to-late game point dump for excess titanium. Skip early if you need to prioritize immediate production.
