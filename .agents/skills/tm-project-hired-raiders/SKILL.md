# Project: Hired Raiders (#124)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 1 MC
- **Tags:** Event
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Steal either 2 Steel or 3 MegaCredits (MC) from any player.


## Strategic Analysis (The Tempo Swing)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN 2-PLAYER GAMES**

Hired Raiders is a low-cost, high-tempo disruption card. While its impact in large multiplayer games is minor, its ability to steal resources in the early game can cripple an opponent's math and provide you with a critical resource boost for a nearly zero-cost investment.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Net Gain:** For a total investment of 4 MC (1 cost + 3 to draft), you are receiving 3-4 MC of value (3 MC or 2 steel worth 4 MC). This makes the card effectively cost-neutral for you while causing a direct loss to an opponent.
2. **2-Player Powerhouse:** In 1v1 games, it is a double-swing. You aren't just gaining resources; you are widening the gap between you and your only competitor. Stealing 2 steel from a player with high metal production can delay their next building by an entire generation.
3. **Event Rebates:** As a Red (Event) card, it triggers rebates from **Media Group** (+3 MC) and **Interplanetary Cinematics** (+2 MC). With these active, you can actually **gain money** while stealing from an opponent.
4. **Milestone Padding:** One of the cheapest ways to add an "Event" tag to your count for the **Legend** milestone (Elysium map).

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Late Game Decay:** In the final generations, 3 MC or 2 steel rarely changes the outcome of the game. Unless you need the event tag for a milestone, do not draft this late.
- **Multplayer Dilution:** In 4-5 player games, attacking one player only helps you and the other players who weren't hit. Use it tactically on the leader or to prevent a specific player from reaching a resource threshold.
- **Target Availability:** Ensure an opponent actually has the resources to steal. Check the player boards before committing your draft action.

### Tips
- **Robinson Industries Counter:** Use Hired Raiders to drop an opponent's MC or Steel just below yours, potentially messing up the **Robinson Industries** ability for that generation.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in Generations 1-3, especially in 2-player games. Essential for anyone with *Media Group*. Skip late unless racing for an event-based milestone.
