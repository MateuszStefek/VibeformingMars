# Project: Artificial Lake (#117)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 15 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** Min -6°C
- **Effect:** Place 1 Ocean tile on an area not reserved for oceans.


## Strategic Analysis (The Board Disruptor)
**Status: A-TIER TACTICAL / MID-GAME UTILITY**

Artificial Lake is a unique and powerful board-control card. Its ability to place an ocean on a non-reserved tile allows for massive tactical swings in points, placement bonuses, and milestone contention.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Rule-Breaking Placement:** Placing an ocean on land allows you to "leech" points for your own cities or the **Capital** in areas where oceans are normally forbidden.
2. **Milestone & Award Catalyst:** On the **Elysium** map, it is the MVP for the **Estate Dealer** award (most tiles next to oceans). You can drop an ocean in the middle of a landmass to suddenly make multiple cities/greeneries adjacent to water.
3. **High-Value Bonuses:** Allows you to target land spots with lucrative placement bonuses (like 2 cards or 4 MC) that are typically reserved for cities or greeneries.
4. **Steel Sink:** As a Building card, it is one of the best ways to spend excess steel. For players with metal production, this is significantly cheaper and more flexible than a 18 MC Standard Project ocean.
5. **Synergies:**
   - **Arctic Algae:** Triggers the 2-plant gain in a location of your choice.
   - **Lakefront Resorts:** Provides an immediate income boost on placement.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Temperature Threshold:** The -6°C requirement is the card's greatest weakness. In fast-paced games, the 9 ocean tiles are often placed *before* the temperature reaches this limit, rendering Artificial Lake a dead card.
- **Ocean Cap:** Once all 9 ocean tiles are on the board, this card **cannot** be played. Monitor the ocean track closely before drafting or holding this card into the late game.
- **Early Game Dead-Weight:** Do not keep this in your opening hand unless you have a path to rapid heat production. It will clog your hand and waste 3 MC of draft capital for several generations.

### Tips
- **Hostile Blocking:** Use Artificial Lake to occupy a spot an opponent is clearly "walking" toward with their greenery chain. An ocean on land is a permanent obstacle that can ruin an opponent's board scoring potential.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in the mid-game (Generations 4-7) if oceans are still available and the temperature is rising. Essential for milestone-focused strategies on Elysium. Skip if oceans are already mostly placed.
