# Corporation: Factorum

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 37 MC, 1 Steel Prod
- **Ability:** Gain Energy production or draw Building cards.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: B-TIER / ENGINE**

Consistent building-tag engine with built-in energy solutions for industrial projects.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
Key for 'Builder' milestone. Claim the steel placement spots early to snowball production.
### Hellas
Extremely strong map due to the abundance of steel placement bonuses in the south.
### Elysium
Watch out for 'Industrialist' award. Placement bonuses are more spread out, so card draw is more vital.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Factorum's unique mechanics.
