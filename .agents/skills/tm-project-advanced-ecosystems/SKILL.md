# Project: Advanced Ecosystems (#135)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Plant, Microbe, Animal
- **Requirements:** 1 Plant tag, 1 Microbe tag, 1 Animal tag.
- **VP:** 3


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Engine Payoff)
**Status: S-TIER VP SOURCE / THE BIO-TRINITY TRIGGER**

Advanced Ecosystems is one of the most point-efficient cards in the game. It serves as the ultimate payoff for players who have specialized in biological engines, providing a massive point swing for a very low cost.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Efficiency:** For 11 MC (+3 to draft), you receive 3 VP. This 4.6 MC/VP ratio is nearly unbeatable for a non-multiplier card.
2. **Triple Tag Synergy:** Having **Plant**, **Microbe**, and **Animal** tags on a single card makes it the premier trigger for biological engines.
   - **Decomposers:** Triggers 3 times (once for each tag), providing an immediate 1 VP.
   - **Ecological Zone:** Triggers for both the Plant and Animal tags, providing 2 animals (1 VP).
   - **Viral Enhancers:** Grants 3 plants immediately upon play.
3. **Milestone Powerhouse:** On the **Elysium** map, this card is a "silver bullet" for the **Ecologist** milestone (4 bio tags), as it provides three of the four required tags in one action.
4. **Vitor Synergy:** As a card with a non-negative VP icon, it triggers Vitor's 3 MC rebate.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Requirement Bottleneck:** You **must** have all three bio-tags already in play. Strategic players use "cheap" enablers like *Tardigrades* (Microbe), *Lichen* (Plant), or *Ccaretaker Coyotes* (Animal) specifically to unlock this card.
- **Solo Mode Inefficiency:** In Solo play, the 14 MC investment provides zero terraforming value. Only play it in the final generation if you have excess cash and need the points for a high score.

### Tips
- **Hate-Drafting:** If an opponent has a biological engine (Ecological Zone or Decomposers), you **must** cut this card. Allowing them to land 5+ VP (3 base + 2 from synergies) for 11 MC is often a game-ending mistake.
- **Greens Party:** If playing *Turmoil*, prioritize playing this when the **Greens** are in power to receive a 3 MC rebate for its tags.

**Recommendation:** A "Must-Draft" for any engine-building strategy. High priority for bio-engineers. Aim to play between Generations 6 and 9 once your enablers are established.
