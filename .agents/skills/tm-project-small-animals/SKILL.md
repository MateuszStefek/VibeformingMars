# Project: Small Animals (#052)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 6 MC
- **Tags:** Animal
- **Requirements:** 6% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease any player's Plant production 1 step.
- **Action:** Add 1 Animal to this card.
- **VP:** 1 VP per 2 Animals on this card.


## Strategic Analysis (The Bio-Disruptor)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER IN 2-PLAYER GAMES**

Small Animals is a high-utility biological card that excels in disruption and tempo. While its point generation is slower than high-tier animal cards, its low cost and offensive potential make it a staple for mid-game stabilization and bio-engine synergy.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost-to-Disruption:** For only 6 MC (+3 to draft), you destroy ~10-12 MC worth of an opponent's investment (1 plant production). In 2-player games, this is a massive swing that can delay an opponent's greenery for several generations.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides a rare early-game Animal tag with a relatively low requirement (6% Oxygen).
   - **Requirement Bridge:** Essential for hitting the Animal tag requirement for **Advanced Ecosystems**.
   - **Bio-Trinity Support:** Triggers *Ecological Zone*, *Viral Enhancers*, and *Decomposers* for a very low upfront cost.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Miranda (Colonies):** Allows you to trade for animals, effectively doubling the card's point yield.
   - **Arklight / Splice:** These corporations benefit specifically from animal tags or resource gains.
   - **Vitor:** Triggers the 3 MC rebate for playing a card with a VP icon.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Low Point Floor:** Without animal-adding effects, Small Animals only generates 0.5 VP per generation. If played after Generation 7, the 9 MC investment is unlikely to return even 1 VP.
- **Predator Buffet:** Small Animals is a prime target for **Predators** or **Ants**. Opponents will often "harvest" your small animals to feed their own 1:1 VP engines. Only play early if you have a way to protect your resources or can convert them to TR/VP quickly.
- **Mandatory Target:** You **must** decrease someone's plant production. If all opponents are at 0, you must hit yourself. Check the board before drafting.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bypassing:** Use **Inventrix** or **Special Design** to drop Small Animals at 4% or 5% Oxygen. This allows you to steal an opponent's plant production right as they are hitting their first major biological requirement, potentially stalling their entire engine.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft in 2-player games or if you have bio-engine synergies. Excellent as a cheap "filler" tag for high-tier requirements. Skip early in large multiplayer games where disruption is less focused.
