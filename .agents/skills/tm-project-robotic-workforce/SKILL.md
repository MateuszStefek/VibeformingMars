# Project: Robotic Workforce (#030)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 9 MC
- **Tags:** Science
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Copy the production box (the lower panel) of any Building-tagged card you have in play.


## Strategic Analysis (The Engine Doubler)
**Status: S-TIER ENGINE PIECE / HIGH-SKILL CEILING**

Robotic Workforce is widely considered one of the most powerful and versatile cards in the game. It allows you to double the output of your most efficient building-tagged card for a fraction of its original cost, creating massive economic and industrial surges.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Unrivaled Production Efficiency:** For only 9 MC (+3 to draft), you can copy a card like **Mohole Area** (+4 heat) or **Strip Mine** (+2 steel, +1 titanium). This provides production at a much better ratio than playing a second standalone proiect.
2. **Double Synergy Catalyst:** Provides a requirement-free **Science tag**. This is essential for unlocking *AI Central* or *Mars University* while simultaneously fixing your economy.
3. **Versatility:**
   - **Early Game:** Copy a Prelude card (like *Martian Industries*) or your Corporation (if it has a Building tag and production, e.g., *Utopia Invest*) to jumpstart your engine in Generation 1.
   - **Late Game:** Copy **Medical Lab** to boost income based on building tags or **Water Splitting Plant** to finish the terraforming tracks.
4. **Hate-Drafting:** Even if you don't have a great target, you should cut this card from the draft to prevent an opponent from doubling a powerful engine piece.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Building Tags Only:** You **cannot** copy production on cards without a Building tag (e.g., *Sponsors*, *Acquired Company*).
- **Production Box Only:** You copy **only** the production (the brown panel). You do **not** receive placement bonuses, immediate TR, or blue card actions from the copied card.
- **Negative Production:** You **must** also copy any negative production. If you copy a card with +4 MC and -1 Energy, you lose 1 energy production.

### Tips
- **Stealth Power:** Hold Robotic Workforce until you have your highest-value target on the board. Playing it early for a +1 production is a waste; wait for the +4 or the complex industrial jump of a *Strip Mine*.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in almost any hand. Priority 1 in drafts for engine-builders. Essential for anyone Pursuing a metal or science strategy. skip only in extreme rush scenarios where you lack building tags.
