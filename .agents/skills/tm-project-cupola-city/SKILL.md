# Project: Cupola City (#065)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 16 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** Max 9% Oxygen
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps. Place a City tile.
- **VP:** 1 VP per adjacent Greenery.


## Strategic Analysis (The Reliable Urbanizer)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER OPENER**

Cupola City is one of the most efficient early-game city cards. While it requires a modest energy investment, its high income boost and role as a "standard project killer" make it a staple for establishing board dominance and economic momentum.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Income Efficiency:** For only 16 MC (+3 to draft), you receive +3 MC production and a city placement. Even accounting for the 1 energy penalty (~7 MC value), the total investment of ~26 MC is only 1 MC more than a Standard Project city, but provides **3x the income**.
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Building** and a **City** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making it an excellent way to convert mineral wealth into liquid income and points.
   - **City Tag:** Contributes toward the **Mayor** milestone and triggers "whenever a city is played" effects.
3. **Board Control:** Its relatively low cost and requirement-free status (if Oxygen is low) make it a premier tool for "sniping" high-value placement bonuses or blocking opponents' greenery paths early.
4. **Synergies:**
   - **Tharsis Republic:** The premier pairing. Triggers their passive income gain and secures board control for a very low net cost.
   - **Rover Construction / Pets:** Triggers these board engines for early resource generation.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Oxygen Barrier:** The "Max 9% Oxygen" limit is a strict cutoff. In fast-paced games, this card can become a "dead draw" as early as Generation 4 or 5. **Play it early** to establish your income snowball.
- **Energy Bottleneck:** Do not play this if you are already energy-starved. Tying up your energy engine in Generations 1-3 can prevent you from trading with high-value colonies.

### Tips
- **Mayor Race:** Use Cupola City specifically to reach the 3-city threshold for the **Mayor** milestone before opponents can react. Its low cost makes it much harder for opponents to calculate your ability to suddenly claim the 5 points.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in Generations 1-3 for any corporation, especially those with Steel synergies or Tharsis Republic. skip after Generation 5 unless needed for a specific milestone.
