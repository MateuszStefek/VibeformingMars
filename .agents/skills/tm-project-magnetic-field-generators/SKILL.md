# Project: Magnetic Field Generators (#036)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 20 MC
- **Tags:** Building
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 4 steps. Increase your Plant production 2 steps. Raise your Terraform Rating (TR) 3 steps.


## Strategic Analysis (The Late-Game Conversion)
**Status: C-TIER ENGINE / S-TIER AWARD SNIPER**

Magnetic Field Generators is a high-cost conversion card that "trades" long-term energy infrastructure for immediate points and biological growth. While its massive energy requirement makes it unplayable in the early game, it is one of the most efficient ways to spend accumulated building resources in the final generations.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Steel Sink Points:** As a Building card, the entire 20 MC cost can be paid with Steel. In the final turn, this is one of the most efficient ways to convert surplus metal into 3 VP (via TR).
2. **Immediate TR Spike:** Provides 3 TR instantly, which increases your income and score. This is vital for "sniping" the **Terraformer** milestone or the **Benefactor** award in the final round.
3. **Bio-Engine Catalyst:** The 2 plant production is a significant boost that can often secure one final greenery conversion before the game ends.
4. **Energy "Laundry":** By the end of the game, energy production often becomes "dead weight" (especially if temperature is maxed). This card allowed you to "wash" that useless stat into guaranteed points.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Bottleneck:** Decreasing energy by 4 steps is a massive economic hit (~28 MC value). **Never play this early** unless you have a massive energy surplus from cards like *Mass Converter*. Crippling your energy engine in Generations 1-4 will prevent you from trading with colonies or powering cities.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** In games with *Colonies*, energy is the currency for trading. Sacrificing 4 energy production is almost always a mistake until the very last generation when trading is no longer possible.
- **Vacuum Efficiency:** Without steel or a massive energy surplus, paying 23 MC (cost + draft) for 3 TR is statistically poor.

### Tips
- **Final Round Finish:** Hold Magnetic Field Generators until the final generation. Use it as a "surprise" 3-point swing to overtake an opponent in TR.
- **Solo Mode MVP:** In the "TR 63" solo challenge, this card is high-priority as it provides one of the largest single-card TR jumps in the game.

**Recommendation:** A low-priority draft in the first two-thirds of the game. A high-priority draft in the final 2 generations if you have high Steel production or surplus Energy. Skip early to preserve trading capacity.
