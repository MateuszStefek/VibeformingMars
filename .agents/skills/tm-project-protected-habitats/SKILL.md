# Project: Protected Habitats (#173)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 5 MC
- **Tags:** None
- **Requirements:** None
- **Effect:** Opponents may not remove your Plant, Microbe, or Animal resources.


## Strategic Analysis (The Game-Ending Insurance)
**Status: S-TIER PASSIVE / TOP-TIER UTILITY**

Protected Habitats is one of the most powerful and undervalued cards in *Terraforming Mars*. For a negligible cost, it removes a major axis of interaction from the game, allowing you to build and "float" massive amounts of resources without fear of destruction or theft.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **The "Plant Floating" Strategy:** Without this card, players must spend plants as soon as they hit 8 to avoid losing them to *Asteroids*. With Protected Habitats, you can safely accumulate 20+ plants to drop multiple greeneries in a single turn, maximizing city adjacency bonuses and "sniping" board milestones.
2. **Hard Counter to Predators:** Completely defuses **Predators**, **Ants**, and **Virus**. It allows you to safely invest in long-term VP engines like *Birds* or *Fish* without the risk of a single card wiping out several generations of progress.
3. **Low Opportunity Cost:** At only 5 MC (+3 to draft), it pays for itself if it saves even 2 plants or 1 animal over the course of the game. It is a "one-and-done" insurance policy that remains active forever.
4. **Turmoil Synergy:** Protects your resources from negative Global Events that target player resources.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Zero Tags:** The card's only real weakness is its lack of tags. It provides zero synergy for *Science* requirements or milestones.
- **Solo Mode Inefficiency:** This card is **completely useless** in Solo play, as there are no opponents to remove your resources. **Never buy this in a solo game.**
- **Production Attacks:** Remember that Protected Habitats does **not** protect your production. Opponents can still use cards like *Sulphur-Eating Bacteria* or *Mining Expedition* to lower your production steps.

### Tips
- **Stealth Power:** Do not play Protected Habitats immediately in Generation 1 if you have zero plants or bio-engines. Hold it in your hand and drop it the moment you establish your first animal or hit 4+ plants. This forces opponents to waste their Asteroids on other players before they realize you are untouchable.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Never-Pass" draft priority for anyone building a bio-engine or a greenery strategy. Priority 1 for **Ecoline** or anyone holding **Large Convoy**. Skip only in Solo mode.
