# Corporation: Splice Tactical Genomics

## Corp Data

- **Included in:** Promo
- **Starting MC:** 44 MC, 1 Microbe Tag
- **Ability:** Find Microbe cards; gain MC when Microbe tags are played.

## Strategic Analysis
**Status: A-TIER / MICROBE ENGINE**

The core of any microbe-based strategy. Benefits from everyone's plays.

## Map Analysis
### Tharsis (Base Map)
High priority for 'Mayor' and 'Gardener'. Use the early game to claim the double-ocean placement bonus for cards/plants.
### Hellas
Relevance of the South Pole for heat bonuses. Target 'Rim Settler' if playing space tags, or 'Polar Explorer' for easy adjacency points.
### Elysium
Focus on the 3-plant placement bonus. 'Generalist' is an easy milestone if you can balance production early.


### Tips
- Standard competitive play applies. Monitor opponent milestones.

**Recommendation:** A solid pick for players who enjoy Splice Tactical Genomics's unique mechanics.
