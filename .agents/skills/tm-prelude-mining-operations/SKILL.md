# Prelude: Mining Operations

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Prelude
- **Effect:** Increase your Steel production 2 steps. Gain 4 Steel.
- **Tag:** Building.

## Strategic Analysis (The Industrial Accelerator)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / S-TIER FOR MINING GUILD**

Mining Operations is a high-efficiency infrastructure card used primarily to jumpstart a building-heavy engine. While it provides significant resources, its value is strictly dependent on having "sinks" (expensive building cards) in your starting hand to utilize the metal.

### Why it is a good pick:
1. **Exceptional Net Value:** The 2 steel production and 4 starting steel is worth ~21 MC of value in Generation 1. This matches the Prelude benchmark and provides a stable industrial foundation for the rest of the game.
2. **Immediate Engine Jumpstart:** The 4 starting steel allows you to play high-cost building projects like **Mohole Area**, **Research Outpost**, or **Space Elevator** in Generation 1 for zero net liquid MC.
3. **Synergies:**
   - **Mining Guild:** The premier pairing. Triggers the corporation's passive income and provides the steel needed for their dominant industrial strategy.
   - **Advanced Alloys:** Each steel becomes worth 3 MC, making the Prelude's production worth 50% more.
   - **Manutech:** Triggers an immediate cash gain for increasing production steps.
   - **Robotic Workforce:** Provides a Building-tagged production box that can be copied to double your steel yield for only 9 MC.
4. **Milestone Support:** Provides a Building tag toward the **Builder** milestone and contributes to the **Industrialist** and **Miner** awards.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Steel Sink Requirement:** Steel production is only valuable if you draw Building tags. If your hand is full of space tags or bio-engines, Mining Operations is a "dead" Prelude that traps your capital in a non-snowballing resource.
- **Colonies Trade-off:** If the **Ceres** colony is in play, players can easily trade for steel. This saturates the market and makes dedicated steel production less valuable compared to pure MC production.

### Tips
- **Requirement Bumping:** Use the Building tag on Mining Operations specifically to hit the requirements for **Research Outpost** or trigger building discounts in Generation 1. This accelerates your industrial rollout by a full generation.

**Recommendation:** A solid "Safe" pick if you have at least 2-3 building cards in hand. High priority for **Mining Guild** or anyone holding **Robotic Workforce**. skip early in favor of flexible MC production if your hand is bio-heavy.
