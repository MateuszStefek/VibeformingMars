# Project: Business Network (#148)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Corporate Era)
- **Cost:** 4 MC
- **Tags:** Earth
- **Requirements:** None
- **Action:** Look at the top card of the deck. You may either buy it for 3 MC or discard it.
- **Production:** Decrease your MegaCredit (MC) production 1 step.


## Strategic Analysis (The Precision Filter)
**Status: B-TIER ENGINE / S-TIER FOR POINT LUNA**

Business Network is a low-cost filtering tool that allows you to "dig" for win conditions without clogging your hand. While it carries a permanent economic penalty, its ability to provide optional card advantage makes it a staple for established engine builders.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Quality over Quantity:** Unlike raw draw engines, you only spend the 3 MC if you actually want the card. This saves you money in the long run by filtering out "junk" cards that you cannot afford or don't need.
2. **Earth Tag Catalyst:** The most valuable part of the card in the 2024-2025 meta. 
   - **Point Luna:** Triggers an immediate free card draw upon play, effectively making the card "free" in terms of card advantage.
   - **Teractor:** Reduces the play cost to 1 MC, making the break-even point much faster.
   - **Cartels:** Contributes toward your Earth-based income engine, effectively neutralizing the card's penalty.
3. **Tempo Stalling:** Like other action cards, it provides a valuable "pass" action to wait out opponents' moves without ending your turn.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **MC Production Penalty:** Decreasing your MC production in Generation 1-2 is a heavy price. Only play this early if you have the Earth synergies or a massive starting budget. It is often best played in Generations 3-5 once your income is stable.
- **Comparison to Inventors' Guild:** *Inventors' Guild* is generally superior for science-heavy engines because it lacks the MC production penalty and has a more valuable Science tag. Use Business Network when you lack the budget for the Guild or need the Earth tag.

### Tips
- **Mid-Game Pivot:** Use Business Network to find the "missing piece" of your engine (e.g., the last science tag for *AI Central* or a specific Jovian multiplier). Its value is highest when you have the income but lack the options.

**Recommendation:** A high-priority draft if you have Earth synergies or are playing as **Point Luna**. High priority for anyone lacking a card-draw engine. skip early if your hand is full of high-cost production cards.
