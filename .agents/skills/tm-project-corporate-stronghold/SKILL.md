# Project: Corporate Stronghold (#053)

## Card Data
- **Included in:** Base Game (Basic)
- **Cost:** 11 MC
- **Tags:** Building, City
- **Requirements:** 1 Energy Production (must be able to decrease it).
- **Effect:** Decrease your Energy production 1 step. Increase your MegaCredit (MC) production 3 steps. Place a City tile.
- **VP:** -2 (but gains 1 VP per adjacent Greenery).


## Strategic Analysis (The Board Presence Anchor)
**Status: B-TIER UTILITY / A-TIER OPENER**

Corporate Stronghold is a high-tempo early-game card used to secure board presence and establish an economic lead. While its -2 VP penalty seems steep, its low cost and city placement make it a premier tool for establishing dominance in the first generations.

### Why it is a top-tier play:
1. **Exceptional Cost-to-City Ratio:** At only 11 MC (+3 to draft), it is one of the cheapest city placements in the game. Even accounting for the 1 energy penalty (~7 MC value), the total investment of ~21 MC is 4 MC cheaper than a Standard Project city, but provides **triple the income** (3 MC prod).
2. **Double Tag Synergy:** Provides both a **Building** and a **City** tag.
   - **Building Tag:** Allows you to pay for the card entirely with **Steel**, making the investment effectively "free" in terms of liquid MC.
   - **City Tag:** Contributes toward the **Mayor** milestone and triggers "whenever a city is played" effects.
3. **Early Game Income:** +3 MC production in Generation 1 provides a steady income boost that helps fund your future industrial engine.
4. **Milestone Support:** One of the most efficient ways to secure the **Mayor** milestone (3 cities) on a budget.

### The "Trap" Check:
- **Energy Requirement:** You **MUST** have 1 Energy production in play to play this card. This often makes it an illegal play in Gen 1 unless you have an Energy-providing Prelude (like *Supplier* or *Power Generation*) or Corporation (*Thorgate*).
- **Victory Point Penalty:** The -2 VP is a significant price. You **must** surround the Stronghold with at least **3 greeneries** just to "break even" on the card's printed value (1 VP per adjacency).
- **Late Game irrelevance:** In the final 2 generations, the 3 MC production is negligible. Only play late if you strictly need the city to block an opponent's high-VP spot or to secure a milestone.

### Tips
- **Adjacency Sniping:** Use Corporate Stronghold to occupy a spot adjacent to several ocean tiles. This grants you the 2 MC per ocean rebate immediately, often making the card effectively cost **zero MC** while securing board dominance.

**Recommendation:** A top-tier "Auto-Keep" in Generations 1-3 for any corporation, especially those with Steel synergies or Tharsis Republic. skip after Generation 5 unless needed for a specific milestone.
